# Micro-Timeline: Trump-Russia Data Flow

The documented timeline of US campaign data flowing to Russian intelligence via CA-adjacent intermediaries.

- `2014-2016` — [[Paul Manafort]] in financial distress; debt to Russian-linked oligarchs
- `2016-03-29` — Manafort joins Trump campaign as convention manager
- `2016-05-19` — Manafort promoted to Trump campaign manager
- `2016-08-02` — Manafort meets [[Konstantin Kilimnik]] at Grand Havana Room (NYC) — shares polling data, battleground state strategy (documented by Mueller and Senate Intel Vol 5)
- `2016-08-19` — Manafort resigns from Trump campaign (Ukraine-related disclosures)
- `2016-09-12` — Manafort meets Kilimnik again in Madrid
- `2017-08-04` — [[Michael Flynn]] amends financial filing to disclose CA advisory role during 2016 Trump campaign
- `2017-12-14` — [[Robert Mueller]] requests CA emails of Trump campaign workers
- `2018-08` — [[Sam Patten]] pleads guilty to FARA violations (Russia-linked Ukrainian work)
- `2018-08-21` — Manafort convicted on multiple charges
- `2019-03` — Manafort sentenced; convicted of tax fraud, bank fraud, etc.
- `2019-03-22` — Mueller report released
- `2020-08-18` — [[Source Senate Intel Vol 5 2020]] establishes Kilimnik as Russian intelligence officer
- `2020-12-23` — Trump pardons Manafort
- `2021-04` — US Treasury OFAC formally identifies Kilimnik as Russian intelligence officer with sanctions

## Why this is a separate timeline

The Trump-Russia data flow is the **most well-documented post-CA continuation thread** that ties the CA-era directly to Russian state intelligence. It belongs as its own timeline because it spans multiple entity timelines and connects [[Cambridge Analytica]] to [[Mueller Investigation]] to [[Source Senate Intel Vol 5 2020]].
