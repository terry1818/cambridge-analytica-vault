# Micro-Timeline: [[Alexander Nix]]

The full chronology of Alexander Nixs career arc from Eton through CA collapse to UK director disqualification.

- `~1975` — born (UK)
- `1990s` — Eton College, then University of Manchester
- `early-2000s` — financial sector / Wesleyan University post-grad work
- `pre-2013` — joins SCL Group / SCL Elections under [[Nigel Oakes]]
- `2013-01` — co-founds [[Cambridge Analytica]] as SCL USA subsidiary; becomes CEO
- `2014` — oversees CA US market entry; 44 US races in 2014 midterms
- `2014` — CA buys data from [[Global Science Research (GSR)]] / [[Aleksandr Kogan]]
- `2015-01` — officially appointed director of CA at Companies House
- `2015-07` — Cruz partnership becomes public ([[Source Vogel 2015-07-07 Politico Cruz psychographic]])
- `2015-10` — WaPo reports Cruz campaign paid CA $750K ([[Source Sellers 2015-10-19 WaPo Cruz 750K]])
- `2015-11` — Bloomberg profile of CAs Cruz work ([[Source Issenberg 2015-11-12 Bloomberg Cruz data miner]])

- `2016-02-20` — Cruz drops CA after South Carolina loss
- `2016-05` — Mercers switch CA to Trump after Cruz drops out
