# Connection: Trump-Epstein social association 1987-2004

## What this connection establishes (and what it does not)

**Establishes** (Tier 1): a long-running documented personal and social acquaintance between [[Donald Trump]] and [[Jeffrey Epstein]] from approximately the late 1980s through the early-to-mid 2000s, including documented events at Mar-a-Lago, Trump Tower, and Epstein's NYC mansion, plus passenger appearances on Epstein aircraft. They moved in overlapping social circles in NYC and Palm Beach for ~15 years.

**Does NOT establish**: any allegation of sexual misconduct by Donald Trump with any Epstein-trafficking victim. No witness has named Trump in sworn testimony as a participant in trafficked sexual contact. No civil or criminal action has alleged this against Trump. The connection is documented social acquaintance, not allegation of criminal conduct.

This is the canonical case where evidence-tier discipline matters most.
