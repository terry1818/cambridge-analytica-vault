# Connection: Mercer Family → CA / Emerdata money flow

## What the link is

Sustained financial flows from [[Robert Mercer]] (and family / Mercer Family Foundation) into Cambridge Analytica's operations, plus direct flows into the pro-Trump [[Make America Number 1 Super PAC]] which then paid CA — and subsequently into [[Emerdata Limited]] as successor.

## Evidence

- **Robert Mercer** invested minimum $15M in CA directly per NYT estimate ([[Source Wikipedia - Cambridge Analytica]])
- **Steve Bannon** held $1-5M CA stake (Mercer-aligned)
- **Make America Number 1 Super PAC** (Mercer-funded) paid CA for 2016 work
- **Rebekah and Jennifer Mercer** became directors of Emerdata in March 2018
- **Mercer Family Foundation** former staffer Jacquelyn James-Varga became SCL Group director

## Confidence

`#confidence/high` — multiple corroborated primary sources

## Implications

Mercer money was a primary enabler of CA's US operations. The transition to Emerdata indicates Mercer interests retained the data infrastructure post-collapse.
