# Wilks brothers to conservative media funding pipeline

A connection note documenting the [[Wilks brothers]] (Farris and Dan Wilks) operational funding of contemporary conservative-media infrastructure. The Wilks brothers are the principal post-2010 religious-right donor family whose operational scale rivals the historical [[Mercer]] / [[DeVos Family Network]] / [[Bradley Foundation]] axis.

## Principal documented funding recipients

**[[Daily Wire]]:** The Wilks brothers were substantial early funders (2015-onward) and have remained continuing operational sponsors. Daily Wire's post-2020 substantial operational expansion (Daily Wire Films, Bentkey children's content, DailyWire+ streaming, podcast network expansion) was substantially Wilks-funded.

**[[PragerU]]:** Substantial multi-year giving from Wilks-aligned funding vehicles. PragerU Kids state-curriculum-adoption programme was substantially Wilks-supported.

**[[Ted Cruz]] 2016 presidential campaign:** Substantial direct-and-PAC giving via the [[Keep the Promise PAC]] structure (Mercer-coordinated but Wilks-funded).

**Various religious-right political-advocacy organisations:** Sustained giving across the broader religious-right institutional infrastructure.

**[[Conservative Partnership Institute]]:** Adjacent funding.

**Anti-abortion litigation infrastructure:** Substantial multi-year giving.

## Operational distinctiveness

The Wilks brothers' funding pattern is distinctive within the broader conservative-movement donor architecture:

- **Operationally independent of CNP coordination** - the Wilks-aligned funding decisions are made principally outside the [[Council for National Policy]] coordination layer that the Mercer-DeVos-broader-CNP donor architecture operates within
- **Direct-relationship-anchored** - Wilks funding decisions are typically made through direct relationships with specific recipient operators rather than through CNP-style coordination
- **Religious-positioning anchored** - the brothers' Assembly of Yahweh denominational positioning produces distinctive theological-political alignment that other donor families do not share
- **Fracking-wealth substrate** - the wealth source (Frac Tech Services sale 2011, approximately .5B family wealth) is operationally distinct from the financial-services-wealth substrate of most CA-era patron-wing donors

## Why this connection is load-bearing

The Wilks-media operational funding picture is one of the principal mechanisms through which contemporary conservative-media-as-cultural-product has scaled. The shift from conservative media as principally news-and-opinion (the Fox News / talk-radio era) to conservative media as entertainment-and-cultural-formation (the Daily Wire / PragerU era) was substantially Wilks-funded.
