# Donors Trust to conservative movement map

A connection note documenting the [[Donors Trust]] pass-through architecture connecting anonymous donor capital to conservative-movement institutional recipients. Donors Trust is the principal anonymizing pass-through for the conservative-movement donor architecture; the connection note maps the recipient network.

## Documented downstream recipients

Substantial journalistic reconstruction (Center for Media and Democracy, Mother Jones, ProPublica, adjacent investigative-journalism) has documented Donors Trust and Donors Capital Fund grants flowing to:

- [[Heritage Foundation]] and [[Heritage Action]]
- [[Federalist Society]]
- [[State Policy Network]] affiliates across the 50-state network
- [[ALEC]] (American Legislative Exchange Council)
- [[True the Vote]] and adjacent election-administration-challenge organisations
- [[Project Veritas]]
- [[Alliance Defending Freedom]]
- [[Family Research Council]]
- [[Heartland Institute]] and adjacent climate-skepticism organisations
- [[Center for Immigration Studies]] and adjacent anti-immigration organisations
- [[CO2 Coalition]] and adjacent fossil-fuel-aligned scientific-positioning organisations
- The broader conservative-movement institutional ecosystem at smaller-recipient scale

Annual grant volume has been in the - range across recent years.

## Operational mechanism

The Donors Trust mechanism: donor contributes to Donors Trust (receiving immediate charitable tax deduction); donor advises Donors Trust on which 501(c)(3) recipients should receive distributions; recipient organisation sees the grant as coming from Donors Trust rather than the original donor. The original-donor identity is operationally invisible in recipient 990 filings.

## Why this connection is load-bearing

Removing Donors Trust from the network would force conservative-movement donor giving back into direct-recipient relationships that would expose original-donor identities. The expected operational consequences of that hypothetical exposure: substantial donor recruitment-and-retention difficulty; substantial reputational consequences for major-donor reputations; substantial increase in advocacy-organisation defensive positioning around donor-relationship visibility.

The Donors Trust architecture is therefore the structural mechanism that makes the broader conservative-movement donor-architecture operationally sustainable at current scale.
