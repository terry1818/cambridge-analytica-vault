# Catholic Integralism to JD Vance positioning

A connection note documenting the intellectual-influence pathway from [[Catholic Integralism]] intellectual venues to [[JD Vance]] political positioning. The pathway is operationally significant because Vance is the highest-profile US political figure to have publicly engaged with Catholic Integralism intellectual material, and his vice-presidential position elevates that engagement to the highest political level.

## Documented intellectual-influence pathway

Vance's documented Catholic Integralism engagement includes:

- **Patrick Deneen reading and engagement** - Vance has publicly cited [[Patrick Deneen]]'s "Why Liberalism Failed" (2018) as a foundational intellectual text; documented multiple Deneen-Vance personal conversations
- **Adrian Vermeule engagement** - Vance has cited [[Adrian Vermeule]]'s "Common Good Constitutionalism" (2022) and the broader Vermeule intellectual programme in policy commentary
- **First Things magazine venue** - Vance has been a recurring presence at [[First Things magazine]] events and adjacent intellectual venues
- **National Conservatism Conference participation** - Vance has been a recurring NatCon speaker; the NatCon ecosystem is substantially Catholic-Integralism-adjacent
- **Compact magazine engagement** - Vance has been engaged with [[Sohrab Ahmari]]'s Compact magazine intellectual circle
- **Personal Catholic conversion 2019** - Vance's 2019 Catholic conversion provides personal-religious-identity grounding that the broader Catholic Integralism intellectual frame depends on

## Operational consequences

The intellectual-influence pathway has produced operational consequences:

- **Vance Senate positioning 2023-2024** - substantial post-liberal policy positioning aligned with Catholic Integralism intellectual frame
- **Vance vice-presidential selection 2024** - the selection was operationally significant as the most explicit post-liberal-aligned vice-presidential selection in US political history
- **Vance Trump 2.0 vice-presidential role** - continuing engagement with Catholic Integralism intellectual ecosystem; substantial influence on Trump 2.0 policy direction on family-policy, religious-liberty, and adjacent substantive policy categories
- **2028 succession positioning** - Vance is the most plausible 2028 Republican presidential nominee; the Catholic Integralism intellectual frame substantially shapes his policy positioning going into that cycle
