# Three-wing coalition operational coordination mechanism

A connection note documenting the operational coordination mechanism by which the three wings of the contemporary Trump 2.0 coalition (patron / theocratic / tech-surveillance) actually integrate across cluster boundaries. The [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]] articulates the analytical claim; this connection note documents the operational mechanism.

## The coordination architecture

The three wings coordinate principally through four operational layers:

**Layer 1: [[Council for National Policy]] cross-organisational membership.** CNP membership includes principals from the patron wing (Mercer family adjacencies, DeVos family, Adelson family, broader large-donor base), the theocratic wing (Family Research Council leadership, Fellowship-adjacent figures, Alliance Defending Freedom leadership, broader religious-right institutional leadership), and increasingly the tech-surveillance wing (Thiel-adjacent figures, broader Tech Right intellectual leadership) at the formal-membership level. CNP's quarterly meetings provide the principal cross-wing leadership coordination venue.

**Layer 2: [[Project 2025]] partner-organisation coordination.** Project 2025's 100+ partner organisations include institutions from all three wings: patron wing (Heritage Foundation as institutional sponsor, multiple donor-funded policy organisations), theocratic wing (Family Research Council, Alliance Defending Freedom, religious-right policy organisations, Council for National Policy adjacencies), tech-surveillance wing (Foundation for American Innovation, Tech-Right-adjacent policy organisations). Project 2025 is the operational integration document where the three wings' policy priorities are reconciled into a single policy programme.

**Layer 3: Trump administration personnel selection.** Trump 2.0 cabinet and sub-cabinet appointments substantially reflect three-wing coordination: [[Russell Vought]] (theocratic-wing OMB), [[JD Vance]] (tech-surveillance-wing VP with theocratic-wing Catholic Integralism positioning), [[Elon Musk]] (tech-surveillance-wing DOGE), [[Stephen Miller]] (theocratic-wing-adjacent policy), [[Mike Huckabee]] (theocratic-wing Israel Ambassador), and adjacent appointees represent the three wings' operational integration at the executive-branch level.

**Layer 4: Cross-wing intellectual venues.** [[First Things magazine]], [[Compact magazine]], [[National Conservatism Conference]], [[Hoover Institution]] adjacent venues, the All-In Podcast circuit, and adjacent intellectual-coordination venues provide ongoing cross-wing intellectual-coordination infrastructure.

## Specific cross-wing coordination episodes

**Project 2025 mandate document production (2022-2023):** The 922-page "Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise" was operationally a cross-wing coordination document. The chapter authorship reflects three-wing personnel integration; the policy substance reconciles three-wing priorities.

**Trump 2.0 transition (November 2024 - January 2025):** The transition process operationally integrated three-wing personnel-pipeline output through the Project 2025 personnel database, the [[Conservative Partnership Institute]] coordination infrastructure, and adjacent transition-team mechanisms.

**Post-October 7 Israel-policy alignment (2023-present):** The substantial Israel-policy consolidation across the three wings reflects coordinated cross-wing positioning rather than ad-hoc convergence.

**Crypto-policy coordination (2024-2026):** The Tech-Right-led crypto-policy programme has substantially incorporated patron-wing donor coordination (Adelson-network, broader large-donor support for crypto-policy positioning) and theocratic-wing tolerance (despite religious-right intellectual scepticism of cryptocurrency, no substantial theocratic-wing opposition has emerged).

## Why this connection is load-bearing

This connection is the operational documentation of the coordination mechanism that makes the broader [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]] analytical claim non-tautological. Without documented cross-wing coordination, the three-wing classification would be principally an analytical convenience rather than a description of operational reality. The documented coordination mechanism through CNP, Project 2025, Trump administration personnel, and cross-wing intellectual venues establishes the operational reality.
