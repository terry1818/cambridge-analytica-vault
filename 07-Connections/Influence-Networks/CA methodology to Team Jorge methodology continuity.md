# CA methodology to Team Jorge methodology continuity

A connection note documenting the explicit methodological continuity from 2018-collapsed [[Cambridge Analytica]] to post-2018 Israeli commercial successor operations principally represented by [[Team Jorge]] / [[Tal Hanan]] / Demoman International.

## Methodological continuity dimensions

**Audience-segmentation and microtargeting:** Both operations used granular audience-segmentation methodology layered on top of social-media-platform advertising infrastructure. The methodology specifics evolved (CA used psychographic-overlay over demographic-and-behavioural segmentation; Team Jorge has used more sophisticated and more automated segmentation methodology), but the underlying operational pattern is continuous.

**Persona-management at scale:** Both operations deployed fake-persona infrastructure at scale across social-media platforms. CA's persona deployment was operationally more limited (the explicit CA pitch was psychographic targeting of real users); Team Jorge's persona deployment ([[AIMS]] platform supporting tens of thousands of fake accounts) has been substantially more aggressive.

**Election-targeting commercial-services model:** Both operations sold election-influence services to political-client customers across multiple jurisdictions. CA's documented client list was substantially smaller than Team Jorge's documented 33+ country deployment record, but the commercial-services model is operationally identical.

**Commercial-pitch methodology (Channel 4 vs Forbidden Stories pattern):** Both operations were eventually surfaced through substantially identical investigative-journalism methodology: undercover journalists posing as prospective clients, multiple meetings, recorded pitch material that the firm's principals did not realise was being recorded. The Channel 4 sting of [[Alexander Nix]] in 2018 and the Forbidden Stories sting of [[Tal Hanan]] in 2023 are methodologically symmetric.

## Personnel-flow evidence

Specific named personnel continuity is partially documented. The broader pattern is that the post-CA Israeli-commercial-intelligence sector substantially expanded post-2018 with personnel inflow from multiple sources, including:

- Former Israeli intelligence personnel (the [[Unit 8200]] pipeline)
- Former CA / SCL personnel (substantially smaller direct flow, but documented adjacency)
- Former Western-commercial-intelligence-sector personnel

The CA-to-Team-Jorge personnel flow is operationally less substantial than the broader methodology-and-business-model continuity.

## Why this connection is load-bearing

The connection supports the broader vault [[Hypothesis - CA methodology survived 2018 bankruptcy]] analytical claim. Team Jorge represents the strongest single piece of evidence that the CA-era methodology did not disappear with the CA corporate collapse; it continued at successor firms with substantially expanded technical sophistication.

The connection also documents the broader pattern of Israeli commercial-intelligence sector absorbing capability that was previously distributed across multiple national commercial-intelligence sectors. The CA-era saw US/UK commercial-intelligence sectors and Israeli commercial-intelligence sectors operating substantially in parallel; the post-CA era has seen substantial consolidation into the Israeli sector.
