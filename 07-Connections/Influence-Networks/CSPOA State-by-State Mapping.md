# CSPOA State-by-State Mapping

The **[[Constitutional Sheriffs and Peace Officers Association]]** (CSPOA) operates the most underdocumented US election-interference risk factor: **the "constitutional sheriff" doctrine** that claims county sheriffs have authority to refuse federal oversight including federal election monitoring.

## Geographic concentration (per ADL + SPLC + Political Research Associates reporting)

### Highest concentration states (CSPOA-aligned sheriff numbers)

| State | Estimated CSPOA-aligned sheriffs | Notable |
|---|---|---|
| **Arizona** | 8-12 | Founder Richard Mack base; major presence |
| **Idaho** | 6-10 | Owyhee County (Larry Kendrick) + others |
| **Wyoming** | 4-7 | Most county sheriffs CSPOA-aligned by proportion |
| **Texas** | 15-25 | Largest absolute count; many county sheriffs |
| **Florida** | 8-15 | Several megacounty sheriffs |
| **Oregon** | 4-8 | Rural-eastern Oregon concentration |
| **California** | 6-10 | Rural-northern + Central Valley counties |
| **Colorado** | 5-8 | Rural counties |
| **Nevada** | 3-6 | Rural counties |
| **Utah** | 4-7 | Multiple county sheriffs |
| **Tennessee** | 5-10 | Multiple counties |
| **Kentucky** | 4-8 | Multiple |
| **Missouri** | 5-9 | Multiple |
| **Oklahoma** | 4-7 | Multiple |
| **Other** | ~80-120 across remaining states | Distributed |

**Total estimated CSPOA-aligned sheriffs: ~200-250** (per ADL + Marshall Project + American Oversight reporting)

**Note:** Many sheriffs self-identify as "constitutional sheriff" without formal CSPOA membership; the ideological reach is broader than formal-membership counts.

## Why state-level concentration matters

In **CSPOA-aligned counties**:

- **County sheriff has authority to refuse federal election oversight**
- **Ballot-drop-box surveillance** without judicial authorization documented in several counties
- **Refusal to enforce federal firearm regulations** documented
- **Refusal to protect Pride events from far-right paramilitary intimidation** documented
- **Cooperation with self-deputized "vote watchers"** documented

### Federal election interference risk concentration

The federal-election-interference risk is highest in **CSPOA-aligned counties within swing states**:

- **Arizona:** Maricopa County (largest swing-state county) has historically resisted; but rural AZ counties are heavily CSPOA-aligned
- **Pennsylvania:** Rural PA counties have growing CSPOA presence
- **Wisconsin:** Rural counties; including some swing-vote counties
- **Michigan:** Upper Peninsula + rural Lower Michigan
- **Nevada:** Rural counties including swing-vote Washoe + Storey
- **Georgia:** Rural counties

## Counter-strategy implications

### Direct counter-tactics

1. **State attorney general litigation** establishing federal supremacy in election administration
2. **State legislation** clarifying sheriff election-monitoring authority limits
3. **Local-prosecutor coordination** establishing parallel enforcement authority
4. **Federal monitoring deployment** under Voting Rights Act remaining provisions

### Indirect counter-tactics

5. **Sheriff-recruiting counter-pipeline** - progressive candidates running for county sheriff (similar pattern to election-officer recruiting)
6. **Public awareness campaigns** specifically about CSPOA doctrine in target counties
7. **Press exposure of specific CSPOA-aligned sheriff actions** (most operate under-the-radar)
8. **Bar Association ethics complaints** against CSPOA-aligned attorneys
9. **National Sheriffs Association engagement** to counter-position CSPOA
