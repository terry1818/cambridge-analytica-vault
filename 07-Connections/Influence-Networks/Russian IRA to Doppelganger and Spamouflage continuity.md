# Russian IRA to Doppelganger and Spamouflage continuity

A connection note documenting the methodological continuity from the 2016-era Russian [[Internet Research Agency]] (IRA) model to the post-2022 Russian [[Doppelganger]] operation and the parallel Chinese [[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]] operation. The continuity demonstrates that state-actor information-operations methodology pioneered in the 2016 cycle has substantially scaled and diversified across the past decade.

## The 2016-era IRA model

The 2016 IRA operation (documented in [[Mueller Investigation]] Volume I and the [[Senate Intel Vol 5 2020]] report) operated approximately 470 Facebook pages, an estimated 80,000+ pieces of organic content reaching approximately 126 million Americans, plus affiliated Twitter and Instagram footprints. The IRA operation focused principally on cultural-wedge content (race, immigration, religion, gun rights) designed to widen existing US political polarisation rather than on direct candidate-advocacy.

The IRA was indicted in February 2018 (13 Russian nationals plus the IRA corporate entity); operational outputs continued at reduced scale through 2018-2022. The Russian state subsequently pivoted to the Doppelganger model.

## Doppelganger (post-2022)

[[Doppelganger]] is the principal Russian-state-aligned information-operations successor to the IRA. Documented from approximately 2022 by [[EU DisinfoLab]] (the 2022 disclosure was the principal initial publication) and [[Meta]] threat-reports. Operational pattern:

- Cloned domain spoofing of major Western news outlets (Bild, Le Figaro, Welt, La Repubblica, The Guardian, Washington Post, etc.)
- Substantial content production seeded across the cloned domains plus amplification across social-media platforms
- Substantial targeting of European and US political environments principally around Ukraine-war, NATO, and electoral-cycle topics
- Operational scale substantially larger than the 2016 IRA operation (more sites, more languages, more sophisticated cloning methodology)

The 2024 OFAC sanctions against Doppelganger-aligned entities documented the operational picture in substantial detail.

## Spamouflage Dragonbridge (parallel Chinese operation)

[[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]] is the Chinese-state-aligned parallel information-operations programme, documented from approximately 2019 by [[Graphika]] (the 2019 disclosure was the principal initial publication) and subsequent Meta threat-reports. Operational pattern:

- Substantial fake-persona infrastructure across social-media platforms
- Substantial content production focused on US-China relations, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Chinese diaspora political dynamics
- Operational scale large in fake-account terms but historically modest in actual reach (substantial documented engagement-failure pattern)
- Substantial post-2022 evolution toward US-domestic-political-content production

## Methodological convergence and divergence

The three operations represent methodological convergence on certain dimensions (fake-persona infrastructure, social-media-platform amplification, cross-jurisdictional targeting) and divergence on others (Doppelganger emphasises substantive content production with cloned-news-domain credibility-anchoring; Spamouflage emphasises volume over content quality; IRA emphasised cultural-wedge targeting over substantive policy content).

The convergent pattern: state-actor information-operations methodology has substantially professionalised across the past decade with parallel evolution across multiple state-actor programmes.

## Why this connection is load-bearing

The connection demonstrates that the 2016-era CA / IRA / parallel-operations were not anomalous one-off events. They were the visible early manifestations of a state-actor methodology that has substantially expanded across the subsequent decade. The post-2022 operational picture is substantially more sophisticated than the 2016 picture, with substantial implications for the contemporary information environment.
