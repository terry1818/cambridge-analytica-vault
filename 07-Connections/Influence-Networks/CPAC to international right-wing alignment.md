# CPAC to international right-wing alignment

A connection note documenting the [[Conservative Political Action Conference]] (CPAC) as the principal institutional venue for international right-wing coordination. CPAC operates as a multi-country conference series organised by the [[American Conservative Union]] with substantial international expansion across the past decade.

## The CPAC International expansion

**CPAC US:** The original annual conference in Washington (since 1974); the principal US conservative-movement annual coordination event. Trump first publicly endorsed by CPAC participants in 2011; substantial Trump-era coordination function.

**[[CPAC Hungary]] (since 2022):** The first European CPAC, launched 2022 with substantial [[Viktor Orban]] / Fidesz coordination. The annual Budapest event has substantially integrated Hungarian-government leadership with US conservative-movement leadership including [[Steve Bannon]], [[Tucker Carlson]] (multiple visits), [[Kari Lake]], Project 2025-affiliated figures, and broader European populist-right leadership.

**CPAC Brazil (since 2019):** Launched in São Paulo with substantial [[Jair Bolsonaro]] / [[Eduardo Bolsonaro]] coordination. The Brazilian CPAC has substantially shaped Brazilian right-wing coordination across the 2019-2022 Bolsonaro administration and the post-2022 continuing operational period.

**CPAC Australia (since 2019):** Sydney-based; substantial coordination with Australian Liberal Party right wing and adjacent infrastructure.

**CPAC Japan (since 2017):** Tokyo-based; substantial coordination with Japanese conservative-political establishment.

**CPAC Korea, CPAC Mexico, CPAC Israel** (various subsequent additions): The CPAC International network has continued expanding through the 2020-2026 period.

## Operational coordination mechanisms

The CPAC International network operationally coordinates through:

- **Speaker-circuit overlap** - the same principal US conservative-movement speakers (Bannon, Tucker Carlson, Kari Lake, various Project 2025-affiliated figures) appear at multiple CPAC venues across the year
- **Strategic-positioning coordination** - CPAC events synchronise principal political-positioning messaging across the international right-wing network
- **Personnel-pipeline coordination** - CPAC venues provide ongoing professional-networking infrastructure that coordinates US and international right-wing operational personnel
- **Funder-coordination** - CPAC events provide venues for international right-wing donor coordination beyond what direct bilateral relationships could achieve

## The Bannon-Movement integration

The CPAC International network operationally complements the [[Bannon Movement international right]] coordination work. Where Bannon Movement operated through informal-and-ad-hoc venues across 2017-2020, CPAC International provides recurring formal venue for the same coordination function. The Bannon-Movement-to-CPAC-International transition substantially institutionalised the coordination architecture.

## Why this connection is load-bearing

CPAC International is the principal venue for institutional right-wing coordination across national boundaries. Without CPAC International, the international right-wing coordination would operate principally through bilateral and informal venues with substantial loss of operational efficiency.
