# Connection: Emerdata ↔ Frontier Services Group (via Johnson Chun Shun Ko)

## What the link is

When [[Cambridge Analytica]] collapsed and [[Emerdata Limited]] became its effective successor, **[[Johnson Chun Shun Ko]] was appointed to the Emerdata board in March 2018**. Ko is a **Frontier Services Group officer**. [[Frontier Services Group]] is the Hong Kong-listed private security firm **chaired by [[Erik Prince]]** (Blackwater founder) with major Chinese state-owned investment (CITIC Group).

## Why this connection matters

- Provides Emerdata with **China-linked institutional capital and intelligence-side expertise**
- Provides FSG with **direct access to CA-lineage data and methodologies**
- Connects the [[Robert Mercer|Mercer]] political-data infrastructure with the [[Erik Prince|Prince]] private-military-intelligence world
- This is among the **most under-examined post-CA developments** in public reporting
