# Ellison Media Empire

## Overview

The **Ellison family controls one of the largest single-family ownership concentrations in modern US media + tech infrastructure history.** Spans three generations + multiple distinct entities operating in coordination + with distinct political positioning. The family's combined holdings include:

1. **Oracle Corporation** (Larry Ellison; Chairman + CTO) - $50B+ revenue enterprise software/cloud; TikTok Project Texas; Stargate AI infrastructure
2. **Paramount Skydance Corporation** (David Ellison CEO; Larry principal backer) - CBS broadcast + 60 Minutes + Paramount Pictures + Showtime + MTV + Nickelodeon + Comedy Central + BET + Pluto TV + Paramount+
3. **The Free Press / CBS News** (Bari Weiss EIC; Paramount Skydance subsidiary post-October 2025 acquisition)
4. **Annapurna Pictures** (Megan Ellison; independent prestige film) - distinctly separate political positioning
5. **Various Oracle-related federal-cloud + AI infrastructure positioning**

## Operational thesis

**The Ellison family represents the most operationally significant single tech-to-media ownership concentration in 2025-2026 US politics.** Three distinct vectors compound:

### 1. Trump-aligned media-editorial repositioning
- David Ellison's Paramount Skydance acquired Bari Weiss / The Free Press → installed her as CBS News EIC → documented post-merger editorial shift at CBS News + 60 Minutes
- Multiple high-profile journalist departures documented
- 60 Minutes Trump lawsuit settlement ($16M July 2025) widely understood as FCC-approval condition

### 2. Federal-infrastructure positioning
- Oracle hosts TikTok US data (Project Texas) → critical Trump 2.0 China policy nexus
- Oracle is primary [[Project Stargate]] tenant ($500B AI infrastructure with OpenAI + SoftBank + MGX-UAE)
- Long-standing CIA / federal-intelligence-community relationship dating to 1977 founding contract

### 3. Trump-administration regulatory alignment
- Skydance-Paramount merger required FCC approval from Trump 2.0 FCC
- [[Brendan Carr]] (Trump-appointed FCC Chair; Project 2025 FCC chapter author) negotiated approval conditions
- Pattern of Ellison-family entity strategic positioning aligned with Trump 2.0 regulatory + foreign-policy posture
