# Connection: Wexner → Mega Group → Israeli philanthropy

## What this connection establishes

[[Leslie Wexner]] is simultaneously: (a) the documented primary financial pipeline to [[Jeffrey Epstein]], (b) a founding member of the [[Mega Group]], and (c) the founder of one of the most substantial US private-sector Israeli philanthropic and leadership-development networks. These three roles overlap operationally during the same decades. The connection is what makes the bridge from the Epstein operation to the broader US-Israel philanthropic-and-influence infrastructure observable in public record.

## The three overlapping roles (all Tier 1 documented)

### Role 1: Epstein's primary funder

- ~1986 onward: Wexner is Epstein's primary financial advisory client
- 1987: Wexner grants Epstein broad power of attorney
- 1996: NES LLC (Wexner-affiliated) transfers 9 East 71st Street mansion to Epstein-controlled entity for nominal consideration
- See [[Leslie Wexner]] and [[L Brands]] for full documentation

### Role 2: Mega Group founding member

- ~1991: Mega Group founded; Wexner is a founding member alongside [[Charles Bronfman]], [[Edgar Bronfman Sr.]], [[Michael Steinhardt]], [[Max Fisher]], [[Lester Crown]], [[Laurence Tisch]], [[Ronald Lauder]]
- Semi-annual meetings; no public agenda
- See [[Mega Group]]

### Role 3: Wexner Foundation and Israeli-network philanthropy

- 1983: Wexner Foundation founded with Abigail Wexner
- 1989: Wexner Israel Fellowship founded at Harvard Kennedy School — leadership development for Israeli public-sector mid-careerists
- Major funder of [[Israeli-American Council]] (IAC) and related Israeli-network organizations
- Substantial Hillel International funding
- Wexner Foundation operates across US-Israel leadership-development space at scale

## Why this matters

The bridge argument: the same financial network that fungibly transferred extraordinary value to [[Jeffrey Epstein]] is the same network that operationally coordinated US-Israel philanthropic and leadership-development infrastructure. This is necessary background for both:

- [[Hypothesis - Epstein had intelligence-service connections]] (specifically, the Mossad-allegation thread)
- [[Hypothesis - Epstein operation continues post-WWII blackmail architecture]] (specifically, the Mega Group as continuity bridge from the Bronfman/Lansky lineage)

- That the Wexner Foundation operationally coordinated trafficking

It DOES document that the same actor (Wexner) operated all three roles concurrently, and that no public investigation has fully traced the financial and operational interactions between them.
