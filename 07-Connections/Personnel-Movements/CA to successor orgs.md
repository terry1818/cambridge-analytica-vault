## Vault context and open research threads

Connection notes are first-class objects in this vault. Each one represents a documented relationship (money flow, data flow, personnel move, contract, alliance, or strategic alignment) between two or more vault entities. The point of a connection note is to make the relationship itself searchable, evidenceable, and contestable, separate from either of the entity nodes it spans.

Use this node to anchor: the two-or-more entities involved, the nature of the relationship (with start and end dates if known), the evidence tier (primary document, regulator finding, investigative reporting, claim), and the analytical significance (why this connection matters to the broader hypothesis structure of the vault).
