Lesson 9: The Plan - Project 2025 in Plain English

In April 2023, the Heritage Foundation published a 922-page book called Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise. Most people call it "Project 2025."

It is a detailed playbook for what to do in the first 180 days of a Republican presidency. It was written by more than 350 people, organized through Heritage. Most of them worked in Trump's first administration. Many are now working in his second one.

The main things it proposes:

  • Fire up to 50,000 federal workers and replace them with people personally loyal to the president. This is the Schedule F mechanism (next lesson).
  • Eliminate the Department of Education. Move money to private and religious schools through "school choice" vouchers.
  • Gut the EPA, FDA, and IRS. Especially the parts that enforce rules on big corporations and wealthy taxpayers.
  • Use the military for domestic immigration enforcement.
  • End birthright citizenship through executive order, then defend it in court.
  • Ban abortion nationwide by reviving an 1873 anti-vice law called the Comstock Act, which makes it a crime to mail abortion drugs.
  • Eliminate independent agencies. Make the Federal Reserve, the Department of Justice, the Federal Communications Commission, and others answer directly to the President.
  • Replace climate policy with expanded fossil fuel production.
  • Restrict press access to government information.

During the 2024 campaign, Trump publicly said he had nothing to do with Project 2025 and did not know who wrote it. About 140 of its contributors are now working in his administration. Many of its policy items have already been enacted.

You can read it yourself. The full text is free online at project2025.org/playbook. The full PDF is 922 pages, but the table of contents alone tells you most of what you need to know.
