Lesson 18: What's Happening Right Now

Here is the state of things in mid-2026, if you have been trying to tune out the news.

Schedule F is active. Trump signed it on his first day back. The Office of Personnel Management has begun reclassifying tens of thousands of positions. Mass firings of career staff in EPA, FDA, USDA, and the Justice Department are underway.

DOGE happened. The Department of Government Efficiency, run by Elon Musk and (briefly) Vivek Ramaswamy, was given broad authority to cut federal agencies. Among the casualties:

  • The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (largely gutted).
  • USAID (largely dismantled).
  • Large parts of the Department of Education.
  • The IRS enforcement division (which audits wealthy taxpayers).

Musk has since publicly broken with the administration over other issues, but the cuts remain.

The federal courts are filling up fast. Trump installed more federal judges in the first six months of his second term than most presidents do in their entire four-year term. Most are under 45 years old.

Immigration enforcement is at full speed. ICE's budget has roughly tripled. Mass deportation operations are underway in major cities. The administration invoked the 1798 Alien Enemies Act for the first time since World War II - the law previously used to intern Japanese Americans.

Birthright citizenship is in the courts. Trump signed an executive order trying to end it. Several federal judges have blocked it. The case is heading to the Supreme Court, where the outcome is uncertain.

The 2028 election is being prepared for now. Convention of States efforts have accelerated in 4 more state legislatures. State election officials in several states have been replaced. New voter ID and registration rules have been enacted in Florida, Texas, Ohio, Georgia, and Arizona.

The press is under pressure. Several major newsroom owners have pulled stories or fired editors at administration request. The FCC has reopened broadcast license reviews of news networks the administration dislikes. Some independent reporters have been denied White House access.

You do not have to believe any of this is bad. You just have to know it is happening, because none of it is hidden. It is on the front page of every newspaper.
