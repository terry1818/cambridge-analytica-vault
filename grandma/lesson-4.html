Lesson 4: The Donors - The Money People

You do not need to memorize all the names. Just know that the same handful of families show up over and over.

Robert Mercer. A hedge fund billionaire from Long Island. Paid for Cambridge Analytica (the firm that scraped Facebook data on 87 million people). Also funded Breitbart News and helped put Steve Bannon in Trump's first White House.

Charles Koch. Now the surviving Koch brother (David died in 2019). Runs a 50-year-old political machine that has spent hundreds of millions of dollars per election cycle, mostly on state legislatures. The Koch network funded the original "Tea Party" rallies of 2009 and 2010.

Peter Thiel. A Silicon Valley billionaire who co-founded PayPal. Paid for J.D. Vance's Senate campaign in 2022. Vance is now Vice President. Thiel also funded the lawsuit that bankrupted Gawker, and runs the surveillance company Palantir.

The DeVos family. The Amway fortune. Betsy DeVos was Trump's Education Secretary. Her brother is Erik Prince, who founded the private military contractor Blackwater.

Miriam Adelson. Continued her late husband Sheldon's political giving. Donated over 100 million dollars to Trump in 2024. Owns the Las Vegas Sands casino company.

Barre Seid. An electronics manufacturer who in 2021 gave 1.6 billion dollars - in a single transaction - to a political group controlled by Leonard Leo. That is the largest known political donation in American history. More on that in Lesson 7.

The Wilks brothers. Fracking billionaires from Texas. Fund state-level Christian Nationalist candidates and groups across the South and Midwest.

These are not the only donors. But if you see one of these names in a news story, you now know what part of the picture they are in.
