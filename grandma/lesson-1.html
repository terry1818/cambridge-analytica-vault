Lesson 1: Welcome - Why You Should Care

Hello. This little book explains something that has been quietly happening in American politics for the last 40 years. You do not need to know anything about politics to understand it.

Twenty short lessons, grouped into five parts. Each lesson takes about 5 minutes to read. You can tap the green "Read to me" button at the bottom and the page will read aloud to you.

The five parts are:

  • Part 1. The framework - the three big ideas this whole book is built on, and how to tell what is true.
  • Part 2. Who is behind it - the donors, the religious right, the tech right, and the legal network.
  • Part 3. What they are doing - the courts, Project 2025, the civil service, the Constitution, and your state.
  • Part 4. How they won - Cambridge Analytica, Russia, January 6, and the elite networks.
  • Part 5. Where we stand right now and what you can do.
One promise: Nothing in this book is made up. Every claim comes from court records, congressional reports, sworn testimony, or major newspapers. If you want the sources, the full vault at terry1818.github.io/cambridge-analytica-vault has them.
