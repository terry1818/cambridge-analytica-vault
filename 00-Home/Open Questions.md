## Vault navigation and feedback loop

The Open Questions list is a living research backlog. It cross-references the [[Vault MOC]] (top-level navigation), the [[Categorization Architecture]] (so questions can be tagged onto the right axis), the [[Evidence Tiers]] (so questions can be staged from speculation to investigation), and the [[Methodology - Verifying a CA claim]] note (so a question can be turned into a verification task).

When a question on this list is answered or partially answered, the answer should be captured in the relevant entity, event, hypothesis, or connection node, and the question entry should be marked resolved (with a link to the answering node). Where multiple questions cluster, they should be promoted to a [[Hypotheses MOC]] entry with explicit falsification conditions.

This list is intentionally generative rather than exhaustive: it is the queue for active research, not a permanent record.
