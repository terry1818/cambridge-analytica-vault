## What is this vault?

A public research collection (~3,500 sourced notes) about how a coalition of billionaires, religious-right organizers, intelligence operators, and Silicon Valley founders has spent 40+ years building the political apparatus that put Donald Trump in power twice.

## What does it claim, in 30 seconds?

The vault argues that **contemporary US politics is being shaped by three interlocking projects** -- not one big conspiracy, but three overlapping movements that share donors, forums, and personnel.

1. **They are trying to capture the state structurally** (courts + executive + constitution), not just win elections. See [[Structural Capture Thesis]].
2. **They are doing it because their voter base is shrinking.** Demographic projections show their coalition cannot sustain electoral majorities, so they pursue non-electoral mechanisms. See [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]].
3. **Their intellectual core has rejected the post-WWII liberal-constitutional order** and is building toward replacing it with a Christian-nationalist / neoreactionary alternative. See [[Post-Liberal Constitutional Replacement Thesis]].

The synthesis of all three: [[Master Theses MOC]].

## How do I know any of this is true?

**Every claim in the vault carries an evidence tier:**

- **Tier 1** -- Court filings, FBI files, FEC records, sworn testimony with corroboration. You can cite this directly.
- **Tier 2** -- Reasonable inference from Tier-1 facts, mainstream investigative journalism. Cite with attribution.
- **Tier 3** -- Contested or single-sourced. **Cite as allegation, not fact.**

If a claim sounds dramatic and is only Tier 3, the vault tells you so explicitly. See [[Evidence Tiers]] for the full framework.
