# Karen McDougal

Born March 23, 1971, Merrillville, IN. Former Playboy Playmate; Playboy Playmate of the Year for 1998. **Alleged a 2006-2007 sexual relationship with [[Donald Trump]]**. Subject of **AMI's August 2016 $150,000 catch-and-kill payment** for the rights to her story; the story was suppressed during the 2016 campaign per the [[David Pecker]] / Trump arrangement.

## Alleged 2006-2007 relationship

McDougal alleges a ~10-month relationship with Trump beginning approximately June 2006 (shortly after Trump's son Barron was born to Melania Trump). She has stated:
- Met Trump at the *Apprentice* taping
- Relationship included multiple encounters across the US
- Trump offered her money after some encounters which she refused
- Relationship ended approximately April 2007

Trump has denied the affair.

## August 2016 AMI catch-and-kill payment

Pre-election period 2016: McDougal, through her attorney Keith Davidson (same attorney who later represented [[Stormy Daniels]]), negotiated with AMI for sale of her story. AMI agreed:
- $150,000 payment
- Rights to her life story
- Some magazine cover and column "opportunities" (not delivered)
- Story would not be published

AMI's 2018 non-prosecution agreement with SDNY admitted the payment was made:
- In coordination with the Trump campaign
- For the purpose of suppressing the story before the election
- As an unlawful corporate contribution to the campaign

## 2018 lawsuit and resolution

April 2018: McDougal sued AMI in California seeking to void the contract on the grounds it had been used to suppress her speech rather than promote her career. AMI settled with McDougal allowing her to discuss her story publicly. McDougal's account became part of the public record.

## Pecker / People v Trump testimony

The McDougal arrangement was substantial trial evidence in [[People v Trump NY Hush Money 2024]] establishing the pattern of catch-and-kill operations that the Daniels payment fit into. [[David Pecker]] testified extensively about the McDougal payment.
