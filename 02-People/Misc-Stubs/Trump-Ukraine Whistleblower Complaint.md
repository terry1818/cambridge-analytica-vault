# Trump-Ukraine Whistleblower Complaint (August 2019)

Anonymous whistleblower complaint filed August 12, 2019 by a CIA officer detailing concerns about the July 25, 2019 Trump-Zelensky phone call and the broader Ukraine pressure campaign.

## Process

- Submitted to ICIG (Intelligence Community Inspector General) Michael Atkinson
- Atkinson found the complaint urgent and credible
- Acting DNI Joseph Maguire initially withheld the complaint from Congress
- Public release September 26, 2019 catalyzed the impeachment inquiry

## Significance

The whistleblower complaint is the proximate institutional trigger of the [[First Impeachment of Donald Trump]]. Without it, the Ukraine pressure campaign would likely have continued without congressional oversight.
