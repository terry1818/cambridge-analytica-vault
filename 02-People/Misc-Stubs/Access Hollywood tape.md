# Access Hollywood tape

## Significance

- The tape's release coincided within hours with WikiLeaks publishing the Podesta emails - a coordination that Mueller documented as not coincidental on the WikiLeaks/GRU side
- The tape produced the largest single-event polling drop of Trump's 2016 campaign
- Trump won the election anyway, demonstrating the coalition's tolerance for documented sexual-misconduct framing

## Subsequent corroboration

- [[Jessica Leeds]] and [[Natasha Stoynoff]] both testified in the [[E. Jean Carroll]] civil trial as corroborating witnesses
