# Palm Beach Police Investigation of Jeffrey Epstein (2005-2008)

State-local investigation by Palm Beach (FL) Police Department into [[Jeffrey Epstein]]'s sexual abuse of minors.

## Documented findings (Tier 1)

- Initiated 2005 after parent of 14-year-old victim reported abuse
- Investigation identified approximately 30 minor victims
- Detective Joe Recarey (lead investigator) recommended multiple felony charges
- Palm Beach State Attorney Barry Krischer presented to grand jury but only obtained one count of soliciting prostitution
- Police chief Michael Reiter took the highly unusual step of contacting the FBI directly to expand the investigation federally

## Significance

The Palm Beach investigation is the documented case of state-local law enforcement attempting to push a politically-connected sex-trafficking case forward against substantial institutional resistance. The federal-level outcome (the [[2007 Non-Prosecution Agreement]]) effectively shut down the case that the Palm Beach PD had assembled.
