# Fani Willis

Fulton County (GA) District Attorney since 2021. Brought the Georgia RICO indictment against [[Donald Trump]] and 18 co-defendants (August 14, 2023).

## Georgia RICO Case (Tier 1)

- Indictment August 14, 2023 - 41 counts under Georgia RICO statute
- Defendants: Trump + 18 including [[Rudy Giuliani]], [[John Eastman]], [[Mark Meadows]], [[Sidney Powell]], [[Kenneth Chesebro]], [[Jeffrey Clark]], plus Georgia fake electors
- Multiple defendants pleaded guilty (Powell, Chesebro, Ellis, Hall)
- 2024: Willis disqualification motion (over her relationship with appointed special prosecutor Nathan Wade); appellate dismissal of Willis December 2024
- Case status uncertain post-Trump 2024 election win

## Significance

Willis brought the most-defendant-comprehensive Trump indictment - the only one casting Trump as a member of a charged criminal enterprise. The case's procedural problems (Wade relationship) and post-election political dynamics complicate its trajectory.
