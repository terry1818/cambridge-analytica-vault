# Evan Corcoran

US attorney. Former Trump lawyer in the Mar-a-Lago documents case. Notable for unusual judicial ruling that crime-fraud exception pierced his attorney-client privilege.

## Crime-fraud privilege piercing (Tier 1)

- March 2023: Chief Judge Beryl Howell (DC Circuit) ruled the crime-fraud exception applied to Corcoran's communications with Trump regarding the documents
- Corcoran was compelled to testify before the grand jury
- His notes and testimony became central evidence in the [[Mar-a-Lago documents case]] indictment
