# Constitutional Sheriffs and Peace Officers Association (CSPOA)

US organization of sheriffs and law-enforcement officers operating on a "constitutional sheriff" doctrine - the position that county sheriffs have constitutional authority that overrides federal law-enforcement actions within their jurisdiction.

## Significance

Constitutional Sheriffs represents the rural / county-level institutional infrastructure of the broader anti-federal-government right-wing movement. Operationally significant for the 2020-2024 period because of cooperation patterns with [[ICE]] / immigration enforcement, [[Stop the Steal]] organizing, and the [[Three Percenters]] / militia ecosystem.
