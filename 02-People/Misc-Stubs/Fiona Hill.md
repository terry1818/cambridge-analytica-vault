# Fiona Hill

UK-born US foreign policy expert. NSC Senior Director for Europe and Russia (2017-2019). Author of "Mr. Putin: Operative in the Kremlin" (2013).

## First impeachment role

Hill's November 21, 2019 public testimony was among the most substantively damaging witness accounts during the [[First Impeachment of Donald Trump]]. Her framing - that the Trump-aligned "fictional narrative" about Ukrainian interference in the 2016 election was Russian propaganda - was particularly significant given her credentials.
