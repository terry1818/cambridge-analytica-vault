# Robert P. Jones

US public-religion sociologist. Founder and CEO of the [[Public Religion Research Institute]] (PRRI). Author of "The End of White Christian America" (2016) and "White Too Long" (2020).

## Research focus

- Demographic changes in US religious identity
- Tracking the decline of white Christian America as a political force
- Documentation of the link between white Christian identity and racial attitudes

## Significance for vault

Jones's empirical research is the demographic backbone of the [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]] (Thesis 2 in the [[Master Theses MOC]]). His tracking of white-Christian-share-of-population numbers provides the documented demographic trajectory that the contemporary conservative coalition is responding to.

PRRI surveys have provided the most-granular ongoing public data on the changing religious-political landscape over the 2010-2025 period.
