# Ruth Ben-Ghiat

NYU professor of history. Author of "Strongmen: Mussolini to the Present" (2020). One of the most-cited contemporary scholars on the application of fascism / authoritarianism analytic frameworks to current US politics.

## Significance

Ben-Ghiat has been consistent in applying the fascism analytic frame to the contemporary US right from 2016+. Her academic work on Italian fascism and her contemporary commentary on the [[Comparative authoritarianism]] of current US politics is one of the most-cited frameworks in vault analysis.
