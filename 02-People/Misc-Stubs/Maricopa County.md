# Maricopa County

Most populous county in Arizona (Phoenix metropolitan area). Approximately 4.5M residents. Politically pivotal in Arizona's competitive presidential and Senate races.

## 2020-2024 election controversies

- 2020: Biden carried Maricopa narrowly; Arizona flipped from R to D for first time since 1996
- 2021: [[2020 Arizona audit]] focused on Maricopa
- 2022-2024: Continuing election-denial litigation and harassment of Maricopa election officials
