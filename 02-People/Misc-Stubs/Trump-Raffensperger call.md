# Trump-Raffensperger call (January 2, 2021)

Phone call between [[Donald Trump]] and Georgia Secretary of State Brad Raffensperger. Trump asked Raffensperger to "find 11,780 votes" - exactly enough to overturn Biden's Georgia win.

## Documentation (Tier 1)

- Recorded by Raffensperger's office
- Released by Washington Post January 3, 2021
- Full transcript available
- Central evidence in the [[Georgia RICO Case]] subsequently brought by [[Fani Willis]]

## Significance

The Trump-Raffensperger call is the cleanest documented case of Trump personally directing a specific election-result alteration. Unlike the broader Stop-the-Steal organizing, the call is a recorded direct request to a named state official to fabricate votes.
