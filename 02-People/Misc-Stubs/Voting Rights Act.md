# Voting Rights Act (1965)

Federal US statute that prohibited racial discrimination in voting. Enacted at peak of Civil Rights Movement under President Lyndon Johnson. Section 5 required Justice Department "preclearance" of voting-law changes in jurisdictions with documented histories of racial discrimination.

## Shelby County v Holder (2013)

Supreme Court struck down the Section 5 preclearance formula (5-4). The decision effectively ended federal preclearance authority over state voting law changes in covered jurisdictions.
