# Disinformation-research infrastructure defunding (2023-2025)

Coordinated political campaign aimed at delegitimizing and defunding US academic, civil-society, and government research infrastructure focused on tracking online disinformation.

## Operations targeted

- [[Stanford Internet Observatory]] - substantially curtailed 2024
- [[Election Integrity Partnership]] - did not operate for 2024 cycle
- DHS CISA disinformation-related programs - reduced
- Various university-based programs - funding cuts

## Political vehicles

- [[Jim Jordan]] House Judiciary subcommittee investigations
- Stephen Miller's America First Legal litigation
- Twitter Files framing
- Murthy v. Missouri litigation

## Significance

The 2024 election cycle had substantially less institutional capacity for tracking and responding to disinformation than 2020 or 2022. The political effect was deliberate and successful.
