# Tanya Chutkan

US District Judge for the District of Columbia. Trial judge in [[Trump January 6 Federal Case]] before the case was dismissed.

## Significance

Chutkan was the assigned judge whose pretrial rulings shaped the case's path through the Supreme Court ([[Trump v United States]] originated as an interlocutory appeal from her court). Her handling of the case - declining to dismiss on immunity grounds before the Supreme Court intervened - is documented in DC Circuit appellate filings.
