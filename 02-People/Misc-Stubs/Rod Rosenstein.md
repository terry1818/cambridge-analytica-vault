# Rod Rosenstein

US Deputy Attorney General (April 2017 - May 2019). Appointed [[Robert Mueller]] as Special Counsel on May 17, 2017.

## Mueller appointment

- Issued the appointment order after [[James Comey]]'s firing made the DOJ Russia investigation's independence questionable
- Drafted the memo that the Trump White House initially used as the public rationale for firing Comey (Rosenstein later resented this framing)
- Supervised Mueller's investigation throughout the period DAG, declining multiple opportunities to constrain its scope

## Significance

Rosenstein is the institutional figure whose decision to appoint Mueller as Special Counsel is the proximate cause of the Mueller investigation existing at all. Had Rosenstein declined to appoint or had appointed a Trump-friendly figure, the documented record of the [[Russian interference 2016]] case might have been substantially different.
