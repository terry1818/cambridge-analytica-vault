# James Comey

FBI Director (2013-May 9, 2017, fired by Trump). Author of "A Higher Loyalty" (2018).

## 2016 election interventions

- July 5, 2016: Public statement closing Hillary Clinton email investigation while criticizing her conduct
- October 28, 2016: Letter to Congress reopening investigation 11 days before election
- November 6, 2016: Letter closing investigation again (no new charges)
- Post-election analysis (Nate Silver and others) has attributed substantial Electoral College impact to the October 28 letter

## Russia investigation

- Briefed Trump January 6, 2017 on the Steele dossier
- Memorialized contemporaneously the February 14, 2017 Oval Office meeting in which Trump asked him to drop the Flynn investigation
- Fired May 9, 2017 (the firing precipitated Mueller's appointment)
- Testified before Senate Intelligence Committee June 8, 2017

## Significance

Comey is the documented case of an FBI Director whose interventions affected two consecutive election cycles (his July/October Clinton statements affected 2016; his firing precipitated the Mueller investigation that shaped 2017-2019 politics). The asymmetric impact of these interventions on the two parties remains controversial.
