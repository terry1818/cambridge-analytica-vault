# Daniel Ziblatt

Harvard professor of political science. Co-author (with [[Steven Levitsky]]) of "How Democracies Die" (2018) and "Tyranny of the Minority" (2023).

## Significance

The Levitsky-Ziblatt framework is the most-cited contemporary scholarly treatment of [[Democratic backsliding]] applied to current US politics. Their argument - that contemporary US institutions are uniquely vulnerable to minoritarian capture via structural mechanisms - maps directly to the vault's [[Structural Capture Thesis]].
