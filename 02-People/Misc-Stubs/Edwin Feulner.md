# Edwin Feulner

Co-founder of the [[Heritage Foundation]] (1973). President of Heritage 1977-2013. Distinguished Fellow at Heritage.

## Significance

Feulner is one of the founding figures of the post-1970 conservative think-tank infrastructure. Heritage under his leadership produced the original [[Mandate for Leadership]] (1981) and subsequent editions that shaped Republican-administration transitions. He represents the institutional continuity between the early-movement conservative project and contemporary [[Project 2025]].
