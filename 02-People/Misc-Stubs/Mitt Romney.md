# Mitt Romney

US Senator from Utah (2019-2025, retired). 2012 Republican presidential nominee. Governor of Massachusetts (2003-2007). Founder of Bain Capital.

## Trump-era voting record

- Only Republican Senator to vote to convict Trump in the first impeachment (February 2020)
- One of seven Republican Senators to vote to convict Trump in the second impeachment (February 2021)
- Did not seek re-election 2024; retired January 2025

## Significance

Romney is the most-credentialed institutional Republican to break definitively with the Trump-coalition project. McKay Coppins's 2023 biography "Romney: A Reckoning" documented Romney's private assessment that the institutional Republican Party had ceased to be a politically functional vehicle for governance.

His retirement represents the structural-establishment-Republican exit from elected politics.
