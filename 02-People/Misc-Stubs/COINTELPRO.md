# COINTELPRO (1956-1971)

FBI Counter Intelligence Program. Series of covert and illegal projects targeting US domestic political organizations including civil rights groups (SNCC, SCLC), the Black Panther Party, anti-war groups, feminist organizations, and others.

## Documented tactics

- Surveillance and infiltration
- Forged correspondence to create infighting
- Coordinated media smears
- Encouragement of violence between targeted groups
- Direct facilitation of police actions (Fred Hampton killing)

## Significance

COINTELPRO is the documented US precedent for the [[Surveillance state]] using domestic surveillance and counter-organizing tactics against politically-disfavored Americans. The Church Committee (1975) public disclosure produced the post-Watergate FBI reforms, most of which have been substantially eroded post-9/11.
