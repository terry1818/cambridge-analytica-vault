# Mark Finchem

Arizona State Representative (R) 2015-2023. Failed 2022 Republican nominee for Arizona Secretary of State. [[Oath Keepers]]-affiliated. Present at the Capitol on January 6, 2021 (not charged).

## Significance

Finchem represents the Arizona case of state-level Republican election-denial candidates becoming major-party nominees for offices controlling election administration. His failed 2022 Secretary of State bid was the documented test of whether the AZ electorate would put an explicit election-denier in direct control of the next election cycle's certification.
