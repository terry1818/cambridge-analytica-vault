# Bellingcat

UK-based investigative journalism organization. Founded 2014 by Eliot Higgins. Pioneer of open-source intelligence (OSINT) investigative methodology.

## Significance

Bellingcat's documented investigations include:
- 2014 MH17 shootdown (Buk missile system tracked to Russian 53rd Anti-Aircraft Missile Brigade)
- 2018 Skripal poisoning (GRU officers identified)
- 2020 Navalny poisoning (FSB officers identified, including the contemporaneous call where one confessed)
- US far-right network mapping

Bellingcat is the contemporary institutional embodiment of the OSINT methodology that [[Forensic Architecture]] and Renee DiResta's work share.
