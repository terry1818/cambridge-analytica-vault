# Fair Fight Action

US voting-rights organization founded 2018 by [[Stacey Abrams]] following her loss in the Georgia gubernatorial race. Focus on combatting voter suppression in Georgia and nationally.

## Programs

- Voter education and registration drives
- Litigation against voter-suppression laws (often against [[Brian Kemp]] / Georgia SOS office)
- Election monitoring
- Mobilization that contributed to Democratic wins in 2020 Georgia presidential and 2020/2021 Senate races
