# Robert Engel

US Secret Service agent. Trump's lead protective-detail agent in the presidential SUV on January 6, 2021. Subject of the [[Cassidy Hutchinson]] testimony about Trump's alleged attempt to seize the steering wheel after being told he could not go to the Capitol.

## Disputed account

- Hutchinson testified [[Tony Ornato]] told her Engel said Trump lunged at him
- Engel through counsel disputes the lunge but not the underlying anger
- The Committee did not formally affirm or deny the specific claim
- The Tier-1 fact is the testimony existed; the Tier-2 question is whether the specific lunge occurred
