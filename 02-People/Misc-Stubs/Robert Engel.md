# Robert Engel

US Secret Service agent. Trump's lead protective-detail agent in the presidential SUV on January 6, 2021.

## Hutchinson testimony context

[[Cassidy Hutchinson]] testified [[Tony Ornato]] told her Engel said Trump lunged at him after being told he could not go to the Capitol. Engel through counsel disputes the lunge but not the underlying anger. The Committee did not formally affirm or deny the specific claim.
