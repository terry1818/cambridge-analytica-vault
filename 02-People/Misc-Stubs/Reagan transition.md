# Reagan transition (1980-1981)

Presidential transition between Carter and Reagan administrations. First transition for which the [[Heritage Foundation]] produced [[Mandate for Leadership]] (1981) - the founding document of the contemporary conservative transition-planning methodology.

## Significance

Reagan reportedly adopted approximately 60% of the 1981 Mandate for Leadership recommendations. The transition is the structural precedent for [[Project 2025]] - same institutional vehicle (Heritage Foundation), same methodology (comprehensive policy book + personnel database + 180-day playbook), implemented at greater scale in 2025.
