# Jerry Falwell Sr. (1933-2007)

US Southern Baptist pastor. Founder of [[Liberty University]] (1971) and the [[Moral Majority]] (1979). Founding architect of the modern [[Religious right]] political coalition.

## Moral Majority

- Founded 1979 with strategist [[Paul Weyrich]]
- Public framing: organized to oppose Roe v. Wade (1973)
- Documented historical evidence (Randall Balmer) indicates the original organizing catalyst was federal pressure on segregated Christian schools (Green v. Connally, 1971, IRS revocation of Bob Jones University's tax exemption); abortion became the public-facing organizing issue after that
- Disbanded 1989

## Significance

Falwell is the founding institutional figure of the modern [[Religious right]] as an organized political force. The [[Council for National Policy]] / [[Heritage Foundation]] / [[Family Research Council]] cluster all trace direct organizational lineage to the Falwell / Weyrich coordination of the late 1970s.
