# L. Lin Wood

US attorney. Long-time defamation lawyer. Co-counsel in multiple post-2020 election-denial cases with [[Sidney Powell]]. Voluntarily resigned from Georgia Bar 2023 amid disciplinary proceedings.

## Significance

Wood and Powell brought the most-aggressive post-2020 election-fraud litigation. Their cases were uniformly unsuccessful and produced sanctions against both lawyers. Wood's voluntary retirement avoided potential disbarment.
