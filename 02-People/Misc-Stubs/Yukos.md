# Yukos

Russian integrated oil company. Largest non-state oil producer in Russia until its 2003-2004 dissolution by Putin's government.

## Khodorkovsky case

- 2003: CEO [[Mikhail Khodorkovsky]] arrested on tax/fraud charges
- 2003-2014: Yukos assets seized and transferred primarily to state-controlled Rosneft
- Khodorkovsky imprisoned 2003-2013
- The case established Putin's intolerance of independent oligarch political activity
