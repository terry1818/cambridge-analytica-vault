# Section 215 (USA PATRIOT Act)

Provision of the USA PATRIOT Act (2001) authorizing FBI access to "any tangible thing" relevant to a foreign-intelligence or counterterrorism investigation. Used as the legal basis for NSA bulk telephone-metadata collection until 2015 (USA FREEDOM Act ended bulk collection in this form).
