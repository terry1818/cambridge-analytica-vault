# Uganda anti-LGBTQ legislation

Series of Ugandan laws criminalizing homosexuality, originally introduced 2009 and substantially advanced 2014 and 2023.

## Connection to US Religious right (Tier 1, documented)

- [[Scott Lively]] and other US Religious right figures conducted 2009 Uganda speaking tour preceding the original bill
- [[Jim Inhofe]] / [[The Family]] / Fellowship network maintained close ties to Ugandan officials promoting the legislation
- [[David Bahati]] (Ugandan MP, bill sponsor) was a regular [[National Prayer Breakfast]] attendee

## Significance

Uganda is the most-documented case of US Religious right movement-organizing producing legislative outcomes in a foreign country. The Family / Fellowship Foundation's Africa portfolio is the structural infrastructure.
