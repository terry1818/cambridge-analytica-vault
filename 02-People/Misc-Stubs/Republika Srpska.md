# Republika Srpska

Serb-majority political entity within Bosnia and Herzegovina. Created by the 1995 Dayton Accords ending the Bosnian War. Continues under separatist political leadership (Milorad Dodik).

## Vault relevance

Republika Srpska is referenced as one of the contemporary cases of a separatist-ethnic political entity operating within a nominally unified state - the comparison case for some [[Convention of States]] / structural-reform analyses concerning sub-national separatism within the US.
