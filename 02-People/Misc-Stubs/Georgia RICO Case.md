# Georgia RICO Case (State v. Trump et al., 2023)

Fulton County (GA) Superior Court racketeering prosecution brought by [[Fani Willis]] against [[Donald Trump]] and 18 co-defendants for the post-2020 election conspiracy in Georgia.

## Indictment (Tier 1)

- August 14, 2023
- 41 counts under Georgia RICO statute
- Trump + 18 co-defendants including [[Rudy Giuliani]], [[John Eastman]], [[Mark Meadows]], [[Sidney Powell]], [[Kenneth Chesebro]], [[Jeffrey Clark]], [[David Shafer]], [[Cathy Latham]], [[Shawn Still]]

## Guilty pleas

- October-November 2023: [[Sidney Powell]], [[Kenneth Chesebro]], [[Jenna Ellis]], Scott Hall all pleaded guilty to reduced charges

## Procedural problems

- 2024: Disqualification motion against Willis over her relationship with appointed special prosecutor Nathan Wade
- December 2024: Georgia Court of Appeals disqualified Willis from the case
- Case status pending as of latest reporting
