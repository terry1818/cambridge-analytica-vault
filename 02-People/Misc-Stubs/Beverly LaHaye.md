# Beverly LaHaye

Founder of Concerned Women for America (CWA) - 1979. Wife of [[Tim LaHaye]] (Left Behind author, [[Council for National Policy]] co-founder).

## Significance

Concerned Women for America was one of the founding-era [[Religious right]] organizations alongside [[Family Research Council]] and the [[Moral Majority]]. CWA has continued operating through the contemporary period as a women's-perspective Religious right vehicle.
