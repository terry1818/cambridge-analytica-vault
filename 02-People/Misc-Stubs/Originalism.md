# Originalism

Legal-philosophical method of constitutional interpretation that constrains judicial decisions to the meaning the constitutional text was originally understood to have at the time of adoption.

## Variants

- Original intent (the framers' subjective intentions)
- Original public meaning (the publicly understood meaning at adoption)
- New originalism (Akhil Amar, Lawrence Solum, Randy Barnett)

## Vault relevance

Originalism is the credentialing-philosophy of the [[Federalist Society]] judicial pipeline. It functions as the screening-test for federal judicial nominations under Republican administrations.

The [[Post-Liberal Constitutional Replacement Thesis]] (Thesis 3) traces the post-2020 conservative intellectual current ([[Adrian Vermeule]] common good constitutionalism, [[Patrick Deneen]]) as an explicit break FROM originalism rather than an extension of it.
