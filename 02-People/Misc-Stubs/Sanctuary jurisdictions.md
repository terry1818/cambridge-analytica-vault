# Sanctuary jurisdictions

US state and local governments that decline to assist federal immigration enforcement beyond what federal law strictly requires. Includes Illinois, California, New York, Massachusetts, multiple counties and cities.

## Trump 2025 conflicts

- Day-1 EO targeting sanctuary jurisdictions
- Multiple lawsuits filed by sanctuary states against federal enforcement actions
- Funding-withholding litigation pending
- Federal raids in sanctuary jurisdictions a key flashpoint of [[Mass deportation operation 2025]]
