# Alexander Vindman

US Army Lt. Colonel (retired). Director for European Affairs at the National Security Council (2018-2020). Born in Soviet Ukraine; immigrated to US 1979.

## First impeachment role

- Listened in on the July 25, 2019 Trump-Zelensky phone call
- Reported the call's content to NSC legal counsel as inappropriate
- Public testimony November 19, 2019 in the House impeachment inquiry

## Retaliation

- February 7, 2020: Removed from NSC days after Senate acquittal
- July 2020: Retired from Army after promotion withheld
- Documented case of post-impeachment retaliation against career federal officials
