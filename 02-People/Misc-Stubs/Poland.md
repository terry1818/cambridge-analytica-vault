# Poland

Central European republic. Period of Law and Justice (PiS) party rule 2015-2023 represents one of the documented contemporary European [[Democratic backsliding]] cases.

## Vault relevance

Poland under PiS implemented many of the operational moves later cited in US conservative discourse: judicial-capture push, media restructuring, family policy, anti-EU framing. The 2023 election that ended PiS rule is one of the documented institutional pushbacks against the European illiberalism cluster.
