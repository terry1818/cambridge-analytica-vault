# Constitution of the United States

Foundational document of the US federal government. Drafted at the [[Constitutional Convention 1787]]. Ratified 1788. In effect 1789.

## Vault relevance

The Constitution is the structural document that the [[Article V Convention]] mechanism allows amending. It is also the framework that [[Originalism]] (Federalist Society) claims to interpret faithfully, that [[Post-Liberal Constitutional Replacement Thesis]] proposes superseding, and that the [[Structural Capture Thesis]] coalition is attempting to entrench in a minoritarian-friendly form.
