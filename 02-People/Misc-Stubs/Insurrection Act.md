# Insurrection Act of 1807

US federal statute authorizing the President to deploy federal military forces or federalized National Guard for domestic enforcement.

## Standard limits

The Posse Comitatus Act (1878) generally prohibits federal military in domestic law enforcement. The Insurrection Act is a statutory exception requiring presidential invocation under specified conditions.
