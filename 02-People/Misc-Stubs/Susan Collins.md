# Susan Collins

US Senator from Maine (R) since 1997. One of a small number of remaining centrist Senate Republicans.

## Vault-relevant votes

- February 2020: Voted to acquit Trump in [[First Impeachment of Donald Trump]] (citing belief Trump had "learned his lesson")
- February 2021: Voted to convict Trump in [[Second Impeachment of Donald Trump]]
- 2022: Co-sponsored [[Electoral Count Reform Act]] (with Manchin)
- 2025: One of three Republican senators voting against [[Pete Hegseth]] confirmation

## Significance

Collins represents the dwindling institutional-establishment Republican vote on key Trump-coalition tests. Her voting pattern - tactically supportive in some cases, opposed in others - is the documented contemporary case of how individual moderate Republicans navigate the coalition's discipline.
