# Michael Reiter

Former Palm Beach Police Chief (2001-2008). Led the [[Palm Beach Police investigation]] of [[Jeffrey Epstein]] (2005-2008).

## Significance

Reiter took the highly unusual step of contacting the FBI directly to expand the Epstein investigation when the local prosecution (Palm Beach State Attorney Barry Krischer) declined to pursue the case at the level the police evidence supported. His push to escalate is one of the documented cases of state-local law enforcement attempting to drive a politically-difficult case forward against substantial institutional resistance.
