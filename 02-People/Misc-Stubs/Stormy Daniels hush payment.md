# Stormy Daniels hush payment (October 2016)

$130,000 payment from [[Michael Cohen]] to [[Stormy Daniels]] (legal name Stephanie Clifford) on October 27, 2016, eleven days before the 2016 election. The payment was for Daniels's silence about a 2006 sexual encounter with Trump.

## Mechanics (Tier 1)

- Cohen paid Daniels personally via shell company Essential Consultants LLC
- Trump Organization subsequently reimbursed Cohen, falsely characterizing the payments as legal fees
- The falsification of those reimbursement records was the basis for the 34 felony counts in [[People v Trump 2024]]

## Subsequent litigation

- 2018: Daniels-Cohen NDA litigation
- 2018: Cohen pleaded guilty to federal campaign finance violations (the underlying federal crime the NY business records prosecution depended on)
- 2024: People v Trump 2024 conviction
- January 2025: Sentencing - unconditional discharge
