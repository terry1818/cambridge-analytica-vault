# Charles Murray

US political scientist and author. Long-time senior fellow at the [[American Enterprise Institute]]. Author of "Losing Ground" (1984), "The Bell Curve" (1994, with Richard Herrnstein), "Coming Apart" (2012), "Human Diversity" (2020).

## Significance

Murray is among the most-cited intellectual figures of the contemporary US conservative movement, despite (or because of) the substantial academic and political controversy his work has generated. His framing of race and intelligence in The Bell Curve and his thesis about white-American class differentiation in Coming Apart have shaped strands of conservative thought relevant to the [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]].
