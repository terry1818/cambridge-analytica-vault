# Charles Bronfman

Canadian-American billionaire philanthropist. Co-founder of Birthright Israel (with [[Michael Steinhardt]]). Brother of Edgar Bronfman Sr. Heir to the Seagram fortune.

## Mega Group

Bronfman was an early member of the [[Mega Group]] - the informal network of ultra-wealthy Jewish philanthropists organized by [[Les Wexner]] in the 1990s for coordinated Israel-supportive philanthropy and US-Israel political work.
