# Trump Tower Moscow

Proposed real estate development in Moscow that the Trump Organization actively pursued during the 2016 US presidential campaign. Documented by the [[Mueller Report]] and [[Michael Cohen]]'s 2018 plea.

## Tier 1 documentation

- Cohen pleaded guilty November 2018 to lying to Congress about the project's timeline
- Negotiations continued into June 2016 (Cohen's original testimony placed them in January 2016)
- Trump made public statements during the campaign denying any business interests in Russia while these negotiations were active
