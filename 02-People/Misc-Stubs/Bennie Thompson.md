# Bennie Thompson

US Representative (D-MS-2) since 1993. Chair of the [[January 6 Select Committee]] (2021-2023).

## J6 Committee leadership

- Chaired the Select Committee that conducted the most thorough public investigation of the January 6 events
- Committee produced the multi-volume [[J6 Committee Final Report]] (December 2022)
- Committee referred criminal charges against Trump to DOJ (March 2022, December 2022)

## Significance

Thompson chaired the most consequential congressional investigation of presidential conduct since the 1974 Watergate hearings. The investigation's documented findings provided the evidentiary backbone for [[Jack Smith]]'s subsequent federal January 6 prosecution.
