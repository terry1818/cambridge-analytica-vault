# Education capture

Strategic project of restructuring US K-12 and higher education systems to redirect public funds to private religious schools (vouchers, ESAs), reshape public curricula toward conservative-Christian framing, and defund/restructure public universities.

## Operational vehicles

- American Federation for Children ([[Betsy DeVos]])
- [[Hillsdale College]] (curriculum / personnel pipeline)
- [[Patrick Henry College]] (homeschool-to-federal-government pipeline)
- [[Project 2025]] (Department of Education abolition + voucher expansion)
- State-level legislative campaigns
