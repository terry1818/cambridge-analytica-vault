# "Stand back and stand by"

Phrase used by [[Donald Trump]] during the September 29, 2020 presidential debate when asked to condemn white supremacists. Trump's specific addressing of "Proud Boys - stand back and stand by" became one of the documented moments of the 2020 campaign.

## Significance

- The [[Proud Boys]] adopted the phrase as a logo within hours
- Trump later said he did not know who the Proud Boys were
- The FBI assessment cited at [[Enrique Tarrio]]'s 2023 trial concluded the phrase functioned as recruitment
