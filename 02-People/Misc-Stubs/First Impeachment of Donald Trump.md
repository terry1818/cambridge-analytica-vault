# First Impeachment of Donald Trump (2019-2020)

US House of Representatives impeachment proceedings against [[Donald Trump]] arising from the Ukraine pressure campaign.

## Timeline (Tier 1)

- September 24, 2019: Speaker Pelosi announced formal impeachment inquiry following whistleblower complaint
- December 18, 2019: House voted to impeach (abuse of power, obstruction of Congress)
- February 5, 2020: Senate acquitted (52-48 on abuse of power; 53-47 on obstruction)
- [[Mitt Romney]] only Republican Senator to vote to convict (on abuse of power)

## Underlying conduct

- July 25, 2019 Trump-Zelensky phone call (released by White House, August 2019)
- [[Rudy Giuliani]] / [[Lev Parnas]] / [[Igor Fruman]] back-channel Ukraine pressure operation
- Withholding congressionally-appropriated Ukraine military aid pending announcement of investigation of [[Hunter Biden]] / Burisma
