# Alien Enemies Act (1798)

US federal statute (50 USC sections 21-24) permitting the President to detain or deport "alien enemies" during declared war or "invasion or predatory incursion" by a foreign nation against US territory.

## Trump 2025 invocation (Tier 1)

- March 2025: Trump invoked the AEA against Venezuelan nationals (Tren de Aragua designation as foreign terrorist organization)
- Multiple lawsuits challenge the legal basis for AEA invocation absent formal war declaration
- Used to deport individuals to [[CECOT]] (El Salvador) without standard immigration due process
- Supreme Court intervention pending on multiple appeals

## Historical use

The AEA had only been invoked three times previously: War of 1812, World War I, World War II. The 2025 invocation absent war declaration is unprecedented.
