# Grover Norquist

President of [[Americans for Tax Reform]] (founded 1985). Architect of the Taxpayer Protection Pledge that has been signed by virtually every Republican federal candidate since 1986. Convener of the influential Wednesday Meeting in Washington DC.

## Wednesday Meeting

Convenes weekly since 1993; approximately 150-200 conservative organization leaders. The single most-cited conservative-coordination forum in DC after the [[Council for National Policy]] (which is quarterly rather than weekly).

## Significance

Norquist has functioned for 40+ years as the operational coordinator of the conservative movement's tax-and-spending priorities. His pledge mechanism has been the single most-effective discipline-enforcement tool the right has wielded against its own elected officials.
