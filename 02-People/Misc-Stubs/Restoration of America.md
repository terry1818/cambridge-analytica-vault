# Restoration of America

US 501(c)(4) organization. Affiliated PAC (Restoration PAC) funded by Illinois billionaire Richard Uihlein. Major spender in state and federal Republican primaries supporting more aggressive conservative candidates over establishment alternatives.

## Significance

Restoration is one of the operational vehicles through which the [[Uihlein family]] has funded the post-2016 shift of the Republican Party toward Trump-aligned candidates and away from establishment-conservative figures.
