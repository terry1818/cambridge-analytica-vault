# Kenneth Marra

US District Judge for the Southern District of Florida. Trial judge in the [[Crime Victims' Rights Act]] litigation arising from the [[2007 Non-Prosecution Agreement]].

## Significance

February 2019: Marra ruled that the NPA's secret negotiation violated victims' CVRA rights. The ruling did not retroactively invalidate the NPA but established the legal record of CVRA violation. The 11th Circuit subsequently held the agreement itself could not be retroactively invalidated (US v. Wright / In re Wild).
