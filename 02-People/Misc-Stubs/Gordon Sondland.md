# Gordon Sondland

Hotel businessman. US Ambassador to the European Union (June 2018 - February 2020). Removed February 2020 days after Senate acquittal in the first impeachment.

## First impeachment role

- Direct participant in the Ukraine pressure campaign
- November 20, 2019 testimony: "Was there a quid pro quo? With regard to the requested White House call and White House meeting, the answer is yes."
- His testimony - given that he was a Trump donor / political appointee, not a career official - was particularly damaging to the Trump defense
