# Jessica Leeds

US woman who publicly accused [[Donald Trump]] of groping her on a 1979 first-class commercial flight. Came forward October 2016 after the Access Hollywood tape became public.

## Trial appearance

- Testified in the [[E. Jean Carroll]] civil trial 2023 as a corroborating witness (prior-similar-conduct evidence)
- Her testimony was admitted as supportive of Carroll's account of similar conduct

## Significance

Leeds is one of approximately 25 women who have publicly accused Trump of sexual misconduct. Her case is notable for the contemporaneous (pre-Trump-political-career) timeline of the alleged conduct and for her credibility as a corroborating witness in the Carroll trial.
