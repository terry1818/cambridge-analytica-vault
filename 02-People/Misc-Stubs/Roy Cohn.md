# Roy Cohn (1927-1986)

US attorney. Chief counsel to Senator Joseph McCarthy during the 1953-1954 Army-McCarthy hearings. Personal attorney to [[Donald Trump]] from 1973 until Cohn's death from AIDS in 1986.

- Taught Trump the operational doctrine that has structured his public conduct for 50 years: "Attack, attack, attack. Never apologize. Never settle. Always claim victory regardless of the actual outcome."
- Connected Trump to organized-crime figures in NYC construction industry during the 1970s-1980s building cycle
