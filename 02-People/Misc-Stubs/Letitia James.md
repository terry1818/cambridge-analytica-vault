# Letitia James

New York State Attorney General (2019-). First African American woman elected to statewide office in New York. Pursued the civil-fraud case against [[Donald Trump]] and the Trump Organization that resulted in the February 2024 [[Engoron verdict]].

## NY AG v Trump Organization Civil Fraud (Tier 1)

- Investigation opened 2019
- Lawsuit filed September 2022
- Trial October 2023 - January 2024
- February 16, 2024 verdict (Judge [[Arthur Engoron]]): Trump and Trump Organization liable;  judgment (later increased with interest); 3-year ban on Trump serving as officer/director of NY business; restrictions on the Trump children
- Appeals pending
