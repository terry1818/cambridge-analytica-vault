# Big Five personality model (OCEAN)

Most-validated empirical personality framework in academic psychology. Five trait dimensions: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism.

## Vault relevance

The Big Five / OCEAN was the methodological basis for [[Cambridge Analytica]]'s psychographic targeting. CA scored individual Facebook profiles on the OCEAN dimensions using the Kogan-derived personality-quiz dataset overlaid on broader Facebook engagement data.

The academic-psychometrics community has been substantially skeptical of CA's specific claims about microtargeting efficacy at scale, but the Big Five framework itself has strong empirical validation.
