# Marie Yovanovitch

Career US diplomat. US Ambassador to Ukraine (2016-May 2019). Removed by Trump May 2019 at the request of [[Rudy Giuliani]] and [[Lev Parnas]].

## First impeachment role

- Key witness in the first impeachment hearings (October 2019)
- Documented contemporaneous evidence of the Giuliani-led pressure campaign
- Her removal was an early indicator of the Ukraine-pressure campaign that culminated in the July 25, 2019 Trump-Zelensky call
