# Christopher DeMuth

Long-time president of the [[American Enterprise Institute]] (1986-2008). Distinguished Fellow at Hudson Institute (current). Co-founder of the [[Edmund Burke Foundation]] / [[National Conservatism Conference]].

## Significance

DeMuth bridges the traditional neoconservative establishment (AEI 1980s-2000s) and the contemporary national-conservative current ([[Edmund Burke Foundation]] / NatCon). The transition is structurally significant: a figure of his establishment credentials moving into the post-liberal / national-conservative camp signals the institutional realignment of senior US conservatism toward Yarvin / Vermeule / Deneen positioning.
