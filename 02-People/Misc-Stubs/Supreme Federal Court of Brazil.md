# Supreme Federal Court of Brazil (STF)

Highest court in Brazil. Has played central institutional role in the post-Bolsonaro accountability process.

## Bolsonaro prosecutions

- 2023+: Multiple proceedings against [[Jair Bolsonaro]] for January 8, 2023 attack, abuse of power, electoral violations
- June 2023: Bolsonaro banned from running for office until 2030

## Significance

The Brazilian Supreme Court's aggressive prosecution of Bolsonaro and senior allies is the comparison-case for what US institutional accountability for January 6 might have looked like absent [[Trump v United States]]. Different institutional structure produced substantially different post-coup-attempt outcomes.
