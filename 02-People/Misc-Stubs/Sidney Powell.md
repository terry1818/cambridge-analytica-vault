# Sidney Powell

US attorney. Senior figure in post-2020 election-denial litigation ("Kraken" lawsuits). Pleaded guilty in [[Georgia RICO Case]] October 2023.

## Documented role

- November 2020: Filed multiple "Kraken" lawsuits alleging voting-machine fraud; all dismissed
- December 18, 2020: Attended Oval Office meeting where Powell, [[Michael Flynn]], [[Patrick Byrne]] urged Trump to seize voting machines
- August 14, 2023: Indicted in Georgia RICO case
- October 19, 2023: Pleaded guilty to reduced misdemeanor charges; cooperating witness
