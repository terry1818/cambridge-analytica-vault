# Eyal Weizman

Israeli-British architect. Founder of [[Forensic Architecture]] (2010) at Goldsmiths, University of London.

## Significance

Weizman developed the methodology for using architectural / spatial analysis combined with open-source intelligence to reconstruct contested events. The Forensic Architecture approach has been deployed against Israeli, US, Russian, and other state actors' contested-fact narratives. His work is the methodological backbone of the [[Counter-Architecture]] framework referenced in the vault.
