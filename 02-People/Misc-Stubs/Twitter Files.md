# Twitter Files

Series of internal Twitter document releases curated by [[Elon Musk]] post-acquisition (October 2022) and distributed via selected journalists (Matt Taibbi, Bari Weiss, Michael Shellenberger).

## Framing controversy

- Twitter Files were framed as exposing systematic Twitter coordination with US government to suppress conservative speech
- Critics (including academic researchers) documented that the substantive content largely confirmed standard content-moderation practice, not a systematic anti-conservative bias

## Significance for vault

The Twitter Files framing was the operational pretext for the Jim Jordan / Stephen Miller-led campaign to defund disinformation research infrastructure ([[Stanford Internet Observatory]], [[Election Integrity Partnership]]) that culminated in those institutions' 2024 effective shuttering.
