# Lewis Rosenstiel (1891-1976)

US businessman. Founder and longtime CEO of Schenley Industries (whisky / spirits empire built during Prohibition).

## Significance for vault

Rosenstiel's connections were documented to span:
- Legitimate corporate America (Schenley)
- Organized crime (Lansky/Meyer Lansky)
- [[J Edgar Hoover]] (close personal relationship)

His ex-wife Susan Rosenstiel was the primary source for [[Anthony Summers]]'s [[Hoover Blue Room Allegation]] claim about Hoover. Whether that specific allegation is true or not, the documented Rosenstiel-Hoover relationship is one of the foundational cases of the cross-cutting business / organized-crime / federal-law-enforcement network the vault [[Compromised-Elites Network]] framework traces.
