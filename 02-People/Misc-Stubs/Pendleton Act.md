# Pendleton Civil Service Reform Act (1883)

US federal statute that established the merit-based civil service system. Replaced the spoils system. Created the Civil Service Commission (predecessor to OPM).

## Vault relevance

The Pendleton Act is the foundational legal-institutional framework that [[Schedule F]] / [[Schedule F implementation 2025]] is restructuring. The Act has structured US federal employment for 140+ years. The second Trump term represents the largest direct challenge to the Pendleton framework in modern US history.
