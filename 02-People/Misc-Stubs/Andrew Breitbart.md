# Andrew Breitbart (1969-2012)

US conservative journalist and entrepreneur. Founded Breitbart News (2007). Served as editorial collaborator with Matt Drudge (Drudge Report). Died at age 43 of heart failure.

## Significance

Breitbart created the institutional template for movement-conservative independent media that supplanted mainstream-media gatekeeping. After his death, [[Steve Bannon]] took over Breitbart News and pivoted it explicitly toward the Trump candidacy 2015-2016.

The phrase "politics is downstream from culture" associated with Breitbart became the organizing slogan of the broader culture-war strategy that [[Christopher Rufo]] subsequently operationalized.
