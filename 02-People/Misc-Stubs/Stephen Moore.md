# Stephen Moore

US conservative economic commentator. Co-founder of the Club for Growth (1999). Founder of Heritage's Project for Economic Growth. Senior economic adviser to Trump campaign 2016, 2020, 2024.

## Significance

Moore is the supply-side / tax-cut economic-policy voice within the broader conservative movement. Has been the bridge between [[Heritage Foundation]] / [[Koch network]] tax-cut policy preferences and Trump-administration economic policy implementation in both terms.
