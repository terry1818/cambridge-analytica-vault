# John Tanton (1934-2019)

US ophthalmologist. Founder and intellectual architect of the contemporary US anti-immigration / nativist movement infrastructure.

## Organizations founded by Tanton

- Federation for American Immigration Reform (FAIR) - 1979
- Center for Immigration Studies (CIS) - 1985
- NumbersUSA - 1996
- Social Contract Press - publisher of "The Camp of the Saints" (Raspail)

## Significance

Tanton is the single most-documented institutional architect of the contemporary US anti-immigration coalition. His network of organizations - all originally Tanton-funded, now operationally semi-independent - produces the policy infrastructure, talking points, and credentialed expertise that the [[Stephen Miller]] / [[Tom Homan]] / [[Project 2025]] immigration agenda draws on.

The SPLC has designated multiple Tanton organizations as hate groups based on Tanton's documented white-nationalist correspondence and ideological positioning.
