# Larry Diamond

Stanford political scientist. Senior fellow at the Hoover Institution. Founder/co-editor of the Journal of Democracy.

## Significance

Diamond is the most-cited contemporary scholar on democratic-quality measurement worldwide. His "democratic recession" framing (originated c.2014) has become canonical academic vocabulary for the post-2010 worldwide decline in democratic governance. The framework is one of the comparative-context scaffolds the vault [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]] and [[Structural Capture Thesis]] situate within.
