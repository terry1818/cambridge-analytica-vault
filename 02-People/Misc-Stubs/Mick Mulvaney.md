# Mick Mulvaney

US Representative (R-SC, 2011-2017). Director of OMB (2017-2020). Acting White House Chief of Staff (2019-2020). [[House Freedom Caucus]] founding member.

## Trump-era role

Mulvaney's October 17, 2019 press conference - in which he publicly confirmed quid-pro-quo Ukraine policy ("Get over it") and then tried to walk it back - was one of the documented admissions during the [[First Impeachment of Donald Trump]] period.

## Post-Trump-administration

Resigned January 2021 over January 6 conduct. Became ambassador. Has subsequently distanced from Trump.
