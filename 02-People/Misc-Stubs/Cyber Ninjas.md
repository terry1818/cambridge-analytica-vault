# Cyber Ninjas

Florida-based cybersecurity firm. Contracted by the Arizona State Senate to conduct the 2021 "audit" of [[Maricopa County]] 2020 election results.

## 2020 Arizona "audit"

- April-September 2021
- Funded substantially by [[Patrick Byrne]] and other election-denial donors (~.7M, less than 5% from state appropriations)
- Methodology criticized by election experts; never adopted by any other audit
- Final report (September 2021) actually found Biden's margin larger than the certified result, but Cyber Ninjas framed multiple "anomalies" without changing the result
- Company shut down January 2022 facing contempt sanctions
