# Murdoch family

Controlling family of [[News Corp]] (print/publishing) and Fox Corporation (TV/cable). Patriarch: Rupert Murdoch (b. 1931). Children: Lachlan, James, Elisabeth, Prudence.

## Succession question

The Murdoch family's intra-generational succession (which child controls the media empire after Rupert) is the operative governance question for the future of the largest privately-controlled US conservative media apparatus. Lachlan has been the most-conservative heir; James the most-liberal (resigned from News Corp board 2020 citing editorial disagreements).
