# Wednesday Meeting

Weekly coordination meeting in Washington DC convened by [[Grover Norquist]] since 1993. Approximately 150-200 conservative organization leaders attend each Wednesday.

## Significance

The Wednesday Meeting is the most-cited conservative-coordination forum in DC after the (quarterly) [[Council for National Policy]]. Less formal than CNP but more operational - the venue for week-by-week message coordination, talking-point distribution, and organizational alignment.

The Wednesday Meeting plus CNP plus Heritage Foundation roundtables constitute the documented institutional coordination infrastructure of contemporary US movement conservatism.
