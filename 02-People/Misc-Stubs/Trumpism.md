# Trumpism

Political movement and ideological tendency centered on [[Donald Trump]]. Combines populist economic framing, nativist immigration policy, demographic-defensive cultural politics, anti-institutional posture, and a personalist leader-centered mode.

## Vault-positioned

Trumpism is one wing of the broader contemporary right coalition - the populist mass-political vehicle. Distinguished from:
- [[Religious right]] (theological / cultural wing)
- [[Tech right]] (secular libertarian-to-post-liberal wing)
- [[Convention of States]] / [[Federalist Society]] (structural-amendment / judicial wing)

All four wings overlap operationally but have distinct internal cultures and ideological orientations.
