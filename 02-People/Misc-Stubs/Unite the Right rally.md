# Unite the Right rally (Charlottesville, August 11-12, 2017)

White-nationalist gathering in Charlottesville, Virginia. Largest public white-supremacist gathering in modern US history.

## Documented events

- August 11 night: Torch march through University of Virginia campus chanting "Jews will not replace us"
- August 12: Counter-protester Heather Heyer killed when James Alex Fields Jr. drove car into crowd
- Multiple injuries, two state police officers killed in helicopter crash
- Trump's "very fine people on both sides" response became one of the defining moments of his first term

## Significance

Unite the Right is the documented case of contemporary US white-nationalist movement-organizing producing real-world violence at scale. Trump's response signaled to the movement that the administration would not treat it as oppositional.
