# Wall Street Journal

US business daily newspaper. Founded 1889. Owned by [[News Corp]] (Murdoch).

## Vault relevance

The WSJ editorial page is the canonical voice of business-class conservatism. The newsroom maintains substantial independence from the editorial side. WSJ broke multiple Trump-era stories including Stormy Daniels hush payment reporting (January 2018) and Saudi-Kushner financial flows.
