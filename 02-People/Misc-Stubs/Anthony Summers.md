# Anthony Summers

British investigative journalist and biographer. Author of "Official and Confidential: The Secret Life of J. Edgar Hoover" (1993) - the source of the contested [[Hoover Blue Room Allegation]].

## Vault relevance

Summers's Hoover biography is the documented primary source for the Hoover Blue Room story. His use of Susan Rosenstiel as primary source for that specific allegation has been criticized by subsequent Hoover biographers (Gentry, Weiner) but the broader pattern of Hoover-Rosenstiel-organized crime connections in the book is largely uncontested.
