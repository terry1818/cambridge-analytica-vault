# Shari Redstone

US media executive. President of National Amusements (controlling shareholder of Paramount Global). Daughter of late Sumner Redstone.

## Skydance merger 2024-2025

- July 2024: Announced sale of Paramount Global to Skydance Media (David Ellison)
- Sale closed 2025 after FCC approval (controversial)
- Reports document Trump-administration pressure regarding 60 Minutes coverage of Trump (Variety, NYT reporting); Paramount/Skydance settled the Trump lawsuit and parted ways with multiple 60 Minutes / CBS News personnel
