# National Enquirer

US supermarket tabloid. Owned by [[American Media Inc]] (AMI) under [[David Pecker]]. Functioned as a "catch and kill" operation for Trump during the 2016 campaign cycle.

## Catch-and-kill role (Tier 1, established at People v Trump 2024)

- AMI purchased the rights to multiple stories damaging to Trump (Karen McDougal affair, Dino Sajudin doorman story) specifically to suppress them
- AMI also coordinated negative stories about Trump's primary opponents
- David Pecker testified to this arrangement at the May 2024 hush-money trial
