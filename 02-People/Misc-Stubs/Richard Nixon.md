# Richard Nixon (1913-1994)

37th President of the United States (1969-1974). Resigned August 9, 1974 facing certain impeachment over the Watergate scandal.

## Vault relevance

Nixon is the standing US precedent for presidential criminal conduct facing institutional accountability. The mid-1974 sequence (House Judiciary articles of impeachment, Supreme Court tapes ruling, Republican delegation visit to White House) established the political-norm baseline that the 2017-2025 Trump-era institutional responses have been measured against.

The contrast - that Nixon was forced from office while Trump's analogous (and more egregious by many measures) conduct produced no equivalent removal - is one of the structural facts the vault [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]] and [[Structural Capture Thesis]] are trying to explain.
