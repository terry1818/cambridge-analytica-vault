# Charles Colson (1931-2012)

US lawyer. Nixon White House Special Counsel (1969-1973). Convicted in Watergate (obstruction of justice). Founded Prison Fellowship 1976 following religious conversion. Co-author of "Evangelicals and Catholics Together" (1994).

## Significance for vault

Colson is the bridge figure between the Watergate-era institutional Republican legal-corruption period and the post-1976 religious-conservative organizing infrastructure. His co-authorship with [[Richard John Neuhaus]] of the Evangelicals and Catholics Together statements helped construct the modern Religious right's traditionalist Catholic-evangelical coalition.
