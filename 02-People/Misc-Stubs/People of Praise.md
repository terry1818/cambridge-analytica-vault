# People of Praise

US charismatic Christian community founded 1971. Headquartered in South Bend, Indiana. Members include Supreme Court Justice [[Amy Coney Barrett]].

## Structure

- Approximately 1,700 members across 22 US cities
- Practices include "head" / spiritual-advisor relationships, intentional community living, charismatic-gift emphasis
- Roots in the post-Vatican-II Catholic charismatic renewal

## Significance for vault

People of Praise membership became publicly relevant during Justice Barrett's 2020 Supreme Court confirmation. The community structure (head-of-household authority over members, gender-role traditionalism, communal property arrangements) generated public scrutiny that the senator-confirmation process largely set aside.

The vault notes People of Praise as one institutional node within the broader [[Religious right]] / [[Christian Nationalism]] cluster - a charismatic-Catholic instance distinct from the more politically-organized FRC / Heritage / CNP ecosystem.
