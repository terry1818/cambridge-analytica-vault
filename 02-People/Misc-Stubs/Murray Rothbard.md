# Murray Rothbard (1926-1995)

US Austrian-school economist. Co-founder (with Llewellyn Rockwell Jr.) of the [[Mises Institute]] (1982). Architect of the paleolibertarian / paleoconservative political current.

## Vault relevance

Rothbard is the intellectual bridge between traditional libertarian economic theory ([[Friedrich Hayek]] / Mises) and the contemporary [[Tech right]] / national-conservative current. The paleo-Rothbard framework (anti-cosmopolitan, anti-immigration, anti-federal-government) is more directly relevant to the contemporary right than Hayekian classical liberalism.

[[Curtis Yarvin]] and other Tech right figures cite Rothbard approvingly.
