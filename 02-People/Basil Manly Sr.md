# Basil Manly Sr. (1798-1868)

US Southern Baptist minister. Slaveholder. President of the University of Alabama 1837-1855. One of the founders of the Southern Baptist Convention (1845) - the denominational split from northern Baptists over slavery.

## Vault relevance

Manly is referenced in the vault primarily for the historical-founding role in the [[Southern Baptist Convention]] - which became the largest US Protestant denomination and a primary institutional vehicle of the contemporary [[Religious right]]. The slaveholding-founding context is one of the structural facts about the SBC's historical positioning that contemporary analyses (Robert P Jones, Anthea Butler) draw on.
