# Ghislaine Maxwell

Born Maisons-Laffitte, France, December 25, 1961. Youngest daughter of media baron [[Robert Maxwell]]. Convicted December 29, 2021 in [[Maxwell 2021 SDNY Trial]] on five of six counts including sex trafficking of a minor. Sentenced June 2022 to 20 years in federal prison.
