# Bari Weiss

## Summary

**American journalist + media executive** born March 25, 1984. **Editor-in-Chief CBS News since November 2025** (post-Paramount Skydance acquisition of The Free Press). **Founder + EIC of [[The Free Press]]** (originally Substack "Common Sense" 2022). **Former NYT Opinion Editor 2017-2020** (resigned July 2020 with high-profile open resignation letter alleging illiberal newsroom culture).

**Editorial positioning:** centrist-to-right; vocal Israel-supportive (particularly post-October-7 2023); anti-woke / free-speech; substantial Israel-cluster + tech-right editorial overlap. **Multiple documented Andreessen Horowitz + Founders Fund + Sequoia adjacencies via The Free Press funding rounds + speaking engagements.**

**Operationally significant** as the **principal editorial bridge between Paramount Skydance acquisition + CBS News editorial repositioning** post-merger. Her installation as CBS News EIC November 2025 was the **catalyst for the documented post-merger CBS News editorial shift** including multiple high-profile journalist departures + 60 Minutes restructuring.
