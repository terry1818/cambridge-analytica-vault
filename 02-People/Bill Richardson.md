# Bill Richardson

Born November 15, 1947, Pasadena, CA. Died September 1, 2023, Chatham, MA, age 75. Democratic politician: US Representative (NM-3, 1983-1997), US Ambassador to the UN (1997-1998), US Secretary of Energy (1998-2001) under Clinton, Governor of New Mexico (2003-2011), 2008 presidential candidate. Named by [[Virginia Giuffre]] in a 2015 deposition; she later partially walked back portions of the testimony; Richardson denied. Never criminally charged. Died before further public-record litigation occurred.

Richardson, alive at the time of the 2024 unsealing, denied the allegations through a spokesperson.
