# Konstantin Malofeev

## Summary

Russian businessman, oligarch, and conservative activist. Founder of **Marshall Capital Partners** investment fund. Founder of **Tsargrad TV** (right-wing Russian Orthodox media network). Major funder of the **Russian Orthodox Church St. Basil the Great Foundation**. **Sanctioned by US Treasury (2014) and EU (2022)** in connection with Russian support for separatists in Ukraine. Has played a significant role in **transnational right-wing networking** including connections to European far-right figures and US conservative Christians.

## Tsargrad TV

Founded 2015; broadcasts conservative Russian Orthodox content. Modeled partly on US Fox News. Promotes:
- Russian Orthodox religious nationalism
- Putin-aligned political messaging
- Anti-Western "traditional values" framing
- Hostility to liberal democracy, LGBTQ+ rights, EU integration

## Transnational right-wing networking

Malofeev has reportedly:
- Funded/coordinated with European far-right parties
- Met with US conservative Christians at religious-conservative conferences
- Supported various "traditional values" international organizations
- Provided financial and operational support to Russian-aligned separatist movements in Ukraine
