# Javier Milei

Javier Milei (born 1970) is the President of Argentina since December 2023. Milei is the principal contemporary Latin American libertarian-right operational figure and has substantial documented US Tech-Right and Atlas Network alignment.

## Pre-presidency trajectory

Milei was previously an academic economist and television personality, building public visibility through aggressive libertarian-economic media commentary. His 2021 election to the Argentine Chamber of Deputies and 2023 presidential campaign produced a substantial domestic operational base substantially anchored in the [[Atlas Network]]-affiliated Argentine libertarian-think-tank ecosystem.

## US conservative-movement alignment

Milei's US conservative-movement alignment has been operationally substantial:

- Multiple high-profile meetings with Donald Trump pre-and-post the 2024 US election
- Public endorsement of Elon Musk and substantial documented operational coordination with Musk personally
- Substantial alignment with the Atlas Network international libertarian think-tank ecosystem
- Featured speaker at multiple US conservative-movement events including CPAC

## Operational policy programme

The Milei administration's operational policy programme has included substantial Argentine peso devaluation, substantial federal-spending cuts (including the elimination of multiple government ministries), substantial privatisation programming, and the broader libertarian-economic shock-therapy programme. The economic-and-political consequences are continuing through 2025-2026.

## Why Milei matters for the broader vault network

Milei represents one of the cleanest international cases of the Tech-Right libertarian-economic intellectual programme being operationally implemented at the national-government level. The Argentine case has been substantially cited in US conservative-movement discourse as a model for what aggressive libertarian-economic policy implementation looks like in practice.
