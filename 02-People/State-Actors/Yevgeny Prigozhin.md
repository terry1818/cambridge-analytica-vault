# Yevgeny Prigozhin

## Summary

Russian oligarch known as "**Putin's chef**" for his catering company's lucrative Kremlin contracts. Funder of the **[[Internet Research Agency]]** (the troll farm that conducted Russia's 2016 US election interference operation). Head of the **Wagner Group** private military company that operated in Ukraine, Syria, Mali, Sudan, Libya, and other locations. **Indicted by [[Mueller Investigation]]** in February 2018 for IRA operations against US 2016 election. **Led abortive military mutiny against Putin in June 2023.** **Killed in plane crash August 23, 2023**, two months after the mutiny.

## IRA operations

The [[Internet Research Agency]] under Prigozhin's funding ran the largest documented Russian state-linked influence operation against the 2016 US election:

- ~80+ "Translator" department staff devoted to US operations
- Hundreds of fake American social media accounts
- Millions in spending
- Reached 126M Facebook users (per Facebook 2017 disclosure)
- Mueller indicted Prigozhin and 12 other Russian nationals + 2 companies in February 2018

## Wagner Group

Private military company that became increasingly important Russian state instrument:

- Operated in Syria (2015+) supporting Assad
- Ukraine 2014+ (Donbass)
- Central African Republic, Mali, Libya, Sudan -- coup-adjacent operations
- 2022-2023 -- major role in Russian invasion of Ukraine (Bakhmut)
- Recruited prisoners from Russian penal system
- Long-running tensions with Russian Defense Ministry

## The June 2023 mutiny

- **2023-06-23 to 06-24** -- Wagner forces marched toward Moscow in armed mutiny
- Prigozhin publicly attacked Russian military leadership
- Mutiny stopped abruptly after deal brokered by Belarus
- Prigozhin reportedly to relocate to Belarus

## Death

- **2023-08-23** -- private jet crashed near Tver, Russia
- Prigozhin and 9 others on board, all killed
- Widely attributed to Russian state assassination in revenge for mutiny
- Russian government denied involvement
- IRA reportedly restructured post-Prigozhin

## CA-relevance

Prigozhin's IRA ran in **parallel to CA** during the 2016 election:
- Both targeted same election environment
- No documented direct coordination
- Both Trump-favorable in net effect
- Some Russian-IP infrastructure overlap with [[Aleksandr Kogan]] / Cambridge Psychometrics Centre per ICO findings
