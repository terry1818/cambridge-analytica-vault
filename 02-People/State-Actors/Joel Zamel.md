# Joel Zamel

## Summary

Australian-Israeli businessman who founded [[Psy-Group]] and [[Wikistrat]]. **Signed Memorandum of Understanding with [[Cambridge Analytica]] on 2016-12-14**, confirmed by Channel 4 and Wall Street Journal reporting. Per Mueller probe, Zamel met with Donald Trump Jr. on 2016-08-03 (alongside George Nader and Erik Prince) and pitched Trump campaign a multi-million-dollar social-media manipulation proposal. Subject of Mueller investigation. ([[Source Wikipedia - Cambridge Analytica]], [[Source Tau Ballhaus 2018-05-23 WSJ Psy-Group CA]])
