# Konstantin Rykov

## Summary

Russian internet entrepreneur and former United Russia Duma deputy. **Posted a 2016 social media boast claiming Russian intelligence operations involving Donald Trump dating to 1987.** Per his account, the operation included long-term cultivation, business interests, and 2016 election support. Rykov's claims are not independently verified but represent a notable open-source-intelligence (OSINT) thread examined by some journalists and Russia analysts. Connected to early Russian internet/media empire (Vzgliad, Dni.ru) and to Konstantin Malofeev's Tsargrad media operation. The Rykov claims are best treated as `#confidence/speculative` but worth documenting given their explicit reference to Trump-era operations.
