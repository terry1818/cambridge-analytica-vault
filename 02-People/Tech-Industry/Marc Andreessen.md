# Marc Andreessen

## Summary

Co-founder of **Andreessen Horowitz (a16z)**; previously co-founded **Netscape** (1994). ~$1.7B+ net worth. **Author of the 2023 Techno-Optimist Manifesto** and **2024 Little Tech Agenda** - the two foundational documents of the Trump-aligned tech-right's public framing. **Endorsed Trump 2024**; coordinates with [[Peter Thiel]], [[David Sacks]], Elon Musk. **Resigned Meta board 2024** to fully commit to Trump-aligned positioning.

a16z is the **largest VC firm in the US by AUM** (~$45B). Major investments in crypto (Coinbase, Solana), AI (OpenAI, Mistral, multiple), defense-tech (Anduril, Skydio), [[Flock Safety]], and broader tech-right portfolio.
