# Sam Altman

## Summary

**OpenAI CEO since 2019.** Born April 22, 1985. Former **Y Combinator President 2014-2019**. **Co-announcer of [[Project Stargate]]** with Donald Trump + Larry Ellison + Masayoshi Son at White House January 21, 2025. **Co-founder of [[Worldcoin]]** / Tools for Humanity (iris-scanning crypto-identity project).

**Briefly removed by OpenAI board November 17, 2023; reinstated within 5 days** following employee + investor + Microsoft pressure. **Restructured OpenAI board post-incident** with more business-aligned membership.

**Trump-curious without explicit Trump endorsement** - documented post-2024-election positioning shift from Biden-era critical posture to Trump 2.0 cooperation including Stargate joint announcement.
