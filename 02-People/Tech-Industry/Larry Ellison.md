# Larry Ellison

## Summary

American technology billionaire; **co-founder, Executive Chairman, and CTO of Oracle Corporation** (founded 1977; one of the largest software companies in the world). World's 3rd or 4th richest person (alongside Musk, Bezos, Zuckerberg). Long-time **major Republican donor**; **explicitly Trump-aligned in 2024-2026 era** despite being older than most other Tech Right principals. Major participant in [[Project Stargate]] (January 2025 $500B AI infrastructure announcement). **Principal financial backer (~$6B+) of son David Ellison's August 2024 Skydance-Paramount merger** that gave the family operational control of one of the largest US media empires.

## Trump alignment

Ellison's Trump alignment is **less ideologically motivated** than younger Tech Right figures but operationally significant:

- Long-time Republican donor
- Hosted Trump fundraisers
- 2020: Oracle / Ellison emerged as TikTok-acquisition white knight in Trump first-term ByteDance divestment saga (didn't ultimately close)
- 2024: major Trump donor
- 2025-01-21: **standing alongside Trump and [[Sam Altman]] for Stargate announcement**
- Oracle is a primary tenant of Stargate AI data centers
- 2024-08: principal backer of son David's Paramount Skydance merger (~$6B+ equity)

## Media empire (via son David)

**Larry Ellison personally backed son [[David Ellison]]'s August 7, 2024 acquisition of Paramount Global** through the Skydance-Paramount merger. **Larry provided ~$6B+ in equity** making the Ellison family the controlling owner of [[Paramount Skydance Corporation]] - one of the largest US media empires controlling:

- CBS broadcast network + CBS News + 60 Minutes
- Paramount Pictures
- Showtime + Paramount+ + Pluto TV
- MTV, Nickelodeon, Comedy Central, BET, plus full cable portfolio
- 15 CBS-owned-and-operated stations in major US markets

**Subsequently backed acquisition of [[The Free Press]]** (Bari Weiss) October 2025 and installation of Weiss as CBS News Editor-in-Chief November 2025.

**See [[Ellison Media Empire]] for full cluster analysis.**

## The Oracle / TikTok / ByteDance thread

A defining **multi-year Big Tech ↔ Trump policy negotiation**:

- 2020 -- Trump first term ordered TikTok divestment from ByteDance
- Oracle emerged as US-side acquirer candidate
- Trump first-term deal never closed; Biden administration largely shelved the issue
- 2022 -- Oracle began hosting ALL US TikTok user data via "Project Texas" in Oracle Cloud Texas data centers
- 2024 -- TikTok divestment legislation passed (PAFACA)
- 2025+ -- Trump 2.0 evolved position: complex deal involving Oracle as US partner
- The Oracle-TikTok arrangement is the **template for "national security through US-partner control"** of Chinese-owned platforms

## Stargate role

Oracle is providing:
- Data center infrastructure for the Stargate AI training facilities

The Oracle-OpenAI-SoftBank-MGX (UAE) **Stargate consortium** represents the largest single AI infrastructure commitment ever announced.
