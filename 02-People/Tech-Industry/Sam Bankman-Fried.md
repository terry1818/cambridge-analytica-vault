# Sam Bankman-Fried (SBF)

## Summary

American former cryptocurrency executive. Founder of **FTX cryptocurrency exchange** (2019) and **Alameda Research** (2017). At peak (late 2021 / early 2022) was valued at ~$26 billion; spectacularly **collapsed in November 2022** amid revelations of fraud and customer-fund misappropriation. **Convicted of seven counts of fraud in November 2023; sentenced to 25 years in federal prison in March 2024.** Currently serving sentence at FCI Mendota.

## Effective Altruism (EA) connection

SBF was the **largest single donor to the Effective Altruism movement** during 2020-2022:

- Aligned with EA's longtermism and AI-safety priorities
- Funded EA organizations including Open Philanthropy adjacent entities, MIRI, multiple AI-safety research groups
- Pledged the majority of his wealth to EA causes ("Earning to give")
- His collapse devastated EA institutional funding base

## Anthropic founding

**SBF was a major early investor in [[Anthropic]]** (2021-2022):

- Pre-collapse: invested approximately $500 million in Anthropic
- The investment was made via FTX / Alameda entities
- After FTX collapse, the Anthropic stake became a **bankruptcy estate asset**
- 2024: Anthropic stake was sold by the FTX bankruptcy estate for substantially higher than original investment, recovering significant value for FTX creditors

This means Anthropic -- positioned as the "AI safety alternative" to OpenAI -- was **substantially funded by what turned out to be misappropriated FTX customer funds**.

## Political donations

In addition to EA donations, SBF was a major political donor:

- **2020 cycle:** ~$5M to Biden campaign and Democratic causes
- **2022 cycle:** ~$40M+ to mostly Democratic causes, with some Republican donations
- Was the **second-largest Democratic donor** in the 2022 cycle
- Argued for crypto-favorable regulation through political donations

After collapse, most of these donations were returned or clawed back via bankruptcy proceedings.

## Connection to broader vault story

SBF's story illustrates the **EA → Tech ecosystem → AI lab funding pipeline**:

1. EA movement provided ideological framework for "longtermism" / AI safety
2. EA-aligned wealthy donors (including SBF) funded AI safety research
3. AI safety research became foundation of Anthropic
4. Anthropic positioned as alternative to OpenAI
5. AI safety discourse generally became politicized 2023-2024 (a16z and Tech Right attacked "AI safety" as regulatory capture)
6. Trump 2.0 administration largely de-prioritized AI safety

The SBF / FTX collapse **discredited the EA movement institutionally** while leaving its operational outputs (Anthropic, AI safety research infrastructure) in place. The Tech Right has since substantially **co-opted or displaced** the EA-adjacent AI safety discourse.
