## Turning Point USA and the campus-conservative pipeline

Charlie Kirk (born 1993) founded [[Turning Point USA]] (TPUSA) in 2012 at age 18, as a campus-organising vehicle for conservative students at high schools and colleges across the US. The TPUSA model is the most operationally successful single conservative-movement youth-organising vehicle of the past two decades: chapter network at 3,000+ high schools and colleges by 2024-2025, annual conferences (Student Action Summit, AmericaFest) drawing 10,000+ attendees, and a substantial digital-media operation that has become the principal MAGA-aligned voice in the younger-conservative demographic.

The TPUSA operational style is distinctive: explicit MAGA alignment from the platform's earlier years (Kirk was an early Trump endorser and has maintained continuous public alignment), aggressive campus-confrontation tactics designed to generate viral video content, integration with the broader MAGA-media ecosystem (Kirk's podcast is among the highest-reach MAGA voices), and substantial political-spending coordination through aligned vehicles (Turning Point Action, the 501(c)(4) advocacy arm).

## The 2024 cycle ground-game operation

The 2024 election cycle saw TPUSA and Turning Point Action deploy what was reportedly the largest single MAGA-aligned ground operation in Arizona, with smaller but still substantial operations in Wisconsin, Michigan, and Pennsylvania. The model: paid canvassers, sophisticated voter-targeting infrastructure, and explicit cooperation with the Trump campaign's broader ground operation. The post-election analyses have reached varying conclusions about the ground operation's actual effect on turnout; the operation's existence and scale is documented.

## Religious-right adjacency
