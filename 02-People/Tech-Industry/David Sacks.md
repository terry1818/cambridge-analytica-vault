# David Sacks

David Sacks (born 1972) is a [[PayPal Mafia]] member (PayPal COO 2001-2002), founder of Yammer (sold to Microsoft 2012), co-founder and general partner of Craft Ventures (VC firm), and co-host of the [[All-In Podcast]]. Since January 2025 Sacks has served as the Trump 2.0 administration's "AI and Crypto Czar," a White House Special Government Employee role.

## The All-In Podcast and the 2022-2024 political realignment

Sacks has been operationally central to the [[All-In Podcast]] cohort (Sacks, [[Chamath Palihapitiya]], [[Jason Calacanis]], [[David Friedberg]]) that has been the principal venue for the 2022-2024 Silicon Valley political realignment. The podcast's weekly long-form format has provided a venue for Sacks and his co-hosts to:

- Endorse Trump (Sacks personally endorsed Trump in 2024)
- Coordinate Tech-Right policy positioning
- Develop the broader Silicon Valley right-aligned intellectual ecosystem
- Provide an alternative-media platform for Trump-administration figures and Tech-Right principals

## The AI and Crypto Czar role

Sacks's January 2025 appointment as White House AI and Crypto Czar is operationally distinctive as a Special Government Employee position. The SGE classification allows Sacks to continue his Craft Ventures operational role while serving in the White House advisory capacity. The arrangement has produced substantial conflict-of-interest concerns because:

- Craft Ventures portfolio companies include substantial crypto-and-AI exposure
- Sacks's White House role directly shapes federal crypto-and-AI policy
- The SGE classification allows Sacks to participate in policy decisions affecting his personal investment portfolio without the standard conflict-of-interest restrictions that full-time federal employees face

## Crypto-policy operational outputs

The Sacks-coordinated Trump 2.0 crypto-policy outputs have included:

- Executive orders on crypto-favourable regulatory positioning
- Coordination with [[Fairshake PAC]] and [[Coinbase]]-aligned policy advocacy
- Substantial reduction in SEC crypto enforcement under post-Gensler leadership
- The proposed Strategic Crypto Reserve programme
- Substantial coordination with [[World Liberty Financial]]-aligned policy decisions

## Why this is load-bearing

Sacks is the single human node connecting the [[Peter Thiel]]-network venture-capital ecosystem (Sacks was a PayPal Mafia member alongside Thiel and Musk), the All-In Podcast media coordination layer, the Tech-Right political-financing infrastructure, and the Trump 2.0 White House operational core on crypto-and-AI policy decisions.
