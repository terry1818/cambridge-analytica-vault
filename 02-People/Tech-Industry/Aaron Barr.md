# Aaron Barr

## Summary

American cybersecurity executive; CEO of [[HBGary Federal]] when **Anonymous hacked the firm in February 2011** and released ~70,000 internal emails. The leak exposed:

- Barr's plan to "out" Anonymous members at a security conference (the precipitating cause)
- HBGary Federal's pitch to **Bank of America** for an anti-WikiLeaks disinformation campaign
- HBGary Federal's pitch to **US Chamber of Commerce** to attack unions and progressive groups
- The **US Centcom "Operation Earnest Voice"** persona-management contract

Barr resigned within weeks. HBGary Federal effectively ceased operations. Barr later worked at other security firms but never returned to public prominence.
