# Stormy Daniels

Born Stephanie Gregory March 17, 1979, Baton Rouge, LA. American adult-film performer, director, and writer; stage name Stormy Daniels. Alleged 2006 sexual encounter with [[Donald Trump]] at the Lake Tahoe American Century Championship celebrity golf event. October 27, 2016 received $130,000 hush-money payment via [[Michael Cohen]]'s shell company Essential Consultants LLC, in exchange for non-disclosure agreement (NDA). The 2016 payment and its 2017 reimbursement structure became the factual predicate for [[People v Trump NY Hush Money 2024]] â€” the first criminal prosecution and conviction of a former US President.

## The 2006 alleged encounter

Per Daniels's accounts (including a 2011 *In Touch Weekly* interview that was not published until 2018, her 2018 memoir *Full Disclosure*, her 60 Minutes interview March 2018, and her 2024 trial testimony):

- Met Trump at the American Century Championship celebrity golf event, July 2006, Lake Tahoe
- Trump invited her to dinner; the dinner became a sexual encounter in his hotel suite
- Trump told her she reminded him of his daughter Ivanka
- They had consensual sex (Daniels has explicitly stated it was consensual; this is not an assault allegation)
- Trump mentioned the possibility of her appearing on *The Apprentice*

Trump has denied the encounter occurred.

## 2011 disclosure attempt

Daniels gave an interview to *In Touch Weekly* in 2011 disclosing the alleged encounter. Cohen reportedly threatened Daniels and the magazine and the story was not published. Cohen's 2018 plea agreement referenced this period.

## 2016 hush-money payment

- $130,000 paid by Cohen to Davidson on October 27, 2016 via Cohen's Essential Consultants LLC
- Non-disclosure agreement using pseudonyms ("Peggy Peterson" / "David Dennison")
- $1M liquidated damages provision

Cohen wired the $130,000 from a Manhattan branch of First Republic Bank. The wire transfer documentation became key trial evidence.

## 2018 disclosure and litigation

- January 12, 2018: *Wall Street Journal* reported the payment
- January 17, 2018: Trump denied the payment and the encounter
- February 13, 2018: Cohen issued statement acknowledging the payment but stating he made it personally
- March 6, 2018: Daniels filed suit in Los Angeles seeking to void the NDA
- March 25, 2018: 60 Minutes interview aired
- October 2018: Federal judge ruled Daniels's defamation suit against Trump could proceed
- 2018-2020: various litigation including Daniels eventually owing Trump some attorney fees on procedural grounds

## 2024 trial testimony

Daniels testified at the People v Trump trial May 7-9, 2024. Her testimony:
- Established the 2006 encounter as factual context
- Established the 2016 negotiation and payment
- Was subject to extensive cross-examination focused on her credibility and motives
- Was not the primary evidence of Trump's knowledge of the falsified records (that came from Cohen and documentary evidence) but provided the factual narrative
