# Nigel Oakes

## Summary

British founder of the [[SCL Group]] (originally **Behavioural Dynamics Institute**, 1990; renamed Strategic Communication Laboratories, 1993). Background in TV production and advertising. Co-founder of [[Cambridge Analytica]] (2013) alongside [[Alexander Nix]] and his brother [[Alexander Oakes]]. Former boyfriend of Lady Helen Windsor. Acknowledged in May 2018 that [[Emerdata Limited]] had intended to acquire SCL/CA assets but said plans were abandoned. ([[Source Wikipedia - SCL Group]])

## Key facts

- **Founded:** Behavioural Dynamics Institute (1990); SCL (1993); co-founded CA (2013)
- **Background:** TV production, advertising
- **Notable connection:** former partner of Lady Helen Windsor
- **2018-05:** stated Emerdata's intended acquisition of SCL/CA was abandoned
- **Vision:** academic insights from BDI psychologists/anthropologists applied to mass behavior change

## Connections

- **Brother:** [[Alexander Oakes]] (SCL co-director)
- **Co-founder:** [[Alexander Nix]]
- **Backers:** [[Robert Mercer]] family (CA-side); Paul David Ashburner Nix (Nix's father); [[Jonathan Marland]]; [[Roger Gabb]]
- **Successor vehicles:** [[Emerdata Limited]], [[Firecrest Technologies]]
