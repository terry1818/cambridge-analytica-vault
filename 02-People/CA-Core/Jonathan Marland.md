# Jonathan Marland

## Summary

British businessman and Conservative politician; **Baron Marland of Odstock** (life peer, House of Lords). Served as **Conservative Party Treasurer 2005-2010** and Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State (BIS) 2010-2013 in David Cameron's government. Marland appears in the vault as part of the **UK aristocratic / Conservative-establishment funding circle** that backed [[SCL Group]] in its pre-CA defence-and-influence-contracting phase ([[Source Cadwalladr 2017-02-26 Observer Robert Mercer]] and follow-on reporting). The early SCL backer roster — including Marland, [[Roger Gabb]], [[Lord Ivar Mountbatten]], [[Vincent Tchenguiz]], and others — is the **UK-side capital tier** that predates Mercer involvement.

## Why he matters

The UK funding tier of SCL is **structurally distinct** from the later Mercer-funded CA operation:

- **Pre-Mercer SCL:** UK aristocratic + Conservative-Party-adjacent capital, defence-contracting orientation, "old establishment" patron class
- **CA-era SCL:** US-based hedge-fund capital (Mercer), election-services orientation, Bannon-aligned ideological patron class

Marland sits in the first tier. Mapping the **UK patron-class transition** between these two funding eras is a key open thread in the [[Hypothesis - Defense-to-elections pipeline]] story — and Marland is one of the names that bridges the pre-CA establishment SCL backer list and the post-CA continuity (Conservative ties to current UK government work; see [[Emic]]).
