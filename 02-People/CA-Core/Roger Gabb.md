# Roger Gabb

## Summary

British businessman; founder of **Western Wines** (later sold to Constellation Brands); long-running **major Conservative Party donor**. Reported among the **UK establishment / aristocratic backers of [[SCL Group]]** in its pre-CA defence-and-influence-contracting phase ([[Source Brown 2018-03-21 Times SCL founders]] and adjacent reporting). Sits in the same UK-funding tier as [[Jonathan Marland]], [[Lord Ivar Mountbatten]], and [[Vincent Tchenguiz]] — the patron class that predates the [[Robert Mercer|Mercer]] capital injection that powered CA proper.

## Key facts

- **Business:** founder and former Chairman of Western Wines (UK wine import / merchant business; one of the largest UK-based wine companies before sale to Constellation in 2005 for ~£100M)
- **Political role:** sustained major Conservative Party donor across multiple election cycles; reported among the larger individual Tory donors
- **SCL relationship:** reported financial backer of SCL Group's early operations #confidence/medium
- **UK establishment circle:** part of the cross-Conservative-business-aristocratic network that funded SCL pre-Mercer

## Why he matters

Gabb's name surfaces in the [[Source Brown 2018-03-21 Times SCL founders|early SCL backer reporting]] as part of the **financial scaffolding** that allowed Nigel Oakes to build SCL into a defence and influence-operations contractor before the Mercer-CA era. Understanding the **UK aristocratic / Conservative-business funding tier** is essential to the [[Hypothesis - Defense-to-elections pipeline|defense-to-elections pipeline]] story:

- The UK SCL backers had **access** to Conservative-government defence and FCO contracts
- The defence-contracting work **created the methodology** that was later commercialized as election services for CA's clients
- This pre-Mercer phase is what made SCL **plausible as a vendor** when the Mercers later invested

Gabb is not a CA-era figure proper; he is part of the **patron-class infrastructure** that enabled CA to exist.
