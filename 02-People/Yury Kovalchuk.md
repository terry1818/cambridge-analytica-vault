## Summary

Yury Kovalchuk is a Russian billionaire who trained as a physicist at Leningrad State University and worked at the Ioffe Physico-Technical Institute before pivoting to business in the 1990s. He is the largest shareholder of [[Bank Rossiya]] — designated by the US Treasury in 2014 as the personal bank of senior Russian officials — and chair of the National Media Group, which controls or holds stakes in Russia's principal television channels and major newspapers. Kovalchuk is a founding member of the [[Ozero Cooperative]] and has been described in multiple Western intelligence and investigative sources as [[Vladimir Putin]]'s closest financial associate. Catherine Belton's *Putin's People* (2020) treats him as the financial architect of the Putin-circle asset network.

## Key Facts

- Born 1951 in Leningrad; PhD-level training in physics.
- Co-founded Ozero Cooperative in November 1996 alongside Putin and six others.
- Became Bank Rossiya's largest shareholder and board chairman in May 2004.
- US Treasury designated him on 20 March 2014 as part of Crimea-response sanctions, citing him as a "close advisor to President Putin" and "personal banker to senior officials."
- National Media Group (NMG), formally consolidated in 2008, assembles media assets contributed by Bank Rossiya and allied shareholders. NMG holds equity in Channel One Russia (25%), REN TV (100%), STS Media (a stake), and the newspaper *Izvestia*, as well as the video platform Rutube. This gives Kovalchuk de facto editorial influence over multiple mass-audience Russian media.
- Hosted the wedding of Putin's daughter Katerina Tikhonova to Nikolay Shamalov's son Kirill at his Igora ski resort in 2013 — a documented detail illustrating personal proximity to Putin.
- Panama Papers (2016): ICIJ investigation linked Bank Rossiya-adjacent offshore structures, nominally held by [[Sergei Roldugin]], to asset movements through Kovalchuk's network.

## Relationships

- **[[Vladimir Putin]]**: Ozero co-founder; described as Putin's banker and close personal friend.
- **[[Bank Rossiya]]**: Principal owner and chairman.
- **[[Ozero Cooperative]]**: Founding member; relationship predates Putin's presidency.

## Public Statements

Kovalchuk has given limited public statements. He has denied that his business success reflects political favoritism and characterized sanctions as politically motivated.
