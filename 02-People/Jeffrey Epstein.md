# Jeffrey Epstein

Born Brooklyn, NY, January 20, 1953. Died Metropolitan Correctional Center, Manhattan, August 10, 2019. Cause of death ruled suicide by hanging by NYC OCME; multiple disputed elements (see [[Epstein 2019 SDNY Case and Death]]).

Epstein's documented operation involved (a) procurement of underage girls primarily in Palm Beach, FL and New York City, (b) trafficking among his four primary properties, and (c) introduction of these victims to wealthy and politically connected men, with the operational characteristics of an intelligence-grade kompromat collection effort.
