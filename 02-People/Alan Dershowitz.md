# Alan Dershowitz

Born September 1, 1938, Brooklyn, NY. Constitutional and criminal-defense lawyer; Felix Frankfurter Professor of Law Emeritus at Harvard Law School. Member of [[Jeffrey Epstein]]'s legal defense team during the 2005-2008 Florida case that produced the [[Epstein 2005-2008 Florida Case|2007 Non-Prosecution Agreement]]. Named by [[Virginia Giuffre]] in sworn testimony as a participant in sexual abuse; he has consistently denied; defamation cross-suits between Dershowitz and Giuffre settled in November 2022 with both parties issuing statements acknowledging the possibility of mistaken identity.
