# Charles Kriel

## Summary

American journalist based in UK. **Specialist advisor to the UK House of Commons DCMS (Digital, Culture, Media and Sport) Select Committee** during its investigation into [[Cambridge Analytica]], Facebook, and Russian influence on the Brexit referendum. **Co-director with Katharina Gellein Viken of [[Source People You May Know 2020|People You May Know]]** (2020) documentary. Went undercover with his young family across America to gain access to the United in Purpose / Gloo / COFI infrastructure.
