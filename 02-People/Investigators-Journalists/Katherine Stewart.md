# Katherine Stewart

## Summary

Leading **investigative journalist on Christian nationalism**. Author of two foundational books on the movement: ***The Power Worshippers: Inside the Dangerous Rise of Religious Nationalism*** (2019) and ***Money, Lies, and God: Inside the Movement to Destroy American Democracy*** (2025). Contributor to *The Guardian*, *The New Republic*, *NYT* op-ed. **Featured prominently in [[Source Bad Faith Documentary 2024|Bad Faith]]**. Her years of reporting on **[[Council for National Policy]]**, the **[[The Heritage Foundation]]**, the **[[New Apostolic Reformation]]**, and the broader Christian-right network make her **the most consequential single contemporary journalist on the theocratic wing**.

## Why she matters

Stewart's work is **the journalism counterpart to [[Anne Nelson]]'s academic / book-length investigation** of [[Council for National Policy|CNP]]. Where Nelson published one major book and continues research, Stewart has produced ongoing investigative reporting plus two books spanning the 2019-2025 period that bracket the Trump-era theocratic-wing surge.
