# Samuel L. Perry

## Summary

Sociologist at the **University of Oklahoma**. Co-author with Andrew L. Whitehead of **[[Source Taking America Back for God|*Taking America Back for God: Christian Nationalism in the United States*]]** (Oxford University Press, 2020) - the **leading quantitative academic study of Christian nationalism**'s prevalence, beliefs, and political impact. Featured in **[[Source Bad Faith Documentary 2024|Bad Faith]]**. Subsequent books include *The Flag and the Cross* (2022) and ongoing peer-reviewed research.

## Why he matters

Perry provides **the empirical academic ground** for vault claims about Christian nationalism. Where [[Katherine Stewart]] and [[Anne Nelson]] do journalism, and theological critics provide ideological critique, **Perry's survey data establishes Christian nationalism as a measurable political-cultural phenomenon with documented effects**. His work is widely cited across the vault entities.

## Network position

His work is the principal academic-tier source the vault uses for empirically grounded analysis of Christian Nationalism as a politically distinct identity within the broader US religious landscape.
