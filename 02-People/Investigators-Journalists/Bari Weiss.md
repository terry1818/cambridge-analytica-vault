# Bari Weiss

## Summary

American journalist; **founder of The Free Press** (2021) -- a subscription-based newsletter / media organization that has become one of the most prominent **center-right contrarian** journalism outlets. Former opinion editor at The New York Times; resigned in 2020 with a public letter alleging hostile work environment for centrist/conservative views. Previously at The Wall Street Journal. One of the **selected journalists who published [[Twitter Files Release 2022-2023]] threads** for Elon Musk in 2022-2023.

## The Free Press

- Founded 2021 as Common Sense newsletter on Substack
- Renamed The Free Press 2023
- **Subscription-based media** model (Substack origins, now independent)
- Reportedly 500K+ paid subscribers
- Stable of contributors including center-right voices and disaffected centrists
- 2024: Bari Weiss married Nellie Bowles (also Free Press contributor)

## Twitter Files role

December 2022 - 2023: published multiple Twitter Files threads on:
- Twitter's content moderation around COVID
- Shadow-banning policies
- Government communications with Twitter
- Selective content suppression

The Twitter Files position aligned Weiss with [[Elon Musk]]'s anti-content-moderation framing while maintaining nominal independence.

## Center-right positioning

Weiss occupies a **distinct ideological niche**:
- Critical of progressive cultural politics
- Pro-Israel hawkish
- Critical of campus / corporate DEI
- Heterodox on COVID policy
- **Not** fully aligned with MAGA / Trump movement
- **Not** part of Tech Right but adjacent
