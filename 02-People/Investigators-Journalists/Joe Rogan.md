# Joe Rogan

## Summary

American podcaster, comedian, and UFC commentator. **The Joe Rogan Experience** (founded 2009) is widely considered the **most popular podcast in the world**, with episodes regularly reaching 10-20+ million listeners. **Endorsed Trump in October 2024** following a 3-hour interview with Trump, in what is widely credited as a significant boost to Trump's young-male voter coalition in the final weeks of the 2024 campaign.

## Political evolution

- Long-time politically heterodox (libertarian-leaning)
- 2016-2020: critical of both parties; supported Bernie Sanders 2020 primary
- 2020-2024: shifted rightward, especially over COVID lockdowns and content moderation
- 2024-10-25: 3-hour Trump interview on JRE
- 2024-11-04: formal Trump endorsement on JRE day before election
- Endorsement and platform amplification widely cited as helping Trump's young-male turnout

## Tech Right alignment

Rogan is **culturally aligned** with Tech Right without being formally part of the network:

- Long-time [[Elon Musk]] friendship and frequent JRE guest spot for Musk
- Frequent interviews with Tech Right figures
- 2024: amplified pro-Trump messaging in final weeks
- Critical of COVID lockdowns, content moderation, AI safety (broadly aligned with Tech Right positions)

## Why he matters for this vault

Rogan is **arguably the single most culturally consequential media figure** in the post-CA era. His audience is:
- Largely young-male
- Heterogeneous politically until recently
- **Highly influential in 2024 election turnout**

The Rogan endorsement of Trump (October 2024) is widely credited as one of the **decisive late-campaign factors** in Trump's 2024 win -- illustrating that the right-wing media ecosystem now extends beyond formal Republican infrastructure into culturally-dominant podcasting.
