# Paul-Olivier Dehaye

## Summary

Swiss-based mathematician (born Belgium). **Identified Cambridge Analytica's violations of EU data-protection law well before the Brexit referendum and 2016 US elections.** Founder of **PersonalData.io**, a nonprofit promoting digital rights and individually-actionable data protection. Recently founded **HestiaLabs**. **Convinced [[David Carroll]] to submit a Data Subject Access Request** to CA in January 2017, beginning the legal sequence that led to the UK High Court lawsuit. Featured in **[[Source The Great Hack Netflix 2019|The Great Hack]]** as the whistleblower exposing CA's internal workings.
