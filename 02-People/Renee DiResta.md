# RenÃ©e DiResta

US researcher of online information operations. Former research manager at the [[Stanford Internet Observatory]] (2019-2024). Author of "Invisible Rulers: The People Who Turn Lies into Reality" (2024).

## Research focus

- Anti-vaccine information networks (pre-dated COVID and informed broader public response)
- 2016 [[Russian interference 2016]] / [[Internet Research Agency]] case study (Senate Intelligence Committee report)
- 2020 election integrity tracking via the [[Election Integrity Partnership]]
- Anti-disinformation infrastructure

## Bespoke realities concept

DiResta coined the term [[Bespoke realities]] to describe the contemporary information environment in which individuals can construct and inhabit highly customized informational worlds that diverge from any shared baseline of facts.

## Significance for vault

One of the most-cited contemporary researchers on the operational mechanics of US-domestic and foreign information operations. Methodological backbone of the [[Information warfare]] / [[Disinformation]] cluster.

Primary target of the Jim Jordan / Stephen Miller-led "Twitter Files" campaign aimed at delegitimizing disinformation research; forced out of SIO in 2024.
