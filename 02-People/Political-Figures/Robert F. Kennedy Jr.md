# Robert F. Kennedy Jr.

## Summary

**Secretary of Health and Human Services in Trump 2.0**, confirmed **February 13, 2025**. Founder of **[[Children's Health Defense]]** (2007 as World Mercury Project; renamed CHD 2018). **2024 independent presidential candidate** (Aug 2023 announced; ran as independent after leaving Democratic primary; **endorsed Trump August 23, 2024** in exchange for HHS role). Nephew of John F. Kennedy and Robert F. Kennedy. **Environmental attorney** prior to anti-vaccine pivot ~2005.

**Architect of the [[Make America Healthy Again movement|MAHA movement]]** - now substantially integrated into Trump 2.0 health policy. **The single most operationally consequential anti-vaccine figure in US history** by virtue of HHS Secretary role.

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1954-01-17
- **Education:** Harvard (BA), University of Virginia (JD), Pace Law (LLM)
- **Family:** Son of RFK; nephew of JFK
- **Prior career:** Environmental attorney (Riverkeeper, Waterkeeper Alliance)
- **Current:** HHS Secretary

## Career arc

- **1980s-2005:** Environmental attorney (Hudson Riverkeeper, Waterkeeper Alliance, NRDC)
- **2005:** Published anti-vaccine *Rolling Stone* / *Salon* article "Deadly Immunity" (later retracted by Salon)
- **2007:** Founded World Mercury Project (anti-vaccine)
- **2018:** Renamed Children's Health Defense
- **2020-2023:** COVID-19 vaccine criticism scaled CHD substantially
- **2023-04:** Announced 2024 Democratic primary challenge
- **2023-10:** Dropped Democratic primary; ran as independent
- **2024-08-23:** Suspended campaign; **endorsed Trump** in exchange for HHS role
- **2025-02-13:** Confirmed as HHS Secretary
- **2025-2026:** Operational MAHA implementation
