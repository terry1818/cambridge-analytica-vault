# Boris Epshteyn

Russian-American attorney and political strategist. Trump 2016 campaign senior adviser; Trump White House special assistant briefly 2017. Returned to Trump orbit 2020 as strategist for the post-November election challenge. **Reported coordinator of the "alternate elector" scheme implementation across multiple states** alongside [[Kenneth Chesebro]]. Continued as Trump 2024 campaign and Trump 2.0 transition adviser. Identified by reporting (consistent with the indictment description) as Unindicted Co-Conspirator 6 in [[US v Trump DC Federal Election Interference Case]]. **Indicted in Arizona** April 2024 for the Arizona false-elector scheme.

## Documented post-2020 conduct

- Coordinated operational logistics for the December 14, 2020 false-elector meetings in seven states
- Worked closely with [[Kenneth Chesebro]] on memo distribution
- Communicated with state Republican officials regarding the scheme
- Continued as Trump strategist through 2024 and into 2025 transition

## Indictments

### Arizona indictment (April 24, 2024)
Maricopa County, Arizona grand jury indicted Epshteyn and 17 others (including [[Rudy Giuliani]], [[John Eastman]], [[Jenna Ellis]], [[Mark Meadows]], [[Kenneth Chesebro]], and the seven Arizona false electors) on:
- Conspiracy
- Fraudulent schemes
- Forgery

Status: active.

### DC federal case
- Identified as UCC6 in Smith's DC indictment based on factual description; not personally indicted federally

### Reported Trump transition role
Despite the indictment, reported to have continued as Trump's principal vetting adviser for personnel during the November 2024 - January 2025 transition.
