# Eric Trump

Born January 6, 1984, New York, NY. Second son of [[Donald Trump]] and Ivana Trump. Executive Vice President of the Trump Organization with operational responsibility for development. **Co-defendant in [[NY AG v Trump Organization Civil Fraud 2024]]**, found civilly liable February 2024 and ordered to pay $4,013,024.

## Civil liability findings (Tier 1)

### NY AG civil fraud trial (February 2024)
- Found civilly liable on multiple causes of action
- Ordered to pay $4,013,024 in disgorgement
- Barred from serving as officer or director of any NY corporation for 2 years (stayed pending appeal)
- Testified at trial November 2023
- Notable testimony: invoked Fifth Amendment 500+ times in 2020 NY AG deposition; testified more openly at trial

## Trump Foundation case (2019)

As Trump Foundation director, required to undergo mandatory training on fiduciary duties of charity directors per [[Trump Foundation NY AG Settlement 2019]].

## Trump Organization role

- Trump Organization Executive Vice President for Development and Acquisitions
- Operational responsibility for Trump golf clubs and resorts
- Co-trustee with [[Donald Trump Jr]] of the trust holding Trump assets while Trump was President 2017-2021
