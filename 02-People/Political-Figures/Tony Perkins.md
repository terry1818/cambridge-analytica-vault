# Tony Perkins

President of the [[Family Research Council]] (2003-present). Former Louisiana state legislator. One of the most prominent public faces of the [[Religious right]].

## FRC leadership

- Took over from Gary Bauer 2003
- FRC designated a hate group by Southern Poverty Law Center 2010 for sustained LGBTQ-targeted misinformation
- FRC has remained one of the most influential evangelical organizations in DC throughout the period

## Trump-era role

- One of the senior evangelical advisers cultivated by the 2016 Trump campaign
- Member of Trump's evangelical advisory board both terms
- Defended Trump through Access Hollywood, Stormy Daniels, etc. on theological grounds
- Senior member of the [[Council for National Policy]]

## Significance

Perkins is the institutional bridge between traditional evangelical organizing, the CNP-coordinated movement-elite layer, and the contemporary Trump-coalition Religious right.
