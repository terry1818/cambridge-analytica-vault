# Howard Lutnick

## Summary

American financial services executive; **Trump 2.0 Commerce Secretary** (confirmed February 2025). CEO of **Cantor Fitzgerald** since 1996 (took over after the firm lost two-thirds of its NYC employees in the September 11, 2001 attacks). Major Trump 2024 campaign donor and **co-chair of the Trump 2024 transition team** alongside Linda McMahon. Has stepped back from but not fully divested Cantor Fitzgerald operational role.

## Commerce Secretary role

- Confirmed February 18, 2025
- Oversees Commerce Department including:
  - Trade policy implementation (tariffs)
  - Census Bureau
  - NTIA (telecommunications)
  - NIST (standards including AI standards)
  - **TikTok divestment oversight**
- Active in [[Stargate Project]] coordination (Commerce Department involvement)

## Tech Right adjacency
