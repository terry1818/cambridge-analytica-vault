# Marc Andreessen

Co-founder Netscape (1994). Co-founder Andreessen Horowitz venture capital firm (a16z, 2009). Co-led the public realignment of a portion of Silicon Valley toward Trump 2024.

## Political shift (Tier 1)

- 2020 election: a16z partners donated mostly to Democratic candidates
- 2024 election: Andreessen personally endorsed Trump publicly July 2024
- a16z became one of the largest tech political donor cohorts for the cycle

## "Techno-Optimist Manifesto" (October 2023)

- Andreessen-published essay framing US progress as historically tied to technological acceleration
- Explicitly named "enemies" including "trust and safety," "tech ethics," "risk management," and ESG
- The manifesto functions as an ideological banner for the [[Tech right]] realignment

## Second Trump term

- a16z partner Sriram Krishnan: AI policy advisor in the White House
- Andreessen himself a frequent advisor on tech policy without a formal role
- Significant a16z portfolio company exposure to federal contracting changes ([[Palantir]] is not a16z but adjacent)
