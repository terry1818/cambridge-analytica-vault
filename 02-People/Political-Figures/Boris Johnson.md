# Boris Johnson

## Summary

British politician; former Mayor of London (2008-2016); **Foreign Secretary** (July 2016 - July 2018); **Prime Minister** (July 2019 - September 2022). Prominent figurehead of the 2016 [[Vote Leave]] Brexit campaign.

## CA-relevance

Per [[Source Philp 2018-03-22 Times FCO sought advice]] and [[Source Nelson 2018-03-21 Spectator Johnson Nix meeting]]:

- **In 2016, while Foreign Secretary, Johnson met with [[Alexander Nix]] of [[Cambridge Analytica]]**
- The Foreign Office sought advice from CA during Johnsons tenure
- The meeting was not initially disclosed
- Came to light during the 2018 scandal

This is one of the most direct documented intersections between CA leadership and senior UK government officials. Although the substantive content of FCO-CA exchanges has never been fully disclosed publicly, the meeting establishes that CAs reach extended to UK ministerial level.
