# Lance Wallnau

## Summary

Current **dominant NAR political operator**. **Popularized the [[Seven Mountains Mandate]]** through his 2013 book *Invasion of Babylon* (co-authored with Bill Johnson). **Widely credited as the first apostle to "prophesy" Trump's 2016 presidency** (in 2015), framing Trump as a modern "Cyrus" - a non-believer divinely chosen to fulfill God's purposes. **Ran the Courage Tour 2024**, taking the NAR political message through **19 bellwether counties in 9 swing states** during the 2024 presidential campaign. Continues active operations into Trump 2.0 era as a primary NAR-political voice.

- **Born:** 1955

- **2015 Trump prophecy:** Framed Trump as Cyrus-figure; quickly became standard NAR view

- **Seven Mountains operationalization:** Speaks at conferences, trains apostles, networks coordination
- **Courage Tour 2024:** 19 counties, 9 swing states; explicit political-mobilization event under religious framing
- **Truth & Liberty podcast / show:** ongoing political-spiritual commentary

## Why he matters

Wallnau is **the single most operationally consequential NAR figure** for current US politics:

- His theological framing (Trump-as-Cyrus) became the dominant NAR view, justifying evangelical Trump support
- His Seven Mountains popularization gave the movement its current operational vocabulary
- His Courage Tour was the most coordinated NAR political-mobilization tour in US electoral history
- His continued post-2024 operations indicate the infrastructure he built is durable

When 60+ Charismatic figures signed the NAR and Christian Nationalism Statement (October 2022) denouncing Christian nationalism, **Wallnau publicly refused** - making him one of the explicit Christian-nationalist NAR figures rather than the moderating-wing.
