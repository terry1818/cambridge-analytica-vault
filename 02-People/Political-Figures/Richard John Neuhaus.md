# Richard John Neuhaus

US Catholic priest (converted from Lutheran), theologian, and political organizer. Founded First Things magazine 1990. Architect of the conservative Catholic-Evangelical alliance. Died 2009.

## Key works

- "The Naked Public Square" (1984) - foundational text arguing religion has been wrongly excluded from public political discourse
- "The Catholic Moment" (1987)
- Co-author of "Evangelicals and Catholics Together" statements (1994, 1997, 2002) with [[Charles Colson]]

## Significance

Neuhaus is the intellectual architect of the modern alliance between traditionalist Catholics and evangelical Protestants that became the religious base of the contemporary [[Religious right]]. The vault treats this alliance as a foundational structural fact of the post-1980 conservative coalition.

## Influence chain

- Neuhaus to [[George Weigel]] (his successor as the public Catholic voice)
- Neuhaus to [[Robert P George]] (Princeton, founder of Witherspoon Institute)
- George to [[Patrick Deneen]] (post-liberal Catholic thread)
- George to [[Adrian Vermeule]] (common good constitutionalism)

This is the intellectual lineage that produced the contemporary [[Christian Nationalism]] / post-liberal current.
