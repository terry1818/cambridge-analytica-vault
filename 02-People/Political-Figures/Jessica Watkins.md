# Jessica Watkins

Ohio [[Oath Keepers]] member, leader of the Ohio State Regular Militia (a smaller affiliated group). US Army veteran (Afghanistan). Transgender woman.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- November 29, 2022: Found NOT GUILTY of seditious conspiracy (jury acquitted)
- Found GUILTY of conspiracy to obstruct an official proceeding, obstruction of an official proceeding, civil disorder
- May 26, 2023: Sentenced to 8.5 years
- Trial evidence: Watkins entered the Capitol with the "Stack 1" formation

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Released
