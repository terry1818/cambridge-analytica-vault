# Marco Rubio

## Summary

Florida Republican Senator (2011-2025) + **US Secretary of State** in **Trump 2.0** (sworn in January 2025; first Latino person to hold the office). **CNP member**. Cuban-American; born in Miami 1971. **Presidential candidate 2016** (lost primary to Trump after extended feud). **Confirmed unanimously** in 2025 (rare for Trump 2.0 cabinet picks).

**As Secretary of State** has executed Trump 2.0 foreign-policy realignment including: USAID dismantling, Latin America focus, Cuba/Venezuela hard line, Israel/Saudi alignment, Russia/Ukraine reorientation. **Substantially more hawkish on China than typical Trump 2.0 figures.**
