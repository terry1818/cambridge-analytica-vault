# Curtis Yarvin

## Summary

American software engineer + political theorist. Wrote the blog ***Unqualified Reservations*** 2007-2014 under the pen name **Mencius Moldbug**, founding the **[[Dark Enlightenment]] / Neoreactionary (NRx)** movement. Now writes ***Gray Mirror*** newsletter (2020+). Founded **Tlon** (Urbit's parent company), in which **[[Peter Thiel]] invested**. **Direct intellectual influence on Peter Thiel and JD Vance** - Vance cited Yarvin's RAGE doctrine by name in his 2022 Senate campaign.

**The single most consequential political-philosophical influence on the tech-right wing of the contemporary American conservative coalition.** Schedule F, DOGE, the Trump 2.0 attack on universities/media/civil-service all map onto Yarvin's framework.

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1973
- **Background:** Software engineer; Brown undergrad; UC Berkeley grad school (didn't finish)
- **Pen name:** Mencius Moldbug (2007-2014); now writes under own name
- **Companies:** Urbit (founded; sold), Tlon (founded; Thiel-invested)
- **Current:** Gray Mirror newsletter, podcast appearances

## Key concepts (see [[Dark Enlightenment]] for full)

- **The Cathedral** - unified institutional consensus across universities, media, foundations, civil service, NGOs
- **RAGE** - Retire All Government Employees (mass federal-civil-service firing)
- **Accountable Monarchy** / CEO-of-America - corporate governance for the state
- **Patchwork** - thousands of competing micro-jurisdictions post-democracy
- **Sovereignty as property** - political authority should be a property right

Per historian Joshua Tait: "Moldbug's relationship with the investor-entrepreneur Thiel is his most important connection." Thiel invested in Yarvin's Tlon.
