# Pat Robertson

## Summary

Televangelist, broadcasting executive, and politician. Founded the **Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN)** in 1960 (still a major Christian-right media operation). **Ran for 1988 GOP presidential nomination** and lost. Founded **[[Christian Coalition]] in 1989** with [[Ralph Reed]] as executive director. Founded **Regent University** in 1977. Long-time **[[Council for National Policy]] member**. Died June 2023.

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1930-03-22
- **Died:** 2023-06-08
- **Founded:** CBN (1960), Christian Coalition (1989), Regent University (1977)
- **Notable for:** Building Christian-right media empire + grassroots political organization

## Why he matters

- CBN became a major media engine for the Christian right
- Christian Coalition became the dominant evangelical political organization after Moral Majority
- His **1988 presidential run** trained a generation of Christian-right political operatives
- His **"700 Club" television show** shaped evangelical political opinion for decades
