# Julian Assange

## Summary

Australian editor, publisher, and activist. **Founder of WikiLeaks** in 2006. Central figure in the 2016 US election cycle for WikiLeaks' publication of:

- **DNC emails** released July 22, 2016 (3 days before Democratic National Convention)
- **John Podesta emails** released October 7, 2016 (less than an hour after the Access Hollywood "grab them by the" tape became public)

Both releases sourced from Russian GRU hacking operations per [[Source Mueller Report 2019]] and [[Source Senate Intel Vol 5 2020]].

## CA-relevance

Per multiple journalistic accounts:

- **[[Alexander Nix]] approached Assange** in 2016 requesting WikiLeaks' help in finding [[Hillary Clinton]]s 33,000 deleted personal emails
- **Assange tweeted October 25, 2017** confirming he had been approached by CA and **rejected the proposal**
- The Nix-Assange overture is one of the most direct documented contacts between the Cambridge Analytica leadership and a major 2016 election information actor

## Asylum and extradition saga

- **2010-2012:** initial Swedish allegations and UK extradition proceedings
- **2012-2019:** granted asylum in Ecuadorian Embassy London (7 years)
- **2019-04:** Ecuador revoked asylum; UK police arrested at Embassy
- **2019-2024:** held at HM Prison Belmarsh; US extradition proceedings ongoing
- **2024-06:** plea agreement with US DOJ; pleaded guilty to single conspiracy charge; released and returned to Australia
