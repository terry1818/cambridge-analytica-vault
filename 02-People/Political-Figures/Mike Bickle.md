# Mike Bickle

## Summary

American pastor; **founder of [[IHOPKC]]** (International House of Prayer Kansas City, 1999). One of the most consequential **New Apostolic Reformation** institutional figures of the 1990s-2010s. Built IHOPKC into the largest NAR-adjacent training center in the world. **October 2023:** "Advocate Group" of former associates publicly accused him of decades of sexual misconduct including allegations involving minors at the start of his ministry (1980s). Multiple women came forward. **Independent investigation (April 2024) substantiated allegations.** Bickle separated from IHOPKC November 2023 + from all ministry roles.
