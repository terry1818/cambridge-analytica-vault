# Mike Huckabee

Mike Huckabee (born 1955) is the former Governor of Arkansas (1996-2007), former Republican presidential candidate (2008 and 2016 cycles), longtime Fox News personality, and Trump 2.0 United States Ambassador to Israel (sworn in March 2025).

## Christian-Zionist positioning

Huckabee's Christian-Zionist positioning is substantially more aggressive than typical Republican-political Israel-positioning. His public commentary and policy positioning have included:

- Substantial advocacy for full Israeli sovereignty over the West Bank ("there are no settlements; only Israeli neighbourhoods in Judea and Samaria")
- Theological framing of US Israel-policy as religious obligation
- Substantial alignment with religious-Zionist Israeli political leadership including Bezalel Smotrich and Itamar Ben-Gvir
- Substantial CUFI participation and adjacent Christian-Zionist organisational engagement

His theological positioning combines Southern Baptist Convention-aligned evangelical Protestantism with dispensationalist eschatology and substantial Christian-Zionist substrate.

## Trump 2.0 Ambassador to Israel

Huckabee's appointment as Ambassador to Israel in the Trump 2.0 administration was operationally significant as the most explicitly Christian-Zionist ambassador appointment in US-Israel diplomatic history. The appointment reflected the Trump 2.0 administration's substantial alignment with religious-right-anchored Israel positioning.

The Ambassador role has produced operational consequences:

- Substantial Trump 2.0 administration alignment with Netanyahu government on policy questions
- Reduced US diplomatic distance from Israeli settler-movement positioning
- The broader operational pattern of Trump 2.0 administration treating Israel-policy questions as religious-right priority issues rather than as foreign-policy-establishment questions
