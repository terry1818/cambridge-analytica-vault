# Mike Johnson

## Summary

**Speaker of the United States House of Representatives** since **October 25, 2023** (after Kevin McCarthy ouster). Republican from Louisiana's 4th congressional district since 2017. **Former [[Alliance Defending Freedom]] litigator** (2002-2010) - direct prior career in Christian-right legal infrastructure. **Southern Baptist; explicit Christian-nationalist-aligned positioning** (though doesn't formally use "Christian nationalist" label). Coordinated **Trump 2.0 legislative agenda** including budget reconciliation + GENIUS Act + multiple culture-war legislative priorities.

**Key vault significance:** Mike Johnson is the **single highest-ranking Christian-nationalist-aligned elected official in the contemporary US government** after JD Vance (VP). His ADF background + Southern Baptist Convention alignment + Project 2025 sympathies + Speaker authority over House floor schedule make him a structurally consequential coalition operator.
