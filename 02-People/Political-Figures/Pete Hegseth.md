# Pete Hegseth

US Secretary of Defense (2025-). Former Fox News weekend host. Army National Guard veteran (Iraq, Afghanistan, Guantanamo).

## Pre-administration background

- Princeton (BA), Harvard Kennedy School (MA)
- Founded Concerned Veterans for America (Koch-affiliated) 2011
- Fox News contributor from 2014; weekend host of "Fox and Friends Weekend" from 2017
- Prominent advocate of pardons for service members charged with war crimes during Trump's first term

## SecDef nomination and confirmation

- Nominated November 12, 2024
- January 24, 2025: Confirmed by 51-50 Senate vote (Vance breaking tie)
- Allegations during confirmation: sexual assault settlement (2017, denied wrongdoing), excessive drinking, mismanagement at veterans nonprofits
- Three Republican senators voted no: Collins, McConnell, Murkowski
