# Rodrigo Duterte

## Summary

President of the Philippines (2016-2022). Prior to presidency: long-serving Mayor of Davao City (1988-1998, 2001-2010, 2013-2016) where he established his "iron-fist" anti-crime reputation that became his presidential campaign brand.
