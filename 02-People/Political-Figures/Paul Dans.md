# Paul Dans

Director of [[Project 2025]] (2022-July 2024). Former Chief of Staff at the US Office of Personnel Management under Trump (2020-2021).

## Trump 1st term

- OPM chief of staff during the period when [[Schedule F]] was first promulgated by executive order (October 21, 2020)
- The Schedule F EO would have reclassified approximately 50,000 career federal employees as political appointees; Biden rescinded it January 22, 2021 before significant implementation

## Project 2025

- Hired by Heritage President [[Kevin Roberts]] to lead the Project 2025 transition project
- Built the personnel database structure
- Authored or contributed to the OPM and federal workforce chapters of Mandate for Leadership

## July 2024 departure

- Stepped down from Project 2025 the same week the Trump campaign began publicly distancing itself from the project
- Heritage framed this as a routine handoff; the timing strongly suggested coordination with the Trump campaign

## Second-term role

- Has not (as of late May 2026) taken a formally announced senior administration role
- Reports indicate he has been involved in personnel decisions in an informal capacity
