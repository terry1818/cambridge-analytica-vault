# Michael Cohen

Born August 25, 1966, Lawrence, NY. Personal attorney and "fixer" to [[Donald Trump]] from approximately 2006 through May 2018. Self-described "Trump's pit bull" â€” handled personal legal matters, business disputes, and image-management interventions including the 2016 hush-money payments to [[Stormy Daniels]] and Karen McDougal. **Pled guilty August 21, 2018** in SDNY to eight felony counts including campaign finance violations (the Daniels payment), tax evasion, and false statements to a financial institution. **Pled guilty separately November 29, 2018** to false statements to Congress about the Trump Tower Moscow project (Mueller case). Served three years federal prison (released early to home confinement May 2020 due to COVID-19). Subsequently a key prosecution witness in [[People v Trump NY Hush Money 2024]] and in [[NY AG v Trump Organization Civil Fraud 2024]].

## SDNY plea (August 21, 2018)

Pled guilty to eight felony counts:
- 5 counts tax evasion (2012-2016)
- 1 count making false statements to a financial institution
- 1 count causing an unlawful corporate contribution (Karen McDougal / AMI scheme)
- 1 count making an excessive campaign contribution (Stormy Daniels payment)

In open court Cohen stated that the campaign finance violations were committed "in coordination with, and at the direction of, a candidate for federal office" â€” referring to Trump.

Sentence: 3 years federal prison + $50K fine + restitution + supervised release.

## Mueller plea (November 29, 2018)

Pled guilty to one count of making false statements to Congress (18 USC Â§ 1001) regarding the **Trump Tower Moscow project** â€” a 2015-2016 effort to develop a Trump-branded tower in Moscow that continued into the 2016 campaign. Cohen had testified to Congress in 2017 that the project ended January 2016; in fact it continued into June 2016 with Cohen and Trump remaining personally informed.

Sentence: 2 months federal prison concurrent with SDNY sentence.

## Subsequent congressional testimony (February 2019)

Cohen testified publicly to House Oversight Committee February 27, 2019, providing:
- Sworn account of Trump's role in the hush money scheme
- Sworn account of Trump's knowledge of Roger Stone's WikiLeaks coordination
- Sworn account of Trump Organization financial practices
- Documentary evidence including a personal check from Trump for Cohen's reimbursement

## Key witness in subsequent trials

### People v Trump 2024
Cohen was the prosecution's key fact witness establishing Trump's personal direction of the falsified records. Testified across six days April-May 2024 including extensive cross-examination. His credibility was a central trial issue; his testimony was corroborated by documentary evidence and other witnesses.

### NY AG v Trump Organization 2024
Cohen testified at Engoron's civil fraud trial about Trump Organization financial statement preparation practices.

## Imprisonment

- May 2019: surrendered to federal custody at FCI Otisville (NY)
- May 2020: released to home confinement under COVID-19 BOP policy
- July 2020: briefly returned to custody after dispute over book-writing terms of home confinement; federal judge ordered release as retaliation
- November 22, 2021: completed sentence

## Mueller Report obstruction episodes involving Cohen

Cohen's conduct figured in **Episode 9** of the [[Deep Dive - Mueller Report 10 Obstruction Episodes]]: Trump's escalating public attacks on Cohen after his cooperation, including statements characterizing Cohen as a "Rat," and the joint defense agreement / coordination of Cohen's earlier false congressional testimony.
