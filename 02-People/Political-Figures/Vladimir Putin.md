# Vladimir Putin

Born October 7, 1952, Leningrad (now St. Petersburg). KGB officer 1975-1991 stationed in Dresden, East Germany. Director of the FSB (KGB successor agency) 1998-1999. **President of Russia** May 7, 2000 - May 7, 2008, and **May 7, 2012 - present**. Prime Minister 2008-2012 (Medvedev served as President during this period). The longest-serving Russian/Soviet leader since Stalin.

## Role in 2016 US election interference (Tier 1)

The Mueller Report ([[Source: Mueller Report 2019]]), the Senate Intelligence Committee bipartisan reports, and the Intelligence Community Assessment (ICA, January 2017) collectively established that Putin personally ordered the Russian active measures campaign targeting the 2016 US election. Per the ICA:

> "Putin and the Russian Government developed a clear preference for President-elect Trump."

> "We assess Putin, the Russian Government, and an array of state and quasi-state actors influenced the 2016 US presidential election."

## Documented operational role

- Approved [[Internet Research Agency (IRA)]] social media campaign
- Approved GRU hacking and dumping operation (DNC, Podesta, DCCC)
- Directed broader active-measures operations
- Met with [[Donald Trump]] multiple times during Trump's first term

## Other US-relevant operations

- Steele Dossier subject (allegations about Trump-Russia compromat; tier-2/3 contested)
- Various other Russian intelligence operations
- Wagner Group operations
- 2022 invasion of Ukraine; ICC arrest warrant (March 17, 2023)

## ICC arrest warrant

March 17, 2023: International Criminal Court issued arrest warrant for Putin for war crimes related to forced deportation of Ukrainian children. Russia is not an ICC member; warrant has not been enforced.
