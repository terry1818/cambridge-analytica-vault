# Joe Lonsdale

Co-founder [[Palantir]] (2003) with [[Peter Thiel]], [[Alex Karp]], and Stephen Cohen. Founder of 8VC venture capital firm. Founder of University of Austin (UATX).

## Tech right activity

- 8VC heavily invested in defense-tech (Anduril, Saronic, Vannevar Labs)
- Vocal political voice during 2024 cycle endorsing Trump
- Hosted Trump fundraisers in Texas
- Founded American Optimist podcast as a parallel platform to All-In

## University of Austin

- Founded 2021 explicitly as a response to perceived ideological capture of US higher education
- Trustees and advisors include figures from the broader Tech right and conservative intellectual world
- Began offering undergraduate degrees fall 2024

## Relationship to Palantir

- Sued Palantir in 2014 alleging fraud (related to his departure); case settled 2016
- Lonsdale remains publicly aligned with the Palantir / Thiel orbit despite the dispute
