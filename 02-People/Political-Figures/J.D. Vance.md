# J.D. Vance

US Senator from Ohio (2023-2025). Vice President of the United States (2025-). Originally "Hillbilly Elegy" (2016) memoirist.

## Political arc

- 2016: Wrote "Hillbilly Elegy"; was an open critic of Trump (called him "America's Hitler" in private texts to a friend that were later published)
- 2018-2021: Worked for [[Peter Thiel]]'s Mithril Capital
- 2022: Won Ohio Senate primary with  from Thiel; reversed his prior Trump position
- January 2023: Sworn in as Senator
- July 2024: Selected as Trump's running mate
- January 20, 2025: Sworn in as VP

## Tech right connections

- Thiel's most-funded political project
- Public admirer of [[Curtis Yarvin]] ("Mencius Moldbug")
- Cited by Yarvin in 2025 New York Times Magazine interview as receptive to neoreactionary framing
- Co-host adjacent to the All-In / Tech right podcast network

## Christian nationalism orbit

- Converted to Catholicism 2019 (traditionalist orientation)
- Has cited [[Patrick Deneen]] and other post-liberal Catholic theorists publicly
- Bridges the Tech right and the Religious right within the second-term coalition
