# Greg Jacob

Counsel to Vice President [[Mike Pence]] (2019-2021). Lead witness in [[January 6 Select Committee]] hearings on the Eastman pressure campaign.

## Role around January 6

- Primary legal brain pushing back on [[John Eastman]]'s theory that Pence could reject electors
- Wrote multiple memos to Pence in late December 2020 / early January 2021 concluding Pence had no such authority
- Was in the underground loading dock with Pence and family as rioters chanted "Hang Mike Pence" on January 6

## Sworn testimony (Tier 1)

J6 Committee Hearing 3, June 16, 2022:

- Jacob said he asked Eastman directly on January 6 morning whether his theory would survive 9-0 at the Supreme Court. Eastman answered "no" and conceded the theory had no legal support
- Jacob's email to Eastman during the riot: "thanks to your bullsh*t, we are now under siege"
- Eastman's reply that same evening still requested Pence delay certification - Jacob testified this showed Eastman placed the political objective above the legal advice

## Significance

Jacob's testimony was the cornerstone of the J6 Committee's case that the Eastman scheme was knowingly illegal - the lawyer pushing it had admitted to its lead opponent that it would lose 9-0 the day it was attempted.
