# Keith Rabois

US venture capitalist. Member of the [[PayPal Mafia]]. Partner at Khosla Ventures, then Founders Fund (Thiel), most recently founded Khosla Ventures partner Joshua Buckley's Conviction venture firm cohort.

## Background

- Stanford Law graduate
- Early executive at PayPal, LinkedIn, Slide, Square
- Vocal opponent of San Francisco progressive politics 2018-2024

## Political activity

- Long-time Republican donor with libertarian framing
- Co-founded Opening Day Partners with Sacks - a vehicle for Tech right political coordination
- Endorsed Trump publicly 2024
- Donated to Trump campaign and aligned PACs

## Significance

Rabois represents the longer-arc continuity case for the Tech right - he did not "convert" in 2020-2024 because he had been consistently Republican. The visible shift is that other figures from the same PayPal cohort (Sacks, Andreessen) joined him publicly during the 2024 cycle.
