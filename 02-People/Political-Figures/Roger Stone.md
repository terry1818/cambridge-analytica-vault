# Roger Stone

## Summary

American political operative; longtime Trump confidant. Acted as **back-channel between Trump campaign and WikiLeaks** in 2016 (via Randy Credico). Convicted in November 2019 on 7 counts including lying to Congress, witness tampering, obstructing official proceeding. Sentenced to 40 months. **Sentence commuted by Trump July 2020; full pardon December 2020.** Continues to operate in right-wing media post-pardon.
