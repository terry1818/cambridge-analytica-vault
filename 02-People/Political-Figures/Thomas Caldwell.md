# Thomas Caldwell

Virginia [[Oath Keepers]]-affiliated Navy veteran.

## Verdict (Tier 1)

- November 29, 2022: Found NOT GUILTY of seditious conspiracy
- GUILTY of obstruction of an official proceeding, tampering with evidence
- May 26, 2023: Sentenced to time served (he had cooperated more substantially and his Capitol breach was not established)
- Notable as the highest-profile J6 defendant to be acquitted on the seditious conspiracy charge

## Significance

The split verdict in the first Oath Keepers trial (4 guilty / 2 NOT guilty on sedition) demonstrated the jury was weighing each defendant's conduct individually - the prosecution did not get a sweeping coordinated-conspiracy verdict but did establish leader-level criminality.

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Pardoned as part of blanket J6 pardon
