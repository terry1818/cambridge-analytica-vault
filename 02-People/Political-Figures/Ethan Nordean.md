# Ethan Nordean

Seattle-area [[Proud Boys]] leader. Convicted of seditious conspiracy for January 6.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- May 4, 2023: Federal jury, DC: GUILTY of seditious conspiracy, conspiracy to obstruct an official proceeding
- September 1, 2023: Sentenced to 18 years
- Trial established Nordean led the Proud Boys "Ministry of Self Defense" element that breached the Capitol perimeter at multiple points

## Pre-J6

- Online persona "Rufio Panman" - gained viral fame after a 2018 Portland street fight video
- Promoted to senior Proud Boys leadership 2019-2020

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Released under Trump's blanket J6 pardon
