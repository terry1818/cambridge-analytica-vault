# Aileen Cannon

US District Judge for the Southern District of Florida. Nominated by Trump and confirmed November 2020 (post-election lame-duck session). Federalist Society member pre-confirmation.

## Mar-a-Lago documents case rulings

- September 2022: Granted Trump's motion for a special master to review seized documents (overturned by 11th Circuit)
- July 15, 2024: Dismissed the entire indictment against Trump on the grounds that Special Counsel [[Jack Smith]] was unconstitutionally appointed
- Smith appealed; case still pending on appeal at Trump's January 2025 inauguration; DOJ dropped the appeal February 2025

## Significance

Cannon's handling of the documents case became the most-cited contemporary example of judicial alignment with a sitting political coalition's interests producing rulings that legal observers across the political spectrum viewed as outside the mainstream of federal jurisprudence.

The structural feature: a single district judge (confirmed in a lame-duck session) was able to materially derail the federal accountability mechanism for the most-documented criminal case against a former president in US history.
