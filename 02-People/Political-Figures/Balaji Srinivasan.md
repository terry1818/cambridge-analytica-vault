# Balaji Srinivasan

US engineer, entrepreneur, theorist. Former CTO of Coinbase. Former general partner at Andreessen Horowitz. Author of "The Network State" (2022).

## "The Network State" thesis

- Crypto-enabled online communities can crystallize into political entities that eventually negotiate sovereignty with existing nation-states
- Functions as a Tech-right exit strategy: rather than capture the existing US state, build a parallel network that supersedes it
- Has attracted both crypto-enthusiast audiences and traditional tech-right backing

## Political alignment

- Heavily critical of US public health response during COVID
- Anti-CDC, anti-WHO, anti-mainstream-media commentary
- 2024: did not formally endorse Trump but participated in the broader Tech right messaging

## Relationship to vault themes

- The Network State concept overlaps in interesting ways with [[Convention of States]] structural-amendment strategy - both treat existing federal authority as fundamentally illegitimate and proposed substitutes for it as conceptually parallel "constitutional moments"
- Differs in that COS works within Article V while Network State proposes exit
