# Jim DeMint

## Summary

Former **US Senator from South Carolina (2005-2013)**. Resigned from Senate to become **President of [[The Heritage Foundation]] in 2013**; ousted from Heritage in 2017 after board lost confidence. Now serves as **senior advisor to [[Convention of States Action]]**. **The most explicit personnel link between Heritage Foundation and the COS movement.** Currently chairs the Conservative Partnership Institute, a key training/operations node for the post-Trump-1.0 conservative apparatus.

## Key facts

- **Roles:** US Senator (SC, 2005-2013); Heritage Foundation President (2013-2017); COS senior advisor; Conservative Partnership Institute chair
- **Affiliations:** [[The Heritage Foundation]], [[Convention of States Action]], Conservative Partnership Institute, Republican Party
- **Notable for:** Linking Heritage institutional credibility to the COS movement
