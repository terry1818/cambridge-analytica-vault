# Richard Donoghue

US Attorney for the Eastern District of New York 2018-2020 (appointed by Trump). Principal Associate Deputy Attorney General 2020. **Acting US Deputy Attorney General** December 24, 2020 - January 20, 2021. Partner with Acting AG [[Jeffrey Rosen]] in resisting Trump pressure on DOJ to falsely declare the 2020 election compromised. **His contemporaneous notes of Trump-Rosen-Donoghue calls and meetings** became key documentary evidence in subsequent investigations.

## Key Donoghue note (December 27 2020)

In a December 27, 2020 phone call from Trump to Rosen and Donoghue, Donoghue took contemporaneous notes that included:

> "Just say the election was corrupt + leave the rest to me and the R. Congressmen."

## January 3, 2021 Oval Office meeting

Donoghue was present and contributed to the mass-resignation threat that caused Trump to abandon the [[Jeffrey Clark]] installation plan. Donoghue specifically described to Trump the cascading resignations across DOJ leadership that would follow.

## Senate Judiciary testimony (August 2021)

Donoghue testified at length to the Senate Judiciary Committee about the post-2020 DOJ pressure campaign. Together with [[Jeffrey Rosen]]'s testimony, formed the documentary foundation of the public record on the DOJ pressure track.

## J6 Committee testimony

Testified at the June 23, 2022 public hearing focused on the DOJ pressure track. His sworn testimony established:
- Trump knew DOJ had found no evidence of widespread fraud
- Trump pressed for false public statements regardless
- The Clark plan would have constitutional implications
