# Allen Weisselberg

Born August 15, 1947, Brooklyn, NY. Worked for the Trump family for nearly 50 years, beginning as accountant for Fred Trump in the 1970s. Chief Financial Officer of the Trump Organization 1986 - August 2022. **Pled guilty August 18, 2022** to 15 felony counts of tax fraud and falsifying business records related to the Trump Organization off-books executive compensation scheme. Served 100 days at Rikers Island. **Pled guilty again March 4, 2024** to two counts of perjury related to false testimony in [[NY AG v Trump Organization Civil Fraud 2024]] proceedings; served additional approximately 100 days.

## First conviction â€” Trump Organization tax fraud (2022)

Pled guilty August 18, 2022 in NY State Supreme Court to all 15 charges including:
- Grand larceny in the second degree (multiple counts)
- Tax fraud
- Falsifying business records
- Conspiracy

Admitted scheme to receive substantial untaxed compensation from the Trump Organization 2005-2017 including:
- Free Riverside Boulevard apartment ($100K+/yr value)
- Free parking spaces
- Mercedes leases for himself and his wife
- Private school tuition for his grandchildren ($350K)
- Cash bonuses

Total personal tax evasion approximately $1.7M.

Sentence: 5 months at Rikers Island + 5 years probation + $2M restitution + agreement to testify against Trump Organization entities at trial.

Served approximately 100 days at Rikers (released early for good behavior) April-July 2023.

## Trial testimony (Trump Organization criminal case, October-December 2022)

Testified as key prosecution witness against Trump Organization entities at trial. Acknowledged the scheme. Entities convicted on 17 of 17 counts December 6, 2022. See [[Trump Organization NY Criminal Trial 2022]].

In April 2023 (during civil fraud trial proceedings) Weisselberg testified about [[Donald Trump]]'s Triplex apartment square footage and other valuation matters in deposition. The testimony was later determined to be false in specific particulars.

Pled guilty March 4, 2024 to two counts of perjury in the first degree (NY Penal Law Â§ 210.15).

Weisselberg did NOT testify at the [[NY AG v Trump Organization Civil Fraud 2024]] trial itself; his role in the trial was limited to documentary evidence and references to his prior testimony.
