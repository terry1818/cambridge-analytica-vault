# Lawrence Lessig

Harvard Law professor. Founded Creative Commons (2001). Long-time scholar of copyright, technology law, and election reform.

## Article V positioning

Lessig has been one of the most prominent left-leaning legal scholars to support an Article V convention - but ONE convened on different subjects than the [[Convention of States Action]] proposes (campaign finance reform, voting rights).

His position differentiates from the [[Runaway convention]] critique: he accepts the constitutional risk of a convention but argues it is justified by the necessity of structural reform on his preferred subjects.
