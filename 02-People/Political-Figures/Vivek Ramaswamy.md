# Vivek Ramaswamy

## Summary

American entrepreneur and political figure. Founder of **Roivant Sciences** (pharmaceutical holding company; public 2021). Co-founder of **Strive Asset Management** (2022; positioned as anti-ESG conservative investment firm). **Ran for the 2024 Republican presidential nomination** (January-January 2024; suspended after Iowa caucuses). Endorsed Trump and joined the campaign. **Appointed co-leader of DOGE with Elon Musk** in November 2024; **departed DOGE early 2025** to run for Ohio Governor.

## 2024 presidential campaign

- Announced February 2023
- Self-funded substantially; also accepted donations
- Distinctive policy positions: end birthright citizenship, mass federal-employee firings, dismantle FBI/EPA/etc.
- **Suspended January 15, 2024** after fourth-place finish in Iowa caucuses
- Immediately endorsed Trump
- Joined Trump campaign as surrogate

## DOGE co-leader

- **2024-11** -- Trump announced Ramaswamy + Musk would co-lead DOGE
- **2025-01** -- DOGE formally established via executive order
- **2025-01** -- Ramaswamy departed DOGE within days of inauguration to focus on Ohio Governor run
- Tension between Ramaswamy and Musk reportedly contributed to departure
- Specific reasons for early departure include controversy over H-1B visa comments
