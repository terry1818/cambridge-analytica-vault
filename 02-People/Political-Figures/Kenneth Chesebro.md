# Kenneth Chesebro

Trump 2020 election challenge attorney; Harvard Law graduate (mentored by Laurence Tribe in 1980s before pivoting politically rightward). **Principal architect of the "alternate elector" or "contingent elector" scheme** documented in a series of memos to the Trump legal team in November-December 2020. Indicted as a defendant in [[Georgia v Trump RICO 2023]]; **pled guilty October 20, 2023** to one felony count of conspiracy to commit filing false documents. Identified as Unindicted Co-Conspirator 5 in [[US v Trump DC Federal Election Interference Case]].

## The Chesebro memos

A series of memos written November-December 2020 to the Trump legal team that became the operational blueprint for the false-elector scheme:

- **November 18, 2020 memo**: framed alternate slates of Trump electors as a "legal" device to preserve options for Pence on January 6
- **December 6, 2020 memo**: detailed mechanics â€” alternate slates meet December 14, 2020 in seven states (the date the actual electors would meet), sign certificates, submit them to the Senate
- **December 9, 2020 memo**: addressed legal vulnerabilities; acknowledged the strategy depended on Pence rejecting or delaying certification
- **December 13, 2020 memo**: final operational instructions sent to state Republican organizers

Chesebro coordinated with [[John Eastman]] on the broader theory of Pence's authority and with [[Rudy Giuliani]]'s team on logistics. The false elector meetings occurred December 14, 2020 in Arizona, Georgia, Michigan, Nevada, New Mexico, Pennsylvania, and Wisconsin.

## Indictments and prosecutions

### Georgia RICO indictment (August 2023)
- Georgia RICO violation + multiple counts including conspiracy to commit filing false documents

### Georgia plea (October 20, 2023)
**Pled guilty** to one felony count of conspiracy to commit filing false documents.
- 5 years probation
- $5,000 fine + restitution
- 100 hours community service
- Letter of apology to Georgia
- Agreed to testify truthfully

### DC federal case
- Identified as UCC5 in Smith's indictment; not personally indicted federally

### State investigations
- Cooperated with Michigan AG (Dana Nessel) investigation of MI false electors
- Cooperated with Arizona AG investigation
