# Igor Fruman

Belarusian-born US businessman. Operated with [[Lev Parnas]] as a Giuliani fixer in Ukraine 2018-2019.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- September 2021: Pleaded guilty to soliciting a foreign campaign contribution
- January 2022: Sentenced to 366 days in federal prison

## Role

- Co-funded the LLC ("Global Energy Producers") through which a ,000 contribution flowed to a pro-Trump Super PAC - the conduct underlying the campaign finance conviction
- Functioned as the business-side partner to Parnas in the Ukraine operation
- Has been less publicly cooperative than Parnas; has not made the same direct allegations against Trump
