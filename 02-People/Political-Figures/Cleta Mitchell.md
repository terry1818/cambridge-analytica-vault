# Cleta Mitchell

Cleta Mitchell (born 1950) is a longtime conservative election lawyer who became the principal architect of the post-2020 Republican election-administration challenge infrastructure. Her operational role through 2020-2026 has been substantially more consequential than her public profile suggests.

## Pre-2020 background

Mitchell was a Democratic Oklahoma state legislator (1976-1984) before moving to the right; her subsequent career was principally in conservative election-and-campaign-finance law (Foley & Lardner, then independent practice). She represented Republican candidates and committees across multiple cycles, became a Federalist Society-adjacent figure on election-law questions, and served on the [[Council for National Policy]] board.

## The January 2, 2021 Trump-Raffensperger call

Mitchell was the principal lawyer on the January 2, 2021 phone call between Trump and Georgia Secretary of State Brad Raffensperger ("find 11,780 votes"). Her participation in the call was the operational role that produced the most documented evidence of post-election pressure on a state election official. She was named as an unindicted co-conspirator in the Fulton County DA's Georgia RICO indictment (2023).

## Election Integrity Network (post-2020)

Following the 2020 election, Mitchell launched the [[Election Integrity Network]] (EIN) at the [[Conservative Partnership Institute]] as the principal coordination vehicle for Republican election-administration challenge work. EIN organises:

- Weekly coordination calls connecting state-level Republican election lawyers, poll-watcher recruitment programmes, election-board candidacies, and litigation strategy across all 50 states
- Training programmes for poll watchers, election officials, and conservative state legislators
- Model-legislation drafting for state-level election restrictions
- Coordination with the Republican National Lawyers Association and the broader conservative-legal infrastructure

The weekly EIN calls have been documented (via leaked recordings and via participant accounts) as the principal operational coordination surface for the post-2020 Republican election-administration programme.

## Why this is load-bearing

Mitchell is the single human node connecting the post-2020 election-denial intellectual programme to the operational election-administration challenge infrastructure. Without Mitchell's coordination role, the various state-level Republican election operations would be substantially less aligned across jurisdictions.
