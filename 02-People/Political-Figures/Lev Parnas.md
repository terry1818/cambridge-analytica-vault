# Lev Parnas

Soviet-born US businessman. [[Rudy Giuliani]] fixer in Ukraine 2018-2019. Arrested October 9, 2019 at Dulles Airport on campaign finance charges - the day before he was scheduled to testify to the House Intelligence Committee.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- October 2021: Convicted of conspiracy, wire fraud, falsifying records
- 2022: Sentenced to 20 months federal prison

## Post-conviction cooperation

Has consistently asserted Giuliani's Ukraine operation was directed by Trump personally. His 2024 House Oversight testimony reiterated those claims.
