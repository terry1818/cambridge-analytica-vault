# Lev Parnas

Soviet-born US businessman. Worked as a fixer for [[Rudy Giuliani]] in Ukraine 2018-2019. Convicted of campaign finance violations (October 2021).

## Role in Trump's first impeachment

- Helped Giuliani assemble the dirt-on-Biden operation in Ukraine
- Introduced Giuliani to Ukrainian sources making allegations against [[Hunter Biden]] and Burisma
- Arrested October 9, 2019 at Dulles Airport with [[Igor Fruman]] on the way to Vienna - the day before he was scheduled to testify to the House Intelligence Committee

## Sworn statements (post-conviction)

- 2022 letter to Congress and 2024 [[House Oversight Committee]] testimony: Parnas asserted Giuliani's Ukraine operation was directed by Trump personally
- Parnas: there was never any credible Biden / Burisma corruption evidence - the operation was reverse-engineered to manufacture an investigation that could be announced publicly

## Evidence tier

- **Tier 1**: Parnas was on the ground with Giuliani; his criminal conviction is for unrelated campaign finance, not for lying about Trump
- **Tier 2**: His direct-line-to-Trump claims are uncorroborated by document discovery in their strongest form
- **Tier 3 / contested**: Whether Trump personally directed each step versus tolerated Giuliani running the operation
