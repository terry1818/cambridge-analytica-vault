# Joseph Muscat

## Summary

Prime Minister of Malta (2013-2020). Leader of the [[Labour Party (Malta)]]. Per [[Source DCMS Final Report 2019]]: "SCL certainly had meetings in Malta, that **Christian Kalin of [[Henley & Partners]] was introduced by SCL to Joseph Muscat in 2011**, and that Christian Kalin met with both political parties before 2013."

## Election + post-election Henley & Partners contract

- 2011 -- SCL introduces Muscat (then opposition leader) to [[Christian Kalin]] of Henley & Partners
- 2013 -- Labour wins Maltese general election
- **Immediately after the election: Henley & Partners runs Maltas Individual Investor Programme** -- CBI scheme selling Maltese (and therefore EU) citizenship to wealthy individuals
- The pattern of pre-election introduction → post-election contract became known as a **Henley & Partners template**

## Caruana Galizia murder

- **Daphne Caruana Galizia** -- Maltese investigative journalist who had relentlessly investigated Muscat-government corruption, including the Panama Papers Maltese figures and the IIP citizenship program
- **2017-10-16** -- Caruana Galizia assassinated by car bomb outside her home
- 2017-2019 -- slow-developing investigation revealed government-connected figures involved
- 2019 -- businessman **Yorgen Fenech** (Muscat ally) arrested
- 2019-12 -- Muscats chief of staff Keith Schembri resigned; tourism minister Konrad Mizzi resigned
- **2020-01** -- Muscat resigned as Prime Minister amid pressure
- 2024 -- Inquiry concluded Maltese state bore responsibility for failing to protect Caruana Galizia
- 2025+ -- Multiple convictions of those who carried out the bombing; Fenech case continues

## Post-resignation

- Various corporate consulting roles
- Subject of ongoing Maltese magisterial inquiries into hospital privatization deal
- 2024 -- Charged in Vitals hospitals corruption case (proceedings ongoing)
