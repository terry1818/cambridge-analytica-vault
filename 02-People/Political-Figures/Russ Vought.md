# Russ Vought

## Summary

**Chief architect of [[Project 2025]]** and current **Director of the Office of Management and Budget** in Trump 2.0 (confirmed by Senate January 2025). Founded the Center for Renewing America after leaving Trump 1.0 OMB. Self-described **Christian nationalist**. [[Stephen Miller]] called him "the guy who's been developing the plan to take down the deep state." Used the **October 2025 government shutdown to gut federal agencies** per ProPublica investigation reported by Democracy Now, earning the "shadow president" label.

## Key facts

- **Roles:** OMB Director (2020-2021, 2025-present); President, Center for Renewing America (2021-2025); RNC platform policy director (2024)
- **Affiliations:** [[The Heritage Foundation]] (P2025 architect), Center for Renewing America
- **Notable for:** Lead author of Project 2025; Schedule F advocate
- **Ideological frame:** Christian nationalist (self-described)
