# Brendan Carr

## Summary

**Trump 2.0 Federal Communications Commission Chair** (since January 2025; elevated from Commissioner role). FCC Commissioner since August 2017. **Author of the FCC chapter of Project 2025's Mandate for Leadership.** **Approved the Skydance-Paramount merger** during the Trump 2.0 FCC transition. **Aggressive Trump-aligned regulatory posture** including:

- **Section 230 reform** advocacy (anti-tech-platform)
- **Aggressive broadcast-license review** posture toward news outlets
- **Pro-merger framework** for broadcasting consolidation
- **Anti-DEI broadcast-policy** initiatives

**Operationally critical** to the Ellison family acquisition of Paramount because:
1. FCC approval was required for CBS broadcast license transfers
2. Carr-led FCC negotiated specific conditions for approval
3. The 60 Minutes lawsuit settlement is widely understood as part of the negotiated package
