# Tom Coburn

US Senator from Oklahoma 2005-2015 (Republican). Physician. Died 2020.

## Convention of States advocacy

- Senator Coburn was the highest-profile early establishment-Republican advocate for an Article V Convention of States
- Authored "Smashing the DC Monopoly" (2017) - the central Convention of States movement strategy book
- Joined [[Convention of States Action]] full-time after leaving the Senate
- His advocacy is the bridge between conventional movement-conservative figures and the broader [[COS movement]]

## The Family connection

- Long-time resident of the C Street House operated by [[The Family]] / [[The Fellowship Foundation]]
- Coburn's residency at C Street, along with Senators John Ensign and others, became briefly controversial in 2009 during the Ensign affair

## Religious profile

- Pro-life absolutist; multiple judicial-confirmation fights over his abortion-related questions
- Frequently traveled internationally on Fellowship-organized prayer outreach
