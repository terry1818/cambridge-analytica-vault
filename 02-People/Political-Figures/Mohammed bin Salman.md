# Mohammed bin Salman al Saud

Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia. Born 1985. Effective ruler of Saudi Arabia since 2017 consolidation. Father King Salman remains nominal head of state.

## 2017 consolidation

- June 2017: Named Crown Prince, replacing Mohammed bin Nayef
- November 2017: Ritz-Carlton "anticorruption" detention of ~200 senior Saudi figures including Prince Alwaleed bin Talal - mass settlement under duress
- The consolidation gave MBS unprecedented control of Saudi political economy

## Khashoggi murder (Tier 1)

- October 2, 2018: Jamal Khashoggi murdered in the Saudi consulate in Istanbul
- US Office of the Director of National Intelligence February 26, 2021 unclassified report: MBS "approved an operation" to capture or kill Khashoggi
- Trump 1st-term administration declined to sanction MBS personally; Biden administration named MBS in the ODNI report but invoked head-of-state immunity in civil litigation
