# Zachary Rehl

Philadelphia [[Proud Boys]] chapter president. Former US Marine. Convicted of seditious conspiracy for January 6.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- May 4, 2023: GUILTY of seditious conspiracy, conspiracy to obstruct an official proceeding
- August 31, 2023: Sentenced to 15 years
- Trial evidence: Rehl personally entered the Capitol, sprayed a chemical irritant at officers, and posted Telegram messages celebrating the attack

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Released
