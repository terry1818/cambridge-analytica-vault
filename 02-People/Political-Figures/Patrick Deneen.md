# Patrick Deneen

## Summary

**Notre Dame political-science professor** and key intellectual voice of **Catholic Integralism** / **post-liberalism**. Author of:

- ***Why Liberalism Failed*** (2018) - influential critique of classical liberalism from the right; Obama recommended it
- ***Regime Change: Toward a Postliberal Future*** (2023) - explicit advocacy for replacing the American liberal order with a Catholic-influenced post-liberal order

**Major intellectual influence on JD Vance** (who converted to Catholicism partly under Deneen's influence; appeared with Deneen multiple times; cited Deneen in speeches). NatCon circuit recurring speaker. Friend of [[Adrian Vermeule]] (Harvard Law; more radical Catholic Integralist).

## Key facts

- **Role:** Notre Dame political-science professor
- **Books:** *Why Liberalism Failed* (2018), *Regime Change* (2023)
- **Notable for:** Catholic Integralism / post-liberal intellectual influence on Vance

## Core argument

- Classical liberalism is structurally self-undermining
- Individual-rights primacy erodes social bonds
- Result: atomized individuals, decaying communities
- **Solution: post-liberal regime** prioritizing family, faith, place, virtue
- **"Aristopopulism"** - elite-led popular movement against current elite
