# Steve Forbes

## Summary

**Chairman + Editor-in-Chief of Forbes Inc.** (Forbes Magazine + Forbes.com). **Heritage Foundation trustee since 2018**. Two-time Republican presidential candidate (**1996 and 2000**) running on **flat-tax + supply-side platform**. Co-founder of Empower America (1993) with Jack Kemp + Jeane Kirkpatrick + Bill Bennett. Longtime **Council for National Policy member**. Major **patron-class voice** on tax + economic + monetary policy. **Generally Trump-aligned** post-2016 despite earlier moderate-Republican positioning + 2016 primary endorsement of John Kasich.

**Documented positioning shifts:**
- **2016:** Endorsed Kasich in GOP primary; later supported Trump general
- **2020:** Endorsed Trump
- **2024:** Endorsed Trump; appeared at Trump events
- **Crypto positioning:** **Major Bitcoin / crypto advocate** since 2017; aligned with [[Lens - The Crypto Wing]] patron-class layer

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1947-07-18
- **Net worth:** ~$430M (substantially below his father Malcolm Forbes Sr. peak)
- **Education:** Princeton (BA 1970)
- **Family:** Father Malcolm Forbes Sr. (deceased 1990; legendary Forbes Inc. expansion)

## Career

- **Forbes Inc.** - took over CEO + chairman role after father's 1990 death; modernized + digital expansion
- **1996 GOP primary** - ran on flat-tax; lost to Dole
- **2000 GOP primary** - ran again; lost to Bush
- **Heritage Foundation** - trustee since 2018
- **Empower America** - co-founder 1993 (merged into FreedomWorks 2004)
- **Foreign Affairs Newsletter** - co-founded
- **Multiple books** - on supply-side economics + flat-tax + monetary policy

## Heritage Foundation role

Trustee since 2018. **Active in Heritage governance** + Project 2025-aligned positioning. Less publicly visible than Rebekah Mercer or Larry Arnn in day-to-day Heritage operations but operationally important on Heritage economic-policy + monetary-policy positions.

## Key policy positions

- **Flat tax** (long-running signature)
- **Gold standard / monetary reform**
- **Supply-side economics**
- **Crypto / Bitcoin advocacy** (post-2017)
- **Free trade** (though deviated under Trump 1.0 on China)
- **Anti-regulatory** broadly
- **Anti-ESG investing**

## Crypto-wing alignment

Forbes Magazine launched dedicated crypto coverage early; Forbes hosted **multiple "Iconoclast" + crypto-friendly events**. Steve Forbes personally advocated for Bitcoin as monetary reform tool. **Patron-class bridge** between traditional conservative-economic-policy infrastructure + the newer [[Lens - The Crypto Wing|crypto wing]].
