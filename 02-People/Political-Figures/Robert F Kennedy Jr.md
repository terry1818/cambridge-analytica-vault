# Robert F. Kennedy Jr.

US Secretary of Health and Human Services (2025-). Founder of [[Children's Health Defense]] (2018). Son of Senator Robert F. Kennedy; nephew of President John F. Kennedy.

## Pre-politics career

- Environmental lawyer (Hudson Riverkeeper, NRDC)
- Author / activist focused initially on environmental causes
- Pivot to anti-vaccine activism c.2005, intensifying through 2010s

## 2024 campaign

- Initially ran in Democratic primary against Biden
- Switched to independent run October 2023
- Suspended campaign August 23, 2024 and endorsed Trump
- Promised in exchange policy authority over HHS and related health agencies

## HHS Secretary role

- Confirmed February 13, 2025 (52-48 Senate vote)
- First HHS Secretary with explicit anti-vaccine record
- Implemented changes to ACIP vaccine advisory committee
- Multiple senior CDC and NIH departures in early months
