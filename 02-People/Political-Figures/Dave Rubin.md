# Dave Rubin

Dave Rubin (born 1976) is a conservative-aligned talk-show host. His Rubin Report podcast/video show is a substantial part of the broader [[Daily Wire]] / Trump-aligned media ecosystem. His trajectory from progressive-aligned commentator (early Young Turks contributor) to explicit conservative-alignment is documented as a case study in the "intellectual dark web" / classical-liberal-to-conservative ideological-pipeline pattern.

## Operational role

Rubin's operational role is principally as a media-personality within the broader conservative-media ecosystem. He has signed distribution deals with Daily Wire, hosted substantial cross-pollination interviews with Tech-Right and adjacent figures, and provided platform amplification for various Trump-administration figures and Project 2025-affiliated voices.
