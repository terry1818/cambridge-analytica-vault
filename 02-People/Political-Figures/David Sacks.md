# David Sacks

Founding COO of PayPal (1999-2002). Founder of Yammer (sold to Microsoft 2012). Co-host of the All-In Podcast. Trump's AI and Crypto Czar in the second term (2025-).

## PayPal Mafia

- One of the original [[PayPal Mafia]] alongside Thiel, Musk, Reid Hoffman, Max Levchin, Roelof Botha
- The PayPal cohort is the founding stratum of what later became the [[Tech right]]

## All-In Podcast

- Co-hosts with Chamath Palihapitiya, Jason Calacanis, David Friedberg
- Podcast became a high-influence vector for the tech-right realignment 2022-2024
- Sacks's commentary on Russia-Ukraine, AI regulation, and election fraud claims aligned with Trump campaign positioning

## June 2024 Trump fundraiser

- Sacks hosted a major Trump fundraiser at his San Francisco home in June 2024 - widely seen as the inflection point where Silicon Valley financial support for Trump became publicly normalized

## Second-term role (Tier 1)

- December 2024: Named White House AI and Crypto Czar
- Has authority over AI regulation, crypto policy coordination, and tech-sector relationships
- Functions as the policy ambassador to the [[Tech right]] donor base
