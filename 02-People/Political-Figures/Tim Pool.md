# Tim Pool

## Summary

American right-wing podcaster and YouTuber. **Among the highest-paid US influencers in the [[Tenet Media DOJ Indictment 2024-09-04|Tenet Media Russian-funded operation]]** - received approximately **$400K/month for ~4 months (~$1.6M total)** from RT-employee-controlled accounts via Tenet. After September 2024 DOJ indictment of the Russian principals, Pool claimed **"victim" status** - that he was deceived about the funding source. **Not criminally charged**. Continues operating Timcast Media. Hosts the **Timcast IRL** show with multiple millions of monthly listeners.
