# Christopher Rufo

## Summary

**The single most operationally consequential education-capture strategist** of the contemporary American right. **Manhattan Institute senior fellow** + City Journal director. **Architect of the anti-Critical-Race-Theory campaign** (2020-2021) that became the foundational template for subsequent education-capture campaigns. Led the **New College of Florida takeover** (2023; DeSantis-appointed to board of trustees; converted previously-progressive Florida public liberal-arts college to right-wing institution). **Published the strategy explicitly**: "We go in there and we defund things we don't like, we fund things we do like... We are laying siege to the institutions."

- "Grooming" framing of LGBTQ+ educators - 2022-2023
- Anti-trans-affirming-care campaign
- Higher-education accreditor restructuring
- Federal-funding cuts to elite universities (Harvard, Columbia)

## Strategic framework (publicly stated)

Rufo has been **unusually transparent about strategy** - publishing essays + giving talks documenting his approach:

1. **Identify culturally-toxic frame** (CRT, DEI, "grooming")
2. **Brand it negatively** with concrete examples
3. **Generate moral panic** via local-news + Twitter / X amplification
4. **Convert moral panic to legislation** (state-by-state)
5. **Convert legislation to funding cuts**
6. **Convert funding cuts to institutional restructuring**
7. **Replace institutional leadership** with aligned personnel
8. **Convert institutions to new ideological program**

**This is the explicit playbook.**

## New College of Florida takeover (2023)

- Florida public liberal-arts college (~700 students)
- **Previously progressive-leaning** academic culture
- **January 2023:** DeSantis appointed 6 new trustees (Rufo + others)
- New trustees fired president, eliminated DEI office, restricted curriculum
- ~36% of faculty left within 18 months
- Restructured to Hillsdale-College-influenced classical-conservative model

**Successful proof-of-concept** for the takeover-from-within model.
