# Cassidy Hutchinson

Former aide to [[Mark Meadows]] in the Trump White House (March 2020 - January 2021). Surprise public witness at June 28, 2022 [[January 6 Select Committee]] hearing.

## Testimony (Tier 1)

Documented:
- Trump's anger at metal-detectors restricting his armed-supporters' access to the Ellipse rally
- Cabinet awareness of J6 risk in advance
- Internal White House conversations between Meadows, Trump, and Trump-orbit lawyers

## Tier 2 (disputed)

- The Ornato-relayed Trump-lunging-at-Engel account (Ornato has disputed; J6 Committee did not formally affirm or deny)

## Significance

Hutchinson's testimony was the most-impactful single witness account of the Committee's hearings. Her credibility was substantially corroborated by White House visitor logs, contemporaneous texts, and other witness accounts.
