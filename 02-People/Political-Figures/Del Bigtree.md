# Del Bigtree

Del Bigtree is a US anti-vaccine media operator whose trajectory from television producer (The Doctors) through Vaxxed film production (2016, with Andrew Wakefield) through founding of [[Informed Consent Action Network]] (ICAN) and the Highwire show has made him one of the principal contemporary anti-vaccine movement principals.

## Operational role

Bigtree's operational role includes:

- The Highwire (weekly streaming-video programme with substantial subscriber base)
- ICAN litigation programme (anti-vaccine-related legal challenges, principally focused on regulatory and FOIA work)
- Movement-coordination engagement across the broader [[Make America Healthy Again]] (MAHA) ecosystem
- Substantial cross-pollination with [[Children's Health Defense]] / [[RFK Jr]] operational programme

## Trump 2.0 alignment

Bigtree's pre-2024 operational positioning was principally outside formal Republican-Party-affiliation. The 2024 endorsement of [[RFK Jr]]'s candidacy and the subsequent integration with the MAHA programme has produced substantial operational alignment with Trump 2.0 health-policy direction. Bigtree's specific advisory engagement with the [[RFK Jr]] HHS programme has been documented.
