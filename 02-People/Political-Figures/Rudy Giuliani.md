# Rudy Giuliani

Born May 28, 1944, Brooklyn, NY. US Attorney for the Southern District of New York 1983-1989 (notable for Mafia commission case and Wall Street insider trading prosecutions). Mayor of New York City 1994-2001 ("America's Mayor" following 9/11). Personal attorney to [[Donald Trump]] 2018-2021. Architect of the post-2020 election challenge effort and central figure in the multi-pronged scheme to overturn the election. **Unindicted co-conspirator 1 in [[US v Trump DC Federal Election Interference Case]]**; **indicted defendant in [[Georgia v Trump RICO 2023]]**; **disbarred in New York (June 2024) and the District of Columbia (September 2024)**; **defamation verdict of $148 million** against him by Ruby Freeman and Shaye Moss (December 2023); declared bankruptcy December 2023.

## Documented Trump-related activities

### Ukraine track (2018-2019)
- Operated a parallel diplomatic channel in Ukraine seeking damaging information about [[Joe Biden]] and his son Hunter
- Coordinated with [[Lev Parnas]] and [[Igor Fruman]] (later indicted on campaign finance charges)
- Pursued removal of US Ambassador [[Marie Yovanovitch]]; she was recalled May 2019
- Central figure in the conduct underlying [[Trump First Impeachment 2019-2020]]

### 2020 election challenge (November 2020 - January 2021)
- Led Trump campaign legal effort
- November 7, 2020 Four Seasons Total Landscaping press conference
- Hair-dye press conference November 19, 2020 with [[Sidney Powell]] and others
- Filed multiple losing election challenges
- Promoted the "Kraken" theory
- Coordinated false-elector scheme in multiple states
- Testified to state legislative committees in Arizona, Georgia, Michigan, Pennsylvania presenting unsupported allegations
- Pressure on state officials including Georgia

### January 6 (2021)
- Spoke at Ellipse rally before Trump's speech; called for "trial by combat"
- Continued in subsequent weeks to claim election fraud

## Investigations and prosecutions

### Georgia RICO indictment (August 2023)
Indicted in [[Georgia v Trump RICO 2023]] on:
- Georgia RICO violation
- 12 additional counts
Status: active, pending Willis-replacement disqualification reassignment.

### DC federal case (August 2023)
- Identified as Unindicted Co-Conspirator 1 in Smith's DC indictment of Trump
- Was not personally indicted federally; uncertain whether DOJ Smith would have charged Giuliani separately had Trump prosecutions continued

### NY AG investigation
- Various NY AG civil investigations into Trump Organization touched on Giuliani's role

## Defamation cases against Giuliani

### Ruby Freeman and Shaye Moss verdict (December 2023)
- Giuliani repeatedly falsely accused Fulton County, Georgia election workers Ruby Freeman and Shaye Moss of election fraud
- December 15, 2023: federal jury in DC awarded $148 million in compensatory and punitive damages
- Giuliani filed bankruptcy December 21, 2023, in part to delay collection
- Trial court ordered Giuliani to surrender significant assets including his NYC apartment

### Dominion Voting Systems case
- Filed January 2021 for $1.3 billion alleging false statements about Dominion machines
- Still pending as of 2026-05

### Smartmatic case
- Pending

## Bar disbarments

- **NY State**: disbarred June 25, 2024 after disciplinary proceedings related to election challenge conduct
- **DC**: disbarred September 26, 2024
- His license had been suspended in NY since June 2021 and in DC since July 2021

## Bankruptcy

December 21, 2023: filed Chapter 11. Case subsequently dismissed July 12, 2024 by Judge Lane after finding Giuliani's lack of transparency made the case unworkable; dismissed without discharge, leaving creditors (including Freeman and Moss) to pursue collection in state court.
