# Ralph Drollinger

## Summary

American evangelical clergyman, former professional basketball player. **Founded [[Capitol Ministries]] in 1996** with his wife Danielle. **Leads weekly Bible-study sessions for Trump cabinet members** since 2017 (continuing into Trump 2.0). Provides personalized Bible-study printouts to Donald Trump himself. **Ideologically explicit**: institutional separation of church/state in name but church should "influence" the state in practice; **anti-LGBTQ, anti-women's-rights (against women in government leadership), anti-Catholic** (calls Catholicism "one of the primary false religions of the world"), anti-Islam, **pro-undocumented-immigrant child separation** (provided 2018 theological justification), pro-lower-taxes-on-wealthy (theological framing). Blamed China and gay people for COVID-19 in March 2020.
