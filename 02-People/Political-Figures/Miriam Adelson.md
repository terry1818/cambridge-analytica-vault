# Miriam Adelson

Miriam Adelson (born 1946) is the widow of [[Sheldon Adelson]] and the continuing principal of the Adelson political-giving programme since Sheldon's January 2021 death. Miriam was a practicing physician (Israeli-trained, addiction-medicine specialty) before her marriage to Sheldon and has continued substantial philanthropic engagement around addiction-medicine and Israeli-American issues alongside her political-giving programme.

## Post-2021 political giving

Miriam Adelson's post-2021 political giving has continued substantially:

- 2024 cycle: principal funder of Preserve America PAC ($100M+ commitment to Trump 2024 campaign infrastructure)
- Continued giving to Republican Jewish Coalition and adjacent vehicles
- Substantial Israel-policy-aligned giving across multiple vehicles
- Trump 2.0 administration adjacent funding through various coordinated channels

The combined post-2021 Adelson-family political-giving footprint is substantially smaller than the pre-2021 Sheldon-era footprint but remains among the largest single-family Republican donor flows.

## Trump Presidential Medal of Freedom

Trump awarded Miriam Adelson the Presidential Medal of Freedom in November 2018, the highest US civilian honor. The award was operationally significant as one of the most visible Trump-administration acknowledgments of the Adelson-family political-and-policy influence.

## Las Vegas Sands ownership

Miriam Adelson inherited substantial Las Vegas Sands ownership upon Sheldon's death. The LVS-derived wealth is the principal source of the continuing political-giving capacity.
