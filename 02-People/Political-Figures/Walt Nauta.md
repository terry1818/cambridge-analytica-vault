## Specific allegations (Tier 1, per indictment)

- Moved boxes back into the Storage Room before [[Evan Corcoran]]'s June 3, 2022 grand jury subpoena document review
- Lied to FBI investigators about whether he had moved boxes
- Was directed by [[Donald Trump]] in this conduct

## Charges

- Conspiracy to obstruct justice
- Withholding documents and records

- Concealing a document in a federal investigation

## Status

Case dismissed by Judge [[Aileen Cannon]] July 15, 2024 on the same Appointments Clause grounds that dismissed the case against Trump. DOJ filed appeal; withdrawn November 2024 following Trump's election.

## Continued role

Nauta continued as Trump's body man through the 2024 campaign and into the Trump 2.0 administration as senior aide.
