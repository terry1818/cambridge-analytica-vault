# John Eastman

US constitutional law professor. Former dean of Chapman University Fowler School of Law. Author of the [[Eastman Memo]] (December 2020 / January 2021) outlining the legal theory for [[Mike Pence]] to reject electors on January 6.

## Tier 1 consequences

- 2022: Resigned from Chapman amid faculty / institutional pressure
- 2023: Indicted in [[Georgia RICO Case]]
- 2024: Indicted in Arizona fake-electors case
- March 27, 2024: California Bar recommended disbarment

## Significance

Eastman is the intellectual architect of the legal theory that produced the [[Fake electors scheme]]. The Committee documented his real-time concession to [[Greg Jacob]] that the theory would lose 9-0 at the Supreme Court - and his persistence in pressing it anyway during the actual January 6 events.
