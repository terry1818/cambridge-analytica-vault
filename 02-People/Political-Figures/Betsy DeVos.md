# Betsy DeVos

US Secretary of Education in Trump's first term (2017-2021). Heir to the Amway fortune via marriage to [[Dick DeVos Jr]]. Long-time chair of American Federation for Children. Sister of [[Erik Prince]].

## Family connections

- Daughter of Richard Prince and Elsa Prince Broekhuizen (heirs to Prince Manufacturing fortune)
- Married to [[Dick DeVos Jr]] (son of [[Richard DeVos Sr]], Amway co-founder)
- Sister of [[Erik Prince]] (founder of Blackwater)

## Pre-government

- Chair of Michigan Republican Party (1996-2000, 2003-2005)
- Chair of American Federation for Children (until 2017) - top school-choice advocacy organization
- Senior figure in [[Council for National Policy]]

## Secretary of Education 2017-2021

- Rolled back Obama-era Title IX guidance (sexual assault adjudication)
- Withdrew Obama-era for-profit college accountability rules (gainful employment)
- Sued by 19 state AGs over loan forgiveness delays
- Resigned January 7, 2021 over Trump's January 6 conduct
