# Yevgeny Prigozhin

Russian oligarch, founder of [[Wagner Group]] private military company and the [[Internet Research Agency]] troll farm. Killed in a plane crash August 23, 2023.

## Indictments (Tier 1)

- February 16, 2018: Indicted by Special Counsel [[Robert Mueller]] alongside 12 other Russian nationals for the IRA election interference operation
- Charges: Conspiracy to defraud the United States, wire fraud, bank fraud, identity theft
- February 23, 2023: Prigozhin publicly admitted "I have interfered, am interfering and will continue to interfere" in US elections

## Wagner Group

- Operated as Russia's deniable military arm in Syria, Libya, Sudan, CAR, Mali, Ukraine
- Spearheaded the prolonged battle for Bakhmut 2022-2023
- June 23-24, 2023: Wagner staged a brief armed march on Moscow demanding the removal of Defense Minister Shoigu and Gen. Gerasimov. Stood down within 36 hours
- Two months later Prigozhin's jet crashed near Tver - widely attributed to Kremlin retaliation but not officially attributed
