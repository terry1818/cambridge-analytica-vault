# Paul Singer

Paul Singer (born 1944) is the founder of Elliott Management Corporation, a New York-based activist hedge fund. Singer is one of the principal libertarian-leaning pro-Israel Republican mega-donors and is a substantial funder of the broader conservative-movement institutional infrastructure.

## Elliott Management wealth source

Elliott Management is one of the largest activist hedge funds globally, with substantial AUM in the multi-tens-of-billions range. The fund's principal operational pattern is activist-investor strategy applied across multiple asset categories, with substantial public-equity activist campaigns producing the firm's principal commercial visibility.

The Argentine-debt-collection campaign (2001-2016) was the most operationally distinctive single Elliott campaign, producing substantial international financial-policy precedents around sovereign-debt restructuring.

## Republican political giving

Singer has been a substantial Republican mega-donor across multiple cycles. Specific giving patterns:

- Substantial 2012 cycle giving to Romney campaign infrastructure and adjacent vehicles
- Substantial 2016 cycle giving to multiple Republican primary candidates (Marco Rubio prominently) before pivoting to Trump
- Substantial 2020-2024 cycle giving including substantial Senate-race independent-expenditure giving
- Sustained funding of conservative-legal infrastructure including [[Federalist Society]], [[Heritage Foundation]], and adjacent organisations
- Substantial libertarian-leaning policy-organisation funding ([[Manhattan Institute]], [[American Enterprise Institute]], adjacent vehicles)

## Israel-policy positioning

Singer's pro-Israel positioning has been substantial and longstanding. He has been a significant funder of [[AIPAC]] / [[United Democracy Project]] adjacent infrastructure, of [[JINSA]], and of various pro-Israel research and policy organisations. The Israel-policy positioning operationally aligns with the broader libertarian-leaning Republican mega-donor pattern.

## Trump-era complications

Singer's relationship with Trump has been operationally complicated. Singer was a substantial 2016-cycle anti-Trump primary funder before reconciling with Trump post-nomination; the relationship has continued through cycles with periodic public friction. The 2024-cycle Singer giving was substantially Republican-aligned without being explicitly Trump-aligned at the level of certain other mega-donors.
