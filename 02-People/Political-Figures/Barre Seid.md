# Barre Seid

## Summary

Reclusive Illinois manufacturing billionaire. Owned **Tripp Lite** (electrical power-protection company). In 2022, transferred **100% ownership of Tripp Lite to [[Marble Freedom Trust]]**, controlled by **[[Leonard Leo]]**. Eaton then acquired Tripp Lite for **$1.65 billion** - all proceeds went to the trust, making this **the single largest known political-advocacy donation in US history**. Structure avoided **~$400M in capital-gains tax**. Long history of funding conservative causes including the **Heartland Institute** (climate denial) and Federalist Society activities. **Died 2024.**
