# Arron Banks

## Summary

British businessman + **largest known UK political donor 2014-2016** (~GBP 8M+ to Leave.EU + UKIP + related vehicles). **Principal funder of [[Leave.EU]]** Brexit campaign. **Multiple documented meetings with Russian Embassy in London 2015-2016** (per Open Democracy + Guardian + UK Intelligence Committee Russia Report 2020 partially-released findings).
