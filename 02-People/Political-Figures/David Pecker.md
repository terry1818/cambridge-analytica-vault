# David Pecker

Born September 25, 1951, Bronx, NY. CEO and Chairman of American Media Inc (AMI), publisher of the *National Enquirer* and other tabloid titles, 1999-2020. Longtime friend of [[Donald Trump]]. **Key witness in [[People v Trump NY Hush Money 2024]]** who testified under immunity about the August 2015 Trump Tower "catch and kill" agreement that became the predicate for the conviction.

## August 2015 Trump Tower meeting (Tier 1, per Pecker trial testimony)

Pecker testified that in August 2015, in a meeting at Trump Tower with Trump and [[Michael Cohen]], Pecker agreed to:
- Be the "eyes and ears" of the Trump campaign for negative stories
- Surface to Cohen any negative stories about Trump so Trump and Cohen could intervene
- "Catch and kill" by purchasing stories' rights and suppressing them

This was the operating framework for the subsequent "catch and kill" payments.

## Documented catch-and-kill payments

- **Dino Sajudin** (former Trump Tower doorman): AMI paid $30,000 for rights to a (false) story about an alleged Trump out-of-wedlock child
- **Karen McDougal** (former Playboy model): AMI paid $150,000 for rights to her story of a 2006-2007 affair with Trump; story never published
- **Stormy Daniels**: Pecker declined to do the Daniels deal himself due to AMI cost concerns and prior payments; instead facilitated Cohen's $130,000 personal payment via Essential Consultants LLC

## 2018 SDNY non-prosecution agreement

AMI entered into a non-prosecution agreement with SDNY September 2018 admitting:
- AMI made the McDougal payment "in concert with" the Trump campaign
- Purpose was to influence the election by suppressing the story
- This was admitted as a federal campaign finance violation

Cohen's August 2018 plea referenced the McDougal payment as a campaign finance violation also.

## 2024 trial testimony (April-May 2024)

Pecker testified across multiple days at the People v Trump trial. His testimony was the foundational establishment of:
- The August 2015 agreement
- The systematic nature of the catch-and-kill operation
- Trump's personal knowledge of and direction of the scheme
