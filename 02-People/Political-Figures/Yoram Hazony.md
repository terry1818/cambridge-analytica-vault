# Yoram Hazony

## Summary

Israeli-American political theorist; **founder of the [[Edmund Burke Foundation]]** and the **[[National Conservatism]]** conference series (annual since 2019). Author of ***The Virtue of Nationalism*** (2018) - the foundational text of the NatCon intellectual movement. **Bridges US conservative coalition with European populist-right + Israeli right-wing infrastructure**. President of the **Herzl Institute** in Jerusalem. **The single most consequential intellectual organizer of the international right-wing alliance**.

## Key facts

- **Role:** Founder Edmund Burke Foundation + NatCon
- **Notable book:** *The Virtue of Nationalism* (2018)
- **Affiliations:** Edmund Burke Foundation, Herzl Institute, NatCon
- **Notable for:** International-right intellectual organization
