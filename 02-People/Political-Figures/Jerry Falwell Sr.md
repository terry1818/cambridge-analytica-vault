# Jerry Falwell Sr.

## Summary

Baptist minister who **founded [[Moral Majority]] in 1979** with strategist [[Paul Weyrich]] (who coined the name). Built Liberty University into a major evangelical educational institution. **Pioneered the direct-mail political mobilization of evangelical voters** as a Republican base. Endorsed Reagan in 1980 and is widely credited with significant impact on Reagan's victory. Died 2007; succeeded by son Jerry Falwell Jr. (later disgraced) at Liberty University.

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1933-08-11
- **Died:** 2007-05-15
- **Founded:** Moral Majority (1979); Liberty University

## Why he matters

First to **systematically convert evangelical Christians into a reliable Republican electoral bloc**. The methodology he developed (church-based voter mobilization; pastor scorecards; direct mail to congregations) became the template for [[Christian Coalition]] and later [[Faith and Freedom Coalition]] and [[United in Purpose]].
