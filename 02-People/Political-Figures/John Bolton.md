# John Bolton

Born November 20, 1948, Baltimore, MD. Republican foreign policy figure. US Permanent Representative to the UN under George W. Bush 2005-2006. **National Security Adviser to [[Donald Trump]] April 9, 2018 - September 10, 2019**. Published *The Room Where It Happened* (June 2020) with substantial allegations about Trump's Ukraine-related conduct and other matters. The book's contents â€” particularly Bolton's account of Trump telling him explicitly that Trump wanted military aid withheld until Ukraine announced investigations â€” became central to public discussion of [[Trump First Impeachment 2019-2020]] although Bolton did not testify in the Senate trial.

## Ukraine conduct allegations in Bolton's book

In *The Room Where It Happened*, Bolton wrote (per published excerpts and the book itself):
- Trump told Bolton explicitly in August 2019 that he wanted military aid to Ukraine withheld until Ukraine announced investigations of Biden and the 2016 election
- Bolton was present for and concerned about multiple irregularities
- Bolton specifically characterized the Trump-Giuliani Ukraine track as "a drug deal" being orchestrated

These accounts directly corroborated the impeachment-witness accounts of [[Bill Taylor]], [[Gordon Sondland]], [[Marie Yovanovitch]], [[Alexander Vindman]], and others.

## Impeachment trial dynamics (January 2020)

- Bolton's book was in galleys during the January 2020 Senate trial
- The book's contents were known to the press before the Senate vote on witnesses
- Republicans voted 49-51 against allowing witnesses (close vote)
- Bolton publicly stated willingness to testify if subpoenaed; refused House subpoena because Trump WH had not formally invoked privilege but House did not force the issue

## Departure from Trump administration

September 10, 2019: Bolton departed amid disputes with Trump over multiple policy matters including Iran, North Korea, Afghanistan, and Russia. Trump and Bolton publicly disagreed about whether Bolton was fired or resigned.

## Publication conflicts

Trump administration sought to enjoin publication of *The Room Where It Happened* citing classification review. Federal judge declined to enjoin publication June 2020; book released June 23, 2020. DOJ subsequently pursued criminal investigation of Bolton's pre-publication classification review; closed 2021 without charges.

## J6 era

- Bolton continued public criticism of Trump throughout 2020-2024
- Publicly supported impeachment over January 6 conduct
- Testified to J6 Select Committee in closed deposition
