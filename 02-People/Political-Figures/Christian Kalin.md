# Christian Kalin

## Summary

Swiss lawyer and chairman of **[[Henley & Partners]]** -- the global leader in the **citizenship-by-investment (CBI) industry**. Known internationally as "Mr Passport." Per [[Source DCMS Final Report 2019]]: "**SCL certainly had meetings in Malta, that Christian Kalin of Henley & Partners was introduced by SCL to Joseph Muscat in 2011**, and that Christian Kalin met with both political parties before 2013."

## The Malta playbook

- 2011 -- Introduced to [[Joseph Muscat]] (then opposition Labour leader) by [[SCL Group]]
- Met with both Maltese political parties before 2013 election
- Labour won 2013 election
- **Immediately after election: Henley & Partners ran Maltas Individual Investor Programme** (CBI sales of Maltese/EU citizenship to wealthy individuals)
- The Maltese CBI program was the most controversial in the EU; eventually EU pressure forced changes
- Pattern of "SCL introduction → favorable politician wins → CBI contract" became known as a Henley & Partners template

## CBI industry pioneer

Kalin pioneered the modern framing of citizenship as a **tradeable asset for high-net-worth individuals globally**. Henley & Partners operates programs in dozens of countries selling residency and citizenship. Critics argue CBI programs:
- Enable money laundering (programs typically have weak due diligence)
- Facilitate tax evasion
- Provide escape routes for sanctioned individuals and oligarchs
- Undermine sovereignty (governments selling citizenship)

## Investigations and controversies

- 2017+ -- multiple media investigations into Henley & Partners due diligence practices
- 2020 -- leaked documents showed Henley & Partners helped Russian oligarchs obtain second passports
- 2022 -- EU Commission criticism of Maltese CBI
- 2023 -- Henley & Partners pushed into new African markets
