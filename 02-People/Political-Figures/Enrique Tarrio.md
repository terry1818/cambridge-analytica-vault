# Enrique Tarrio

National Chairman of the [[Proud Boys]] 2018-2021. Cuban-American. Convicted of seditious conspiracy for January 6 conduct.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- May 4, 2023: Federal jury, DC: GUILTY of seditious conspiracy, conspiracy to obstruct an official proceeding, obstruction, destruction of government property
- September 5, 2023: Sentenced to 22 years - longest sentence handed down for J6 conduct
- Tarrio was not physically present at the Capitol on January 6 - he had been arrested January 4, 2021 in DC on an unrelated burning-of-BLM-banner charge and ordered to leave the city
- The trial established he directed Proud Boys movements via Telegram while away from the scene

## Pre-J6 history

- Worked for the FBI as a "prolific" informant 2012-2014 per court documents revealed during 2021 sentencing for an unrelated fraud case (Reuters reporting, January 2021)
- Tarrio and the FBI dispute the characterization; his lawyer said he was a cooperator, not a source

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Trump issued blanket J6 pardons. Tarrio released from federal prison
