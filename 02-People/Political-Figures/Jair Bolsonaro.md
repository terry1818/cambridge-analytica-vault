# Jair Bolsonaro

President of Brazil 2019-2022. Brazilian far-right politician. Banned from running for office until 2030 (June 2023, Brazilian Superior Electoral Court).

## January 8, 2023 attack on Brazilian institutions

- Three-Powers Square (Congress, Supreme Court, Presidential Palace) attacked by Bolsonaro supporters
- Direct parallel to [[January 6 Capitol Attack]] - widely reported as "Brazil's January 6"
- [[Steve Bannon]] documented coordination with Bolsonaro figures pre-attack
- Bolsonaro was in Florida during the attack, having traveled there December 30, 2022

- Bannon's "The War Room" supported Bolsonaro through both 2018 and 2022 campaigns
