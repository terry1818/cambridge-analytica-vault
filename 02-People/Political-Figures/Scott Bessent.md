# Scott Bessent

## Summary

American hedge fund manager; **Trump 2.0 Treasury Secretary** (confirmed January 2025). Founder of **Key Square Group** hedge fund. Former **Chief Investment Officer of Soros Fund Management** (2011-2015) under George Soros -- notable given Trump's frequent demonization of Soros. **First openly gay Treasury Secretary in US history.** Major Trump 2024 donor and economic advisor during the campaign.

## Treasury Secretary role

- Confirmed January 27, 2025
- Oversees Treasury Department including:
  - Fiscal policy
  - IRS (with substantial restructuring under Trump 2.0)
  - International economic policy
  - Tariff implementation coordination
  - Crypto regulation (with [[David Sacks]] as AI/Crypto Czar)

## Why notable

Bessent represents the **traditional finance bridge** to the Trump 2.0 economic team:
- Provides legitimacy to traditional Wall Street
- Coordinates between Tech Right priorities and traditional banking
- Manages tariff implementation alongside Lutnick
- Less ideological than other Trump 2.0 figures
