# Charlie Kirk

## Summary

**Founder of [[Turning Point USA]]** at age 18 (2012). Built TPUSA into the **most operationally consequential conservative youth-mobilization organization in the US** with 3,000+ campus chapters. **Council for National Policy member**. Bridge figure between the conservative establishment (CNP, Heritage) and the energized Trump base. **Assassinated September 10, 2025** at a TPUSA event at Utah Valley University (see [[Assassination of Charlie Kirk 2025-09-10]]).

## Key facts

- **Born:** 1993-10-14, Arlington Heights IL
- **Died:** 2025-09-10, Utah Valley University, age 31
- **Founded:** Turning Point USA, 2012 (age 18)
- **Host:** Charlie Kirk Show (Salem Radio + podcast)
- **Affiliations:** Turning Point USA, CNP

## Operational accomplishments

- Built TPUSA to 3,000+ campus chapters
- AmericaFest annual conference (~20K attendees)
- Multi-million social media following
- Critical role in Trump 2024 youth-vote field operations
- Bridge to NAR via Turning Point Faith expansion

## Theological evolution

- Founded TPUSA as libertarian-conservative
- **Increasingly Christian-nationalist** in last 5 years
- Frequent Lance Wallnau-style framing
- Father of two; positioned as Christian-family-man

## Death + aftermath

See [[Assassination of Charlie Kirk 2025-09-10]] for event details.

- Stephen Miller at memorial: "We are on the side of God!"
- Erika Kirk (widow) emerged as public-facing TPUSA figure
- Coalition mobilization framing: Christian-martyr
