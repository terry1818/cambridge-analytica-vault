# Brad Raffensperger

Born May 18, 1955, Pittsburgh, PA (later Georgia resident). Republican politician. Member of Georgia House of Representatives 2015-2019. **Georgia Secretary of State** January 14, 2019 - present. Refused [[Donald Trump]]'s January 2, 2021 demand to "find 11,780 votes" in a recorded phone call that became foundational evidence in [[Georgia v Trump RICO 2023]] and [[US v Trump DC Federal Election Interference Case]].

## January 2, 2021 phone call (Tier 1)

Trump called Raffensperger January 2, 2021. The call lasted over an hour and was recorded. Full transcript available at [[Source: FullText Trump Raffensperger call 2021-01-02]].

Key Trump statements:
- "I just want to find 11,780 votes, which is one more than we have, because we won the state"
- "So what are we going to do here folks? I only need 11,000 votes. Fellas, I need 11,000 votes. Give me a break"
- Repeated claim that he won Georgia
- Stated awareness of potential criminal exposure for not investigating fraud claims

Raffensperger and his General Counsel Ryan Germany:
- Calmly and repeatedly explained why each fraud claim was unfounded
- Cited specific evidence including security camera footage showing no "suitcases" of ballots (the [[Ruby Freeman]] / [[Shaye Moss]] false claim)
- Refused to take any action to alter the certified results

## Other Trump pressure (Tier 1)

- November-December 2020: Trump repeatedly attacked Raffensperger publicly
- Trump-aligned figures (including [[Lindsey Graham]]) called Raffensperger separately to discuss whether absentee ballots could be discarded
- Trump and Mark Meadows pressed Georgia officials including Raffensperger's office repeatedly
- Death threats against Raffensperger and his wife required FBI involvement

## J6 Committee testimony (June 21, 2022)

Testified publicly in the J6 Select Committee hearing on pressure on state officials. Established:
- The Trump call's content
- The Trump campaign's awareness that fraud claims lacked merit
- The decision to certify Georgia for Biden despite pressure

## Georgia RICO case witness

Raffensperger is a key witness in [[Georgia v Trump RICO 2023]]. The Trump call is a predicate act in the indictment.

## Re-election

Won 2022 Republican primary despite Trump backing his opponent Jody Hice; won general election; remains Georgia Secretary of State.
