# Carlos De Oliveira

Property manager of [[Mar-a-Lago]]. **Co-defendant in [[US v Trump Mar-a-Lago Documents Case]] as named in the July 27, 2023 superseding indictment** â€” charged with obstruction of justice related to a documented attempt to direct the deletion of Mar-a-Lago surveillance camera footage that captured [[Walt Nauta]] moving boxes of classified documents.

## Specific allegations (Tier 1, per superseding indictment)

Per the superseding indictment:
- De Oliveira had conversations with the Mar-a-Lago IT director about the surveillance camera footage
- De Oliveira asked the IT director: "the boss [Trump] wanted the server [footage] deleted"
- The IT director responded he did not believe he had the rights to do so
- De Oliveira lied to FBI investigators about his role

The conversations with the IT director are documented in cooperating-witness testimony; the IT director cooperated with the investigation.

## Charges

- Conspiracy to obstruct justice
- Altering, destroying, mutilating, or concealing an object
- Corruptly concealing a document or record
- False statements

## Status

Case dismissed by Judge [[Aileen Cannon]] July 15, 2024 along with Trump and Nauta. DOJ appeal withdrawn November 2024.
