# Kelly Meggs

Florida [[Oath Keepers]] chapter leader. Convicted of seditious conspiracy.

## Convictions (Tier 1)

- November 29, 2022: GUILTY of seditious conspiracy alongside [[Stewart Rhodes]]
- May 25, 2023: Sentenced to 12 years
- Trial established Meggs led the "Stack 1" Oath Keepers formation that breached the Capitol East Rotunda doors on January 6

## Pre-J6 communications

- December 19, 2020 Signal message (admitted at trial): "He called us all to DC and made it clear we need to be there" - referring to Trump's tweet calling for "wild" protests
- December 22, 2020: "We have orchestrated a plan with the Proud Boys" - establishing inter-militia coordination

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Released
