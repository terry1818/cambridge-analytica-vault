# Prince Andrew

Born February 19, 1960, Buckingham Palace, London. Second son of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip. Brother of King Charles III. Bears the title Duke of York. Named by [[Virginia Giuffre]] in sworn testimony in Giuffre v. Maxwell as a participant in three separate sexual encounters during 2001-2002, when Giuffre was 17. Civilly settled with Giuffre in February 2022 for an undisclosed sum. Never criminally charged.
