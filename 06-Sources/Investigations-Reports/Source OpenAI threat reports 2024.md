# Source: OpenAI threat reports 2024

## Citation

OpenAI. (2024-present). Quarterly Threat Intelligence Reports.

## What these reports document

Since 2024, OpenAI has published **quarterly threat intelligence reports** documenting identified use of OpenAI products (ChatGPT, DALL-E, GPT API) in influence operations. The reports identify:

- Specific state-linked operations using OpenAI products
- Examples include Russian, Chinese, Iranian, and commercial operators
- Use cases: content generation, translation, persona development, code generation for ops infrastructure
- Disrupted accounts and operations
- Aggregate volume and pattern metrics

## Significance

OpenAIs threat reporting is **the first significant public accounting** of LLM use in influence operations from a foundation-model provider.
