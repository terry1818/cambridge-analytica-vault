# Source Reuters Oracle TikTok Project Texas

## Citation

> Reuters staff. (2025-12-18). *TikTok signs deal to give US operations to Oracle-led investor group*. Reuters — corroborated by NPR (2025-12-18) and subsequent Reuters/financial coverage of the January 2026 close.

## What it is

Reuters reporting tracing [[Oracle Corporation]]'s involvement with [[TikTok]], from the 2022 **Project Texas** data-localization initiative through the December 2025 binding agreement that placed TikTok's US operations under an Oracle-led investor consortium. Project Texas was TikTok's multi-billion-dollar plan to store US user data on Oracle-run American servers and have Oracle audit its recommendation algorithm.

## Key claims / findings

- **Project Texas** began in 2022: a data-security arrangement under which TikTok routed US user data to Oracle Cloud infrastructure inside the United States.
- On **December 18, 2025**, TikTok signed binding agreements selling its US unit to an investor-led venture, named **TikTok USDS Joint Venture LLC**.
- Ownership: a new investor consortium holds **50%** — with **Oracle, Silver Lake, and MGX each holding 15%** — existing ByteDance investors hold ~30.1%, and ByteDance retains a 19.9% stake.
- US user data is to be stored domestically in **Oracle-run systems**; the recommendation algorithm is to be retrained on US data only.
- The joint venture **closed January 22, 2026**, one day before a Trump-imposed deadline.

## Notable quotes

> The company plans to retrain its recommendation algorithm "to ensure the content feed is free from outside manipulation." — paraphrased from deal disclosures
