## Documented inflated valuations (key trial findings)

### Mar-a-Lago

- Trump SFC valued: $612M-$739M (treating as residential development potential)
- Trial finding: actual fair market value $18-28M (treating as a club with deed restrictions)
- Inflation factor: 25-40x

### Trump Tower Triplex apartment

- Trump SFC valued: $327M based on **30,000 sq ft** stated square footage
- Actual square footage: approximately **10,996 sq ft**
- Inflation factor: ~3x square footage, ~3x value (~$200M+ overstatement)

### 40 Wall Street

- Inflated based on undisclosed lender-imposed restrictions on use

### Trump golf clubs

- Valued based on hypothetical development of homes that legal restrictions prohibited

### Seven Springs estate

- Valued at $291M based on assumed development of mansions that lacked permits

## Engoron summary judgment (September 26, 2023)

Engoron granted partial summary judgment on Cause of Action 1 (persistent fraud) **before trial began**, finding:

> "When the Defendants conjured for the Statements of Financial Condition fanciful values for Mar-a-Lago, 40 Wall Street, the Triplex and other properties...they were creating a false reality, a fantasy world."

The pre-trial summary judgment substantially defined the legal framework â€” the trial focused on causes 2-7 and on damages.

## Trial verdict (February 16, 2024) â€” key findings

Engoron's 92-page decision:

> "Defendants' refusal to admit error indicates that they are likely to continue their fraudulent ways unless judicially restrained. Donald Trump, Donald Trump, Jr., Eric Trump, Allen Weisselberg, and Jeffrey McConney are unrepentant fraudsters who are not chastened by the wide-ranging publicity surrounding this case."

> "Their complete lack of contrition and remorse borders on pathological."

On Trump specifically:

> "[Donald Trump] knew exactly what was happening. He testified to that effect when he stated he was familiar with each of his properties, their location, and approximate fair market value... He was kept apprised of the SFC's contents and the financial activities of the Trump Organization."

## Disgorgement

- $354,868,768 in disgorgement (reduced from prosecution-requested amount)
- Trump personal liability: $354,868,768
- Donald Trump Jr: $4,013,024
- Eric Trump: $4,013,024
- Allen Weisselberg: $1,000,000

Plus pre-judgment interest at 9% per annum from various dates (totals approximately **$454M+ with interest** as of judgment).

## Additional remedies (stayed pending appeal)

- Trump barred from serving as officer or director of any NY corporation for 3 years
- Donald Trump Jr and Eric Trump barred for 2 years each
- Trump barred from applying for loans from NY-chartered financial institutions for 3 years
- Independent monitor (Barbara Jones) continued; independent compliance director added
- Trump Organization Inc. ordered dissolved (stayed by appellate court)
