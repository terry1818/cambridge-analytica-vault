## Vault context

"Bad Faith" (2024) is a documentary film by Stephen Ujlaki and Christopher Jacob Jones that traces the rise of Christian Nationalism in US politics from the 1970s ascendancy of [[Jerry Falwell Sr]] and the Moral Majority through the contemporary [[New Apostolic Reformation]] / [[Project 2025]] convergence. The film uses interview material with former insiders, archival footage, and contemporary expert commentary to construct a continuity narrative across five decades.

The vault references the film as a documentary-tier source for the [[Religious Right]] historical arc. Specific vault uses: archival material on the founding of [[Council for National Policy]]; on-camera interviews with movement insiders including [[Russell Vought]] (in earlier footage from his pre-OMB career); contemporary commentary on the connection between the [[Heritage Foundation]] / [[Project 2025]] policy programme and the underlying theological movement.

For paired sources see [[The Family Netflix 2019]], [[Source Sharlet 2008 The Family Book]], [[Anne Nelson Shadow Network 2019]], and the academic-tier [[Taking America Back for God]].
