## Vault context

This Wikipedia entry documents the [[Federalist Society]] for Law and Public Policy Studies, founded 1982 by Steven Calabresi, David McIntosh, and Lee Liberman Otis as a network of conservative and libertarian law students. The vault treats the Federalist Society as one of the three load-bearing institutional pillars of contemporary US conservative power, alongside [[Heritage Foundation]] (policy) and [[Council for National Policy]] (coordination). The Federalist Society supplies the judicial machinery that implements and durably entrenches the policy and coordination outputs of the other two pillars.

The current US Supreme Court includes five Federalist Society-affiliated justices (Roberts, Alito, Gorsuch, Kavanaugh, Barrett). The federal circuit and district courts include hundreds of additional Federalist Society-affiliated judges.
