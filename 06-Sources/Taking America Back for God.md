## Vault context

"Taking America Back for God: Christian Nationalism in the United States" (Andrew Whitehead and Samuel Perry, Oxford University Press 2020) is a sociological study of Christian Nationalism as a measurable belief constellation in the US population. The book introduces the Christian Nationalism Scale (CNS) that quantifies belief in the desirability of an explicitly Christian American national identity, and uses survey data to show how CNS scores correlate with positions on race, immigration, gender, and political authority.

The vault references this book as part of the academic-tier evidence base for the [[New Apostolic Reformation]] / [[Dominion Theology]] / [[Christian Reconstructionism]] cluster. It is one of the few studies that distinguishes empirically between traditional evangelical Protestantism and Christian Nationalism as a politically distinct identity.
