# Source: Giuffre v Maxwell 2024 Unsealing

**Court**: US District Court for the Southern District of New York, **Giuffre v. Maxwell**, Case No. 15-cv-07433 (Sweet, J. originally; Preska, J. later)

**Unsealing order**: December 18, 2023 by Judge [[Loretta Preska]]
**Released**: January 3-5, 2024 in three tranches
**Approximate page count**: 943 pages of formerly-sealed exhibits and depositions
**Underlying case**: 2015 defamation suit by [[Virginia Giuffre]] against [[Ghislaine Maxwell]]; settled 2017

## What it actually IS â€” and what it is NOT

**It IS**: exhibits and deposition transcripts from civil litigation. The documents include sworn testimony under oath about the Epstein operation, primarily from victims and from people in Epstein's orbit.

**It is NOT a "client list"**: no document titled or functioning as an Epstein client list exists in the public record. Social-media discourse around the unsealing routinely conflated names being mentioned in the documents with allegations against those persons. These are categorically different evidentiary claims.

## The Stephen Hawking email (illustrative case)

A widely-cited document from the unsealing is a Maxwell-Epstein email exchange in which Maxwell speculates about asking [[Stephen Hawking]] (who attended a scientific conference at Little St. James) about an alleged orgy. This document:

- Establishes Maxwell's awareness of the allegations
- Establishes Hawking's attendance at a scientific conference at the property
- Establishes NOTHING about whether the speculated orgy occurred or about whether Hawking was a participant in anything inappropriate
- Was widely misreported in social media as evidence of Hawking misconduct

This case is the canonical example of how unsealing-document mentions get conflated with allegations. The vault treats Hawking as: "named in one Maxwell email; no allegation against him."

## Sjoberg-Trump deposition passage (illustrative)

The Sjoberg deposition includes a passage where she recounts Epstein offering to take her to meet Trump at Atlantic City. Reading the actual passage:
- Establishes that Epstein had access to introduce Sjoberg to Trump
- Does NOT establish any sexual contact between Sjoberg and Trump
- Does NOT establish Trump knew of trafficking

Was widely mischaracterized in 2024 social media as Trump sworn allegation. It is not.

## What the unsealing actually established

- Confirmation and detail of allegations already in the public record
- Documentary evidence of Epstein operation's breadth
- Sworn testimony from multiple victims and Epstein-orbit witnesses
- A baseline factual record going forward

## What the unsealing did NOT establish

- A "client list"
- New criminal liability against any named figure
- Adjudication of contested claims (just exhibits, not verdicts)

## Subsequent treatment

- 2024 media cycle widely covered the unsealing with varying degrees of evidence discipline
- No new criminal cases against named figures were initiated based on the unsealing
- The DOJ Statement on the Epstein Files (July 2025, Trump 2.0 era) characterized the underlying investigation as closed
