# Williams, Daniel K. God's Own Party: The Making of the Christian Right (2010)

## Publication

- Oxford University Press, 2010
- ~358 pages
- Academic history of the Religious right from 1940s-2000s

## Author standing

Daniel K. Williams is an academic historian (University of West Georgia) specializing in 20th-century US religion and politics.

## Significance for vault

God's Own Party is one of the standard academic histories of the [[Religious right]]'s political formation. Provides documented institutional history complementing more recent journalistic and movement accounts (Sharlet, Stewart, Posner). Useful for the long-arc framing of the [[Demographic Defensiveness Thesis]].
