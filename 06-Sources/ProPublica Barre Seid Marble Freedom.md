## Vault context

This source documents the [[Marble Freedom Trust]] / Barre Seid / [[Leonard Leo]] transaction that constructed what is reportedly the largest single conservative-movement donor vehicle in US history. The 2020-2021 transaction structure: Barre Seid donated his company Tripp Lite to Marble Freedom Trust (a 501(c)(4) vehicle) in 2020; the subsequent sale of Tripp Lite to Eaton Corporation in 2021 produced approximately $1.6 billion in proceeds for Marble Freedom Trust. The (c)(4) recipient structure avoided substantial capital-gains tax liability.

Leo's operational control over Marble Freedom Trust gives him discretionary deployment authority over the largest single conservative-movement donor pool. Subsequent disbursements have flowed to [[Concord Fund]] (formerly Judicial Crisis Network), [[The 85 Fund]] (formerly Judicial Education Project), [[CRC Advisors]] umbrella entities, and the broader conservative-legal infrastructure complementing the [[Federalist Society]] proper.
