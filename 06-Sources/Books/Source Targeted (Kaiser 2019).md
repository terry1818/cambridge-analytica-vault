# Source: Targeted (Kaiser 2019)

## Citation

Kaiser, Brittany. (2019). *Targeted: The Cambridge Analytica Whistleblowers Inside Story of How Big Data, Trump, and Facebook Broke Democracy and How It Can Happen Again*. New York: HarperCollins.

## Structure / coverage

Kaiser's account differs from Wylies by **focusing on the business-development side**: client pitches, contract negotiations, country-specific operations, the international scope of CAs work. Where Wylie was Director of Research (methodology, data), Kaiser was Director of Business Development (clients, pitches, money).

## Key disclosures unique to this account

- **Leave.EU work specifics** which Kaiser confirmed despite CAs denials (ICO ultimately found "no significant breach" but Kaisers account documents the work that did occur)
- **[[Bongbong Marcos]] pitch** — the Marcos family approach to CA for image rebrand
- Multiple international pitch meetings (Africa, Asia, Latin America)
- Internal disputes about taking on questionable clients
- Her transition from operator to whistleblower (slower than Wylies)
- The provenance of the [[HindsightFiles]] archive she would later release
- Specific Mercer-Bannon-Nix internal dynamics from a business-development vantage

## Complementarity with Wylie

- **Wylies account is methodology + Bannon + strategic vision**
- **Kaisers account is client list + international scope + Leave.EU detail**
- Both should be cross-referenced for any specific factual claim
- Together they constitute the **richest public window** into CA from inside the firm

## Significance

Second major insider account; gives a different angle from Wylies. Combined the two memoirs provide the most comprehensive documentary record of CA from the inside. Both volumes were referenced extensively in [[Source DCMS Final Report 2019]] and subsequent academic / journalistic work.

## Subsequent activity

- 2020-01-04 — Kaiser began releasing [[HindsightFiles]] (100K+ documents)
- 2020+ — Founded **Own Your Data Foundation**
- Continuing advocacy on data rights and AI ethics
