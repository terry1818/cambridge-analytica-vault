# Source — Money, Lies, and God: Inside the Movement to Destroy American Democracy (Katherine Stewart, 2025)

## Bibliographic
Katherine Stewart; *Money, Lies, and God: Inside the Movement to Destroy American Democracy*; Bloomsbury Publishing; 2025. NYT Bestseller; Foreign Affairs Best Books of 2025.

## Thesis & scope
Stewart argues that the various strands of the American right — Christian nationalists, Catholic integralists, anti-vaxx conspiracy networks, militia movements, dark-money funders, authoritarian intellectuals, and hard-right political operatives — constitute a single coordinated movement with the explicit goal of dismantling liberal democratic governance in the United States. The book builds on *The Power Worshippers* (2020) but extends the scope to include Catholic conservative infrastructure (Opus Dei, pronatalist networks) and the post-2020 radicalization of the movement, including its capture of key institutions and the emergence of a neo-fascist intellectual current.

## Key content relevant to the vault
- Documents **[[Tom Monaghan]]** (Domino's founder) as a major funder of **Opus Dei** operations in the US and of Catholic hard-right institution-building — including founding **Ave Maria University** in Florida as a theocratic educational project. This represents the Catholic wing of the patron capital story the vault covers primarily through evangelical channels.
- Profiles **Opus Dei** as a secretive Catholic organization founded in fascist Spain that has become structurally integrated into the US theocratic-wing coalition — bridging Catholic integralism and evangelical Christian nationalism.
- Documents **[[Michael Flynn]]**, **[[John Eastman]]**, and **[[Roger Stone]]** as nodes of a "well-paid apparatus of political and juridical reaction," showing their continued organizational roles post-January 6.
- Reports on **pronatalist networks** — explicitly racialized organizations preoccupied with white birth rates — as a newly institutionalized wing of the hard right with billionaire backing. Connects to anti-feminism and "gynocracy" ideological threads.
- Covers **anti-vaxx internet provocateur** networks as a radicalization-to-action pipeline feeding the broader anti-democracy movement — links RFK Jr. / CHD orbit to the theocratic coalition.
- Documents conference networks where **conspiracy-theory entrepreneurs, militia members, and dark-money-funded political operatives share platforms and strategy**, demonstrating operational integration of what appear to be separate movements.
- Extends analysis of **Koch network** and **billionaire funder** infrastructure, documenting how atheist libertarian funders (Koch ecosystem) coordinate with Opus Dei Catholics and NAR evangelicals around anti-democratic governance goals despite theological incompatibility.
- Profiles **neo-fascist intellectual networks** including "pseudo-Platonist" thinkers providing academic legitimacy to authoritarianism — likely covering figures in the Claremont / integralist / NatCon orbit.
- Documents the **Ave Maria School of Law** and similar Catholic hard-right educational institutions as judicial pipeline infrastructure parallel to the Federalist Society.
