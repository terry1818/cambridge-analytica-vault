## Vault context

The [[Family Research Council]] (FRC) is a US-based conservative Christian advocacy organisation, founded in 1981 (as part of [[Focus on the Family]], later spun out as an independent 501c3 in 1992). It is one of the principal Religious-Right policy and political-advocacy vehicles tracked by the vault, sitting in the convergence of the [[Council for National Policy]] membership, the [[The Fellowship]]-adjacent prayer-network ecosystem, and the [[Faith and Freedom Coalition]] grassroots organising base.

FRC's documented activity includes opposition to LGBTQ-rights legislation (which the [[Source SPLC FRC Hate Group]] designation is based on), opposition to abortion-rights legislation, advocacy on judicial appointments via coordination with the [[Federalist Society]] pipeline, and direct lobbying via its affiliated [[FRC Action]] 501c4 vehicle.

Leadership has included Gary Bauer (1988-1999), Ken Connor (2000-2003), and [[Tony Perkins]] (2003-present, also a [[Council for National Policy]] member).
