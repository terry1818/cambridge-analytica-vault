## Vault context

The [[State Policy Network]] (SPN) is a US network of state-level free-market think tanks, founded in 1992 with seed funding from the Coors family and subsequent funding from the [[Koch Network]] and the broader conservative-donor architecture. As of 2025-2026 it includes ~65 affiliated organisations across 50 states plus DC.

SPN's operational role in the vault is as the state-level analogue to [[Heritage Foundation]]: it supplies model legislation, policy research, and personnel pipelines into state legislatures, governorships, and state-level regulatory bodies. Where Heritage operates at the federal level (and via [[Project 2025]] now at the executive-branch level), SPN operates at the state level on a coordinated programme that the [[ALEC]] (American Legislative Exchange Council) coordinates from the legislative-drafting side.
