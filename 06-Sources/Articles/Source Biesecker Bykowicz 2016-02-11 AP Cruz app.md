# Source: Biesecker Bykowicz 2016-02-11 AP Cruz app

## Citation

Biesecker, Michael; Bykowicz, Julie. (2016-02-11). "Cruz app data collection helps campaign read minds of voters." *Associated Press*.

## What this article established

AP investigation of the **"Cruz Crew" mobile app** used by the Cruz 2016 presidential campaign. The app:

- Tracked supporter physical movements
- Accessed contact lists
- Per AP, "invaded personal data more than previous presidential campaign apps"
- Was a CA-affiliated tool for in-field voter contact

## Significance

The Cruz Crew app exemplifies how CAs methodology extended beyond Facebook-derived data into **direct mobile-app collection of supporter behavioral data**. The AP piece is one of the earliest mainstream documentations of campaign-side mobile-app intrusiveness â€” a methodology that subsequent campaigns have widely adopted.
