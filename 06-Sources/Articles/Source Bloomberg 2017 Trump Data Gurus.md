# Source: Bloomberg 2017 Trump Data Gurus

## Citation

Bloomberg L.P. (2017-04-03). "Trump Data Gurus Leave Long Trail of Subterfuge, Dubious Dealing."

## What this article established

Bloomberg investigation published ~10 months after Trumps election win and ~12 months before the global scandal break, documenting Cambridge Analyticas pattern of operational opacity and questionable client engagements. Established several facts that became central in 2018:

- CAs international operations beyond US elections
- Methodological gaps between marketing claims and operational reality
- Personnel rotation across multiple SCL-family corporate entities
- Earlier-than-2018 awareness in mainstream business press of CA-related concerns

## Significance

This piece is one of several mid-2016 to early-2017 mainstream business-press investigations that **could have triggered the scandal earlier**. The fact that it didnt — that the story still required the March 2018 Wylie disclosures + Channel 4 sting + multi-newsroom coordination to break — illustrates the difficulty of converting documented concerns into actionable public pressure.
