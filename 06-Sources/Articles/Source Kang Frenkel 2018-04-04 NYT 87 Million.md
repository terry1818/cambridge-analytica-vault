# Source: Kang Frenkel 2018-04-04 NYT 87 Million

## Citation

Kang, Cecilia; Frenkel, Sheera. (2018-04-04). "Facebook Says Cambridge Analytica Harvested Data of Up to 87 Million Users." *The New York Times*.

## What this article established

Facebook's formal **upward revision of the harvested-profile count** from 50M (the initial figure in Cadwalladr's March 17 piece) to **up to 87M**. The revision was based on Facebook's own internal audit triggered by the scandal.

Geographic breakdown disclosed:
- **70.6M** US users
- **6.7M** California
- **5.6M** Texas
- **4.3M** Florida
- **310K+** Australia
- **562K** India
- **1M+** Philippines

## Significance

Established the **definitive scope** of the data harvest from Facebook's own accounting. Confirmed that CA's initial claim of "30M profiles" was a significant under-count. Set the stage for the FTC's enforcement action and the eventual $5B fine.
