- [[Alexander Nix]]s Eton education and family banking background
- The presence of multiple Old Etonians in SCL/CA senior roles
- [[Lord Ivar Mountbatten]]s SCL directorship

- The mismatch between CAs aristocratic-establishment social positioning and its mercenary operational practice
