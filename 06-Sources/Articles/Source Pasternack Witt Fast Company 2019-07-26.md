## Citation

## What this article established

**The most important single source on the Cambridge Analytica afterlife.** Pasternack and Witt's investigation documented:

- **[[Emerdata Limited]] "fully acquired" SCL/CA assets** during bankruptcy proceedings
- Emerdata paid SCL companies' legal bills throughout 2018-2019 bankruptcy and investigation
- Emerdata paid millions to acquire what remained of the companies as they were liquidated
- Multiple ex-CA staff dispersed across [[Auspex International]], [[Data Propria]], [[Firecrest Technologies]], and other vehicles
- The Emerdata board structure (Mercers, Nix, [[Johnson Chun Shun Ko]] via FSG)
- The geographic dispersal of CA personnel across UK, US, Hong Kong
