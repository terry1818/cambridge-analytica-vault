# Source: Accesswire 2018-05-29 CloudCommerce launches

## Citation

CloudCommerce Inc. (2018-05-29). *CloudCommerce Launches Data Propria Inc., a New Data Analytics Division.* Press release via Accesswire. <https://www.accesswire.com/501773/CloudCommerce-Launches-Data-Propria-Inc-a-New-Data-Analytics-Division>.

## What it is

A **corporate press release** issued by **CloudCommerce Inc.** via the **Accesswire** PR-wire distribution service on **2018-05-29** — three days after the public effective date of [[Cambridge Analytica]]'s May 2018 insolvency. The release announces the launch of **[[Data Propria]] Inc.** as a "**new data analytics division**" of CloudCommerce. It is the **primary corporate-document evidence** that Data Propria was incorporated as a CloudCommerce subsidiary, providing **regulatory-disclosure-level documentation** of the corporate structure that subsequent journalistic reporting ([[Source Horwitz AP 2018-06-15 Data Propria]]) then unpacked.

## Key claims / findings

- **Corporate structure at launch:** [[Data Propria]] launched as a **wholly-owned subsidiary of CloudCommerce Inc.**, a publicly-traded US company
- **Effective date:** **2018-05-29** — within days of CA's May 2018 insolvency
- **Self-description:** Data Propria described as a "data analytics division" — generic framing that obscured the **personnel composition** (ex-CA staff) and **client focus** (US political work) that subsequent reporting documented
- **Personnel hint:** the release indicates **experienced data-analytics personnel** without naming specific ex-CA staff (Matt Oczkowski's role surfaced subsequently in AP reporting)
- **Operational targeting:** the release mentioned political and commercial analytics — the political-work emphasis became central to the AP follow-up story

## Reliability assessment

- **Type:** corporate press release distributed via paid PR wire (Accesswire)
- **Authoritative weight:** **medium** — the release is **factually accurate about the corporate structure** (verifiable via SEC filings and state incorporation records) but represents **CloudCommerce's preferred framing** of the launch
- **What it gets right:** corporate structure, launch date, parent-subsidiary relationship — all verifiable
- **What it omits:**
  - **Personnel composition** (ex-CA staff including Matt Oczkowski — surfaced in AP reporting weeks later)
  - **Client roster** (Trump 2020 campaign and RNC — documented in [[Source Horwitz AP 2018-06-15 Data Propria]])
  - **CA-methodology continuity** (substantive operational continuity with CA's prior work)
- **Disputed by:** the omitted context was filled in by subsequent investigative reporting; CloudCommerce did not publicly contest those follow-ups
- **Independent corroboration:** SEC filings (CloudCommerce was publicly traded), Delaware/state corporate registries, and follow-on journalism all confirm the structural facts
