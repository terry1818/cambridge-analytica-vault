# Source: Nelson 2018-03-21 Spectator Johnson Nix meeting

## Citation

Nelson, Fraser. (2018-03-21). "Boris Johnsons undisclosed meeting with Alexander Nix of Cambridge Analytica." *The Spectator*.

## What this article established

Spectator (right-of-center UK political magazine) confirmed that **[[Boris Johnson]] met [[Alexander Nix]] in 2016 while Johnson was UK Foreign Secretary**. The meeting had not been disclosed at the time and was not on Johnsons published ministerial diary in the form expected. Nelson confirmed:

- The Johnson-Nix meeting occurred in 2016
- The meeting was not properly disclosed in line with ministerial transparency expectations
- This was part of the broader FCO-CA engagement pattern documented by Philp (Times)

## Significance

The Spectator coverage carries particular weight given the magazines right-of-center political position (often Johnson-aligned). Nelsons confirmation of the Johnson-Nix meeting from that direction makes it harder to dismiss as left-press attack. The undisclosed nature of the meeting raised questions under UK Ministerial Code that were never formally pursued.
