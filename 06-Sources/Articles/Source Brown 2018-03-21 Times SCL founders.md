# Source: Brown 2018-03-21 Times SCL founders

## Citation

Brown, David. (2018-03-21). "SCL Groups founders were connected to royalty, the rich and powerful." *The Times*.

## What this article established

Times investigative piece on the **British aristocratic and political establishment network** around [[SCL Group]] from its 1990 founding. Documented:

- [[Nigel Oakes]]s relationship with Lady Helen Windsor (former girlfriend)
- The role of [[Sir Geoffrey Pattie]] as founding chairman (former Conservative defense minister)
- [[Lord Ivar Mountbatten]]s directorship
- Conservative donor network (Paul David Ashburner Nix, [[Jonathan Marland]], [[Roger Gabb]], [[Vincent Tchenguiz]])
- Old Etonian operational leadership

## Significance

Browns piece is **one of the best-documented accounts** of why SCL was able to operate for ~25 years as a defense contractor before the CA-side scandal exposed the methodology. The aristocratic-political network provided **social cover and access** that ordinary commercial firms would not have had. Pairs well with [[Source Knight New Yorker - Life Inside SCL]] for the social-anthropology view.
