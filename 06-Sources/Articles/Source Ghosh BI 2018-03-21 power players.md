# Source: Ghosh BI 2018-03-21 power players

## Citation

Ghosh, Shona. (2018-03-21). "The power players behind Cambridge Analytica have set up a mysterious new data company." *Business Insider*.

## What this article established

Four days after Wylie disclosures, Business Insider published the **first significant mainstream-press coverage of [[Emerdata Limited]]** as a CA-successor entity. Documented:

- Emerdatas August 2017 incorporation (predated CAs collapse by months)
- Director appointments including [[Rebekah Mercer]], Jennifer Mercer, [[Alexander Nix]], [[Alexander Tayler]]
- The Hong Kong connection via [[Johnson Chun Shun Ko]] (Frontier Services Group)
- The Erik Prince linkage via FSG
- Same London building as CA

## Significance

This is one of the **foundational sources** for the post-CA continuity story. Ghoshs Emerdata reporting predated the more comprehensive [[Source Pasternack Witt Fast Company 2019-07-26]] by ~16 months and established the basic facts that subsequent reporters built on. Without Emerdata documentation in March 2018, the narrative would have been "CA is dead" rather than "CA migrated."
