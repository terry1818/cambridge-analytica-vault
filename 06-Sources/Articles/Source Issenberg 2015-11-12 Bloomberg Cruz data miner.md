# Source: Issenberg 2015-11-12 Bloomberg Cruz data miner

## Citation

Issenberg, Sasha. (2015-11-12). "Cruz-Connected Data Miner Aims to Get Inside U.S. Voters Heads." *Bloomberg*.

## What this article established

Detailed profile of CAs methodology by **Sasha Issenberg** — the leading US journalist on data-driven campaigning (author of *The Victory Lab*). Issenberg documented:

- CAs psychographic targeting approach in detail
- The "5,000 data points on every adult" marketing claim
- The Cruz campaigns reliance on CA infrastructure
- The Mercer funding flow

## Significance

Issenbergs technical depth on data-driven campaigning made this one of the **most authoritative early profiles** of CAs methodology. Subsequently used as foundational reference by other journalists covering the scandal.

## Note on Issenberg

His expertise in data-driven campaigning gave the early CA reporting an analytical edge that simpler political-press coverage lacked. Issenberg has continued reporting on related issues through 2026.
