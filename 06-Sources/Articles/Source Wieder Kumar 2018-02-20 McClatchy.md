# Source: Wieder Kumar 2018-02-20 McClatchy

## Citation

Wieder, Ben; Kumar, Anita. (2018-02-20). "Bannon sold Cambridge Analytica stake, fined for late ethics report." *McClatchy*.

Reporting that [[Steve Bannon]] had divested his **$1-5 million stake in Cambridge Analytica in April 2017**, as required by his role as Trumps White House Chief Strategist (January-August 2017). McClatchy also reported Bannon was fined for filing a late ethics report. The piece:

- Confirmed Bannon's stake size
- Confirmed divestment date and reason
- Documented the ethics-filing failure
