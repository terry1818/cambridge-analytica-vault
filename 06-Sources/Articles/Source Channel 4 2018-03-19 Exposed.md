# Source: Channel 4 2018-03-19 Exposed

## Citation

Channel 4 News. (2018-03-19). "Exposed: Undercover secrets of Trump's data firm Cambridge Analytica." (Three-part broadcast series, March 19-20, 2018.)

## Significance

**Four-month undercover investigation** in which a reporter posed as a potential client seeking help for Sri Lankan candidates. [[Alexander Nix]] caught on hidden camera offering: honey traps, bribery stings, "beautiful Ukrainian girls", and Israeli intelligence (later confirmed as [[Psy-Group]]) for entrapment. Nix said CA "ran all of [Donald Trump's] digital campaign" and that communications were designed to be self-destructive and untraceable. Resulted in Nix's suspension and accelerated the investigation cascade. Footage available at Channel 4 News archive.
