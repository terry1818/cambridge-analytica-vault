# Source: 60 Minutes 2017-10-08 Stahl Parscale

## Citation

Stahl, Lesley (interviewer). (2017-10-08). Brad Parscale on Trump 2016 digital operation. *60 Minutes*, CBS News.

## What this interview established

Five months before the global CA scandal broke, [[Lesley Stahl]] interviewed [[Brad Parscale]] (Trump 2016 digital director) on *60 Minutes*. Parscale:

- Took credit for Trumps 2016 victory via Facebook-based microtargeting
- Discussed the Facebook embedded-employees support during the campaign
- **Downplayed [[Cambridge Analytica]]s role**, saying he thought "Cambridge Analyticas use of psychographics doesnt work"
- Was already distancing the campaign from CA before the scandal broke

## Significance

Parscales pre-scandal (October 2017) public denial of CAs effectiveness, then his repetition of the same denial during/after the 2018 scandal, made his testimony a key data point in the **disputed-effectiveness debate** around CAs psychographic targeting. The 60 Minutes interview is the **best pre-scandal video evidence** of the inside-campaign view that CAs role was modest.
