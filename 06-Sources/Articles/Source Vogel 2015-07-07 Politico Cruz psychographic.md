# Source: Vogel 2015-07-07 Politico Cruz psychographic

## Citation

Vogel, Kenneth P. (2015-07-07). "Cruz partners with donors psychographic firm." *Politico*.

## What this article established

**Among the earliest mainstream US political-press reporting** on the Cruz campaigns engagement with Cambridge Analytica. Vogel established:

- The Ted Cruz - Cambridge Analytica relationship
- The Mercer family role as common funder of both Cruz and CA
- The "psychographic" framing CA was using publicly
- The cost structure of CAs services to Cruz

## Significance

Predates [[Source Davies 2015-12-11 Guardian]] by 5 months. Together with the Davies piece, establishes that **the CA-Cruz-Mercer connection was public US political knowledge by mid-2015**. The lack of regulatory or institutional response between 2015 and 2018 is a feature of the scandal that deserves more attention than it has received.
