## Vault context

The SPLC's hate-group designation of the [[Family Research Council]] (effective 2010, sustained since) is one of the principal third-party assessments of FRC's anti-LGBTQ advocacy posture. SPLC's designation criteria focus on documented propagation of demonstrably false claims about LGBTQ individuals as a class; the FRC designation has been contested by FRC and its allies and has been the subject of subsequent litigation.

The vault references this document because (a) it is the most-cited third-party source on FRC's documented advocacy record, (b) the controversy around the designation is itself part of the [[Council for National Policy]] / [[Religious Right]] political map, and (c) the designation has been used by both supporters and critics of FRC as a reference point in subsequent political discourse.
