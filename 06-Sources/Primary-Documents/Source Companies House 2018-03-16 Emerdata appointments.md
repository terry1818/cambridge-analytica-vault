# Source: Companies House 2018-03-16 Emerdata appointments

## Citation

Companies House. (2018-03-16 and adjacent filings). *EMERDATA LIMITED — Officers and Appointments.* gov.uk Companies House register, company number 10911848. <https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/10911848>.

## What it is

**Primary-document evidence** from the UK Companies House register documenting [[Emerdata Limited]]'s incorporation and the **timing and personnel of director appointments** during the period immediately surrounding the 2018 Cambridge Analytica scandal. The filings are the single cleanest piece of evidence for [[Hypothesis - Successor entities preserve operational capability]] — they show that the **successor structure was being established before CA's public collapse**, not in response to it.

## Key claims / findings

- **Incorporation:** Emerdata Limited incorporated **2017-08-11** in the UK — six months before the [[Channel 4 Sting 2018]] and [[2018 Wylie Disclosures]]
- **Initial directors:** include [[Alexander Nix]], [[Alexander Tayler]] (CDO of CA), [[Julian Wheatland]] (SCL Group chairman), and members of the Mercer family
- **March 2018 appointments around the scandal:**
  - **2018-03-23:** [[Rebekah Mercer]] and **Jennifer Mercer** appointed directors
  - **2018-03-28:** [[Alexander Tayler]] appointed director — **eight days after [[Alexander Nix]]'s suspension** on 2018-03-20 following the Channel 4 sting
  - **2018-03-26:** **[[Johnson Chun Shun Ko]]** appointed — bringing the [[Frontier Services Group]] / [[Erik Prince]] linkage into the successor structure
- **January 2018:** Emerdata raised approximately **$19M** in funding shortly before the scandal broke ([[Source Wikipedia - SCL Group]] reports this)
- **Asset acquisition:** Subsequent filings (2018-2019) document Emerdata's "full acquisition" of SCL/CA assets through the bankruptcy proceedings — see [[Source Pasternack Witt Fast Company 2019-07-26]]

## Significance of the timing

The Companies House timeline is **load-bearing evidence**:

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| 2017-08-11 | Emerdata incorporated |
| 2018-01 | Emerdata raises ~$19M |
| 2018-03-16 | Companies House filings updated showing director slate forming |
| **2018-03-19** | Channel 4 sting broadcasts |
| **2018-03-20** | Alexander Nix suspended |
| 2018-03-23 | Rebekah Mercer + Jennifer Mercer appointed directors |
| 2018-03-26 | Johnson Chun Shun Ko appointed |
| 2018-03-28 | Alexander Tayler appointed director |
| **2018-05** | CA enters insolvency |
| 2018-2019 | Emerdata "fully acquires" CA/SCL assets |

The director appointments in the **eight days following Nix's suspension** are inconsistent with an emergency-response framing. Combined with the August 2017 incorporation and January 2018 funding round, the Companies House record supports the reading that the successor structure was **pre-planned**, not improvised after the scandal.
