# Source: FEC — Cambridge Analytica Records

## Citation

US Federal Election Commission. Disbursement records to Cambridge Analytica LLC and related entities (2014-2018).

## Why this is foundational

FEC disbursement data shows exactly **which US political committees paid Cambridge Analytica, when, and how much**. The data is fully public and downloadable.

## Key documented payments

### 2016 Republican primary
- **Cruz for President**: ~$5.8 million total per FEC filings
- **Carson America**: ~$220,000

### 2016 general election
- **Donald J. Trump for President Inc.**: payments September, October, November 2016 per FEC filings (PolitiFact analysis)
- **Make America Number 1 (Super PAC, Mercer-funded)**: substantial payments
- **John Bolton Super PAC**: 2014 work

### Other payments
- Multiple downballot races (Tillis NC, Cotton AR, Bolton-aligned)
- 44 US races claimed by CA in 2014 midterms

## How to use

For any US political campaign or PAC that appears in this vault, **search the FEC disbursement database**:

https://www.fec.gov/data/disbursements/?recipient_name=cambridge+analytica

Returns: every payment, date, amount, payer, purpose code.
