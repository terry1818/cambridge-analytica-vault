## Citation

UK Companies House. Public corporate filings for Cambridge Analytica and related SCL entities.

## Why this is the foundational primary source

Companies House is **the** public registry of UK companies. All UK-registered CA / SCL entities have filed records here covering: incorporation, director appointments and resignations, share allocations, persons of significant control, annual returns, accounts, and dissolution proceedings.

## How to use

For any UK entity in this vault, **search the Companies House public register** to obtain:
- Exact incorporation date
- Director appointment dates and resignations
- Share allocations and ownership changes
- Annual filed accounts (financial scale)
- Persons of Significant Control declarations
- Dissolution / insolvency proceedings

This is the **single best public primary source** for the corporate-veil portion of the investigation.
