## What it is

The English Wikipedia encyclopedia article on **Paramount Skydance Corporation**, the American mass-media and entertainment conglomerate created when [[Skydance Media]] merged with [[Paramount Global]] and National Amusements. It is a continuously edited reference summarizing corporate structure, leadership, holdings, and the merger history.

## Key claims / findings

- Paramount Skydance Corporation was **formed August 7, 2025**, on completion of the three-way merger.
- [[David Ellison]] serves as **chairman and CEO**; the company trades publicly (ticker PSKY).
- Holdings include **Paramount Pictures**, the **CBS** broadcast network and [[CBS News]], Nickelodeon, MTV, Comedy Central, Showtime, BET, and the Paramount+ streaming service.
- The merger received **FCC approval in July 2025** under the second Trump administration, with conditions on programming diversity and a CBS ombudsman.
- The article notes the merger ended the **Redstone family's** control of the company through National Amusements and [[Shari Redstone]].
