## What it is

The English Wikipedia biographical article on **Megan Ellison**, American film producer and entrepreneur, founder of [[Annapurna Pictures]], and daughter of [[Larry Ellison]]. She is the sister of [[David Ellison]], chairman/CEO of [[Paramount Skydance]].

## Key claims / findings

- Megan Ellison founded **Annapurna Pictures in 2011** as a production-and-financing company focused on auteur-driven cinema.
- Her productions include *Zero Dark Thirty* (2012), *American Hustle* (2013), *Her* (2013), and *Phantom Thread* (2017) — all Best Picture Oscar nominees.
- In 2014 she became the **first woman** (and fourth person) to receive two Best Picture nominations in the same year, for *Her* and *American Hustle*.
- Annapurna later expanded into television, theatrical production, and video-game publishing, and resumed feature production in the 2020s.
- The article situates her within the **Ellison family**: she and David are the children of Oracle co-founder Larry Ellison and Barbara Boothe Ellison.
