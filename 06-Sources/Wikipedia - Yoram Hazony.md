# Wikipedia - Yoram Hazony

The Wikipedia entry on [[Yoram Hazony]] documents his trajectory from Princeton (PhD) through Israeli academic-and-policy work (Shalem Center) through founding of the Edmund Burke Foundation and the National Conservatism Conference series (NatCon) that has substantially influenced contemporary US national-conservatism intellectual development.

## Why this source matters

Hazony's "The Virtue of Nationalism" (2018) and the broader NatCon Conference series are the principal intellectual venues that articulated the national-conservative-intellectual frame as distinct from both libertarian-conservatism and religious-right-conservatism. NatCon participation has included [[JD Vance]], [[Kevin Roberts]], [[Russell Vought]], [[Patrick Deneen]], [[Adrian Vermeule]], and the broader contemporary right-intellectual ecosystem.
