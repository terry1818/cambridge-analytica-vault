## What it is

A community-maintained encyclopedia biography of [[Marco Rubio]] (Marco Antonio Rubio, born 1971), an American politician and attorney. The article aggregates biography, Senate career, presidential campaign, and current cabinet role into one narrative. It serves as a navigational secondary source for US political-actor mapping and the foreign-policy thread.

## Key claims / findings

- Rubio was born in Miami in 1971, the son of Cuban immigrants.
- He was elected to the US Senate in 2010 and represented Florida from 2011 to 2025.
- He ran for the Republican presidential nomination in 2016 and later won a second Senate term.
- In November 2024, President-elect [[Donald Trump]] announced his intent to nominate Rubio as Secretary of State.
- Rubio was confirmed unanimously by the Senate and took office as the 72nd US Secretary of State on 21 January 2025.
- He also served as acting national security advisor and is the first Latino to hold the Secretary of State office.
