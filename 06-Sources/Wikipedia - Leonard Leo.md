## Vault context

This Wikipedia entry documents [[Leonard Leo]]'s biographical and operational background. Leo is the principal operational figure of the [[Federalist Society]] (longtime executive vice president, now co-chair) and the operational principal of the [[Marble Freedom Trust]] / [[CRC Advisors]] / [[Concord Fund]] / [[The 85 Fund]] downstream-funding ecosystem that has substantially reshaped the conservative-legal infrastructure since 2020.

His operational role in Republican-administration judicial nominations is substantially documented: every Republican-appointed federal judge since the early 1990s has had Federalist Society involvement, with Leo as the principal external advisor through the relevant period.
