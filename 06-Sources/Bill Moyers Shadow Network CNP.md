## Vault context

The Bill Moyers reporting on the [[Council for National Policy]] (variously across the Moyers and Company podcast, BillMoyers.com, and earlier PBS Frontline collaboration) is one of the canonical journalistic reconstructions of the CNP donor-and-leadership network in the mainstream-broadcast tier. Moyers's interviews with [[Anne Nelson]] (around the publication of [[Anne Nelson Shadow Network 2019]]) are the most-cited vehicle for the broader public-facing argument about CNP's centrality in the conservative political infrastructure.

The vault treats this reporting as Tier-2 journalism (high-quality investigative work, but not court-adjudicated or primary-record). It supplements [[New Republic CNP Shadow Network]], [[SourceWatch CNP]], [[SPLC CNP Behind the Curtain]], and [[Wikipedia - CNP]] in building the converged picture the [[Council for National Policy]] entity node summarises.

For the religious-right historical arc that contextualises CNP, see [[The Fellowship]], [[Sharlet 2008 The Family Book]], [[The Family Netflix 2019]], and [[Bad Faith Documentary 2024]].
