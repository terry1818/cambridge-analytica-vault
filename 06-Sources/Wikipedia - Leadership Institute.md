## Vault context

The [[Leadership Institute]] is a US training organisation founded by Morton Blackwell in 1979 that trains young conservatives in political-campaign skills, journalism, and public-affairs operations. It has trained an estimated 250,000+ alumni since founding and is a principal source of the GOP and conservative-movement personnel pipeline.

Leadership Institute alumni include a substantial fraction of conservative congressional staff, GOP campaign operatives, conservative-movement nonprofit staff, and (via the Institute's Campus Reform programme) conservative student-organising leadership on college campuses.

In vault terms, the Leadership Institute is the personnel-pipeline counterpart to the [[State Policy Network]] (state-level think tanks), the [[Federalist Society]] (legal-pipeline), and the [[Heritage Foundation]] / [[Project 2025]] (federal-policy pipeline). It is one of the load-bearing infrastructure pieces that makes the conservative movement operationally durable across administrations.

Blackwell himself is a long-standing [[Council for National Policy]] member; the Institute sits inside the broader CNP-adjacent network ecosystem.
