## What it is

The English Wikipedia article on the **Stargate Project** (incorporated in Delaware as Stargate LLC), an American multinational AI joint venture. It summarizes the venture's founding, funders, leadership, and data-center build-out.

## Key claims / findings

- Stargate is a joint venture created by **[[OpenAI]], [[SoftBank]], [[Oracle Corporation]], and MGX** (an Abu Dhabi state-backed investment firm).
- It was **formally announced January 21, 2025**, by President [[Donald Trump]] at the White House, alongside Sam Altman, Masayoshi Son, and [[Larry Ellison]].
- The venture intends to invest **$500 billion over four years** in US AI infrastructure, deploying $100 billion immediately.
- SoftBank and OpenAI are lead partners — SoftBank with financial responsibility, OpenAI with operational responsibility — each holding roughly **40% ownership**; Oracle and MGX hold smaller stakes.
- Initial data centers were built in **Abilene, Texas**; by September 2025 five more US sites were announced (in Texas, New Mexico, and Ohio), with planned capacity near 7 gigawatts.
- The article notes skepticism about whether the $500B figure represents secured funding.
