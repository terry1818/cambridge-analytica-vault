# Family Research Council

US conservative Christian advocacy and lobbying organization. Founded 1981 by [[James Dobson]]. President: [[Tony Perkins]] (2003-).

## Designation

- 2010: Designated a hate group by Southern Poverty Law Center for sustained LGBTQ-targeted misinformation
- The designation has been politically controversial but cited by mainstream civil rights organizations

## Programs

- Washington Watch radio show (Perkins host)
- Pray Vote Stand Summit (annual gathering)
- Values Voter Summit (until 2021)
- Major participant in [[Council for National Policy]] coordination

## Funding

- ~-20M annual revenue (recent 990s)
- Major recipients include Religious right and conservative-Christian individual donors
- Has received significant Bradley Foundation, DeVos network, and individual mega-donor contributions

## Significance

FRC is the highest-profile DC-based Religious right lobbying organization with sustained access to the Trump-era White House and congressional caucus. The pipeline from FRC to Religious right policy priorities (judicial appointments, anti-LGBTQ policy, abortion restrictions) is well-documented.
