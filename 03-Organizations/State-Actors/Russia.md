# Russia

## Major operations attributed to Russian state actors

- **[[Internet Research Agency]] (IRA)** — Prigozhin-aligned commercial-private-front operation; the 2014-2018 Russian social-media-influence work most heavily documented in the [[Source Mueller Report 2019|Mueller Report]] and [[Source Senate Intel Vol 5 2020|Senate Intel Vol 5]]
- **[[Doppelganger]]** — the 2022-present operation cloning Western mainstream-media outlets to push pro-Russian narratives, documented in [[Source EU DisinfoLab 2022 Doppelganger]]
- **GRU cyber operations** — including the 2016 DNC hack and the broader infrastructure of state-cyber operations adjacent to influence work
- **Diplomatic / intelligence-service-adjacent vehicles** including the historical KGB / FSB / SVR signals and human-intelligence influence apparatus
