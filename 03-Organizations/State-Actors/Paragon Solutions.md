# Paragon Solutions

Paragon Solutions is an Israeli commercial spyware vendor founded 2019 by Ehud Schneorson (former [[Unit 8200]] commander) and Idan Nurick. Paragon is the principal post-2019 entrant in the Israeli commercial spyware market, positioned as a competitor to [[NSO Group]] and [[Candiru]] with deliberately more restrictive customer-selection positioning.

## Commercial positioning

Paragon's commercial-positioning has emphasised:

- Restriction of customer base to NATO-aligned democracies
- Refusal of customers in sanctioned or human-rights-restricted jurisdictions
- Substantial US-government customer engagement positioned as the principal commercial anchor
- The Graphite spyware toolkit as the principal product

The positioning is operationally distinct from NSO and Candiru, which both pursued broader customer bases including substantial sales to non-democratic and human-rights-restricted clients. Whether the Paragon positioning is operationally maintained or principally PR-positioning has been a continuing question.

## AE Industrial Partners acquisition 2024

In December 2024, US private equity firm AE Industrial Partners acquired Paragon Solutions for approximately $900 million. The acquisition is operationally significant for the broader vault network because:

- It represents the first major US-PE acquisition of an Israeli commercial-spyware vendor
- It places Paragon under US regulatory jurisdiction in a way that Israeli-domiciled NSO and Candiru are not
- It creates a US-corporate-structured offensive-cyber vendor with substantial US-government customer access
- The post-acquisition operational positioning of Paragon within AE Industrial Partners' broader defence-technology portfolio is a continuing thread

## US government customer base

Paragon's documented US government customers reportedly include components of the US intelligence community and US law-enforcement at multiple agency levels. The specific customer composition has been partially disclosed through investigative reporting; the comprehensive customer reconstruction has not been done.
