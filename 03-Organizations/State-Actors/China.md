# China

The People's Republic of China appears across this vault as a **state-actor source of large-scale persistent coordinated-inauthentic-behavior operations**, principally through the [[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]] network and adjacent state-aligned commercial vehicles.

## Major operations attributed to Chinese state actors

- **[[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]]** — the persistent China-attributed cross-platform persona network first publicly identified in [[Source Graphika 2019 Spamouflage Dragon]], subsequently documented in continuing [[Source Meta Threat Reports 2022-2024|Meta Threat Reports]]
- **Pro-Beijing political messaging operations** — including anti-Hong-Kong-protest content, anti-dissident messaging, and narratives favorable to Chinese geopolitical positioning
- **Generative-AI integration** — late-2023 onward Meta Threat Reports document AI-generated content integrated into Chinese state-linked operations
