# Doppelgänger

## Summary

**Russian state-coordinated disinformation campaign** active since **~2022**. Operates by **cloning legitimate news-site URLs and content** with one-character or near-identical domain changes (e.g. washingtonpostt.com), then **publishing pro-Russia / anti-Ukraine / culture-war content** to those clones to mislead readers. Targets US + Germany + France + Poland + Ukraine + multiple Western democracies. **Substantially attributed to the Russian Social Design Agency (SDA)** + **Structura LLC** (sanctioned by US Treasury 2024).

**Operationally durable despite multiple US Treasury sanctions:**
- **DOJ September 2024 indictment** of two RT employees (also covered in [[Tenet Media DOJ Indictment 2024-09-04]])
- **July 2025:** US Treasury sanctioned **AEZA Group** (Russian bulletproof hosting provider) for hosting Doppelgänger infrastructure
- **January 2025:** Operation **expanded to Bluesky** per New Geopolitics Research Network
- **February 2025:** Targeted **German federal election** per Recorded Future
- **2025:** Targeted **Polish elections** per multiple investigators

**The most consequential Russian state-coordinated disinformation operation of the post-2022 period.**
