# Marble Freedom Trust

US 501(c)(4) dark-money entity controlled by [[Leonard Leo]]. Recipient of a single .6B donation from Chicago billionaire [[Barre Seid]] in 2021 - the largest single political dark-money transaction on public record.

## Structure (Tier 1)

- 501(c)(4) registered in Utah 2020
- August 2021: Seid donated 100% of his company Tripp Lite to MFT
- MFT then sold Tripp Lite to Eaton Corporation for .65B in March 2022
- The donor-of-stock-then-sale-by-charity structure avoided approximately  in capital gains tax Seid would have owed on a direct sale

## Reporting

- ProPublica + The Lever broke the story August 22, 2022 from leaked tax documents
- Confirmed by Seid through counsel; Seid declined further comment
- No legal action by IRS as of latest filings - the structure is tax-legal albeit aggressive

## Downstream spending

- MFT has funded [[Concord Fund]] (formerly Judicial Crisis Network), [[85 Fund]], Honest Elections Project, and a network of state-level conservative political vehicles
- Spending pattern aligns with Leo's broader judicial / election-policy organizing

## Significance for vault

MFT is the single largest concentrated dark-money fund in US political history at the time of the 2022 reporting. It is the institutional backbone of the post-2020 Leo apparatus and connects directly into [[Federalist Society]] judicial pipeline funding.
