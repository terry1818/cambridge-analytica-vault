# Creative Commons

US 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded 2001 by [[Lawrence Lessig]] and others. Develops and stewards copyright licenses (CC BY, CC BY-SA, etc.) that allow creators to selectively grant permissions while preserving copyright.

## Vault relevance

Creative Commons is referenced primarily through [[Lawrence Lessig]]'s broader work on technology law and structural reform. Functions as the operational backbone of significant portions of the open knowledge / open access movement that intersects with vault themes around information environments and corporate platform control.
