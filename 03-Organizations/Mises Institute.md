# Ludwig von Mises Institute

US think tank founded 1982 by Llewellyn H. Rockwell Jr. Promotes Austrian-school economics and libertarian (often paleoconservative) political theory.

## Significance for vault

The Mises Institute is one of the institutional homes of the paleoconservative / libertarian / Austrian-school current that bridges between conventional libertarianism and the contemporary [[Tech right]] / national-conservative current. Multiple [[Tech right]] figures have cited Mises Institute thinkers (Rothbard, Hoppe) approvingly.
