# COS movement

The institutional ecosystem pursuing an [[Article V Convention]] of states under the [[Convention of States Action]] umbrella.

## Components

- [[Convention of States Action]] (501(c)(4) operational arm)
- Citizens for Self-Governance (501(c)(3) parent)
- [[Mark Meckler]] (president)
- [[19 state applications]] (current count)
- Multi-state legislator network
- Funding stream from [[Mercer Family Foundation]], [[Bradley Foundation]], and other Council for National Policy-aligned donors

## Significance

The COS movement is the constitutional-amendment leg of the [[Structural Capture Thesis]] coalition. It pursues structural change via Article V threshold rather than via election of pro-amendment Congresses.
