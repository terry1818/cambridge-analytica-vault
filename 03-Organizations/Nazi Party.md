# Nazi Party (NSDAP)

German far-right political party founded 1920. In power 1933-1945 under [[Adolf Hitler]]. Responsible for World War II and the Holocaust.

## Vault relevance

Referenced as the canonical historical reference for far-right authoritarian movement organizing, ideology, and constitutional capture. Scholars of [[Democratic backsliding]] (Ben-Ghiat, Levitsky/Ziblatt, Snyder) draw extensively on NSDAP's path-to-power as a comparative case for contemporary US analysis.

The vault treats NSDAP comparisons carefully - historically informative but should not be deployed casually as rhetorical weapons.
