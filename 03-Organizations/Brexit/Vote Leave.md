# Vote Leave

## Summary

**Official Leave-side campaign for the UK 2016 EU referendum**, designated by the UK Electoral Commission as the official "Leave" campaign in April 2016. Directed by **Dominic Cummings**; principal political figures **Boris Johnson + Michael Gove + Matthew Elliott**. **Paid GBP 2.7M directly to [[AggregateIQ]] for data services** + **GBP 625K via BeLeave** (a youth-affiliate; UK Electoral Commission found this BeLeave-routed spending to be illegal coordinated spending).

**Won the referendum** June 23, 2016 with 51.9% Leave. **Dissolved 2017** post-victory; Cummings later became Boris Johnson's Chief of Staff (2019-2020) when Johnson became PM.

**Operationally:** Vote Leave's data operation - executed primarily through AggregateIQ - was the single most consequential data-driven political-campaign operation in modern UK political history.
