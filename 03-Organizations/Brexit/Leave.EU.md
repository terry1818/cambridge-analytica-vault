# Leave.EU

## Summary

**Unofficial Leave-side campaign** for the UK 2016 EU referendum (NOT designated as the official Leave campaign; that was [[Vote Leave]]). Funded primarily by **[[Arron Banks]] + Andy Wigmore**; political figurehead **Nigel Farage**. **Multiple disputed Cambridge Analytica contacts** - Leave.EU representatives met with CA multiple times 2015-2016 + publicly claimed CA was working for them.

**ICO ultimately found no significant CA-direct breach** (CA work for Leave.EU was minimal in scope) but the **pattern of contacts + documented relationships + Banks's Russian-Embassy meetings + the broader influence-operations question** make Leave.EU one of the most investigated single political campaigns in modern UK history.

**Operationally:** while losing the official-campaign designation, Leave.EU's media operation - particularly Farage's mass-mobilization capacity + Banks's funding - was substantially consequential to the Leave victory.
