# Federation for American Immigration Reform (FAIR)

US restrictionist immigration advocacy group. Founded 1979 by [[John Tanton]]. Designated a hate group by SPLC.

## Significance

FAIR is the senior institutional node of the [[John Tanton]] anti-immigration network alongside [[Center for Immigration Studies]] and NumbersUSA. Provides policy infrastructure that has shaped multiple Republican-administration immigration policies.
