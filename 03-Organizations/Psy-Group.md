# Psy-Group

Israeli private intelligence firm specializing in **disinformation and social-media influence campaigns**. Operated 2014-2018 with offices in Israel and the US. Reportedly contracted to support Trump 2016 campaign efforts including operations against then-candidate Trump's primary opponents and against Hillary Clinton. **Investigated by [[Mueller Investigation]]** in connection with potential foreign influence in 2016. Dissolved 2018 following Mueller scrutiny.

## Operations

### Reported 2016 election work (Tier 1 + Tier 2 documented)

Per investigative reporting (*The New Yorker*, *NYT*, *Times of Israel*) and Mueller-related documentary evidence:
- Psy-Group prepared a proposal (dubbed "Project Rome") for influence operations targeting Republican primary opponents and Hillary Clinton on behalf of Trump campaign-adjacent figures
- Discussions reportedly involving [[George Nader]] (UAE-connected operative) and Trump campaign figures
- Specific operational deployment scope disputed; some sources suggest substantial work; others suggest the proposal was not fully executed

### Mueller scrutiny

- Psy-Group founder Joel Zamel was interviewed by Mueller investigators
- Connections to UAE adviser George Nader documented
- Connections to broader Russia investigation context
