# US Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

US federal antitrust and consumer protection agency. Lina Khan chair 2021-2024. Substantially refocused on tech-platform antitrust during her tenure.

## Vault relevance

The FTC under Khan was one of the structural drivers of the [[Tech right]] political realignment 2022-2024. Tech industry hostility to Khan's antitrust agenda (especially the Microsoft-Activision and Amazon cases) helped consolidate the contemporary tech-right turn toward Trump.

Post-Khan: Andrew Ferguson appointed chair under Trump 2.0; agenda substantially rolled back.
