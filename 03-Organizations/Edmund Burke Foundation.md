# Edmund Burke Foundation

US-Israel think tank founded 2019 by [[Yoram Hazony]], [[Christopher DeMuth]] (former AEI president), and David Brog. Organizing body of the [[National Conservatism Conference]] (NatCon).

## NatCon Conferences

- 2019: Inaugural - DC
- 2020-2024: Annual, with DC and international editions
- 2023: First UK NatCon (London) - significant in UK Conservative Party realignment
- The conferences have been the primary venue for the intellectual formation of post-liberal / national-conservative ideology
