# CECOT (Centro de Confinamiento del Terrorismo)

El Salvador mega-prison. Capacity approximately 40,000 inmates. Operated as part of President Nayib Bukele's gang-suppression operation.

## Trump-era deportation destination (Tier 1)

- 2025: Trump administration began deporting US-resident immigrants to CECOT under invocation of the Alien Enemies Act
- Multiple lawsuits challenge the legality of the deportations and the conditions of detention
- CECOT confinement has been documented as effectively without due process for individuals deported there

## Significance

CECOT is the documented destination case for the most-due-process-restricted deportation operations of the second Trump term. The legal status of US-resident individuals confined in CECOT remains contested in ongoing litigation.
