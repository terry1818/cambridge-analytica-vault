# Focus on the Family

Christian Right nonprofit organization founded **1977** by [[James Dobson]] in Arcadia, CA (later relocated to Colorado Springs). Produced extensive **radio programming, educational materials, and books** promoting conservative Christian views on family, gender, sexuality, parenting, and politics. **Central institutional node of the Religious Right since the 1980s**. Affiliated with state-level **Family Policy Councils** in dozens of states.

Annual budget approximately $90-100M. Founder Dobson departed in 2010; current President Jim Daly.

## Programs

- Daily radio broadcast (peaked at 200+ stations)
- Books and educational materials
- Family Policy Council network (state-level)
- Various ministries

## Political influence

Per [[Source: Stewart Power Worshippers 2019]] and broader scholarship, Focus on the Family is treated as one of the principal cultural-political nodes of the Religious Right movement. Founder Dobson was for decades one of the most-influential Evangelical political figures.

## Position in vault

Focus on the Family is identified throughout the [[Religious Right MOC]] and [[Deep Dive - Three Wing Coalition Anatomy]] as one of the central institutional nodes through which the theocratic wing of the contemporary Republican coalition maintains its mass base and coordinates messaging.

## State Family Policy Councils

Network of state-level affiliated organizations operating in 38+ states. Provide:
- State-level lobbying
- Legislative drafting
- Grassroots organizing
- Coordination with national policy infrastructure
