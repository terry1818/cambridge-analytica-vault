# The Lynde and Harry Bradley Foundation

Milwaukee-based conservative philanthropy. Founded 1942; gained scale 1985 after sale of Allen-Bradley Co. to Rockwell. Approximately .2B in assets (2023 990).

## Strategic positioning

- One of the top-five conservative grantmaking foundations historically (alongside Coors, Scaife, Mercer, Olin)
- Long-term backer of [[Heritage Foundation]], [[Federalist Society]], [[American Enterprise Institute]], Manhattan Institute, [[Cato Institute]]
- Significant funder of state-policy organizations (State Policy Network affiliates)

## Election-policy posture

- Funded the True the Vote organization (multiple cycles)
- Funded the Honest Elections Project ([[85 Fund]] affiliated)
- Funded election-integrity-framed organizing 2020-2024 that overlaps with [[Stop the Steal]] adjacent infrastructure

## Significance

Bradley represents the older institutional layer of conservative philanthropy - heavily aligned with the post-2010 right-shift but originating in the 1980s-1990s Reagan-era movement infrastructure. Distinct from newer dark-money players like [[Marble Freedom Trust]] in being a more publicly accountable private foundation.
