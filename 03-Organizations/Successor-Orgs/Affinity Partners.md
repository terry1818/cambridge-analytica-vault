# Affinity Partners

## Summary

Private equity firm founded 2021 by **[[Jared Kushner]]** after leaving the first Trump administration. **$6.16B AUM by 2025** (up from ~$4.8B in 2024 and ~$3B in 2023). **99% of AUM is non-US** ($6.097B of $6.16B). Largest single LP is the **Saudi Public Investment Fund ($2B commitment)**, with additional LPs from UAE and Qatar sovereign wealth. House Judiciary Committee opened an investigation into the Saudi conflicts. **September 2025: brokered the $55B Electronic Arts buyout** with PIF and Silver Lake, taking ~5% Affinity stake; Kushner personally brokered the EA-PIF connection ([[Source Variety 2025-09 EA PIF Affinity]]).
