# Proud Boys

The Proud Boys is a male-only nationalist-fraternal organisation founded in 2016 by Gavin McInnes (Vice Media co-founder turned right-wing media figure). The organisation has been variously characterised as a fraternity, a street-violence group, and a designated hate group depending on the framing source.

## Operational structure

Proud Boys operates as a loosely-federated chapter network with substantial local autonomy. The organisation grew through 2016-2020 through Trump-era street-protest cycles, frequently positioning at counter-protests to leftist demonstrations and at MAGA rallies. The 2017 Unite the Right rally in Charlottesville saw substantial Proud Boys participation though McInnes was not present.

## January 6 Capitol attack

Proud Boys had substantial documented operational participation in the January 6, 2021 Capitol attack. The organisation's then-chair Enrique Tarrio was convicted of seditious conspiracy in May 2023 and sentenced to 22 years federal prison (the longest single sentence handed down for a January 6 defendant). Multiple other Proud Boys members were convicted on related charges.

## Post-conviction status and the Trump 2.0 pardons

The Trump 2.0 administration's January 2025 pardons of January 6 defendants freed Tarrio and the other convicted Proud Boys leadership. The post-pardon operational trajectory of the organisation is a vault research thread; the operational rebuilding has been documented but at smaller scale than the pre-2021 footprint.

## Relationship to broader vault network

Proud Boys are operationally adjacent to but distinct from the broader CSPOA / sheriff-sovereignty / militia ecosystem. Where CSPOA operates within law-enforcement institutional structures, Proud Boys operates as street-level political-violence-capable organisation. The two clusters are operationally complementary in the broader vault analytical frame even though they are not formally coordinated.
