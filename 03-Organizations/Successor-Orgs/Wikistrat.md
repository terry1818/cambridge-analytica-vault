# Wikistrat

## Summary

"Crowdsourced geopolitical analysis" firm founded by [[Joel Zamel]] in 2010. Headquartered in Washington DC; operations also in Israel. Operates on a model of recruiting hundreds of part-time geopolitical analysts globally who participate in scenario-modeling exercises for paying clients (governments, militaries, corporations, investment firms).
