# World Liberty Financial

## Summary

Crypto venture launched **September 2024** by then-candidate Donald Trump with sons Don Jr. and Eric. **60% owned by a Trump business entity**. Issues the **USD1 stablecoin**. In May 2025, USD1 was used in a **$2 billion transaction between Binance and MGX** (Emirati investment firm) - the largest single-deal documentation of WLF's foreign-payments role. **Trumps have cashed out $1.2B+ in actual dollars from WLF in 16 months** per multiple investigative reports. **Applied for US bank charter January 2026** - converting unregulated crypto revenue into regulated banking license. The single most consequential Trump-family financial vehicle.
