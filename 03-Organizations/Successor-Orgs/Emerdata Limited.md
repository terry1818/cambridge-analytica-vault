# Emerdata Limited

## Summary

UK holding company established **August 2017** by [[Alexander Tayler]] (CA Chief Data Officer) and [[SCL Group]] chairman. Same London building as [[Cambridge Analytica]]. Widely portrayed as CA's effective successor. Raised **$19 million** from international investors in January 2018. Board has included [[Rebekah Mercer]], Jennifer Mercer, [[Alexander Nix]], and **[[Johnson Chun Shun Ko]]** (Hong Kong businessman linked to [[Erik Prince]] / Blackwater founder, via Frontier Services Group). Per July 2019 reporting, Emerdata "fully acquired" SCL/CA assets and has been paying SCL companies' legal bills amid bankruptcy proceedings. ([[Source Wikipedia - SCL Group]])
