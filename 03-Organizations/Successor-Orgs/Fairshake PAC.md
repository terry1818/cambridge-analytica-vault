# Fairshake PAC

## Summary

Crypto industry SuperPAC founded 2023. **Deployed ~$130M in 2024 election cycle** - including approximately **$40M to defeat Senator Sherrod Brown (D-OH)**, then-chair of the Senate Banking Committee and the most prominent crypto skeptic in Congress. **53 of 58 Fairshake-favored candidates won 2024**, making it functionally the **most successful single-cycle PAC in US history measured by win rate per dollar spent**.

## Funding cluster

Top contributors (2024):

- **Coinbase** - ~$48M
- **Ripple Labs** - ~$48M
- **Andreessen Horowitz** - ~$23M
- **Jump Crypto** - smaller
- **Kraken** - smaller
- Others including Gemini, Circle, MoonPay, Robinhood

## Operational pattern

- Spends in both parties (technically nonpartisan)
- **Targets:** crypto-skeptic Democrats AND crypto-skeptic Republicans
- **Defends:** any incumbent willing to vote for crypto industry priorities
- Coordinates with affiliated PACs (Defend American Jobs, Protect Progress)

## Major 2024 victories

- **Sherrod Brown defeated** by Bernie Moreno (R-OH); $40M spent
- **Katie Porter defeated** in CA Senate primary
- Multiple House races flipped

## Why it matters

Fairshake represents **the political-financing arm of the crypto wing**. Its structural significance:

1. **Single-industry PAC outspending most cycles' total PAC spend**
2. **Bipartisan-by-design** - cannot be defeated by partisan flipping
3. **Created a new electoral fear class** - candidates avoid crypto-skeptic positions to avoid becoming targets
4. **Locked in 119th Congress as pro-crypto** by composition
