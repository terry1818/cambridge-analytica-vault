# Patriot Front

Patriot Front is a US white-nationalist street-organising network founded in 2017 by Thomas Rousseau as a splinter of Vanguard America following the 2017 Unite the Right rally in Charlottesville. The organisation has become the principal contemporary US white-nationalist organisational vehicle, with operational visibility substantially exceeding its actual membership scale.

## Operational style

Patriot Front operates through coordinated flash-action street deployments: small groups of uniformed members (typically 20-50) marching in formation through public spaces, distributing propaganda materials, and producing media-friendly imagery for online amplification. The operational style is deliberately optimised for media virality rather than for traditional political-organising metrics.

## Specific operational episodes

Documented Patriot Front operations have included:

- The June 2022 Coeur d'Alene Idaho mass-arrest incident (31 PF members arrested en route to a Pride event with riot gear)
- Multiple coordinated flash marches across major US cities through 2022-2024
- Substantial flyering and propaganda-distribution operations targeting college campuses

## Funding and operational scale

Patriot Front's funding model has been substantially reconstructed through the 2022 internal-records leak (when an internal chat-server archive was published by Unicorn Riot). The funding is reportedly primarily member-dues with limited large-donor involvement; the operational scale is correspondingly modest compared to historical white-nationalist organisations.
