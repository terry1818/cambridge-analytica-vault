# Black Cube

## Summary

Israeli private intelligence firm founded in 2010, headquartered in Tel Aviv with offices in London and Madrid. Staffed largely by **former Mossad officers and Israeli intelligence operators**. Operates corporate and political intelligence services for paying clients globally.

## Notable engagements (publicly reported)

### Harvey Weinstein (2016-2017)
- Hired by Harvey Weinstein to surveil women who had accused him of sexual misconduct, and the journalists investigating him
- Operatives used **fake identities** to get personal information from accusers
- Exposed by Ronan Farrows November 2017 New Yorker investigation
- The single most damaging public exposure of Black Cubes operating methods

### Obama Iran-deal officials (2017-2018)
- Black Cube operatives targeted former Obama administration officials who had worked on the Iran nuclear deal
- Posed as researchers and journalists to extract information
- Per The Observer reporting, targeted figures included Colin Kahl and Ben Rhodes
- Clients not fully publicly identified; speculation focused on Trump-aligned figures opposed to the Iran deal

### Various corporate targets
- Multiple ongoing corporate-espionage engagements
- Has worked for governments, oligarchs, hedge funds
- Detailed in [[Source Farrow 2017-11-06 New Yorker Black Cube Weinstein]] and subsequent investigations
