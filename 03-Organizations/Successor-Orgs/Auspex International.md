# Auspex International

## Summary

Political consultancy launched in **July 2018** -- two months after [[Cambridge Analytica]]'s May 2018 insolvency -- by several former CA staff including [[Mark Turnbull]] (CAs Managing Director of Political Global). Intended to influence politics and society in **Africa and the Middle East**. Self-described in some accounts as an "ethical" data analysis firm -- a framing widely criticized as cosmetic given the personnel overlap with CA.

## Founders / leadership

- **[[Mark Turnbull]]** -- former CA MD Political Global (the same Turnbull featured in the [[Channel 4 Sting 2018]] discussing CA operations in Mexico, Malaysia, Brazil, Australia, China)
- **Ahmad Al-Khatib** -- Egyptian-British operative; networking into MENA region
- Multiple other ex-CA staff (not all publicly named)

## Self-description vs reality

Auspex marketed itself as a clean break from CA -- focused on "ethical" data analysis, "purpose-driven" campaigns. Per [[Source Ram FT 2018-07-11 Ex-CA launch venture]] and [[Source Jardin Boing Boing 2018-07-11 Auspex]]:

- Critics noted the same personnel were operating with the same methodology
- The "ethical" framing was widely viewed as PR positioning rather than substantive change
- Specific Africa/Middle East engagements have not been fully publicly documented

## Operational reach (claimed/reported)

- Multiple African political clients
- Middle East government and corporate clients
- Less visibility than CA had in its peak years -- operates more quietly

## CA-successor pattern

Auspex is one node in the **CA afterlife** pattern documented in [[Source Pasternack Witt Fast Company 2019-07-26]]:
- Personnel migration from CA → multiple successor firms
- Methodology continuity
- New corporate shells; less media attention
- Continued operations across same client base CA had built

## Connections

- **Founders / personnel:** [[Mark Turnbull]] and other ex-CA staff
- **Sister successor firms:** [[Emerdata Limited]], [[Data Propria]], [[Firecrest Technologies]], [[Emic]]
- **CA parent:** [[Cambridge Analytica]] / [[SCL Group]]
