# RTX Corporation (formerly Raytheon Technologies)

US aerospace and defense conglomerate. Rebranded from Raytheon Technologies to RTX in 2023. Headquartered in Arlington, Virginia.

## Significance

RTX is among the top US defense contractors. Vault references primarily concern the broader military-industrial-complex framing rather than specific incidents. The contractor structure (concentrated revenue from federal defense spending) is one of the structural features that produces the documented bipartisan support for high defense budgets.
