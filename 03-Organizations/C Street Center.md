# C Street Center

Townhouse residence at 133 C Street SE, Washington DC operated by [[The Family]] / [[The Fellowship Foundation]]. Provides below-market-rate housing to members of Congress.

## Significance

C Street has been the documented residence of multiple US Senators and Representatives - particularly Republican members affiliated with the Fellowship network. The arrangement (subsidized housing to elected officials by a religious-political organization) raised intermittent ethics questions that did not produce institutional reform.
