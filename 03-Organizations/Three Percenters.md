# Three Percenters

US anti-government militia movement founded 2008 by [[Mike Vanderboegh]]. Name derives from the (historically inaccurate) claim that only 3% of American colonists fought the British.

## Structure

- Not a single organization - a label adopted by dozens of local groups
- No central command; led to internal splits in 2020 over J6 participation

## January 6 (Tier 1)

- Multiple Three Percenters convicted of J6-related felonies
- A California-based cell led by [[Alan Hostetter]] convicted of conspiracy to obstruct an official proceeding (June 2023)

## Ideology

- Hard 2nd Amendment maximalism
- Anti-federal-government in posture but pro-local-sheriff in operational doctrine
- Significant overlap with the [[Constitutional Sheriffs]] movement and [[Ammon Bundy]] orbit
