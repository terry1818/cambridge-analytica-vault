# Americans for Tax Reform

US 501(c)(4) advocacy organization. Founded 1985 by [[Grover Norquist]] at the request of President Reagan.

## Operations

- Operates the Taxpayer Protection Pledge (signed by virtually all Republican federal candidates since 1986)
- Holds the influential weekly Wednesday Meeting in DC bringing together conservative organization leaders
- Functions as the coordinating body for anti-tax movement organizing

## Wednesday Meeting

- Founded 1993
- Approximately 150-200 weekly attendees from conservative organizations
- The single most-cited coordinating forum in DC for the broader conservative movement after [[Council for National Policy]]
- Less formal than CNP; weekly rather than quarterly; more operational than strategic
