# Citizen Lab

## Summary

**University of Toronto interdisciplinary research laboratory** focused on **information-controls research at the intersection of ICTs, human rights, and global security**. Founded **2001 by Ronald Deibert**. Operates within the Munk School of Global Affairs and Public Policy.

**The primary investigative resource on Pegasus + commercial-spyware ecosystem worldwide.** Documented hundreds of NSO Pegasus targeting cases including:
- Saudi-state targeting of Jamal Khashoggi associates (pre + post murder)
- Mexican-state targeting of journalists + activists
- Spanish-state targeting of Catalan independence figures
- Indian-state targeting of journalists + politicians
- US-side targeting cases (rare; including limited Hatice Cengiz post-Khashoggi)

**Operational methodology:** combines technical forensic analysis (device imaging + traffic analysis) with policy + legal + investigative research. **Substantial documented track record** of validated findings; high-reliability source-grade in this vault.
