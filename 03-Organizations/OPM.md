# US Office of Personnel Management

US federal agency responsible for civil service administration. Sets hiring rules, manages federal employee benefits, oversees workforce policies.

## Vault relevance

OPM is the central institutional vehicle for the [[Schedule F]] / [[Schedule F implementation 2025]] project. Whoever leads OPM controls the operational mechanism for reclassifying career civil servants as at-will political appointees.

- 2020: [[Paul Dans]] (OPM Chief of Staff) was the operational architect of the first Schedule F EO
- 2025: Charles Ezell appointed OPM Director; implementing the second-term Schedule F revival

The "Fork in the Road" deferred-resignation offer was OPM-administered.
