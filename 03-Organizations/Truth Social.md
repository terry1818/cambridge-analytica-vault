# Truth Social

## Summary

Truth Social is a social-media platform operated by Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. (TMTG). The company was founded in 2021 by Andy Litinsky and Wes Moss — former contestants on Trump's *The Apprentice* — and Truth Social launched on iOS on February 21, 2022. It was created as a direct response to [[Donald Trump]]'s deplatforming: Trump was banned from Twitter on January 8, 2021 after the January 6 Capitol attack, and Truth Social was positioned as a "free speech" alternative where he could communicate with supporters without intermediation. The platform became Trump's primary channel for political messaging, campaign announcements, and attacks on opponents and institutions. In December 2021 TMTG raised $1 billion in private investment and contracted with Rumble for technical infrastructure. In March 2024 TMTG completed a SPAC merger with Digital World Acquisition Corp (DWAC) and began trading on NASDAQ under the ticker DJT, briefly producing a multibillion-dollar valuation despite minimal revenue. Truth Social matters to this vault as the convergence point of three threads: the tech-right's "alternative platform" movement, the [[MAGA media ecosystem]]'s drive for disintermediated channels, and an unusual fusion of a sitting politician's communications apparatus with a publicly traded company in which he is the controlling shareholder.

- Andy Litinsky and Wes Moss — Co-founders; former *Apprentice* contestants; later in litigation with the company over their equity stakes

## Funding & money flows

- December 2021: TMTG raised $1 billion in private investment in public equity (PIPE) funding ahead of its planned public listing.
- The company's path to public markets ran through Digital World Acquisition Corp (DWAC), a special-purpose acquisition company (SPAC); the merger faced SEC scrutiny over the timing of merger discussions.
- March 25, 2024: The DWAC–TMTG merger completed; the combined company began trading as DJT with an ~$8 billion valuation on its first day — a valuation widely noted as disconnected from the company's reported revenue (~$4.1 million the prior year against a ~$58 million net loss).
- The structure means [[Donald Trump]]'s personal net worth is directly tied to DJT's share price — a documented and unusual conflict-of-interest concern.

## Activities

Truth Social operates as a Twitter-style microblogging platform ("truths" rather than "tweets"). Its principal activity is hosting [[Donald Trump]]'s political communications, which are then redistributed across the broader [[MAGA media ecosystem]]. TMTG has also announced expansion into streaming (TMTG+) and, per late-2025 reporting, into financial and cryptocurrency products.
