# American Enterprise Institute (AEI)

US conservative think tank. Founded 1938. Headquartered in DC.

## Historical positioning

- Older than [[Heritage Foundation]] (1973) and traditionally more academic / less movement-organizing
- Has housed leading neoconservative figures: Irving Kristol, Norman Podhoretz, Charles Murray, Lynne Cheney, Newt Gingrich, John Bolton

## Recent realignment

- 2010s: Tension between AEI traditional neoconservatives and the Trumpian populist current
- [[Christopher DeMuth]] (AEI president 1986-2008) was a co-founder of the [[Edmund Burke Foundation]] / NatCon
- AEI has retained establishment-conservative positioning while losing influence relative to Heritage in the Trump era
