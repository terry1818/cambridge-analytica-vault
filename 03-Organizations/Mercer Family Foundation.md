# Mercer Family Foundation

Private foundation controlled by [[Rebekah Mercer]]. Funding vehicle of the broader [[Robert Mercer]] political project 2010-present.

## Funded entities (selected)

- [[Breitbart News]] (Mercer family was the lead minority shareholder 2014-2018)
- [[Cambridge Analytica]] (via direct Mercer investment - documented in 2018 reporting)
- Reclaim New York
- Government Accountability Institute (Peter Schweizer's research outfit)
- Media Research Center
- Heartland Institute (climate skepticism)

## Significance for vault

The Mercer financial pattern 2014-2018 is the core funding spine of the [[Cambridge Analytica]] / [[Breitbart News]] / [[Steve Bannon]] orbit that fed into the 2016 Trump campaign. The same family was funding the data operation, the media amplification, and the strategist.

Post-2018, the Mercers have stepped back from public political donating - the foundation's public 990 disclosures show continued funding but at reduced national-political prominence.
