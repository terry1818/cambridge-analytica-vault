# Apollo Global Management

Major US private-equity / alternative-asset-management firm. Co-founded by Leon Black, Joshua Harris, and Marc Rowan in 1990. Manages hundreds of billions in AUM across private equity, credit, real estate, infrastructure. NYSE listed.

The Apollo connection to the Epstein operation runs through co-founder [[Leon Black]], who paid Epstein an estimated $158M for purported "tax and estate planning" services 2012-2017 -- a sum vastly exceeding any conventional advisory engagement. Apollo retained Dechert LLP to conduct an internal investigation after public reporting; the resulting report (Jan 2021) documented the payments and prompted Black's resignation.

## Key facts

- **$158M payment 2012-2017** (tier 1): documented by Dechert LLP internal report commissioned by Apollo. Payments were personal -- from Black to Epstein -- not from Apollo corporate. Apollo's investigation concluded Apollo itself had no Epstein-related liabilities. [[Source: Apollo Dechert Report on Black-Epstein Payments 2021]].
- **Black's resignation 2021** (tier 1): Black announced he would step down as Apollo CEO and chairman in early 2021 following the report; transition completed by March 2021.
- **Giuffre civil suit 2023** (tier 1): [[Virginia Giuffre]] filed a civil suit against Leon Black personally alleging she had been trafficked to him. Case settled.
- **Stake retention**: Black remained a substantial Apollo shareholder post-resignation; family wealth not materially affected.
