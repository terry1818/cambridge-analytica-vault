# News Corp

US-headquartered global media conglomerate. Owned and controlled by the [[Murdoch family]] (Rupert Murdoch and heirs). Originally Australian, restructured 2013 split into News Corp (print + book + Australian) and Fox Corporation (TV / cable).

## Holdings (selected)

**News Corp (print/publishing):**
- Wall Street Journal
- New York Post
- The Times (London) / The Sun (UK)
- HarperCollins
- Dow Jones

**Fox Corporation (TV/cable, separately listed since 2019 Disney sale):**
- Fox News
- Fox Business
- Fox Sports
- Fox local TV stations

## Political alignment

- Editorial pages and Fox News programming have been the primary establishment-media vehicles of the post-2000 conservative political project
- Wall Street Journal editorial page has been the canonical voice of business-class conservatism
- Fox News has been the canonical voice of populist conservatism

## Significance

News Corp / Fox Corp is the largest single privately-controlled US conservative media apparatus. The Murdoch family succession question (Rupert is 94 in 2025; multiple children have publicly diverged on political direction) is the operative governance question for the future of the apparatus.
