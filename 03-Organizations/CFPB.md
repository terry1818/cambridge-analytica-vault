# Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

US federal agency created by the Dodd-Frank Act (2010). Designed by [[Elizabeth Warren]]. Regulates consumer financial products.

## DOGE dismantling (Tier 1)

- February 2025: CFPB substantially shuttered as part of the [[DOGE]] operations
- Lawsuits pending challenging the dismantling
- The CFPB case is one of the documented operational cases of administrative-agency-by-agency dismantling under second-term Trump

## Significance

CFPB is one of the central operational case studies of the [[Schedule F]] / [[Project 2025]] administrative-state restructuring agenda. The agency's effective dissolution despite statutory creation by Congress demonstrates the post-Dodd-Frank regulatory infrastructure's vulnerability to executive-action dismantling.
