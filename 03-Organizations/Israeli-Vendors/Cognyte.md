# Cognyte

## Summary

**Israeli SIGINT + bulk-surveillance commercial vendor.** Spun out of **Verint Systems** in **February 2021** as standalone public company (NASDAQ: CGNT). **Major commercial-intelligence vendor** providing law-enforcement + intelligence agencies with mass-surveillance + signals-intelligence capabilities. **Controversial client base** including documented sales to authoritarian governments (Indonesia, Myanmar, others per Citizen Lab + Meta + other research).

**Operationally important** as one of the principal **Israeli SIGINT-vendor commercial exports** parallel to NSO + Quadream + AnyVision + Candiru + Insanet. **Founding personnel substantially from Israeli Defense Force Unit 8200** (Israel's SIGINT unit).

**Meta 2021 takedown action** specifically named Cognyte as a "surveillance-for-hire" vendor operating against Facebook + Instagram users worldwide.
