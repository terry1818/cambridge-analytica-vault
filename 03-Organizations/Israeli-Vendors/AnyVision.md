# AnyVision (Oosto)

## Summary

**Israeli face-recognition commercial vendor.** Founded 2015 as AnyVision; rebranded as **Oosto** in 2021. **Documented Israeli government + IDF + West Bank checkpoint deployments** of face-recognition technology against Palestinians (documented by NBC News, +972 Magazine, Forensic Architecture).

**2019 NBC News investigation** revealed AnyVision powered a covert Israeli military face-recognition surveillance program in the West Bank ("Red Wolf"). **Microsoft divested its AnyVision stake** following the investigation citing inability to verify compliance with Microsoft's ethical-AI principles.

**Operationally significant** as part of the **broader Israeli commercial-surveillance export ecosystem** along with NSO, Cognyte, Quadream. **Substantial Israeli-government + military-procurement integration.**
