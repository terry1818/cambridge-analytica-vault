# Civil Service Reform Act of 1978

US federal statute that restructured the federal civil service system. Created the OPM, MSPB, and FLRA. Reaffirmed the merit-system principles of the [[Pendleton Civil Service Reform Act|Pendleton Act]] (1883).

## Vault relevance

The CSRA is the statutory framework that [[Schedule F]] and the [[Schedule F implementation 2025]] effort substantially modifies. The legal question pending in [[NTEU v. Trump]] and parallel cases is whether the Schedule F reclassification is consistent with CSRA's merit-system requirements.
