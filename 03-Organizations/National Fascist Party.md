# National Fascist Party (PNF)

Italian fascist political party founded 1921 by Benito Mussolini. Ruling party of Italy 1925-1943.

## Vault relevance

The PNF and Mussolini's [[Italy]] are the founding historical case study for [[Fascism]] as a political phenomenon. [[Robert O Paxton]]'s "The Anatomy of Fascism" (2004) draws extensively on the Italian case. Contemporary scholars (Ben-Ghiat, Stanley) reference Italian fascism for comparative analysis.
