# Israeli-American Council

US-based nonprofit organization founded 2007 (originally as Israeli Leadership Council). Mission per its own description: strengthening the Israeli-American community in the United States and ties between the US and Israel. Substantial funding from [[Sheldon Adelson]] and Miriam Adelson, [[Leslie Wexner]], and other major US-Israel philanthropic donors. **Documented as among the most well-funded US-Israel political and cultural-influence organizations operating in the US.**

## Operations (Tier 1)

### Programs
- Israeli-American community organizing
- Annual National Conference (one of the largest US-Israel political gatherings)
- Educational programming including Hebrew language education
- Advocacy on US-Israel policy issues
- Political engagement (substantial 501(c)(4) operations through related entities)

### Funding base
- Sheldon Adelson and Miriam Adelson: substantial founding-era and continuing support
- Wexner Foundation: documented funding
- Other major US Jewish philanthropic donors
- Combined annual budget reportedly $50M+

## Political activity

- IAC hosts annual conferences with major US and Israeli political figures
- Affiliated 501(c)(4) and PAC entities engage in election-related activity
- Documented overlap with Republican Jewish Coalition operations
