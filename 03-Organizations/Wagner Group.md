# Wagner Group

Russian private military company founded c.2014 by [[Yevgeny Prigozhin]] and Dmitry Utkin (Wagner is Utkin's call sign, reportedly derived from his Nazi-era sympathies).

## Deployments (Tier 1)

- 2014-present: Eastern Ukraine (Donbas, then full-scale invasion)
- 2015-present: Syria (alongside Russian regulars)
- 2016-present: Libya (backing Haftar)
- 2018-present: Central African Republic, Sudan, Mali, Mozambique
- 2022-2023: Spearhead of the Bakhmut campaign in Ukraine

## Structure post-Prigozhin

- After the August 2023 Prigozhin plane crash, Wagner forces were partially absorbed into Russian regular military and partially rebranded as "Africa Corps" under direct Kremlin control
- The "private" deniability function has been preserved while the Prigozhin patronage was severed

## Significance

Wagner is the operational arm of Russian hybrid warfare - kinetic action paired with the [[Internet Research Agency]]'s information operations. Both were funded by the same Prigozhin food-service empire and operated under similar deniability cover.
