# All-In Podcast

Weekly US podcast co-hosted by [[Chamath Palihapitiya]], [[David Sacks]], Jason Calacanis, and David Friedberg. Launched 2020.

## Why it matters for vault

- Became one of the most influential vectors for the [[Tech right]] realignment 2022-2024
- Mainstream-credible by-the-numbers reach combined with ideological coherence (broadly anti-Khan-FTC, anti-COVID-mandates, anti-Biden, anti-Ukraine-aid)
- Hosted Trump for interview September 2024; Sacks's June 2024 SF fundraiser for Trump grew out of the podcast network

## Political-financial overlay

- All four hosts are major tech investors / executives
- Sacks particularly used the platform to coordinate Tech right donor positioning
- Audience includes a substantial portion of Silicon Valley founder/operator class - making the podcast functionally a coordination mechanism for the broader Tech right cohort
