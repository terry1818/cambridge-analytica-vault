# Proud Boys

US far-right group founded 2016 by [[Gavin McInnes]]. Self-described "Western chauvinist" street-fighting fraternity. Designated a terrorist entity by Canada and New Zealand (2021).

## January 6 (Tier 1, established at trial)

- May 4, 2023: Federal jury, DC convicted [[Enrique Tarrio]] (national chairman), [[Ethan Nordean]], [[Joseph Biggs]], [[Zachary Rehl]] of seditious conspiracy
- Trial established the Proud Boys organized a "Ministry of Self Defense" structure pre-January 6 and led the breach at multiple Capitol points
- Tarrio sentenced May 2023 to 22 years - longest J6 sentence handed down

## "Stand back and stand by" (Tier 1)

- September 29, 2020 presidential debate: Trump, asked to condemn white supremacists, said "Proud Boys - stand back and stand by"
- Proud Boys adopted the phrase as a logo within hours
- Trump later said he did not know who they were; the FBI assessment cited at Tarrio's trial concluded the phrase functioned as recruitment

## Sources of growth

- Capitalized on the 2020 Portland street-clashes cycle, the COVID lockdown protests, and the BLM counter-protest cycle to grow chapters
- Active in election worker harassment in [[Maricopa County]] and Pennsylvania 2020-2021

## Pardon

- January 20, 2025: Trump issued blanket J6 pardons; all four convicted leaders released
