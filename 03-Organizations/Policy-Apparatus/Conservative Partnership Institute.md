# Conservative Partnership Institute (CPI)

## Summary

**Jim DeMint-led personnel-pipeline + training hub** for the contemporary conservative movement. Founded **2017** by [[Jim DeMint]] after his ouster from Heritage Foundation. **Mark Meadows joined as senior partner** after leaving Trump 1.0 as Chief of Staff. **Operationally the personnel-and-coordination switchboard for the Trump-era movement** - hosts staff training programs, houses dozens of affiliated conservative nonprofits as fiscal sponsor, runs the **Election Integrity Network** ([[Cleta Mitchell]]), and coordinates personnel placement across the broader coalition.

**Heritage Foundation peer at the personnel-and-coordination level**, not the policy-authoring level. Where Heritage authors Project 2025-style blueprints, **CPI staffs and trains the people who implement them**.

## How CPI fits the architecture

CPI is the **personnel-and-coordination keystone** linking:

- **[[The Heritage Foundation]]** (policy authoring) - DeMint's prior employer
- **[[Council for National Policy]]** (donor coordination) - DeMint senior advisor
- **[[Convention of States Action]]** (constitutional capture) - DeMint senior advisor
- **[[America First Legal]]** (litigation) - aligned personnel
- **[[Election Integrity Network]]** (election-denial) - CPI-hosted
- **Trump 2.0 administration** (executive personnel) - placement pipeline

**The single most operationally consequential personnel-pipeline institution of the contemporary conservative movement post-2017.**
