# National Right to Life Committee (NRLC)

## Summary

The **oldest US pro-life advocacy organization**, founded 1968 (pre-Roe). **Federation of state-level Right to Life affiliates** in all 50 states. Less politically aggressive than SBA Pro-Life America but more institutionally durable. Annual **National Right to Life Convention**. Operates a model-legislation drafting arm (NRLC Model Legislation) - **drafted many of the state heartbeat / gestational-limit bills now in force**.
