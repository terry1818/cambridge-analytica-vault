# NRA (National Rifle Association)

## Summary

**Gun-rights advocacy + political-lobbying organization** founded **November 17, 1871**. Originally established for marksmanship training; pivoted **1977 ("Cincinnati Revolt")** to aggressive political-lobbying mode. **One of the most operationally consequential single-issue political organizations in US history** during 1980s-2010s peak. **Substantially diminished post-2018** due to:

- Russian-NRA scandal (Maria Butina conviction 2018-2019)
- New York Attorney General investigations + dissolution attempt (2020-2024)
- Wayne LaPierre lifestyle scandal + ouster (2024)
- Membership decline (~3M from ~5M peak)
- Financial difficulties ($120M+ debt at lowest)

**Operationally:** still major Republican-aligned political force but with **reduced operational capacity** compared to 2010s peak. **CNP member**. Continues NRA-PVF political-action operations + congressional candidate ratings.
