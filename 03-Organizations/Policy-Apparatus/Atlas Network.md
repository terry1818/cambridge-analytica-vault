# Atlas Network

Atlas Network (originally Atlas Economic Research Foundation, founded 1981 by Antony Fisher) is the principal international coordination structure for free-market think-tanks. Atlas operates as the umbrella organisation for 500+ affiliated think-tanks across 100+ countries, providing strategic support, training, and funding-coordination services.

## Operational scope

Atlas Network's operational scope includes:

- Annual Liberty Forum (international gathering of affiliated think-tank leadership)
- Atlas Leadership Academy (training programme for think-tank executives)
- Templeton Freedom Award (annual award for the most-impactful affiliated think-tank work)
- Funding-coordination services connecting US-and-European donors to affiliated think-tanks in target countries
- Strategic-communications support for affiliated think-tanks

## Connection to vault network

Atlas Network's funder base overlaps substantially with the [[Koch Network]], the [[Bradley Foundation]], the [[Templeton Foundation]] (which provides the Templeton Freedom Award funding), and the broader US conservative-movement donor architecture. The international affiliated think-tanks have substantially aligned with the broader libertarian-and-conservative political-economic positioning the funder base advocates.

Specific country-level operational consequences have included:

- Substantial Atlas-affiliated think-tank involvement in the [[Mauricio Macri]] (Argentina, 2015-2019) and [[Javier Milei]] (Argentina, 2023-present) administrations
- Atlas-affiliated think-tank involvement in the [[Jair Bolsonaro]] (Brazil, 2018-2022) administration policy
- Substantial Atlas-affiliated infrastructure in European right-aligned policy ecosystems

## Why this is load-bearing

Atlas Network is the principal institutional infrastructure for the global libertarian-and-conservative think-tank ecosystem. The international-coordination function complements the US-domestic [[State Policy Network]] coordination function; together they constitute the international think-tank-infrastructure architecture.
