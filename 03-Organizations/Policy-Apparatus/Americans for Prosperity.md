# Americans for Prosperity (AFP)

## Summary

**Koch brothers' flagship grassroots political organization.** Founded 2004 (succeeded earlier Citizens for a Sound Economy). **~3.6 million activists in chapter network across all 50 states.** Operates entity stack: **AFP Foundation** (501(c)(3) educational), **AFP** (501(c)(4) advocacy), **AFP Action** (SuperPAC). **$100M+ per cycle** in deployments (2010s peak).

**2024 cycle deviation from broader MAGA:** AFP Action **endorsed Nikki Haley** for 2024 Republican primary (vs. Trump). Haley lost; AFP largely sat out general election. **Documented Koch-network tension with Trumpism** on immigration, tariffs, foreign policy. **But AFP infrastructure remains operationally essential** to broader conservative coalition.
