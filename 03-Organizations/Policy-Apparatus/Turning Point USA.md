# Turning Point USA (TPUSA)

## Summary

Conservative youth-mobilization organization founded **2012 by Charlie Kirk** (at age 18). Grew to **3,000+ campus chapters** across US universities and high schools, ~600K annual conference attendees, mass social-media reach. **Charlie Kirk was assassinated September 10, 2025** at a TPUSA event at Utah Valley University (see [[Assassination of Charlie Kirk 2025-09-10]]). TPUSA continues operating under interim leadership.

**The single most operationally consequential conservative youth-mobilization organization in the US**, and **the bridge entity between the theocratic wing ([[New Apostolic Reformation]]) and the general Trump coalition** for younger demographics. Has a Christian-nationalist faith arm: **[[Turning Point Faith]]**.

- **Founded:** 2012

## 2024 cycle role

TPUSA was central to Trump's 2024 youth-vote mobilization. Field operations in swing states; campus-chapter activation; massive social-media reach via Kirk's personal podcast (Charlie Kirk Show).
