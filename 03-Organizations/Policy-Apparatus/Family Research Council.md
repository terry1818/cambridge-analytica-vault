# Family Research Council (FRC)

## Summary

Christian-right policy + lobbying organization. Founded **1981 by James Dobson** (then originally as part of Focus on the Family; separated to standalone status 1992). Currently led by **Tony Perkins**, who also served as **[[Council for National Policy|CNP]] president 2018-2021**. **Designated a hate group by the Southern Poverty Law Center** since 2010 over its anti-LGBTQ+ positions and disseminations. **One of three principal CNP-affiliated operational arms** (alongside [[State Policy Network]] and [[Leadership Institute]]) - functions as the Christian-right's K-Street-style lobbying / policy-shop in DC.

Hosts the **Values Voter Summit** annual conference (now renamed **Pray Vote Stand Summit**) - a Republican-presidential-candidate must-stop event and key Christian-nationalist coordinating gathering.

## Why it matters

FRC is the **most operationally consequential Christian-right lobbying organization in DC** outside of the Heritage / ALEC / CNP coordination triangle. Perkins's dual role as FRC president AND CNP president (2018-2021) makes FRC the most-integrated of the three principal CNP operational arms.
