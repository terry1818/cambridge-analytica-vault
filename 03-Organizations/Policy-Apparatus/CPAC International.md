# CPAC International

## Summary

The **international franchise network** of the **Conservative Political Action Conference (CPAC)**, operated by the **American Conservative Union (ACU)** under chairman Matt Schlapp. CPAC franchises operate as **right-wing-international coordination venues** combining US conservative figures with European, Latin American, and Asian populist-right leaders. Started 2017 with CPAC Japan; major expansion 2022 with CPAC Hungary. **Hungarian taxpayer money was revealed in 2026 to have funded CPAC operations** per Hungary's new PM disclosure.
