# Marble Freedom Trust

## Summary

**501(c)(4) political-advocacy trust** funded by the **single largest known political donation in US history**: **$1.6 billion from Barre Seid in 2022**. Chaired by **[[Leonard Leo]]** as **sole trustee**, giving him unilateral control over disbursements. The structure: Seid donated his 100% Tripp Lite ownership to Marble; Eaton then acquired Tripp Lite for $1.65B; all proceeds went to Marble. **Avoided ~$400M in capital-gains tax** through the structure. **Doubled the available dark-money capacity of the conservative coalition in a single transaction**.
