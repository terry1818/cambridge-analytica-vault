# United Democracy Project

United Democracy Project (UDP) is a super PAC affiliated with [[AIPAC]] (American Israel Public Affairs Committee). UDP was launched 2022 as the principal pro-Israel super-PAC operational vehicle and has substantially reshaped Democratic-primary politics across the 2022-2024 cycles.

## 2022-2024 cycle operational outputs

**2022 cycle (~$30M deployed):**

- Defeat of Donna Edwards (Maryland House primary, defeated by Glenn Ivey)
- Defeat of Nida Allam (North Carolina House primary)
- Multiple additional defeats of progressive challengers across Democratic primaries

**2024 cycle (~$100M deployed):**

- Defeat of Jamaal Bowman (NY-16 primary), with UDP spending approximately $14M against
- Defeat of Cori Bush (MO-1 primary), with UDP spending approximately $8M against
- Defeat or substantial reduction of margin in multiple additional Democratic-primary races
- Substantial support for selected centrist Democratic incumbents in primary contests

## Operational mechanism

UDP's operational mechanism is the single-issue super-PAC capture model:

- Deploy substantial primary-cycle independent-expenditure spending against specific anti-AIPAC-aligned incumbents
- The spending typically does not advertise as pro-Israel; it focuses on adjacent vulnerability vectors (constituent-service criticism, intra-Democratic issue contrasts, character attacks)
- The aggregate deterrent effect on remaining members of Congress is operationally as significant as the specific defeats produced

The model is operationally analogous to [[Fairshake PAC]]'s crypto-industry capture of congressional crypto policy. Single-issue super PACs with concentrated donor bases can effectively rewrite congressional issue-positioning incentives at modest aggregate-spending scale.

## Funding base

UDP funding has been substantially concentrated:

- [[Miriam Adelson]] and the broader Adelson-family-adjacent donor network (substantial contributions)
- [[Paul Singer]] / Elliott Management network donors
- Pritzker-family adjacent pro-Israel donors
- Various smaller pro-Israel mega-donors

The concentration of funding makes UDP operationally robust to substantial individual-donor turnover while maintaining strategic-direction coherence through the AIPAC institutional anchor.
