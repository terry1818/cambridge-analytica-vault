# Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC)

The Republican Jewish Coalition is the principal Republican-aligned Jewish-American political organisation in the US. Founded 1985; substantially expanded through the 1990s and 2000s; substantially Adelson-family-anchored from the late 2000s through the 2024 cycle.

## Operational scope

RJC operates through:

- Annual Leadership Summit (drawing substantial Republican presidential candidates and party leadership)
- Year-round political-engagement programming targeting Jewish-American voters in battleground states
- Substantial 501(c)(4) and aligned super-PAC political-spending operation
- Coordination with [[AIPAC]] and adjacent pro-Israel infrastructure on shared policy priorities
- Personnel-pipeline programming for Republican-aligned Jewish-American political and policy professionals

## Funding and donor base

The RJC donor base substantially overlaps with the broader [[Adelson]]-family-anchored Republican mega-donor architecture:

- Adelson family (principal historical funder, continuing post-Sheldon-death engagement)
- [[Paul Singer]] / Elliott Management adjacent giving
- Pritzker family adjacent giving (some)
- Various smaller pro-Israel Republican-aligned donors
- Substantial coordination with [[Council for National Policy]] adjacent donor architecture

## Trump-era operational expansion

The Trump first-term and Trump 2.0 administration period has substantially expanded RJC's operational influence within Republican Party politics. The pattern: RJC has become a principal coordination venue for Republican-aligned Israel-policy positioning, with substantial influence on candidate selection, campaign messaging, and post-administration personnel decisions.

## Connection to broader vault network

RJC sits at the intersection of:

- Patron Wing (substantial donor coordination)
- Religious Right (CNP overlap, substantial Christian-Zionist allied operations)
- Israel cluster (principal Republican-aligned Israel-policy coordination)
- International Right (international right-wing coordination via Israel-policy alignment)
