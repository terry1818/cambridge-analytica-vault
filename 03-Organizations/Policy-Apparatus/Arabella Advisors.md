# Arabella Advisors

## Summary

**For-profit DC consulting firm** founded **2005 by Eric Kessler**. Manages four large progressive 501(c)(4) and 501(c)(3) nonprofits as fiscal sponsors:

- **New Venture Fund** (501(c)(3))
- **Hopewell Fund** (501(c)(3))
- **Windward Fund** (501(c)(3))
- **Sixteen Thirty Fund** (501(c)(4))

Combined **~$730M+ revenue** across the four nonprofits in 2019 alone. **The single largest progressive dark-money apparatus** - functions as the **Left's structural equivalent of [[DonorsTrust]] + [[Donors Capital Fund]]** combined.
