# Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America (SBA)

## Summary

**The largest pro-life political advocacy organization in the US.** Founded 1992. Led by **Marjorie Dannenfelser** since 1993. Operates 501(c)(4) + 527 PAC + 501(c)(3) entity stack. **$80M+ deployed in 2024 cycle**. Post-Dobbs operational pivot to **state-level abortion-restriction strategy** + federal **Comstock Act revival** legal framework. **Trump 2.0 access**: Dannenfelser meets regularly with administration; SBA priorities reflected in Project 2025 health-policy chapters.

## Identity

- **Founded:** 1992
- **HQ:** Arlington, VA
- **President:** Marjorie Dannenfelser (1993-present)
- **Type:** 501(c)(4) + PAC + (c)(3)
- **Annual budget:** ~$30M ongoing + cycle SuperPAC

## Post-Dobbs operational priorities

- State-level abortion restrictions (12-week, 6-week, near-total bans)
- Mifepristone restrictions (Comstock Act revival framework)
- Federal **Heartbeat Protection Act** advocacy
- Crisis pregnancy center expansion
- Anti-IVF positioning (more recent, after Alabama embryo ruling)
- Personhood-amendment state-by-state strategy
