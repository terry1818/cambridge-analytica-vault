# IHOPKC (International House of Prayer Kansas City)

## Summary

Charismatic prayer + training center founded **May 7, 1999 by [[Mike Bickle]]** in Grandview, MO (Kansas City suburb). Built around the concept of **"24/7 prayer and worship"** sustained continuously since founding. **One of the most operationally consequential NAR-adjacent training centers in the world** - alumni placed throughout the US and international NAR pastoral pipeline. Operated **International House of Prayer University (IHOPU)** training institution.

**Collapsed October 2023** after sexual-misconduct allegations emerged against Mike Bickle covering decades (1980s-2010s). Multiple victims came forward. Independent investigation (April 2024) substantiated allegations. **IHOPKC formally separated from Bickle November 2023**; renamed **Forerunner Church** 2024 under interim leadership. **Training-pipeline operations substantially damaged** but core community continues at reduced scale.
