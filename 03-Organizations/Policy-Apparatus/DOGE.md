# Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE)

## Summary

**Trump 2.0 executive-office initiative** established **January 20, 2025 via Executive Order**. Created by renaming the existing **U.S. Digital Service** + expanding scope. Initially **co-led by [[Elon Musk]] + Vivek Ramaswamy** until Ramaswamy departed January 2025 for Ohio gubernatorial campaign. **Musk departed mid-2025** after public split with Trump. Operations continued under **Amy Gleason (acting administrator)** + **Steve Davis (operational lead; ex-X engineer)**.

**Operationalized [[Curtis Yarvin]]'s RAGE doctrine** in practice: **mass federal-workforce firings** (~280K+ federal employees terminated, retired-out, or buyout-departed by mid-2026 per CBO estimates), **agency consolidation/elimination** attempts (USAID, Department of Education, multiple smaller agencies), **Palantir-style data integration** across federal databases (see [[Federated Master Database]]).

**Heavy ex-Palantir + ex-Thiel-portfolio personnel.** 3+ DOGE employees ex-Palantir; 2 more from Thiel-funded companies; federal CIO ex-Palantir.

## Trump-Musk split

Musk and Trump publicly split **mid-2025** over Trump's "Big Beautiful Bill" tax legislation (Musk opposed; Trump dismissed). Musk subsequently:
- Departed DOGE leadership role
- Launched separate "America Party" (briefly; minor electoral impact)
- Pivoted X content moderation toward Trump-critical positioning
- Continued operational X / xAI / SpaceX activity

DOGE continued under Amy Gleason + Steve Davis after Musk departure.
