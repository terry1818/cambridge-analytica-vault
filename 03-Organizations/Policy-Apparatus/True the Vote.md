# True the Vote

True the Vote (TTV) is a Houston-based 501(c)(3) founded in 2009 by Catherine Engelbrecht as the operational vehicle for what became the principal Republican poll-watcher and voter-roll-challenge infrastructure. The organisation grew from a local King Street Patriots Tea Party offshoot into a national operation with substantial post-2020 visibility.

## The 2000 Mules narrative

TTV is the principal source for the "2000 Mules" narrative that [[Dinesh D'Souza]] developed into a 2022 film. The narrative claims that geolocation data from cell-phone tracking and ballot-drop-box surveillance footage demonstrate coordinated ballot trafficking that altered the 2020 election outcome. The narrative has been substantially debunked: the geolocation methodology cannot distinguish ballot delivery from any other activity at a drop-box location; the surveillance footage TTV claims as evidence does not show what TTV claims it shows; multiple state-level reviews found no evidence supporting the trafficking claim.

The narrative remains operationally central to the broader Republican election-skepticism programme despite its debunking. TTV's commercial-and-ideological infrastructure has continued to amplify the narrative through 2026.

## EagleAI integration

TTV has partnered with [[EagleAI]] (an automated voter-roll-challenge software platform) to industrialise the voter-roll-challenge programme. EagleAI generates challenges en masse based on database cross-referencing; TTV provides the legal-and-operational infrastructure to file those challenges with county election officials. The combined operation has produced hundreds of thousands of challenges in specific battleground states.

## Funding and operational scale

TTV's annual operating budget is in the low single-digit millions; its operational reach is substantially larger through volunteer infrastructure and through the EIN coordination layer. Funders include traditional conservative-movement donors and post-2020-aligned populist-right donors.

## Why this is load-bearing

TTV is the principal organisational vehicle for the ground-level voter-suppression and voter-roll-challenge operation. The broader election-denial intellectual programme depends on TTV-style operational vehicles to translate intellectual positions into operational state-by-state pressure on election administration.
