# StandWithUs

## Summary

International pro-Israel **education + advocacy** organization founded **2001** in Los Angeles by Roz Rothstein + Esther Renzer in response to the Second Intifada. **Significant US campus presence** + social-media operations. **Primary US mission: combat BDS (Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions) movement on college campuses**. International offices in Israel + UK + multiple. **~$20M+ annual budget**.

**Operates "Emerson Fellows" program** training pro-Israel college students. **Coordinated with AIPAC + JINSA** on US Israel-advocacy infrastructure but operates as **separate org with distinct campus-focused mission**. Major activity post-October-7-2023: surge in campus Israel-defense operations + faculty-targeting campaigns.
