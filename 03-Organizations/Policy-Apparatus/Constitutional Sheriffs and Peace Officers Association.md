# Constitutional Sheriffs and Peace Officers Association (CSPOA)

## Summary

Founded **2011 by Richard Mack** (former Graham County AZ sheriff; co-plaintiff in 1997 SCOTUS case Printz v. United States striking down portions of Brady Act). Promotes the **"constitutional sheriff" doctrine** - that the county sheriff is the supreme law-enforcement officer in their county and **has authority to refuse to enforce federal laws** they deem unconstitutional. Approximately **200+ identified CSPOA-aligned sheriffs** in the US.

**Operationally:** CSPOA-aligned sheriffs have refused federal election-monitoring oversight, threatened ballot-drop-box surveillance, refused gun-law enforcement, and provided cover for far-right paramilitary activity. **The most underdocumented US election-interference risk factor**.
