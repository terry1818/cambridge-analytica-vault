# Election Integrity Network

The Election Integrity Network (EIN) is a Cleta Mitchell-founded coalition housed at the [[Conservative Partnership Institute]] (CPI, the Mark Meadows-affiliated post-2020 Republican coordination infrastructure). EIN is the principal operational coordination vehicle for Republican election-administration challenge work across the 50 states.

## Funding

EIN funding flows through [[Conservative Partnership Institute]] and adjacent (c)(4) vehicles. The [[Concord Fund]] (formerly Judicial Crisis Network, [[Leonard Leo]]-controlled) is the principal large-dollar funder of the broader CPI-EIN ecosystem.

## Why this is load-bearing

EIN is the operational layer that translates the broader conservative election-skepticism programme into ground-level state-by-state election-administration challenges. Without EIN coordination, the various state-level operations would be operationally fragmented; with EIN, they execute coordinated programmes across jurisdictions.
