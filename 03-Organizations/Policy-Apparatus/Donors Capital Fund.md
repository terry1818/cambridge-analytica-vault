## Why two parallel funds matters

The **DT + DCF dual structure** is operationally significant because:

1. **Legal redundancy** - if either is enforcement-targeted, the other can continue operations
2. **Different donor tiers** - allows DCF to serve larger-grant donors without DT's lower-tier overhead
3. **Anonymity reinforcement** - donor money can route through either, providing additional separation from final-recipient attribution
4. **Operational scaling** - parallel funds can absorb ~$200M+ annual contributions without straining either's processing capacity
