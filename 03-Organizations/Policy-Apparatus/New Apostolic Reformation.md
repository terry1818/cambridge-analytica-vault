# New Apostolic Reformation (NAR)

## Summary

The **dominant current operational form of the theocratic wing's mass-mobilization layer**. Named by **C. Peter Wagner** in the 1990s to describe what he saw as "the most radical change in how churches operate since the Protestant Reformation." Combines **Pentecostal/charismatic Christianity** with the **[[Seven Mountains Mandate]]** ([[Dominionism]]) to advocate **spiritual warfare** for **Christian dominion over all spheres of society**. Wagner explicitly grounded NAR in [[R.J. Rushdoony]]'s [[Christian Reconstructionism]] - he wrote: *"Our theological bedrock is what has been known as Dominion Theology... do whatever is necessary, by the power of the Holy Spirit, to retake the dominion of God's creation."*

**Not a single legal entity** - operates as a distributed network of "apostles" and "prophets" who lead congregations, ministries, and conferences globally. **Trump's spiritual advisor [[Paula White]] is NAR**. [[Lance Wallnau]] (NAR figure) is widely credited as the first to "prophesy" Trump's 2016 presidency. **NAR is the current charismatic operational engine that translates dominionist theology into mass political mobilization** - it does for the contemporary moment what the [[Christian Coalition]] did in the 1990s.

## Identity

- **Term coined:** ~1990s by C. Peter Wagner
- **Movement form:** Distributed; not a single 501(c)(3)
- **Coordinating structures:** International Coalition of Apostolic Leaders (ICA); Global Spheres; various apostolic networks
- **Estimated US adherents:** Disputed; estimates range 3-30M

## Theological / strategic distinctives

1. **Modern-day apostles and prophets** - Wagner's central innovation; rejects traditional Protestant view that the apostolic office ended with the New Testament
2. **Five-fold ministry** - apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors, teachers as active offices today
3. **Spiritual warfare** - politics framed as cosmic battle against demonic forces
4. **Strategic-level intercession** - prayer campaigns aimed at specific geographic territory or political outcomes
5. **[[Seven Mountains Mandate]]** - explicit operationalization
6. **[[Dominionism]]** - explicit theological foundation
7. **Spiritual mapping** - identifying "demonic strongholds" in cultural and political institutions
