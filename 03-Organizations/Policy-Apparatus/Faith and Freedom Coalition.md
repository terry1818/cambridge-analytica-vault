# Faith and Freedom Coalition

## Summary

**Founded 2009 by [[Ralph Reed]]** as the effective successor to the [[Christian Coalition]] (Reed having departed in 1997). **Operates as the current principal evangelical-voter mobilization vehicle** of the GOP coalition. Annual "Road to Majority" conferences are now obligatory Republican-presidential-candidate stops. **Heavily integrated with [[Council for National Policy]] coordination** (Reed is a longtime CNP officer). **Reed personally re-mobilized evangelical voters for Trump from 2016 onward** despite Trump's personal incongruity with evangelical norms.

## Identity

- **Founded:** 2009
- **Founder / Chairman:** [[Ralph Reed]]
- **HQ:** Duluth, GA
- **Type:** 501(c)(4) with 501(c)(3) Faith and Freedom Foundation arm
- **Annual conference:** "Road to Majority" (Washington DC)

## Activities

- Voter registration drives in evangelical churches
- Voter scorecards distributed in churches
- Pastor training programs
- "Road to Majority" annual conference
- Candidate vetting / endorsements
- State-level chapter networks
