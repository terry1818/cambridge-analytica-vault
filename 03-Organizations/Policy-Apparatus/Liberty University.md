# Liberty University

Liberty University is a private Christian university in Lynchburg, Virginia, founded by [[Jerry Falwell Sr]] in 1971 as Lynchburg Baptist College. Liberty has grown into the largest evangelical-Christian university in the US, with substantial influence in conservative-movement personnel-pipeline and political-coordination work.

## Operational scale and reach

Liberty's enrolment exceeds 100,000 students across residential and online programmes, making it one of the largest universities in the US by total enrolment. The institutional reach extends through Liberty Counsel (the affiliated religious-liberty-litigation organisation, distinct from but coordinated with [[Alliance Defending Freedom]]), Liberty Online (the substantial online-education operation), and Liberty's broader conservative-policy-engagement infrastructure.

## Falwell Jr era and aftermath

Jerry Falwell Jr served as president from 2007 to 2020. His tenure substantially expanded Liberty's operational scale but ended in 2020 following the disclosure of personal and financial controversies. The post-Falwell Jr leadership transition has been a continuing institutional thread.

Liberty's substantial 2016 endorsement of Trump (Falwell Jr was one of the earliest evangelical-leadership endorsers) was a documented operational pivot point in the broader evangelical-leadership consolidation behind the Trump candidacy.

## Personnel pipeline

Liberty graduates and Liberty-affiliated faculty have featured substantially in Trump first-administration and Trump 2.0 administration personnel. The Liberty-Heritage Foundation-CNP overlap is substantial; multiple Project 2025-affiliated figures have Liberty institutional ties.
