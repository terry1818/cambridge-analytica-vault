# CO2 Coalition

## Summary

Climate-denial 501(c)(3) founded **2015 by Princeton physicist [[Will Happer]]** and others (spun out from earlier "CO2 Is Green" group). **Promotes the framing that increased atmospheric CO2 is beneficial for plant growth + agricultural productivity** - the most academically-credentialed wing of US climate denial. Approximately **120+ scientist + economist members**. Co-founder **Patrick Moore** (early Greenpeace member turned climate-skeptic).

**Trump 1.0 influence:** Happer served on the National Security Council 2018-2019 + attempted to organize a "Presidential Committee on Climate Security" specifically to challenge climate-science consensus. **Trump 2.0 ongoing influence:** CO2 Coalition members appearing as expert witnesses in DOJ EPA-rollback litigation + agency reform processes.
