# Heartland Institute

## Summary

Conservative-libertarian think tank founded **1984 by Joseph Bast** in Chicago suburbs. **The single most operationally consequential climate-denial think tank in the US.** Publishes ***The Climate Change Reconsidered*** report series (NIPCC; deliberately mimicking the IPCC name). Hosts annual **International Conference on Climate Change** (ICCC; recurring denial-ist gathering). **Barre Seid was longtime major funder** (the same Seid whose 2022 $1.6B donation went to [[Leonard Leo]]'s [[Marble Freedom Trust]]).

**2012 "Unabomber billboard" scandal**: Heartland deployed an Illinois billboard with Ted Kaczynski's face captioned "I still believe in global warming. Do you?" - implying climate-change-believers are Unabomber-equivalents. Resulting public-relations disaster cost Heartland multiple major corporate funders. Operations continued at reduced corporate-funding base.

**State Policy Network member** (Illinois affiliate).
