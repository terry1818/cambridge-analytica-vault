# Alliance Defending Freedom

## Summary

Major **Christian-right legal advocacy organization** founded 1994. Headquarters Scottsdale AZ. **The legal engine behind the Dobbs decision** (2022 overturn of Roe v Wade) and other landmark Supreme Court conservative legal wins. **[[Michael Farris]] was CEO and General Counsel from 2017 through 2022**, taking the helm immediately after stepping down from HSLDA. Designed and executed the litigation strategy that brought Dobbs to the Court. **Farris's involvement directly links the [[Convention of States Action]] / Article V movement to the same legal infrastructure that delivered Dobbs.** Current CEO: Kristen Waggoner.

## Why ADF matters in this domain

ADF is the **Christian-nationalist legal counterpart** to the [[Article V Convention]] strategy. If COS succeeds in convening a convention and ratifying amendments:

- ADF is the legal infrastructure ready to defend those amendments
- ADF litigators have track record arguing before SCOTUS
- ADF positions on religious freedom, abortion, LGBTQ+ rights map directly to amendment proposals that would emerge from a runaway convention

**Farris is the structural bridge:** he co-founded the legal-defense org for homeschoolers (HSLDA), founded a Christian-nationalist college (Patrick Henry), led the Christian-right legal powerhouse (ADF), and co-founded the constitutional-amendment mechanism (COS). All four arms work in concert.
