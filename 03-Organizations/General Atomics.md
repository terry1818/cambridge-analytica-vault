# General Atomics

US privately-held defense and energy company. Founded 1955 (originally as General Dynamics nuclear division; spun off 1986). Headquartered in San Diego.

## Notable products

- Predator drone (RQ-1, MQ-1) - the defining counterterrorism platform of the 2001-2020 period
- Reaper drone (MQ-9)
- Avenger drone (MQ-20)
- Inertial confinement fusion research

## Significance for vault

One of the key US drone-warfare contractors. Predator/Reaper platforms central to the US drone-strike program in Pakistan, Yemen, Somalia, and other theaters. Increasingly relevant to domestic surveillance applications.
