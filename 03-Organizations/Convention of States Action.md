# Convention of States Action

US 501(c)(4) organization. Operating arm of the modern Convention of States movement. President: [[Mark Meckler]]. Parent: Citizens for Self-Governance (501(c)(3)).

## Stated goal

Mobilizing state legislatures to pass applications calling for an Article V convention of states with three stated subjects:
1. Term limits for federal officials (Congress and judiciary)
2. Limits on federal spending and taxing
3. Limits on federal power and jurisdiction

## State applications passed (Tier 1, current count 19 as of latest reporting)

Active applications (as of latest filings):
Alabama, Alaska, Arizona, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Indiana, Louisiana, Mississippi, Missouri, Nebraska, North Carolina, North Dakota, Oklahoma, South Carolina, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, West Virginia, Wisconsin

(Some applications are subject-limited; not all are unrestricted Article V calls. The count of "Article V applications" varies by definition.)

## Threshold

Article V requires applications from 2/3 of state legislatures (34) to compel Congress to call a convention. The COS application count is the operative metric for the movement's progress toward that threshold.

## Funding

- Donor disclosure limited by 501(c)(4) opacity

- Annual revenue ~-20M per recent 990 filings
