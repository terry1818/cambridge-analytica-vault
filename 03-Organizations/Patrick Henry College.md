# Patrick Henry College

Private Christian college in Purcellville, Virginia. Founded 2000 by Michael Farris. Approximately 400 students.

## Strategic positioning

- Founded explicitly to train homeschooled evangelicals for government service
- Mission: provide a Christian-foundation pipeline into federal administration, judiciary, congressional staff
- Documented placement of graduates in White House internships, congressional staff, federal agencies
- Notable pipeline to Republican congressional offices and conservative DC think tanks

## Significance for vault

Patrick Henry College is the explicit institutional embodiment of the [[Seven Mountain Mandate]] approach applied to the "government mountain" - training a credentialed cadre to occupy federal government positions on an explicitly Christian-nationalist foundation. Differs from [[Hillsdale College]] (older, larger, more academically broad) in being narrower and more direct in mission.
