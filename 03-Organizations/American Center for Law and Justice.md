# American Center for Law and Justice (ACLJ)

US conservative Christian legal-advocacy organization. Founded 1990 by Pat Robertson. Currently led by Jay Sekulow.

## Significance

ACLJ is one of the founding-era [[Religious right]] litigation organizations. Jay Sekulow was personal legal counsel to Donald Trump during the [[First Impeachment of Donald Trump]] period and beyond.
