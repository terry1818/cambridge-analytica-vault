# Donald J. Trump Foundation

US charitable foundation founded 1988 by [[Donald Trump]]. Dissolved by court order December 2018 following New York State Attorney General lawsuit.

## NY AG lawsuit (Tier 1)

- 2018: AG [[Barbara Underwood]] sued the foundation alleging "pattern of persistent illegal conduct"
- Documented misuse: foundation funds used to settle Trump Organization legal disputes, purchase Trump-portrait artwork, fund Trump's 2016 presidential campaign
- December 18, 2018: Settlement included dissolution
- November 2019:  judgment + restitution to 8 charities
