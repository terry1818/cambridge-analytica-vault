# Bob Jones University

US fundamentalist Christian college in Greenville, South Carolina. Founded 1927.

## Civil rights history (Tier 1)

- 1971: Green v. Connally - IRS revoked tax-exempt status of segregated private schools including BJU
- BJU resisted desegregation through the 1970s and into the 1980s
- 1983: Bob Jones University v. United States - Supreme Court upheld IRS revocation 8-1
