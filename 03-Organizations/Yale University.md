# Yale University

Private research university in New Haven, Connecticut. Founded 1701.

## Vault relevance

Yale Law School was the founding venue (with University of Chicago Law) of the [[Federalist Society]] in 1982. Multiple Federalist Society co-founders (Steven Calabresi, Lee Liberman Otis) were Yale Law students. Multiple Trump-administration figures including [[J.D. Vance]] are Yale Law graduates.
