# Founders Fund

## Summary

Venture capital firm co-founded in 2005 by **[[Peter Thiel]]**, Ken Howery, and Luke Nosek. Manages multiple funds totaling tens of billions in assets under management. **Portfolio includes some of the most consequential tech companies of the past 20 years**: SpaceX, Palantir, Airbnb, Stripe, Facebook (early), Lyft, Spotify, and others.

## Tech Right ecosystem role

Founders Fund is **central to the Thiel network's operational reach**:

- Vehicle for Thiel's investment activity since 2005
- Major investor in **[[Anduril]]** (defense AI startup; Palmer Luckey founder)
- Major investor in **[[Palantir Technologies]]** (Thiel co-founded; Founders Fund early backer)
- Backed multiple Thiel Fellow-founded companies
- Partners include figures who later moved into government / political roles
