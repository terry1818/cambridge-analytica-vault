# Narya Capital

## Summary

US venture capital firm founded in **2019** by **[[JD Vance]]** in Cincinnati, Ohio. **[[Peter Thiel]] served as anchor investor** for the firms first fund (~$93M raised). Named after one of the Three Elven Rings from Tolkien's *The Lord of the Rings* (alongside Thiels other "ring"-themed entities: Mithril, Palantir, the broader Tolkien naming convention popular in Thiel ecosystem).

## Why Narya matters

Narya is the **direct financial vehicle** through which Thiel transitioned Vance from venture-capital principal (Mithril) to politically-visible executive (Senate candidate). The pattern:

1. Thiel anchors Vance's own VC firm (Narya, 2019)
2. Narya gives Vance an Ohio-based business presence and "successful businessman" narrative
3. Vance simultaneously builds political profile (Tucker Carlson appearances, etc.)
4. 2022: Thiel funds Vance Senate campaign
5. Vance wins Senate seat
6. 2024: Vance becomes VP nominee
7. 2025: VP

Without the Narya step, Vance would have been a Yale Law / Mithril figure without independent Ohio business presence. **Narya manufactured the political-biography component** that made the Senate run plausible.
