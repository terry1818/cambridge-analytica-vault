# PragerU

## Summary

Conservative video-content organization founded **2009 by [[Dennis Prager]]** and Allen Estrin. **Not an actual university** - operates as 501(c)(3) producing short conservative video content. **1+ billion total video views**; major social-media reach. **PragerU Kids** initiative produces K-12 video curriculum content - **now adopted as official supplemental curriculum in Florida, Oklahoma, Texas, New Hampshire, Montana, and Louisiana public schools** (2023-2026).

The single most operationally consequential **conservative K-12 curriculum infrastructure** in US history measured by states-adopted-as-public-school-curriculum. Funded by Wilks brothers (Texas oil), Bradley Foundation, DeVos family, Adelson family, and broader conservative donor network.
