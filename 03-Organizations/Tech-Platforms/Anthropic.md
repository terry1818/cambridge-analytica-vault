# Anthropic

## Summary

US AI research and product company founded in **2021** by **Dario Amodei** (former OpenAI VP of Research), **Daniela Amodei** (former OpenAI VP of Safety and Policy), and other ex-OpenAI researchers. Created the **Claude** family of large language models. Positioned publicly as the **"AI safety alternative" to OpenAI** -- emphasizing constitutional AI training and red-teaming methodology.

## Backers

- **Amazon** -- invested $4B+ in two tranches 2023-2024; major cloud-compute partnership (AWS)
- **Google** -- invested $2B+
- **Salesforce, Zoom**, others -- strategic investors

## CA / influence-operations relevance

Anthropic is **central to AI-era influence operations response** alongside OpenAI:

- Publishes detailed responsible-scaling policy
- Publishes regular threat reporting (less granular than OpenAI but comprehensive)
- Claude has been used by both influence-ops actors and defenders
- Constitutional AI methodology attempts to make Claude robust against misuse

## Political positioning

Anthropic has positioned **more independently** than OpenAI from Trump 2.0:

- No major Trump donations or alignment statements
- Continued AI safety focus through 2025
- Some Trump 2.0 administration figures publicly critical of Anthropic for "wokeness"
- Less directly involved in DOGE / federal data initiatives

## Effective Altruism heritage

Anthropic was founded with substantial **Effective Altruism (EA)** community alignment:

- Multiple founders had EA backgrounds
- Initial investors included EA-aligned figures
- Sam Bankman-Fried (FTX, pre-collapse) was a major early investor (~$500M)
- EA influence reduced after FTX collapse but cultural elements remain
