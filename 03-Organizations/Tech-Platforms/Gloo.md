# Gloo

## Summary

Religious software company founded **2013 in Boulder, CO**. Built the **"Insights" data platform** for churches, in partnership with **[[United in Purpose]]** and **[[COFI]]**, allowing churches and political operatives to acquire **demographic + psychographic + behavioral data on potential congregants and voters**. Per [[Source People You May Know 2020]] reporting, Gloo's platform was **commissioned with Koch-network funding** as part of a collaboration with **[[Cambridge Analytica]]** to apply CA-style psychographic targeting to **vulnerable populations (mental illness, grief, addiction) for evangelical recruitment** and downstream **far-right political mobilization**. Described in Religion Dispatches reporting as **"Cambridge Analytica on steroids."**

## What Gloo does

- Aggregates data from church management systems, online surveys, social media
- Builds psychographic profiles of potential congregants and seekers
- Targets people in vulnerable states (grief, addiction, mental illness) for evangelical outreach
- Connects churches with vetted service-provider partners
- "Insights" platform sells access to this data to churches, charities, and per investigative reporting, political operatives
