# Anduril Industries

## Summary

Defense-tech company founded **2017 by Palmer Luckey** (Oculus VR founder ousted from Facebook 2017 after Trump donation disclosure) with **Trae Stephens** (Founders Fund partner; Thiel orbit) and others. Builds AI-powered autonomous defense systems including **Sentry Towers** (border surveillance), **Roadrunner** (counter-drone), **Lattice** (command-and-control AI), **Anvil** (counter-drone interceptor). **$14B+ valuation** as of 2024; substantially higher post-Trump-2.0 contract surge. **Thiel-orbit defense-tech complement to Palantir**: where Palantir does data integration, Anduril builds the physical systems.

**Major Trump 2.0 contracts:** Customs and Border Protection surveillance tower expansion, DoD autonomous-systems programs, JADC2-adjacent work. **Palmer Luckey + Trae Stephens are explicit Trump-aligned tech-right operators**.
