# a16z (Andreessen Horowitz)

## Summary

US venture capital firm founded in **2009** by [[Marc Andreessen]] and Ben Horowitz. Manages tens of billions in assets under management. Major early investor in Facebook, Twitter, Airbnb, Stripe, Coinbase, OpenSea, and hundreds of others. **By 2020-2026, a16z has become the most politically-active major Silicon Valley VC firm** -- operating effectively as a political infrastructure organization alongside its investment activities.

## Political activity 2020-2026

### Cryptocurrency advocacy
- **a16z crypto** arm has been the principal industry-wide political-advocacy organization for crypto regulation
- Major funder of **[[Fairshake PAC]]** -- the largest crypto-industry political-spending vehicle in 2024
- a16z partners testify regularly at Congressional hearings
- Lobbied hard against SEC enforcement and for crypto-favorable legislation

### AI policy advocacy
- a16z published "Little Tech" agenda 2024 -- opposing federal AI safety regulation
- Argued AI safety concerns are "regulatory capture" by large incumbents
- Aligned with Trump 2.0 deregulation approach to AI
- Funded various lobby groups opposing AI regulation

### "American Dynamism" investment thesis
- Coined by a16z partner Katherine Boyle 2022+
- Argued for investing in defense, energy, manufacturing, biotech aligned with US national interests
- Aligned with broader Tech Right defense-tech orientation
- Major investor in defense-tech startups including some [[Anduril]] adjacent ecosystem

### 2024 election
- Andreessen and Horowitz publicly endorsed Trump July 2024
- Major donations from a16z partners (individual capacity)
- Hosted Trump fundraisers
- Andreessen reportedly key Trump transition advisor on tech policy

### "Techno-Optimist Manifesto" (October 2023)
- Andreessen-authored manifesto became foundational Tech Right text
- Aligned with broader e/acc (effective accelerationism) movement
- Named specific institutional enemies: trust & safety, tech ethics, sustainability

## Tech Right ecosystem position

a16z is **central to the 2020-2026 Tech Right consolidation** alongside the Thiel network. While Thiel was the first Silicon Valley billionaire Trump backer (2016), a16z represents the **broader institutional Tech Right alignment** that became consensus by 2024.
