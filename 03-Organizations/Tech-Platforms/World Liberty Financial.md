# World Liberty Financial

World Liberty Financial (WLF) is a cryptocurrency venture launched in 2024 with substantial documented Trump-family involvement. The venture's structure (a DeFi-aligned operation with token issuance, governance-token mechanics, and adjacent crypto-financial-services products) has been the subject of substantial scrutiny because of the Trump-family financial interest combined with the Trump 2.0 administration's crypto-policy decisions.

## Operational structure

WLF launched in September 2024. The corporate structure includes:

- Trump family members (Donald Trump Jr., Eric Trump, Barron Trump publicly named in various capacities)
- Steve Witkoff and Zach Witkoff (Trump-administration adjacent figures, Witkoff later named Trump Middle East envoy)
- Various crypto-industry technical co-founders

The token launch and the subsequent governance-token mechanics have produced substantial revenue flows to the Trump-family-affiliated entities.

## Policy-decision intersection

The Trump 2.0 administration's crypto-policy decisions through 2025-2026 have substantially benefited WLF-and-adjacent ventures:

- Executive orders on crypto-favourable regulatory positioning
- SEC restructuring under the Atkins-and-adjacent-leadership that has substantially reduced crypto enforcement
- Department of Justice positioning on crypto-prosecution that has reduced prosecutorial pressure on the broader crypto-industry ecosystem
- The proposed Strategic Crypto Reserve and adjacent federal-procurement programmes

The Trump-family financial interest in crypto-favourable policy combined with Trump-administration policy decisions affecting that interest has produced documented conflict-of-interest concerns that the [[Source Quartz Trump World Liberty Financial Corruption]] and adjacent reporting has catalogued.

## Trump memecoins

Separate from WLF, Donald Trump and Melania Trump each launched personal memecoins in January 2025 ( and  respectively). The memecoins produced substantial initial valuation followed by substantial declines documented in [[Source Gizmodo Trump Memecoin 95 Down]]. The memecoin launches are operationally significant as the most direct documented case of a sitting US President launching a personal-financial-instrument that members of the public could purchase, with no documented disclosure of holding-related conflicts.
