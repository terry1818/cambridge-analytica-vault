# PayPal Mafia

## Summary

Informal network of approximately **15-20 individuals** who worked at PayPal in the late 1990s / early 2000s. After PayPal was sold to eBay in **2002 for $1.5 billion**, these individuals went on to found, fund, or lead some of the most influential technology companies of the subsequent 20 years. The term "PayPal Mafia" was coined by Fortune magazine in 2007.
