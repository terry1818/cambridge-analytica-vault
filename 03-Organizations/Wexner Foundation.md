## Programs (Tier 1)

### Wexner Israel Fellowship (1989-present)
- Leadership development program for Israeli mid-career public-sector professionals
- Operates at Harvard Kennedy School
- Approximately 10 fellows per year
- 700+ alumni across Israeli government, NGO, and public-sector roles

### Wexner Heritage Program
- US-based program for emerging Jewish-American leaders
- Two-year program
- 2,500+ alumni in US Jewish community leadership
