# The Heritage Foundation

Conservative US think tank founded 1973 by [[Paul Weyrich]], [[Edwin Feulner]], and [[Joseph Coors]]. Headquartered in Washington DC.

## Founding and trajectory

- 1973: Founded by Weyrich (also co-founded Moral Majority, ALEC, Free Congress Foundation) and Feulner with Joseph Coors capital
- 1981: Published the first "Mandate for Leadership" - 1,000+ page transition blueprint for the Reagan administration. Reagan adopted ~60% of recommendations
- 1980s-2000s: Tax policy, Cold War policy, welfare reform
- 2010s: Heritage Action 501(c)(4) wing became more aggressively political under [[Jim DeMint]]
- 2021-present: Under [[Kevin Roberts]], shifted hard toward Christian nationalist / national conservative framing

## Mandate for Leadership lineage

- 1981 (Reagan)
- 1985, 1989, 1993, 1997, 2001, 2005, 2009, 2013, 2017 - sequential transition books
- 2025 ([[Project 2025]]) - 922 pages, most ambitious in scope
