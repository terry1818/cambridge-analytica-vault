# Black Cube

Israeli-owned private intelligence and business intelligence firm. Founded 2010 by former [[Mossad]] officers including former Mossad Director Meir Dagan (board member) and CEO Avi Yanus. Headquartered in Tel Aviv with offices in London, Madrid, Paris, and previously in New York. **One of the most-cited private-sector intelligence-for-hire firms.** Clients have publicly included [[Harvey Weinstein]] (to target Weinstein's accusers), Israeli political actors, corporate clients in disputes, and various political clients.

In this vault, Black Cube is one of the load-bearing examples in the [[Hypothesis - Israeli intel-for-hire is parallel to Russian state operations]] structural argument.

## Documented operations

### Harvey Weinstein operation (2016-2017)

Black Cube was retained by [[Harvey Weinstein]] beginning 2016 to investigate Weinstein's accusers and journalists investigating him (Ronan Farrow, others). Operatives used false identities including:
- "Diana Filip" -- a fictitious women's-rights activist who met repeatedly with Rose McGowan
- Other false-identity operations against journalists

The Weinstein operation was documented by Ronan Farrow in *The New Yorker* (November 2017) and the book *Catch and Kill* (2019).

### Wirecard operation

Investigated and pressured journalists investigating the German payment company Wirecard during its accounting fraud period; documented in Wirecard scandal reporting.

### Other reported clients

Per reporting in Israeli press, *Haaretz*, *The New York Times*, and others:
- Israeli political actors
- Various corporate clients in litigation
- Government clients (some Israeli-government-adjacent)
