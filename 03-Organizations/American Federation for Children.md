# American Federation for Children

US 501(c)(4) organization. Top advocacy organization for school-choice and voucher policies. Chaired by [[Betsy DeVos]] until her 2017 confirmation as Secretary of Education.

## Programs

- 50-state school-choice / voucher policy organizing
- Annual policy summit
- Endorsement / opposition of state legislative candidates based on school-choice records
- Major spender in state-level legislative races

## Significance

AFC is the operational arm of the long-running [[Education capture]] project. The DeVos family has provided substantial funding through their family foundation and the broader [[DeVos network]]; multiple other major donors have aligned.

The acceleration of US voucher-program adoption in red and purple states 2020-2024 (Arizona, Iowa, Indiana, Utah, West Virginia, etc.) is substantially attributable to AFC's state-by-state organizing.
