# Skydance Media

## Summary

**David Ellison-founded film + TV production company** (founded 2010 with funding from [[Larry Ellison]] + Paul Allen). **Merged with Paramount Global August 7, 2024** to form [[Paramount Skydance Corporation]]; Skydance Media operations were absorbed into the combined entity.

**Notable productions during 2010-2024 standalone era:**
- **Mission: Impossible franchise** (Ghost Protocol 2011 onward; with Tom Cruise + Paramount)
- **Top Gun: Maverick** (2022; $1.5B box office)
- **Jack Reacher franchise**
- **World War Z, Terminator: Dark Fate, 6 Underground**
- **Skydance Animation** (Apple TV+ partnership including Luck, Spellbound)
- **Skydance Television** (Reacher series on Amazon, Foundation on Apple TV+, multiple Tom Clancy adaptations)
- **Skydance Sports** (founded 2022; combat sports + boxing)

## Operational significance

Skydance positioned itself **2010-2024 as the principal financial-partner to Paramount Pictures** for tentpole franchise films. **This positioning enabled the 2024 merger** by establishing operational integration with Paramount well before the corporate combination.
