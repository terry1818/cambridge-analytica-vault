# CBS News

## Summary

**Legacy US broadcast news division** of [[Paramount Skydance Corporation]] (since August 2024 Skydance-Paramount merger). **One of the "Big Three" US broadcast news organizations** alongside NBC News + ABC News. **Historical legacy includes Edward R. Murrow, Walter Cronkite, Mike Wallace, 60 Minutes.**

**Programs include:**
- **60 Minutes** (longest-running news magazine; Sunday evenings)
- **CBS Evening News**
- **CBS Mornings**
- **Face the Nation** (Sunday political)
- **48 Hours** (true crime/investigative)
- **CBS News Streaming Network** (24/7 streaming)

**Post-merger editorial shift (documented):**
- **July 2025:** Trump 60 Minutes lawsuit settled $16M (widely understood as FCC-approval condition)
- **November 2025:** [[Bari Weiss]] installed as Editor-in-Chief
- **December 2025+:** Multiple high-profile departures including 60 Minutes correspondents, CBS News digital staff, Face the Nation production team
- **Multiple editorial decisions** documented as ideologically rightward
