# The Free Press

## Summary

**Bari Weiss-founded media publication** started 2022 as **"Common Sense"** Substack; renamed **The Free Press** in 2023. Built ~$0 to ~$150M acquisition valuation in ~3 years via Substack subscription + later subscription website + podcast (Honestly with Bari Weiss). **Acquired by Paramount Skydance Corporation October 2025 for ~$150M.** **Weiss subsequently installed as CBS News Editor-in-Chief November 2025.**

**Editorial positioning:** centrist-to-right; heavy on Israel/Palestine coverage, free-speech / anti-cancel-culture, anti-woke, Trump-curious without endorsing Trump explicitly. **Substantial overlap with [[Israel Cluster MOC|Israel-cluster]] editorial positioning.**

**Operationally significant** as:
- The first major **Substack-to-broadcast-news editorial transition** at this scale in US media
- The **mechanism by which Paramount Skydance imported new editorial leadership** for CBS News
- A documented vector for **CBS News editorial shift** post-Ellison acquisition
