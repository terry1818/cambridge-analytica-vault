# Forbidden Stories

International collaborative investigative-journalism network founded 2017 by Laurent Richard (French journalist). Mission: continue investigations of journalists killed or silenced.

## Major investigations

- 2021: [[Pegasus Project]] - exposing NSO Group's Pegasus spyware deployments (with Amnesty International)
- 2023: [[Team Jorge]] / "Story Killers" - exposing the Israeli for-hire information-warfare firm of Tal Hanan
- 2024: Multiple investigations of state-sponsored journalist surveillance

## Methodology

- Coordinates dozens of international news outlets to publish simultaneously
- Distributes risk across multiple jurisdictions
- Makes silencing individual outlets ineffective
