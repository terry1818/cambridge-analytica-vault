# Manhattan Institute

US conservative think tank founded 1978. New York-based. Originally focused on urban policy; expanded to cover crime, education, race policy, energy.

## Historical influence

- 1980s-1990s: Major influence on Rudy Giuliani's NYC mayoralty (broken windows policing, welfare reform)
- 2000s-2010s: Charles Murray's "Coming Apart" and related work
- 2020s: [[Christopher Rufo]]'s anti-CRT / anti-DEI campaign was MI-hosted

## Funding

- Long-time recipient from Bradley Foundation, Scaife, Olin, JM Foundation
- Has maintained more elite-academic positioning than Heritage; less explicitly movement-organizing

## Significance

Manhattan Institute provides the academically-credentialed face for movement-conservative policy work. Rufo's installation at MI in 2021 marked the institute's pivot toward more aggressive culture-war strategic operations.
