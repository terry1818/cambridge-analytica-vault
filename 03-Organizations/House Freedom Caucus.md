# House Freedom Caucus

US House of Representatives Republican caucus founded 2015. Hard-right faction within the broader Republican conference.

## Founding members

- Jim Jordan, Mark Meadows (later White House CoS), Raul Labrador, Mick Mulvaney

## Significance

The Freedom Caucus has functioned as the operational vehicle for hard-right policy positions within the House GOP. Multiple Speaker negotiations have hinged on Freedom Caucus support or withholding. The 2023 Kevin McCarthy speakership / removal cycle was substantially Freedom Caucus-driven.
