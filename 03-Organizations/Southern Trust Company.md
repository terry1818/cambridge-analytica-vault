# Southern Trust Company

Epstein-operated entity registered in the US Virgin Islands. Purportedly engaged in "data analytics" and DNA-database business, though the substantive business activity has never been publicly documented. Granted USVI Economic Development Authority (EDA) tax incentives in February 2014.

## Key facts

- **USVI EDA benefits 2014** (tier 1): Southern Trust received tax benefits saving an estimated $73M+ from 2014 through 2019, conditional on operating a substantive business in USVI and employing a stated headcount. [[Source: USVI EDA records]].
- **Purported business** (tier 2): documents and Epstein public statements describe Southern Trust as a "data mining" / "DNA database" operation. No public-facing product, customer roster, or substantive output has been identified.
- **USVI estate lawsuit 2020** (tier 1): the Government of the USVI filed suit against Epstein's estate, alleging Southern Trust was a shell used to facilitate trafficking-related travel and to abuse the EDA program. The case resulted in a 2022 settlement of approximately $105M from the estate to the USVI government.
- **Source of Epstein's post-2008 wealth** (tier 3 / open question): Southern Trust is widely speculated to be one vehicle through which Epstein generated or laundered post-NPA wealth, given his Wall Street advisory business effectively ended with the conviction. No public investigation has fully traced the cash flows.
