# Founders Fund

US venture capital firm founded 2005 by [[Peter Thiel]], [[Ken Howery]], [[Luke Nosek]], and Sean Parker. Approximately  AUM (2024).

## Portfolio (selected)

- [[SpaceX]] - early lead investor
- [[Palantir]] - co-investor; Thiel co-founded both
- [[Anduril]] - defense tech founded by Palmer Luckey; Founders Fund lead
- [[Stripe]], Facebook (early stake via Thiel personally), Airbnb, Lyft
- Most recently: Stargate-adjacent compute and AI infrastructure investments

## Ideological positioning

Differs from typical VC firms in explicit ideological framing:

- Founders Fund "manifesto" (2011) - "We wanted flying cars, instead we got 140 characters"
- Decade-long focus on "frontier" technologies (defense, space, biotech, energy) over consumer software
- Heavy overlap with the [[Tech right]] political project; Founders Fund partners and portfolio CEOs are core 2024 Trump donors
