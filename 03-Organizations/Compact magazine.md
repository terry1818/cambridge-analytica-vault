# Compact magazine

US online journal launched March 2022. Subtitled "a radical American journal." Founded by Sohrab Ahmari (post-liberal Catholic), Matthew Schmitz, and Edwin Aponte (left-populist).

## Editorial positioning

- Self-described as "Marxist-Catholic" or "post-liberal left-right coalition"
- Critical of mainstream conservatism (anti-Cold-War-consensus) and mainstream liberalism
- Has published [[Patrick Deneen]], [[Adrian Vermeule]], Glenn Greenwald, Slavoj Zizek, Christopher Caldwell

## Significance for vault

Compact is the most-visible media institution at the boundary of post-liberal Catholic intellectualism and the broader [[Tech right]] / national-conservative ideological space. It functions as the publication-of-record for figures whose views fit neither traditional liberalism nor conventional conservatism but who are intellectually upstream of [[J.D. Vance]] and the contemporary post-liberal current.
