# OpenAI

## Summary

**Frontier AI laboratory** founded December 11, 2015 by Sam Altman + Greg Brockman + Elon Musk + Ilya Sutskever + others. **Originally non-profit 501(c)(3)**; subsequently created OpenAI LP (2019) for-profit subsidiary; **complex multi-entity restructuring to for-profit ongoing 2024-2026**. **Sam Altman CEO since 2019** (briefly removed November 2023; reinstated within days). **Microsoft majority financial relationship 2019-2024** (~$13B+ investment cumulative).

**Operationally important** as:
- **Frontier-model leader** (GPT-3/3.5/4/4o/5 series; o-series reasoning models)
- **Primary [[Project Stargate]] tenant** ($500B AI infrastructure with Oracle + SoftBank + MGX-UAE)
- **Documented Trump 2.0 + tech-right operational alignment** (Altman standing with Trump at Stargate announcement)
- **Restructuring to for-profit** has been politically + legally contested
