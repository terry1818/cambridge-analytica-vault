# Facebook (Meta Platforms)

## Summary

**Major US social-media platform + parent company Meta Platforms Inc.** Founded 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg. **The platform on which the Cambridge Analytica data harvest occurred** via Facebook's **Graph API v1.0** which (until 2014) allowed apps installed by ~270K users to harvest data from those users' entire friend networks (~87M people total in the CA case).

**Enforcement aftermath:**

- **2018-10:** UK ICO £500K fine (maximum then-available under pre-GDPR law)
- **2019-07:** US FTC $5B settlement (largest privacy settlement in US history at time)
- **2019:** DCMS Final Report (UK Parliament)
- **2020-2025:** Continuing EU + state-level investigations

**Operationally:** Facebook's targeting infrastructure (custom audiences, lookalikes, friend-of-friend dynamics) remains the primary platform-side ad-targeting capability for political campaigns. Meta now controls Instagram + WhatsApp + Facebook + Threads.
