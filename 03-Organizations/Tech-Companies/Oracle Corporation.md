# Oracle Corporation

## Summary

**Enterprise-software + cloud-infrastructure giant** founded **June 16, 1977** by **[[Larry Ellison]]** + Bob Miner + Ed Oates. Originally **Software Development Laboratories** working on a **CIA database contract codenamed "Oracle"** (later adopted as company name). **One of the largest enterprise-software companies in the world** ($50B+ annual revenue, 160K+ employees).

**Operationally:** Oracle is **structurally important to the Trump 2.0 architecture** through multiple distinct vectors that compound:

1. **TikTok US data hosting (Project Texas)** - Oracle hosts US TikTok user data per the 2022 ByteDance arrangement; positioned as US-side white-knight in TikTok divestment saga 2020-2025
2. **[[Project Stargate]] tenant** - Oracle is primary data-center provider for the $500B Stargate AI infrastructure commitment (announced January 21, 2025; Ellison stood with Trump + [[Sam Altman]] + Masayoshi Son at White House announcement)
3. **Federal contracts** - massive ongoing federal cloud + database contracts including DoD, intelligence community, civilian agencies
4. **Foundational CIA relationship** - the company's original 1977 contract was with the CIA; relationship has continued throughout company history
5. **Israeli operations** - substantial Israeli engineering presence; Ellison personally close to Netanyahu

## TikTok / ByteDance / Project Texas

**The Oracle-TikTok thread is one of the most operationally consequential single relationships in Trump-era tech-policy.**

- **2020:** Trump first-term EO ordered TikTok divestment; Oracle + Walmart emerged as US-side acquirer candidate (deal never closed)
- **2022:** ByteDance + Oracle finalized "Project Texas" - Oracle hosts ALL US TikTok user data on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure in dedicated Texas data centers
- **2024-04:** PAFACA (Protecting Americans from Foreign Adversary Controlled Applications Act) signed; mandates ByteDance divestiture
- **2025:** Trump 2.0 extended PAFACA deadline multiple times; complex deal architecture involving Oracle as US partner ultimately emerged
- **Strategic positioning:** Oracle is the **template for "national security through US-partner control"** of Chinese-owned platforms

This is the single most operationally important Oracle-government nexus.

## Stargate Project

[[Project Stargate]] (announced January 21, 2025) is the **$500B AI infrastructure commitment** by Oracle + OpenAI + SoftBank + MGX (UAE sovereign wealth). Oracle's role:

- Primary data-center infrastructure provider
- Initial Stargate location: Abilene TX (Oracle data center campus)
- Cloud infrastructure for OpenAI training workloads
- Multi-year build-out across multiple US locations

The Ellison-Altman-Son-MGX consortium represents the **largest single AI infrastructure commitment in history** at announcement.

## CIA origin

The company's name + first contract came from **CIA Project Oracle** (1977). Ellison + Miner + Oates were ex-Ampex employees who had worked on CIA video-storage projects; their RDBMS work for the CIA became the foundation of the company. **This founding connection has shaped Oracle's deep + continuous federal-intelligence-community business throughout its 50-year history.**
