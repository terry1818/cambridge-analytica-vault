# Liberty Counsel

Christian nonprofit legal organization founded **1989** by Mathew Staver. Litigates on behalf of conservative Christian causes including:
- Opposition to abortion rights
- Opposition to LGBTQ rights
- Religious-exemption claims
- COVID-related public health measures challenges
- Education-related religious-establishment claims

Headquartered in Orlando, FL. Founder/Chairman: Mathew Staver. Annual budget approximately $7-10M. One of several major Religious Right litigation infrastructure organizations alongside [[Alliance Defending Freedom]] · [[First Liberty Institute]] · [[American Center for Law and Justice]].

## Major cases

- **Davis v. Beason** (Kim Davis Kentucky marriage license case, 2015-2017): represented Davis
- **Multiple amicus briefs** in SCOTUS cases including Obergefell, Dobbs, 303 Creative
- **County clerks marriage license cases**: multiple jurisdictions
- **COVID public health measures challenges**: multiple jurisdictions 2020-2022
