# US Civil Service

Federal workforce system established by the Pendleton Civil Service Reform Act of 1883 to replace the spoils system with merit-based hiring and protected employment.

## Pendleton Act framework

- Merit-based competitive hiring
- Protection from arbitrary termination
- Political neutrality requirements (Hatch Act)
- Approximately 2 million federal civilian employees (excluding postal service)

## Schedule F threat

[[Schedule F]] / [[Schedule F implementation 2025]] is the operational vehicle for restructuring the civil service away from the Pendleton Act framework. The structural change - reclassifying policy-influencing positions as at-will political appointments - is the most-significant institutional transformation of federal employment since 1883.
