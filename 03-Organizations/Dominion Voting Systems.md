# Dominion Voting Systems

Canadian-American company that designs and manufactures voting machine systems used in many US jurisdictions including in the 2020 presidential election. Founded 2002 in Toronto by John Poulos. **Subject of extensive false election-fraud claims** by [[Donald Trump]], [[Rudy Giuliani]], [[Sidney Powell]], and others following the November 2020 election. **Plaintiff in major defamation lawsuits** against Fox News (settled April 2023 for $787,500,000), Powell, Giuliani, Mike Lindell, Newsmax, OAN, and others.

## False claims (November 2020 - 2021)

In the weeks following the 2020 election, Trump, his attorneys, and allied media outlets repeatedly made false claims about Dominion including:

- Dominion machines "flipped" votes from Trump to Biden
- Dominion software was hacked by foreign actors
- Dominion was connected to Venezuela / Hugo Chavez / Smartmatic
- Dominion was part of a CIA / German military server seizure operation in Frankfurt (the "Kraken" theory)
- Dominion had backers including [[George Soros]] and other unnamed figures
- Various other technical claims about machine manipulation

All of these claims were investigated by election officials, recounted in state-level audits, addressed in court proceedings, and found to be without basis.

## Investigations and court findings (Tier 1)

- Georgia hand recount produced results consistent with Dominion machine counts
- Multiple state election system reviews confirmed Dominion machines functioned correctly
- Federal Judge findings in defamation cases established the claims were false
