# US Department of Justice

US federal department responsible for federal law enforcement and administration of justice. Headed by the Attorney General. Includes the FBI, DEA, US Marshals, ATF, US Attorney's Offices, Civil Rights Division, Criminal Division, Antitrust Division, and others.

## Trump-era controversies (Tier 1, vault-relevant)

- 2017-2019: Mueller investigation conducted under DOJ oversight
- 2018-2020: [[William Barr]] tenure marked by allegations of using DOJ to protect Trump-allied defendants (Flynn dismissal motion, Stone sentencing intervention)
- 2020-2021: [[Jeffrey Rosen]] / [[Richard Donoghue]] resisted Trump's pressure to declare election fraud
- 2022-2024: [[Jack Smith]] special counsel investigations of Trump
- 2025+: Substantial restructuring under second Trump administration; multiple investigations into prior DOJ personnel announced
