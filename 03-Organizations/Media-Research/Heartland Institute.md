## CA-relevance

Heartland is **one of the institutional pillars of the Mercer-funded conservative ecosystem** that operates in parallel to the CA targeting infrastructure. The Mercer funding pattern includes:

- [[Heritage Foundation]] (mainstream conservative policy)
- **Heartland Institute** (climate skepticism / libertarian)
- [[Government Accountability Institute]] (opposition research)
- [[Breitbart News]] (media distribution)
- [[Cambridge Analytica]] (targeting tool)

- [[Parler]] (platform ownership; later)

The collective Mercer-funded portfolio represents a **coordinated long-term political-infrastructure investment** with Heartland playing the role of climate-skepticism amplification.

## 2012 "Unabomber billboard" controversy

In 2012, Heartland ran a billboard comparing climate scientists to Unabomber Ted Kaczynski. The campaign caused major donor defections and reputational damage. Heartland survived in significant part due to continued Mercer family funding.
