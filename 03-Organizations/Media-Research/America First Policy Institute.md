# America First Policy Institute (AFPI)

## Summary

US Trump-aligned think tank founded in **2021** by former Trump White House officials including **Brooke Rollins** (former Trump Domestic Policy Council director, later Trump 2.0 Agriculture Secretary), Larry Kudlow (former NEC chair), and others. Functioned as a **between-term policy and personnel bridge** for the Trump 2.0 administration, parallel to and overlapping with [[Heritage Foundation]] [[Project 2025]] and [[America First Legal]].

## Operational role

- Policy development continuing Trump first-term priorities
- Personnel pipeline for Trump 2.0 administration
- Coordination with broader Trump-readiness infrastructure
- Various publications and policy proposals

## Trump 2.0 alumni

Multiple AFPI personnel joined Trump 2.0 administration:
- **Brooke Rollins** -- Trump 2.0 Agriculture Secretary
- Various other AFPI alumni in cabinet and sub-cabinet positions

## Connection to Project 2025 and broader infrastructure

AFPI operated **in parallel and partial coordination** with:
- **[[Project 2025]]** (Heritage Foundation; broader policy blueprint)
- **[[America First Legal]]** ([[Stephen Miller]]; legal infrastructure)
- **Center for Renewing America** ([[Russell Vought]]; OMB-focused policy)

This **multiple parallel infrastructure** approach gave Trump 2.0 unprecedented readiness compared to the chaotic first-term transition.

## Connections

- **Founders:** Brooke Rollins, Larry Kudlow, others
- **Sister Trump-readiness orgs:** Project 2025, AFL, Center for Renewing America
- **Trump 2.0 personnel pipeline**
