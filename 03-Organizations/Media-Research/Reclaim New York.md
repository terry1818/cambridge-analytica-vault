Backed by [[Rebekah Mercer]] and the [[Mercer Family Foundation]]. **Rebekah Mercer chaired the board.** Other figures associated included [[Steve Bannon]] and **Kellyanne Conway** (who later became Trump 2016 campaign manager and White House Counselor).
