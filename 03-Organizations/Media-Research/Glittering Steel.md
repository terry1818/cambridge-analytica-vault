# Glittering Steel

## Summary

US film production company founded by [[Steve Bannon]] with [[Mercer Family Foundation]] / Mercer family backing. Produced **political documentaries** that fed the right-wing media ecosystem during the 2014-2018 peak Mercer-Bannon political infrastructure period.

## Role in the Mercer-Bannon infrastructure

Glittering Steel completed the **content-production layer** of the Mercer-Bannon stack:

1. **Opposition research:** [[Government Accountability Institute]] (Schweizer)
2. **Narrative distribution:** [[Breitbart News]]
3. **Documentary content:** Glittering Steel
4. **Targeting tool:** [[Cambridge Analytica]]
5. **Direct political vehicles:** [[Make America Number 1 Super PAC]]
6. **Platform ownership:** [[Parler]] (later, after CA)

Each layer produced output that the next layer could amplify or weaponize. Glittering Steel turned Schweizers GAI research into video content that Breitbart could distribute and CA could target.
