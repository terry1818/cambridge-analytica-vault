# Daily Wire

Daily Wire is a conservative media company co-founded in 2015 by Ben Shapiro, Jeremy Boreing, and Caleb Robinson (later named Wilkes brothers funding). The company has substantially expanded since founding from a primarily-news operation into a broader entertainment, publishing, and political-engagement company.

## Operational expansion

The Daily Wire's operational expansion has included:

- DailyWire+ subscription-streaming service (launched 2021), competing with mainstream streaming services through conservative-aligned content
- Daily Wire Films (entertainment production, including children's programming directly positioned against Disney-aligned content)
- Daily Wire Kids (children's content and educational programming)
- Bentkey (the children's-streaming brand launched 2023)
- Substantial podcast network (Ben Shapiro Show, Matt Walsh Show, Jordan Peterson Podcast distribution, Andrew Klavan Show, Michael Knowles Show, Megyn Kelly Show)
- Publishing operations and book-launch infrastructure

## Funding and ownership

The Wilks brothers (Farris and Dan Wilks, fracking-industry-wealth Texas funders) were substantial early funders and have remained operational sponsors. The broader funder ecosystem includes conservative-movement donor networks.

## Why this is load-bearing

Daily Wire is the operational scaling-up of conservative-media-as-cultural-product. Where traditional conservative media (Fox News, talk radio, conservative-opinion online) operates principally in the news-and-opinion space, Daily Wire has expanded into entertainment, children's content, and educational programming. The operational consequence is direct cultural-formation infrastructure that conservative families can substitute for mainstream-cultural products.
