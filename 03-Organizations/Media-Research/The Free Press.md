# The Free Press

## Summary

US subscription-based digital media organization founded by [[Bari Weiss]] in **2021** (originally as Common Sense newsletter on Substack; renamed The Free Press in 2023; spun off independent platform). Center-right contrarian positioning; emphasizes "heterodox" journalism critical of progressive cultural politics.
