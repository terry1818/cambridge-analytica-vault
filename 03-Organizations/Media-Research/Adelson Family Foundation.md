# Adelson Family Foundation

The Adelson Family Foundation is the principal Adelson-family philanthropic vehicle. The foundation operates substantial Israeli educational and political-aligned giving programmes and has been a principal funder of pro-Israel US institutional infrastructure across multiple decades.

## Operational scope

Adelson Family Foundation grantmaking has substantially focused on:

- Israeli higher-education funding (substantial multi-year gifts to Israeli universities)
- Birthright Israel programme (Adelson family was a principal early-stage funder of the Israel-trip programme for diaspora Jewish-Americans)
- US-based Israel-policy and pro-Israel-advocacy organisations
- Conservative-movement institutional infrastructure with Israel-policy alignment
- Addiction-medicine research (Miriam Adelson's personal philanthropic priority)

Annual grantmaking has been substantial across multiple decades, with the post-2021 (post-Sheldon-death) operational direction continuing under Miriam Adelson's leadership.

## Connection to broader vault network

The foundation operates principally as a philanthropic vehicle but its grantmaking is operationally adjacent to the broader Adelson-family political-giving programme. The pattern is operationally similar to other vault-tracked donor-family foundations ([[Mercer Family Foundation]], [[DeVos Family Network]], [[Koch Network]] foundations): formal foundation grantmaking on charitable categories that is operationally complementary to the family's adjacent political-giving programme.
