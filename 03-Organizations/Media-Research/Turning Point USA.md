# Turning Point USA

## Summary

US conservative youth-organizing 501(c)(3) founded in **2012** by [[Charlie Kirk]]. Has grown to become the **dominant conservative youth-organizing institution** in the United States, with **chapters at 800+ universities and high schools**. Sister 501(c)(4) **Turning Point Action** handles direct political organizing. **Annual budget reportedly $80M+ as of 2024.**
