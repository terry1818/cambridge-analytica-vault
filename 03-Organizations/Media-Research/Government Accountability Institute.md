# Government Accountability Institute (GAI)

## Summary

Non-profit "**investigative research**" institute co-founded in **2012** by **[[Peter Schweizer]]** (president) and **[[Steve Bannon]]** (chairman). **Heavily funded by the [[Mercer Family Foundation]]** since founding. Produced **[[Source Clinton Cash 2015]]** (book by Schweizer plus documentary film, 2015) -- the investigation of Clinton Foundation financial dealings that became central anti-Hillary content for the 2016 election cycle.

## Operational model

GAI operates as a **right-wing investigative research factory**:

- Long-form research into Democratic political figures (financial conflicts, foreign dealings, etc.)
- Material is published as books, films, and substantive reports
- Output is then amplified through [[Breitbart News]], conservative media, and broader infrastructure
- "Mainstream" media outlets sometimes follow GAI research (the New York Times notably followed Clinton Cash reporting)

This **research → media-laundering** pattern allows highly partisan research to gain mainstream-press uptake.

## Clinton Cash (2015)

GAI's defining product:
- **Book** by Schweizer published May 2015
- **Documentary film** released July 2016 (just before Democratic National Convention)
- Investigated Clinton Foundation financial dealings during Hillary's State Department tenure
- NYT, WaPo, and other major outlets did follow-up investigations based on GAI research
- Central anti-Clinton content piece for the 2016 election cycle
- Distributed by [[Glittering Steel]]

## Continuing operations

- 2016+ -- continued production of conservative investigative research
- Schweizer continues as president
- 2020 -- investigated Hunter Biden / Biden family business dealings
- 2024+ -- continues with various Democratic-figure investigations

## CA-era operational role

GAI was the **opposition-research factory** of the Mercer-Bannon-CA-era stack:

1. **GAI** produced the underlying research
2. **[[Breitbart News]]** published and amplified
3. **[[Glittering Steel]]** produced documentary versions
4. **[[Cambridge Analytica]]** targeted resulting content
5. **[[Make America Number 1 Super PAC]]** funded ad versions
