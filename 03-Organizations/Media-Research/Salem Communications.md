# Salem Communications

Salem Communications (now Salem Media Group) is the largest Christian-radio network in the US, founded in 1986 by Edward Atsinger and Stuart Epperson. Salem operates substantial radio, podcast, and online properties with explicit conservative-Christian editorial direction.

## Properties

Major Salem properties include:

- AM/FM radio stations across 100+ US markets
- The Salem Radio Network (syndicated talk-radio programming)
- Townhall.com (online conservative-opinion publication)
- The Hugh Hewitt Show (national conservative talk-radio)
- The Eric Metaxas Show (Christian-conservative talk-radio)
- The Charlie Kirk Show (syndicated distribution)
- Salem Now (streaming-video operation)
- Multiple conservative-Christian book and music publishing operations

## Why this is load-bearing

Salem is the principal institutional vehicle connecting traditional Christian-radio infrastructure to contemporary MAGA-aligned political programming. The integration of Salem-syndicated programming across local-market Christian-radio stations gives MAGA-aligned political content systematic distribution into evangelical-Christian audience environments that would otherwise be operationally separate from political talk-radio infrastructure.

## Salem and the Christian-radio market

Salem's principal operational scale comes from its Christian-radio market dominance rather than its political-talk-radio programming. The Christian-radio market is substantial: weekly listener base in the tens of millions across the US, with substantial concentration in suburban-and-rural Christian audiences that traditional political talk-radio has had less penetration into.

The integration of Salem-syndicated political programming (Hugh Hewitt, Eric Metaxas, Charlie Kirk, and adjacent voices) across local-market Christian-radio stations is the operational mechanism through which Salem connects political-talk-radio infrastructure to the Christian-radio audience. The cross-format integration is operationally distinctive within the broader US talk-radio market.

## Townhall.com and the online operation

Townhall.com is Salem's principal online operation, providing conservative-opinion content distribution that complements the radio properties. Adjacent Salem properties include HotAir, RedState (acquired through various transactions), and PJ Media. The combined online operation provides substantial online-traffic-and-engagement metrics that complement the radio audience reach.
