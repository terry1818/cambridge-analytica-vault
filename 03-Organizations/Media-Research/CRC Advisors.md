# CRC Advisors

## Summary

US **public-relations and public-affairs firm** that serves as the **operational coordinator and message-discipline vehicle** for the **Leonard Leo network** of conservative-political dark-money entities including [[Concord Fund]] and [[The 85 Fund]]. CRC Advisors (formerly **Creative Response Concepts** / **CRC Public Relations**) is **not itself a 501(c) entity** — it is a for-profit PR firm — but it functions as the **operational PR-and-strategy hub** for the broader Leo-aligned ecosystem.

CRC Advisors has handled **judicial-confirmation campaign messaging** (the three Trump SCOTUS confirmations and adjacent lower-court confirmation work), **policy-issue advertising**, and the **public-facing narrative discipline** that allows the Leo network to operate consistent messaging across multiple 501(c)(3) / (c)(4) vehicles.
