# Renaissance Technologies

## Summary

US quantitative hedge fund founded by mathematician **Jim Simons** in 1982. One of the most successful hedge funds in history. **[[Robert Mercer]] was co-CEO from 2008 until November 2017**, when he stepped down following media pressure related to his political activities and the [[Source Cadwalladr 2017-02-26 Observer Robert Mercer]] profile. Renaissance is the source of the wealth that funded Mercer's investments in [[Cambridge Analytica]] (~$15M+), [[Breitbart News]], [[Government Accountability Institute]], and other elements of the Mercer political infrastructure.

## CA-relevance

Renaissance Technologies itself had no direct role in CA. But the firm is the **wealth-generation engine** that enabled the entire Mercer-funded political infrastructure. The 2017 pressure on Mercer to step down from Renaissance demonstrated that **Renaissance's institutional standing was incompatible with continued public political activism** -- establishing a kind of corporate-governance limit on the Mercer political project.
