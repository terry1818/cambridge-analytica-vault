# Heritage Action for America

## Summary

US conservative 501(c)(4) advocacy organization founded in **2010** as the **political-action arm of the [[Heritage Foundation]]**. The 501(c)(4) structure allows for political advocacy that Heritage's 501(c)(3) structure cannot do. Operates the **Heritage Action Scorecard** rating Republican members of Congress, applying pressure for conservative voting records.

## Operational role

- **Congressional Scorecard** -- rates Republican legislators' voting records
- **Grassroots lobbying** -- mobilizes Heritage-aligned grassroots conservatives
- **Coalition building** -- coordinates with other conservative advocacy organizations
- **Trump 2.0 era:** active coordination on Project 2025 implementation, judicial nominations, federal restructuring

## Why this matters for this vault

Heritage Action represents the **political-pressure arm** of the broader Heritage Foundation infrastructure (which the vault has documented as Mercer-funded). The combination of:

- [[Heritage Foundation]] (501(c)(3) policy work)
- Heritage Action (501(c)(4) political work)
- [[Project 2025]] (Trump 2.0 personnel and policy blueprint)
- Coordination with [[Federalist Society]] judicial pipeline

-- makes Heritage / Heritage Action **arguably the most operationally important conservative institutional infrastructure** for Trump 2.0.
