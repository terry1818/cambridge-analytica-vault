# Council for National Policy (CNP)

## Summary

US conservative organization founded in **1981** by Tim LaHaye (Christian fundamentalist author of the *Left Behind* series) and others. **Operates as a coordination mechanism** for hundreds of conservative donors, activists, religious leaders, and politicians. Meetings are **strictly off-the-record**; membership list is **not public**. Has been called by some observers "the most powerful conservative organization in America that you've never heard of."

## How it operates

- **3 closed-door meetings per year** typically
- Membership invitation-only
- **Strict no-record / no-press rules**
- Members include major conservative donors, religious leaders, political operatives, and elected officials
- Functions as a **networking and coordination layer** sitting above visible conservative institutions (Heritage, FedSoc, Claremont, etc.)

## Known historical members / participants

Per various leaks and journalism over decades:
- Tim LaHaye (founder)
- Various Heritage Foundation leaders
- Federalist Society figures
- Major conservative donors
- Trump-era figures: Steve Bannon, Kellyanne Conway, others
- Multiple US Senators and Representatives over decades
- Christian Right leadership
