# Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN)

US evangelical Christian television network founded 1960 by [[Pat Robertson]]. Operates The 700 Club (daily flagship program), CBN News, multiple international affiliates.

## Significance

CBN is the longest-running and one of the most-watched evangelical television operations in the US. It provided the broadcast infrastructure that supported Pat Robertson's 1988 Republican presidential primary campaign and continues to be a major information channel for the [[Religious right]] coalition.

The 700 Club has been the venue for substantial movement-coordination interviews throughout the post-1980 conservative period.
