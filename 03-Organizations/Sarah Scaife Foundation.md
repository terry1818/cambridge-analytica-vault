# Sarah Scaife Foundation

Pittsburgh-based conservative philanthropy. Part of the Scaife family complex (Sarah Scaife Foundation, Carthage Foundation, Allegheny Foundation). Founded 1941 by Sarah Mellon Scaife; gained scale under son [[Richard Mellon Scaife]] (d. 2014).

## Strategic positioning

- Top-five conservative grantmaker historically
- Major early funder of [[Heritage Foundation]] (1973+), [[Federalist Society]] (1982+)
- Funded the "Arkansas Project" 1990s investigations of Bill Clinton via American Spectator
