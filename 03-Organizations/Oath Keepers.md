# Oath Keepers

US far-right anti-government militia founded 2009 by [[Stewart Rhodes]]. Recruits heavily among current and former military, law enforcement, and first responders.

## January 6 (Tier 1, established at trial)

- Multiple Oath Keepers entered the Capitol in a "stack" formation
- Maintained a Quick Reaction Force of armed members in Arlington VA prepared to cross the Potomac if called
- Five members convicted of seditious conspiracy (Rhodes, [[Kelly Meggs]], [[Jessica Watkins]], [[Kenneth Harrelson]], [[Thomas Caldwell]] - Caldwell convicted of related felonies, acquitted on sedition count)
- Total over 20 Oath Keepers convicted of J6-related crimes prior to the January 2025 pardons

## Leaked membership rolls (Tier 2, journalism)

- 2021: Hacked Oath Keepers data published by Distributed Denial of Secrets
- ADL and journalists matched membership to hundreds of active-duty military and police officers
- Membership names appeared from sheriffs, state legislators, and at least one sitting US Congressman (Rep. Brian Mast, R-FL, per ADL - Mast disputes active membership)

## Ideology

- Frames itself as enforcing the oath to defend the Constitution against "domestic enemies"
- Invokes a maximalist Insurrection Act doctrine - the President can suspend normal civil-military boundaries
- Rhodes repeatedly called for Trump to invoke the Insurrection Act to keep Trump in power post-2020
