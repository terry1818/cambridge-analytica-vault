About this Explorer

This Explorer is a visual companion to the Cambridge Analytica Research Vault -- a public research collection of ~3,500 sourced notes about the political infrastructure shaping contemporary US politics.

What you can do here

  • Topics - 9 curated entry-point cards covering the major analytic clusters.
  • Timeline - 47 documented events from 1981 to 2026, color-coded by cluster.
  • Power Map - 81 entities + 97 documented connections visualized as a force-directed graph.
  • Ask the Vault (chat button, bottom right) - coming soon. Architecture sketch in the vault under 00-Home/Future Builds.

Every claim is evidence-tier-labeled

  • Tier 1 -- Court filings, sworn testimony, official records. Citable directly.
  • Tier 2 -- Strong inference from Tier-1 facts. Cite with attribution.
  • Tier 3 -- Contested or single-sourced. Cite as allegation, not fact.

Source code on GitHub. PRs welcome with sourced additions.
