# January 6 Capitol Attack

Violent attack on the US Capitol building on January 6, 2021 by supporters of [[Donald Trump]] seeking to prevent congressional certification of the 2020 presidential election results during the joint session of Congress that day. **Resulted in 5 deaths in the immediate aftermath**, 138+ Capitol Police and DC Metropolitan Police officer injuries, evacuation of Members of Congress and Vice President [[Mike Pence]], multi-hour halt of certification proceedings, and an ongoing federal prosecution effort that as of 2026 has charged 1,500+ individuals.
