# Assassination of Charlie Kirk (2025-09-10)

## Summary

On **September 10, 2025**, **Charlie Kirk** (founder of [[Turning Point USA]]) was speaking to students at **Utah Valley University** at a Turning Point-sponsored event when he was **shot and killed**. A **single shot fired from approximately 175 yards** (160 meters) hit Kirk in the neck. **Tyler Robinson** (22, Utah) turned himself in after being identified from images and evidence; charged with aggravated murder; prosecutors will seek death penalty.

**The single most consequential political-violence event in Trump 2.0.** Triggered massive coalition mobilization including:
- **JD Vance hosted The Charlie Kirk Show** September 15, 2025
- **Stephen Miller spoke at Kirk memorial** declaring "we are on the side of God!" (archived in vault video collection)
- **Widespread reprisal campaign** against commentators perceived as celebrating
- **TPUSA leadership transition** to ongoing operations
