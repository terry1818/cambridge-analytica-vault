# Mar-a-Lago

Private club and residence located in Palm Beach, Florida. Originally built 1924-1927 by Marjorie Merriweather Post. Acquired by [[Donald Trump]] in 1985. Operated as a private club since 1995. Designated by Trump as his primary residence after his presidency. **Site of the August 8, 2022 FBI search** that recovered 102 classified documents and became the basis for [[US v Trump Mar-a-Lago Documents Case]]. Also documented as **Epstein-Trump social interaction site** including the 1992 NBC video footage, and the **2000 recruitment site of [[Virginia Giuffre]]** by [[Ghislaine Maxwell]].

## Documents case (2022 onward)

- Trump retained classified documents at Mar-a-Lago after leaving the presidency
- NARA recovered 15 boxes including classified documents January 2022
- Grand jury subpoena May 11, 2022
- June 3, 2022 attorney Evan Corcoran search of Storage Room yielded limited additional documents
- August 8, 2022: FBI executed search warrant
- 102 additional classified documents recovered including TS/SCI materials
- The documents and search procedure became central to the federal prosecution

See [[US v Trump Mar-a-Lago Documents Case]].

## Documented Epstein-Trump events at Mar-a-Lago

- **1992 video footage** (released by NBC 2019): Trump and Epstein at Mar-a-Lago party with cheerleaders
- **2000**: [[Ghislaine Maxwell]] recruited [[Virginia Giuffre]] (then Roberts, age 16-17) while Giuffre was working as a spa attendant at Mar-a-Lago
- **Epstein membership**: Epstein was a Mar-a-Lago member. Trump has stated he revoked Epstein's membership; the timing and reason have varied in accounts (some accounts: 2007 after Florida investigation; some accounts: earlier dispute)

See [[Connection - Trump-Epstein social association 1987-2004]].

## Operations as Trump's primary residence (2021+)

- Designated by Trump as primary residence post-presidency
- Site of multiple consequential political events including announcement of 2024 candidacy
- Hosted foreign dignitaries during and after Trump's presidency
