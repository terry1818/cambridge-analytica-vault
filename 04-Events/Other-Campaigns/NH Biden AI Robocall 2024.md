# 2024 NH Biden AI Robocall

## Summary

Two days before the **2024 New Hampshire Democratic primary (2024-01-23)**, an estimated **~25,000 robocalls** went to NH voters featuring an **AI-cloned voice of Joe Biden** telling them not to vote, and to "save your vote for the November election." Calls spoofed the personal number of a prominent NH Democratic operative. Investigation by NH Attorney General, FCC, and FBI traced the calls to **[[Steve Kramer]]**, a longtime political consultant working at the time for the **Dean Phillips campaign** (a Biden primary challenger). Kramer admitted commissioning the deepfake.

## Consequences

- **FCC fine $6 million** on Kramer (announced 2024-05)
- **Lingo Telecom paid $1M settlement** to FCC (2024-08)
- **Kramer indicted** in New Hampshire on multiple counts of voter suppression and impersonation of a candidate (May 2024)
- **FCC rule (2024-02)** explicitly made AI-voice robocalls illegal under TCPA
- **FTC voice-cloning challenge** issued
- Triggered legislative debate on AI-voice regulation

## Significance

First major US enforcement action against an AI-generated political deepfake. Established that:
- Voice cloning of a candidate falls under existing voter-suppression and impersonation laws
- Telecom carriers can be held liable for transmitting AI-generated calls
- Attribution is achievable when traceable telecom infrastructure is used
