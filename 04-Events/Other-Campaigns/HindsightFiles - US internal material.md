# HindsightFiles -- US Internal Material

## What HindsightFiles documents cover

A core portion of the [[HindsightFiles]] is **internal CA correspondence covering the 2016 US election cycle and after**. This material is the most journalistically-examined portion of the archive.

### Disclosures

- Internal emails discussing the Trump campaign engagement and payment flows
- Material on the **switching of primary data source from CA to RNC** in late September 2016
- Correspondence with [[Make America Number 1 Super PAC]]
- Material on the post-election plans for CA expansion (which the scandal interrupted)
- **Successor planning correspondence** -- emails referencing [[Emerdata Limited]] and the transition planning

## Significance

The US internal material is the **richest documentary record** of how CA actually operated internally vs. its public claims. Corroborates several Wylie/Kaiser disclosures:

- CA over-claimed in marketing what it actually delivered
- Trump campaign relationship was more contractual than ideological
- Emerdata transition was planned before CA's formal collapse
