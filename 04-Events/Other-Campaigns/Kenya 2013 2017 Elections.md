# Kenya 2013 / 2017 Elections

## Summary

**CA ran campaigns in secret during Kenya's 2013 and 2017 elections** supporting [[Uhuru Kenyatta]]. A CA employee said his predecessor at the company had been **found dead in his hotel room in Kenya while working on Kenyatta's 2013 campaign**. CA claimed on its website to have conducted a survey of 47,000 Kenyans during the 2013 elections. Per verified sources, CA worked with **[[360 Media]]** -- a company formed by Simon Gicharu (founder of Mount Kenya University) and Tom Mshindi (Editor-in-Chief of Nation Media Group). 360 Media developed online campaigns in 2017 portraying opposition leader **[[Raila Odinga]] as "a blood-thirsty individual who is also sympathetic to Al-Shabaab"** while portraying Kenyatta as "tough on terrorism." Norman Magaya of the National Super Alliance (NASA) called for investigation. ([[Source Wikipedia - Cambridge Analytica]])
