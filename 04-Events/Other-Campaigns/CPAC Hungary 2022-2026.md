# CPAC Hungary

CPAC Hungary is the first European CPAC, launched 2022 as a joint operation between the [[American Conservative Union]] (which organises the US CPAC) and the Hungarian government-aligned Centre for Fundamental Rights think-tank. The annual Budapest conference has substantially integrated Hungarian-government leadership with US conservative-movement leadership across multiple cycles.

## Operational pattern

The CPAC Hungary annual format has included:

- Keynote addresses from [[Viktor Orban]] and Hungarian-government ministers
- US conservative-movement speaker delegation including (across various cycles) [[Steve Bannon]], [[Kari Lake]], [[Mike Pence]] (earlier), [[Tucker Carlson]] (multiple visits), various Project 2025-affiliated figures, and US members of Congress
- Substantial European right-wing party representation including AfD, Brothers of Italy, VOX (Spain), and broader European populist-right leadership
- Coordinated programming on shared US-European intellectual themes including [[Catholic Integralism]] / post-liberal positioning, anti-immigration policy, and family-policy

## Significance

CPAC Hungary represents the operational institutionalisation of US-European right-wing coordination. Where the [[Bannon Movement international right]] had previously coordinated US-European populist-right leadership through informal-and-ad-hoc venues, CPAC Hungary provides recurring formal venue for the coordination.
