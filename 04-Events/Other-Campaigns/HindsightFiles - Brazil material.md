# HindsightFiles -- Brazil Material

## What HindsightFiles documents cover

[[Brittany Kaiser]]'s [[HindsightFiles]] release included a substantial chunk of material on **CA's Brazilian operations** -- material covered most extensively by **Folha de São Paulo** following the January 2020 release.

### Key disclosures

- Documents describe CA's outreach to Brazilian political parties **before the 2018 presidential election** (won by [[Jair Bolsonaro]])
- Material indicates discussions with multiple parties (consistent with CA's pattern of pitching multiple sides)
- Documents reference data-sourcing arrangements with Brazilian data brokers and consumer-research firms
- Some material on subnational/regional electoral work in addition to federal

## Significance

Brazil is significant because:
- 2018 Bolsonaro victory was a major populist-right milestone
- [[Steve Bannon]] (via "The Movement") advised Bolsonaro
- The Brazilian election is widely cited as having significant disinformation/WhatsApp-driven elements
- HindsightFiles establishes that **CA was actively pitching in the same election cycle**
