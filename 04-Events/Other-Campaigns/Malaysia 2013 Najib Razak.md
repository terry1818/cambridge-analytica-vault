# Malaysia Election (Najib Razak)

## Summary

[[SCL Group]] / [[Cambridge Analytica]] supported Malaysian Prime Minister **[[Najib Razak]]** and his **[[Barisan Nasional]]** coalition during their 2013 election campaign and continuing into 2014-2018 governance period. CA later admitted Malaysian work on its website per [[Source Wikipedia - Cambridge Analytica]]; [[Source HindsightFiles 2020]] material expanded the documentation.

## Documented operations

Per HindsightFiles materials released by [[Brittany Kaiser]] in January 2020:

- CA strategy work for [[Barisan Nasional]] coalition
- Audience-targeting work segmenting Malaysian Chinese voters and rural Malay voters separately
- References to specific Najib-era political messaging strategies
- Material on 1MDB-era political messaging coordination

## 1MDB context

CAs Malaysian work overlapped **temporally and operationally with the 1MDB scandal**:

- **1MDB** = 1Malaysia Development Berhad -- sovereign wealth fund established 2009 under Najib
- US DOJ investigation found ~$4.5 billion was misappropriated from 1MDB during the period CA was working for Najib
- Funds traced to luxury real estate, art, yachts (including financing of *The Wolf of Wall Street*), gambling, political activities globally
- Hundreds of millions traced directly to Najibs personal accounts
- Goldman Sachs paid $3.9B in 2020 settlements over its 1MDB role
- **[[Jho Low]]** alleged mastermind; remains fugitive

## The open question

**Whether 1MDB-laundered funds were used to pay CA** is not publicly resolved. The temporal and operational overlap is clear:

- CA was actively engaged with Najibs Barisan Nasional government during 2013-2018
- Najibs government was the source of the 1MDB diverted funds
- Substantial portions of 1MDB diverted funds went to political activities including foreign consultants and PR work
- CA's payments from Malaysian sources have not been publicly broken down by ultimate source

This open question is a **standing investigation lead** that has not received sustained journalist or regulator attention.

## 2018 election

- **2018-05-09** -- Najib lost the Malaysian general election to Mahathir Mohamad
- Loss occurred two weeks after CA's London insolvency
- 1MDB prosecutions began under Mahathir government
- 2020 -- Najib convicted on first set of 1MDB-related charges
