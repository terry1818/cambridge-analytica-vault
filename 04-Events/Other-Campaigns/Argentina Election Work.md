# Argentina Election Work

## Summary

[[Source Wikipedia - Cambridge Analytica]] lists Argentina among countries where CA/SCL executives publicly claimed to have worked. Specific Argentine engagements are **less publicly documented than CAs operations in other countries** but appear on CA's stated client list and were partially corroborated in [[Source HindsightFiles 2020]] material.

## Documented in HindsightFiles

Brittany Kaisers HindsightFiles release included material on Argentine campaign discussions. Per @HindsightFiles tweets and subsequent journalist follow-up:

- Documents indicate CA had pitched and/or worked for unnamed Argentine political clients
- Material on data-sourcing arrangements with Argentine consumer-research firms
- Timing likely overlapping with 2015 Argentine presidential election cycle (Mauricio Macri vs Daniel Scioli)
- Specific party/candidate identification has not been publicly confirmed

## Why the gaps

Several reasons Argentine CA operations are less well-documented:

- Lower profile in English-language journalism than US/UK/India/Kenya cases
- No equivalent of Wylie/Kaiser focused on Latin America
- Argentine press coverage has been sporadic
- HindsightFiles material on Argentina has received limited dedicated analysis
