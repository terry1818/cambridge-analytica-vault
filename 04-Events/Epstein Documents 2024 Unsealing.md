# Epstein Documents 2024 Unsealing

In December 2023, US District Judge Loretta Preska ordered the unsealing of remaining sealed exhibits and depositions from [[Virginia Giuffre]]'s 2015 defamation lawsuit against [[Ghislaine Maxwell]] (Giuffre v. Maxwell, 15-cv-07433 SDNY). The unsealing occurred in three tranches beginning January 3, 2024, ultimately releasing approximately 943 pages of documents.

## What the documents are

**They are NOT a "client list."** No client list exists as a discrete document in the public record. The unsealed materials are exhibits, depositions, and motion papers from civil litigation, including:
- Depositions of Giuffre, Maxwell, Sarah Ransome, Johanna Sjoberg, Juan Alessi
- Exhibits including flight logs, message excerpts, financial records
- Email correspondence
- Photographs

## What the documents did NOT establish

- They did NOT identify a "client list"
- They did NOT prove any specific public figure was a client
- They did NOT criminally implicate anyone who had not been previously named

## What the documents DID establish

- The breadth of Epstein's social network and access to powerful figures
- Confirmation of allegations against named individuals that had already been in the public record
- Operational details of the trafficking operation
- A baseline for the documented public record going forward
