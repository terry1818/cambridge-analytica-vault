# GENIUS Act signed (2025-07)

## Summary

Trump signed the **GENIUS Act** (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for U.S. Stablecoins Act) into law in **July 2025**. The legislation **legalizes dollar-pegged stablecoin operations at scale**, providing federal regulatory framework for USDT (Tether), USD1 ([[World Liberty Financial]]), USDC (Circle), and other dollar-stablecoins. **Was moving toward bipartisan Senate floor passage until 9 Democrats pulled back at the last minute**, citing:

- Weakened anti-money-laundering (AML) safeguards relative to prior drafts
- Trump-family direct financial benefit (WLF's USD1 is now federally regulated)
- Concerns about stablecoin use in foreign-state sanctions evasion

Eventually passed with Republican-near-unanimous + handful Democratic votes; signed by Trump. **The single most consequential financial-regulation event of the Trump 2.0 first year.**

## What it does

- Establishes federal regulatory framework for dollar-pegged stablecoins
- Allows non-bank entities to issue regulated stablecoins
- Establishes federal preemption over state regulation
- Weakens AML reporting thresholds vs prior drafts
- Establishes reserve-requirement framework

## Why it matters

- **First federal crypto-specific regulatory framework**
- **Legalizes operations that previously sat in regulatory gray zone**
- **Direct Trump-family benefit** via WLF's USD1
- **Foreign-state sanctions evasion concerns** - Tether is already documented Russia/Iran channel
