# Constitutional Convention 1787

Convention held in Philadelphia May-September 1787. Originally called to revise the Articles of Confederation; produced an entirely new constitution.

## Significance for vault

The 1787 Convention is the founding-era precedent cited by [[Runaway convention]] critics of the contemporary [[Article V Convention]] / [[Convention of States]] movement: a convention called for one purpose can produce something far broader than its stated mandate.
