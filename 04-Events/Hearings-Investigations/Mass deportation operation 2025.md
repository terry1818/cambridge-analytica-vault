# Mass deportation operation (2025-)

Coordinated escalation of US Immigration and Customs Enforcement ([[ICE]]) deportation activity beginning January 2025. Stated administration goal: deport "millions" of undocumented immigrants.

## Day 1 executive orders

- January 20, 2025: Multiple immigration EOs including:
  - "Securing Our Borders"
  - "Protecting The American People Against Invasion"
  - "Designating Cartels And Other Organizations As Foreign Terrorist Organizations"
  - End of asylum at the southern border
  - Suspension of refugee admissions

## Documented enforcement scope (Tier 1)

- ICE arrests Q1 2025: approximately 33,000 per administration figures
- Daily arrest target reported at 3,000 (Bloomberg, Reuters)
- Operations expanded to courthouses, schools, churches (categories previously protected as "sensitive locations" - protections rescinded Jan 21, 2025)
- Use of Alligator Alcatraz / Florida Everglades detention facility opened July 2025
- Activation of National Guard (Texas, Florida) for ICE support roles
- Deployment of Border Patrol Tactical Unit (BORTAC) to interior US cities

## Civil liberties incidents

- Multiple US citizens detained and held by ICE (federal court cases pending)
- Deportations to El Salvador CECOT mega-prison (under Alien Enemies Act invocation; SCOTUS decisions pending on the AEA invocation legality)
- Reported deportations without due process for individuals with pending asylum or legal status

## Litigation

- Multiple class actions on AEA deportations
- Sanctuary jurisdiction conflicts (Illinois, California, New York)
- Birthright citizenship EO blocked by multiple district courts, Supreme Court ruled June 2025 partially in administration's favor (limiting universal injunctions)
