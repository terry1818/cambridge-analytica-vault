# Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization (2022)

US Supreme Court decision, June 24, 2022. 6-3 (5-4 on overruling Roe). Held that the US Constitution does not protect a right to abortion, overruling Roe v. Wade (1973) and Planned Parenthood v. Casey (1992).
