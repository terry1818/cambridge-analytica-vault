# Seychelles January 2017 Meeting

## Summary

On **January 11, 2017** -- nine days before Donald Trump's inauguration -- [[Erik Prince]] met with **Kirill Dmitriev** (head of Russian Direct Investment Fund, the Russian sovereign wealth fund) in the **Seychelles**. The meeting was reportedly arranged by [[George Nader]] (UAE backchannel) and Mohammed bin Zayed (UAE crown prince, later UAE president). The meeting was investigated by [[Mueller Investigation]] and detailed in [[Source Senate Intel Vol 5 2020]].

## Significance

The Seychelles meeting is the **single most-documented direct contact between a Trump-campaign-adjacent figure and a Russian state actor in the transition period**. Prince was not formally part of Trump's team but his sister Betsy DeVos was about to become Trump's Education Secretary, and Prince had been involved in the August 2016 Trump Tower meeting with Zamel.

Per Mueller findings:
- Prince claimed the meeting was incidental and not authorized by the Trump team
- Mueller documented evidence contradicting Prince's "incidental" characterization
- The meeting topic per multiple sources was establishing a back-channel between Trump and Russia
- The UAE was acting as intermediary
