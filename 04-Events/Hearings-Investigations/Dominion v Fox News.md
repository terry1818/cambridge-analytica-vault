# Dominion Voting Systems v. Fox News (2023)

US defamation lawsuit. [[Dominion Voting Systems]] sued [[Fox News]] for .6B over Fox's 2020-2021 broadcast of election-fraud claims about Dominion machines.

## Settlement (Tier 1)

April 18, 2023: Fox settled for .5M - the largest defamation settlement in US media history. Settlement reached on day jury selection was to begin. Fox did not retract the on-air statements.

## Significance

The Dominion v Fox settlement is one of the documented institutional consequences for the 2020 election-fraud information operation. Per pre-trial document discovery: Fox hosts and executives privately knew the fraud claims were false while broadcasting them publicly. The settlement amount established the price of that conduct at .5M without producing institutional reform at Fox.
