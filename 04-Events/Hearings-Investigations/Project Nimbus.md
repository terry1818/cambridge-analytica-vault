# Project Nimbus

\.2 billion cloud-computing contract awarded April 2021 by the Israeli government to Google and Amazon Web Services to provide cloud infrastructure and AI services to the Israeli military and government.

## Documented terms (Tier 1)

- 7-year contract
- AI services include face recognition, sentiment analysis, image classification, object tracking
- Israeli government retains data sovereignty
- Google and AWS agreed to terms restricting their ability to deny service for Israeli military uses

## Worker protests

- April 2024: Google fired ~50 employees who protested Project Nimbus
- The firings have become a reference point in tech-industry labor / political-orientation discussions
- The Google workers framed Project Nimbus as direct technological support of the Gaza military operation

## Significance for vault

Project Nimbus is one of several documented cases where US-based tech infrastructure providers have entered contractual arrangements that complicate the analytical separation between "civilian tech" and "state surveillance." [[Palantir]] is the more politically visible case in this category; Google + AWS + Nimbus are the more financially significant.
