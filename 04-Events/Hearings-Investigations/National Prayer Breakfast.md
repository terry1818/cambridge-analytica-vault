# National Prayer Breakfast

Annual event held in Washington DC (since 1953) hosting the US president, members of Congress, and approximately 3,000 attendees including international guests.

- Historically organized by [[The Family]] / [[The Fellowship Foundation]] - secretive religious-political network led by [[Abraham Vereide]] (founder) and [[Doug Coe]] (longtime leader)
- 2023+ : Formal split between the National Prayer Breakfast Foundation (more publicly accountable) and the broader Fellowship apparatus
