# DCMS Disinformation Inquiry

## Summary

## Findings (per Final Report February 2019)

The report concluded:

- CA worked for [[Leave.EU]] (contra CA's denials)
- [[AggregateIQ (AIQ)]] helped [[Vote Leave]] circumvent campaign finance laws
- Multiple campaign finance violations in 2016 Brexit referendum
- Russian influence operations targeting Brexit
- Facebook's inadequate response to data privacy concerns
- Comprehensive recommendations for UK regulation
