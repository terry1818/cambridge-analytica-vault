# Citizens United v. Federal Election Commission (2010)

US Supreme Court decision, January 21, 2010. 5-4 ruling holding that the First Amendment prohibits restrictions on independent political expenditures by corporations, associations, and unions.

## Holding

- Corporations and unions have free-speech rights to spend unlimited amounts on independent political advocacy
- Disclosure requirements upheld (though enforcement varies)
- McCain-Feingold provisions limiting "electioneering communications" struck down
