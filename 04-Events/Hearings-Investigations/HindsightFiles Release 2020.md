# HindsightFiles Release (2020+)

## Summary

On **January 4, 2020**, [[Brittany Kaiser]] began publishing material from her personal archive of [[Cambridge Analytica]] documents via the **@HindsightFiles** Twitter account. The release ultimately encompassed **100,000+ documents** covering CA's work in **68 countries**.

## Significance

Largest single document release on CA outside of the Wylie disclosures. Establishes scope of CA's global operations far exceeding what was publicly reported during the 2018 scandal.

## Limitations

- Curated by Kaiser (selection bias)
- Most material unanalyzed publicly
