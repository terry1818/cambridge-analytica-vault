# Articles of Confederation

First constitution of the United States. In effect 1781-1789. Replaced by the current US Constitution at the 1787 [[Constitutional Convention 1787]].

## Vault relevance

The Articles are referenced in the vault primarily for the founding-era precedent of the [[Constitutional Convention 1787]] - a convention called to revise the Articles produced an entirely new document. This is the historical basis for [[Runaway convention]] concerns about contemporary [[Article V Convention]] proposals.
