# Patron Wing MOC

The Patron Wing is one of the three operational wings of the contemporary US conservative coalition the vault tracks (alongside the Religious Right and the Tech-Surveillance Wing -- see [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]]). This MOC surfaces the donor families, foundations, PACs, and connection notes that constitute the cluster.

## What this cluster operationally supplies

The Patron Wing supplies the capital substrate that capitalises the operational infrastructure of the other two wings. Without sustained large-donor capital, the religious-right's institutional infrastructure does not scale, and the tech-right's frontier-AI-and-data-integration build-out does not happen. The Patron Wing is therefore not just a funding source; it is the substrate that determines what gets funded.
