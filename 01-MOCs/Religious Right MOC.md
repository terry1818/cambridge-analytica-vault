# Religious Right MOC

The Religious Right cluster is one of the three operational wings of the contemporary US conservative coalition the vault tracks (alongside the Patron Wing and the Tech-Surveillance Wing -- see [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]]). This MOC surfaces the deepest nodes per sub-cluster.

## What this cluster operationally supplies

The Religious Right supplies the popular base and the multi-decade institutional continuity that the patron-wing's capital and the tech-wing's infrastructure cannot purchase. It provides: electoral turnout at scale (the principal Republican electoral base), policy-advocacy infrastructure (the coordinated legislative and judicial-priority pipeline), and cultural-narrative legitimacy that frames the broader coalition's policy programme in moral-and-religious terms.
