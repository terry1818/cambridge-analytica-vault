# Tech Right MOC

The **Tech Right** is the contemporary network of Silicon Valley figures who have politically aligned with the populist-conservative movement led by Donald Trump. While [[Peter Thiel]] was the first major Silicon Valley billionaire to back Trump (2016), the **broader Tech Right consolidation** occurred during 2020-2024, culminating in unprecedented Big Tech alignment with the Trump 2.0 administration in 2024-2026.
