## Three load-bearing insights

1. **The infrastructure is now inside the government.** Where pre-2024 it operated as outside-the-government litigation + organizing, Trump 2.0 has put election-denial personnel inside DOJ + DHS. The same operations now have federal-power backing.

2. **CSPOA / constitutional-sheriff doctrine is the unique American risk factor.** No other Western democracy has the "sheriff as supreme local law enforcement" doctrine. CSPOA exploits this to claim authority to refuse federal election oversight, allowing local-level election interference with thin legal cover.

3. **The paramilitary layer is operationally active.** Trump's January 2025 pardon of Jan 6 convicts restored Oath Keepers, Proud Boys, and Three Percenter leadership to operational status. The vault's existing [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]] needs updating: there's a **fifth wing** that is street-level violence-capacity.

## Counter-strategy implications

- **Voter-roll defense:** match PILF lawsuits with progressive-side litigation
- **Election-official training:** invest in non-partisan election-officer training (run by Brennan Center, Lawyers' Committee for Civil Rights Under Law)
- **County clerk recruiting:** mirror the right's election-denier-candidate strategy
- **CSPOA counter:** local-prosecutor + state-attorney-general coalitions
- **Paramilitary monitoring:** SPLC, Anti-Defamation League, Bridging Divides Initiative (Princeton) tracking
