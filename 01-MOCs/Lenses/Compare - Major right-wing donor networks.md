# Compare: Major Right-Wing Donor Networks

The contemporary US right-wing political infrastructure is sustained by **multiple distinct billionaire-donor networks** with different ideological emphases and different operational targets. Understanding their **differences and overlaps** is essential to understanding what has happened in 2020-2026.

## The five major networks

### 1. Mercer Network
- **Wealth source:** Renaissance Technologies (Robert Mercer co-CEO 2008-2017)
- **Ideology:** Populist nationalist; Bannon-aligned
- **Operational targets:** Cambridge Analytica, Breitbart, GAI, Parler, Heritage Foundation, Project 2025
- **Key figures:** [[Robert Mercer]], [[Rebekah Mercer]], [[Steve Bannon]]
- **Trump 2024-2026 relationship:** Strong but lower public profile than 2016-2018
- **Documented spending scale:** $100M+ across CA, MA1 PAC, GAI, Parler combined

### 2. Thiel Network (Tech Right central)
- **Wealth source:** PayPal/Palantir/Founders Fund/Mithril
- **Ideology:** NRx-adjacent; libertarian-evolved-to-authoritarian; anti-democratic
- **Operational targets:** Palantir, Anduril, Mithril/Narya/JD Vance, Curtis Yarvin, PayPal Mafia mobilization
- **Key figures:** [[Peter Thiel]], [[Elon Musk]] (adjacent), [[JD Vance]] (protégé)
- **Trump 2024-2026 relationship:** Operationally central; VP Vance is Thiel's protégé
- **Documented spending scale:** $15M+ to Vance Senate; multi-billion via Palantir and Founders Fund portfolio

### 3. DeVos Network
- **Wealth source:** Amway
- **Ideology:** Christian conservative; school choice
- **Operational targets:** Federalist Society, school choice, [[Erik Prince]] / Frontier Services Group via family connection
- **Key figures:** Betsy DeVos, Dick DeVos, [[Erik Prince]] (sibling)
- **Trump relationship:** Strong (Betsy in first-term Cabinet)
- **Documented spending scale:** Hundreds of millions over decades to Republican causes

### 4. Leonard Leo Network (Legal architecture)
- **Wealth source:** Marble Freedom Trust ($1.6B Barre Seid 2021 donation) + DeVos donations + other major donors
- **Ideology:** Conservative legal infrastructure; originalism / textualism
- **Operational targets:** Federal judiciary, Federalist Society, Judicial Crisis Network
- **Key figures:** [[Leonard Leo]]
- **Trump relationship:** Aligned (judicial nominations pipeline)
- **Documented spending scale:** $1.6B+ over coming years from Marble Freedom Trust alone

### 5. Koch Network (Older libertarian)
- **Wealth source:** Koch Industries
- **Ideology:** Libertarian (anti-tariff, pro-immigration historically)
- **Operational targets:** Americans for Prosperity, Cato, Mercatus, state-level political infrastructure
- **Key figures:** Charles Koch, David Koch (deceased)
- **Trump relationship:** **Strained** -- refused to back Trump 2016; backed Haley 2024; cooled to forced alignment
- **Documented spending scale:** Hundreds of millions across multi-decade infrastructure

## Convergence and divergence in Trump 2.0

The networks have **converged** on supporting Trump 2.0 (even Koch reluctantly) but **diverge significantly** on policy priorities:

| Issue | Mercer | Thiel | DeVos | Leo | Koch |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tariffs / trade restriction | Support | Support | Mixed | Neutral | **Oppose** |
| Immigration restriction | Support | Support | Mixed | Neutral | **Oppose** |
| Federal employee firings | Support | **Strong support** | Support | Neutral | Mixed |
| Christian conservative agenda | Support | Mixed | **Strong support** | Strong support | Neutral |
| Tech regulation rollback | Support | **Strong support** | Neutral | Neutral | Support |
| Crypto deregulation | Support | Support | Neutral | Neutral | Mixed |
| Judicial nominations | Support | Support | Strong support | **Strong support** | Support |
| AI safety regulation | Mixed | **Strong oppose** | Neutral | Neutral | Oppose |

## The historic shift

The **biggest single shift in US right-wing donor politics** in the 2016-2026 decade has been the **rise of the Thiel network to operational dominance** at the expense of the older Koch network. Specifically:

- 2016: Koch network refused Trump; Mercer + Thiel backed him
- 2020-2024: Tech Right consolidation; Thiel network protégés (Vance) gained political position
- 2024-2026: Trump 2.0 administration is **operationally dominated by Mercer-Thiel-DeVos alignment** with Koch in reduced supporting role
- Leo network operates **across all populist factions** via judicial pipeline regardless of ideological tensions

## Implications

The contemporary Republican Party is structurally a **coalition of right-wing donor networks** rather than a single unified institution. The networks coordinate via:
- [[Council for National Policy]] (historical coordination layer)
- Heritage Foundation [[Project 2025]] (contemporary integration)
- Various 501(c)(4)s providing dark-money-friendly inter-network coordination

Future political analysis of the Trump 2.0 era and beyond should track **which donor networks are gaining or losing operational influence** as more determinative than traditional left-right or partisan analysis.
