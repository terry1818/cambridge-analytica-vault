# Lens: The Crypto Wing

A new structural wing of the post-2024 coalition that the original [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]] did not name. Where the patron, theocratic, and tech-surveillance wings existed in identifiable form by 2016, **the crypto wing emerged operationally only in 2024-2025**. By 2026 it has its own funder cluster, regulatory capture apparatus, direct presidential family entanglement, and dollar-flow infrastructure that bypasses traditional campaign finance.

**Crypto is structurally as significant as the surveillance wing because crypto enables foreign payments directly to Trump-family entities in ways that traditional campaign finance law cannot reach.**

## How the crypto wing intersects the other three wings

| Wing | Crypto-wing overlap |
|---|---|
| **Patron class** | a16z, Founders Fund, Coinbase founders are patron-class actors; Fairshake = patron-class political vehicle |
| **Theocratic wing** | NAR-adjacent prosperity-gospel + crypto pump theology emerging; less developed |
| **Tech-surveillance** | Heavily overlapping; Thiel, Andreessen orbit; Palantir-adjacent personnel |

The crypto wing **doesn't displace** any of the three original wings - it **adds a fourth**.

## Updated coalition map

The three-wing coalition is now arguably a **four-wing coalition**:

1. **Patron class** (multi-generational; capital direction)
2. **Theocratic / mass-mobilization** (90+ years deep; voters)
3. **Tech-surveillance** (~20 years; methods)
4. **Crypto / financial-flows** (~2 years; direct foreign-payments channel)
