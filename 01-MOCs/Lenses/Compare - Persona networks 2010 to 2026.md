# Compare: Persona Network Infrastructure, 2010-2026

The capability to operate **large networks of fake online identities at scale** has been documented across the entire CA era and beyond. Tracking its evolution is essential.

## Timeline of documented persona-management platforms

### 2010-2011: HBGary Federal era
- **[[HBGary Federal]]** pitched persona-management software to Bank of America and US Chamber of Commerce
- **US Centcom Operation Earnest Voice** â€” military contract for managing fake personas in Middle East
- Each operator could manage ~10 personas
- Required significant manual effort per persona
- Exposed by **2011 Anonymous hack of HBGary Federal**

### 2014-2018: IRA / Russian state era
- **[[Internet Research Agency (IRA)]]** scaled to hundreds of staff
- Each operator managed dozens of personas
- Per Mueller indictments: industrial-scale operation with budgets, KPIs, shifts
- Targets: 2016 US election, ongoing
- Documented by Mueller, Senate Intel Vol 5

### 2018-2020: Spamouflage / Doppelganger emergence
- **[[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]]** Chinese network â€” thousands of accounts
- **Persona accounts increasingly automated** â€” less per-operator manual work
- Cross-platform coordination
- Identified by Graphika, Stanford Internet Observatory, Meta

### 2022-2024: AI-content era
- **[[Doppelganger]]** uses AI-generated content + automated translation + persona amplification
- Massive scale: 50,000+ assets taken down by Meta
- AI-generated profile pictures defeat reverse-image-search detection
- Cross-platform: Facebook, X, Telegram, YouTube

### 2023+: Team Jorge AIMS platform
- **[[Team Jorge]] / [[Tal Hanan]]** sells AIMS (Advanced Impact Media Solutions)
- **AIMS platform automates persona management at unprecedented scale**
- Undercover journalists shown demos manipulating Twitter, Facebook, Telegram, Amazon, Gmail simultaneously
- Sold as a service to any paying client

## Why this matters for CA-era thinking

CA itself **did NOT primarily operate persona networks** â€” it targeted real users. But the post-CA influence-operations ecosystem has **collapsed the distinction between targeting and persona networks**. Modern operations:

1. Build a persona network (AI-managed)
2. The personas profile their own audiences (engagement-based)
3. The personas generate content tailored to the audience (AI-generated)
4. The personas amplify each other (coordinated)

All in one infrastructure, requiring minimal human input. CA's 2014 architecture looks artisanal by comparison.
