# Lens: Education Capture

The **mass cultural-reproduction infrastructure** of the conservative coalition. Where the [[New Apostolic Reformation]] supplies religious mass-mobilization and [[Council for National Policy]] supplies elite coordination, **education-capture supplies the multi-generational cultural reproduction** that determines what voters believe in 20 years.

**Operates at three levels:**
1. **K-12 public schools** - Moms for Liberty + PragerU Kids + book bans
2. **Higher education** - Christopher Rufo + Project 2025 + funding cuts
3. **Parallel education infrastructure** - Classical Christian schools + Hillsdale College network + homeschooling (HSLDA-Farris pipeline)

## Why this matters

Education capture is **structurally the longest-time-horizon weapon** in the coalition's arsenal:

- A student exposed to PragerU content in K-12 votes for 50-70 years
- A university restructured under Rufo template produces aligned graduates for generations
- A homeschooling infrastructure produces ideologically-formed adults
- The cultural-reproduction multiplier is generational

**Counter-strategy implications:**

- **Teachers' unions** are operationally on the front lines (AFT, NEA)
- **Parent counter-coalitions** exist but are 10x smaller scale than Moms for Liberty
- **Higher-education leadership** is being purged faster than counter-coalitions can build
- **Public-library counter-mobilization** is the most-active counter-front
- **Multi-generational investment required** - this is decades, not cycles
