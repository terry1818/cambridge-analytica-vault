# Israel Cluster MOC

## Overview

The **Israel cluster** describes the structural overlap between:
1. **Israeli-state defense + intelligence apparatus** (post-1995 commercial-export era)
2. **Israeli commercial intel-for-hire + spyware-vendor ecosystem** (NSO, Black Cube, Cognyte, AnyVision, Quadream, Candiru, Insanet, Saito Tech)

4. **US-side patron-class Israeli-aligned donors** (Adelson family, Bernard Marcus, Paul Singer, Mortimer Zuckerman, Ellison family)
