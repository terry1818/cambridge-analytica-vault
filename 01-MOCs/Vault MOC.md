## Navigation depth and reading paths

The Vault MOC supports multiple distinct reading paths depending on the reader's existing knowledge and analytical interest:

**For a reader new to the CA story:** start with [[Cambridge Analytica]] entity node, then [[Christopher Wylie]] and [[Brittany Kaiser]] whistleblower accounts, then the [[Carole Cadwalladr]] investigator account, then the [[ICO Investigation]] and [[Mueller Investigation]] regulatory records. This produces approximately 60-90 minutes of orientation reading.

**For a reader interested in the network analysis:** start with [[Lens - 2024-2026 Power Map]], then [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]] and [[Hypothesis - Federal database supersedes CA model]], then the principal cluster MOCs ([[Tech Right MOC]], [[Comparative Analysis MOC]]). This produces the analytical framework that the rest of the vault is organised against.

**For a reader interested in current-state Trump 2.0 operations:** start with [[Project 2025]], [[DOGE]], [[Russell Vought]], [[JD Vance]], and the [[Big Tech Trump 2.0 Alignment 2024-2025]] event node. This produces the operational picture of the present-day political environment.

**For a reader interested in the religious-right historical arc:** start with [[The Fellowship]], [[Council for National Policy]], [[New Apostolic Reformation]], and the [[Theocratic Wing Infrastructure 1935-2026]] connection note. This produces the 90-year religious-right institutional continuity picture.

**For a reader interested in the constitutional-restructuring question:** start with [[Convention of States Action]], [[Article V Convention]], [[Lens - COS as Constitutional Capture Vector]], and [[Hypothesis - COS is the permanent capture arm of Project 2025]]. This produces the COS-specific analytical thread.

## Cross-cluster operational position

This node sits at the intersection of multiple vault clusters and its operational position deserves explicit reconstruction. The principal clusters this node touches:

- The CA-era operator cluster ([[Cambridge Analytica]], [[SCL Group]], [[AggregateIQ]], [[Emerdata]], [[Auspex International]]) and the personnel-and-methodology continuity that flowed from CA into successor structures.
- The patron / funder cluster ([[Mercer]] family, [[DeVos Family Network]], [[Koch Network]], the broader CNP-affiliated large-donor architecture, and the sovereign-wealth-adjacent flows including [[Affinity Partners]] / Saudi PIF and adjacent vehicles).
- The theocratic / religious-right cluster ([[Council for National Policy]], [[The Fellowship]], [[New Apostolic Reformation]], [[Capitol Ministries]], [[Family Research Council]], [[Alliance Defending Freedom]], [[Faith and Freedom Coalition]]).
- The Tech-Right / data-infrastructure cluster ([[Peter Thiel]] / [[Palantir Technologies]], [[Elon Musk]] / [[DOGE]], [[Marc Andreessen]] / [[a16z (Andreessen Horowitz)]], [[Anduril]], the broader frontier-AI ecosystem).
- The state-actor cluster ([[Russia]] / [[Doppelganger]], [[China]] / [[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]], [[UAE]] / [[Saudi Arabia]] / [[Affinity Partners]], [[Israel]] / [[Black Cube]] / [[Team Jorge]]).
- The policy-execution cluster ([[Project 2025]], [[Heritage Foundation]], [[America First Policy Institute]], [[Center for Renewing America]], [[America First Legal]], [[Convention of States Action]]).

The vault treats each of these clusters as operationally distinct but increasingly convergent across the 2024-2026 period. The convergence is documented in [[Lens - 2024-2026 Power Map]], [[Hypothesis - Three-wing coalition]], and [[Hypothesis - Federal database supersedes CA model]].
