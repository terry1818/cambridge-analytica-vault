# Trump Investigations MOC

A structured index of every formal investigation, indictment, civil action, and impeachment involving [[Donald Trump]] as principal subject from 2016 through 2026. Each linked note documents: the legal vehicle, charges/claims, primary evidence, witness testimony, court findings, appeals status, and the explicit gap between documented court findings and contested public-debate claims.

**Convention**: Tier 1 = court findings, primary documents (indictments, verdicts, sworn testimony with corroboration), public-record filings. Tier 2 = mainstream investigative reporting with named sources, multiple independent reportings. Tier 3 = single-source or contested claims.

## What the public record establishes vs what is contested

**Established by court findings (Tier 1)**:
- 34 felony convictions in People v Trump
- $83.3M and $5M civil verdicts in Carroll I + II
- $354M ($454M+ with interest) civil fraud finding by Engoron
- Trump Organization criminal conviction
- 10 separately documented obstruction episodes in Mueller Report
- Jury finding (Carroll II) that Trump sexually abused E. Jean Carroll
- Bipartisan J6 Committee findings of multi-pronged conspiracy

**Contested or undetermined**:
- Whether DC and SDFL cases would have resulted in conviction (dismissed pre-trial)
- Whether Georgia case will proceed at all
- Whether any pardons or self-pardons would be lawful for federal cases
- The full scope of co-conspirator culpability beyond those who pled
