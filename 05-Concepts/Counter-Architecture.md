# Counter-Architecture / Forensic Architecture

Investigative methodology developed by [[Eyal Weizman]] at Goldsmiths University of London (2010+). Combines architectural analysis, open-source intelligence, and forensic visualization to reconstruct contested events.

## Notable investigations

- Israeli airstrikes in Gaza (multiple)
- US Border Patrol shooting incidents
- Saydnaya prison Syria (chemical weapons attacks)
- Right-wing extremist attacks (Halle, Hanau)
- The murder of Walter Lubcke
- Bombing of Mariupol Drama Theatre

## Significance for vault

Forensic Architecture's methodology has become the gold standard for reconstructing contested events where state actors deny responsibility. The vault references this methodology as the model for how the same techniques applied to other contested cases (US police shootings, J6 documentation) shift the contested-fact dynamic.

The Israel-Palestine framing has been controversial - Forensic Architecture has investigated multiple IDF operations and its work has been criticized by Israeli officials.
