# Education capture

Strategic project of restructuring US K-12 and higher education systems to:
- Redirect public funds to private (often religious) schools via vouchers and tax credits
- Reshape public school curricula toward conservative-Christian framing
- Defund / restructure public universities

## Operational vehicles

- American Federation for Children ([[Betsy DeVos]])
- [[Hillsdale College]] (curriculum and personnel pipeline)
- [[Patrick Henry College]] (homeschool-to-federal-government pipeline)
- [[Project 2025]] (Department of Education abolition + voucher expansion)
- State-level legislative campaigns

## Significance

Education capture is one of the operational fronts of the broader [[Structural Capture Thesis]]. Reshaping the credentialing infrastructure of the next generation has multi-generation political effects.
