# Fascism

Far-right political ideology characterized by dictatorial leadership, suppression of opposition, ultranationalism, hierarchical society, mass mobilization, and forcible suppression of pluralism.

## Vault relevance

The contemporary debate about whether to apply the "fascism" label to the Trump-coalition coalition is one of the active analytic questions in the vault. Scholars including [[Robert O Paxton]] (initial reluctance, then explicit application post-J6), [[Ruth Ben-Ghiat]] (consistent application from 2016+), and [[Jason Stanley]] (consistent application) have provided varying frameworks.

The vault treats the analytic question seriously without political reflex. The structural-capture / demographic-defensiveness / post-liberal-replacement framework is partially overlapping with classical fascism but is also distinct in several respects (less mass mobilization, more institutional capture).
