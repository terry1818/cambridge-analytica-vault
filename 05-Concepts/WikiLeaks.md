# WikiLeaks

International non-profit organization launched 2006 by [[Julian Assange]]. Original mission: publish leaked classified documents. Became central node in [[Russian interference 2016]] after publishing GRU-hacked DNC and Podesta emails.

## 2016 publications (Tier 1)

- July 22, 2016: DNC emails (first batch released three days before Democratic Convention)
- October 7, 2016: Podesta emails (released within hours of the Access Hollywood tape becoming public; widely characterized as a deliberate counter-narrative)
- Both sets were obtained by GRU Units 26165 and 74455 per the Mueller indictment July 13, 2018
- The Mueller indictment identifies WikiLeaks as "Organization 1" through which the GRU laundered the stolen materials

## Assange status

- 2012-2019: Asylum in Ecuadorian embassy London
- 2019-2024: Belmarsh Prison UK
- June 2024: Plea agreement with US DOJ - pleaded guilty to one count of conspiracy to obtain and disclose national defense information; sentenced to time served

## Significance for vault

WikiLeaks is the documented operational example of how a putatively-independent transparency organization became an effective laundering channel for state-sponsored information operations. The 2016 case is the cleanest evidence of the methodology; whether WikiLeaks understood its role in real time vs was unwittingly used remains contested.
