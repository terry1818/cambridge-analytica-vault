# Seditious conspiracy

US federal criminal statute (18 U.S.C. Section 2384) prohibiting conspiracy to "overthrow, put down, or to destroy by force the Government of the United States... or by force to prevent, hinder, or delay the execution of any law of the United States."

## Maximum penalty

20 years federal imprisonment.

## Modern usage

The statute was rarely charged in the 20th century (Puerto Rican independence cases 1936, 1950, 1954; some 1980s domestic terrorism cases). Post-January 6, 2021 it has been used at greater scale than in any prior period.
