# Conspiracy theory

Explanation for an event invoking a covert plot, typically by powerful actors, that diverges substantially from mainstream/expert-consensus accounts.

## Distinguishing real conspiracies from conspiracy theory

The vault treats this analytic question seriously. Some "conspiracies" are documented (Watergate, Iran-Contra, COINTELPRO, the 2007 NPA). Some are uncorroborated speculation (most flat-earth, most chemtrail content).

The Epstein network is one of the cases that started as widely-dismissed "conspiracy theory" framing and ended as documented criminal-conspiracy. The vault treats specific claims on their evidentiary merits.

## Vault relevance

QAnon, Pizzagate, Stop-the-Steal subvariants, and post-2020 election-denial frameworks are documented cases of operationally-significant conspiracy-theory content moving from fringe to political-mainstream during the period the vault covers.
