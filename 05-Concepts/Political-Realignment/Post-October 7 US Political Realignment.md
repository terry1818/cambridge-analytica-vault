# Post-October 7 US Political Realignment

## Definition

**Post-October 7 2023 US political realignment** describes the structural shifts in US political alignment that followed the October 7, 2023 Hamas attack on Israel + subsequent Israel-Hamas war. **Key shifts:**

1. **Intensification of [[Israel Cluster MOC|Israel-cluster]] electoral spending** - AIPAC + UDP + adjacent infrastructure mobilized record spending against Israel-critical Democrats in 2024 primaries
2. **Progressive-Democrat primary targeting** - [[Jamaal Bowman]] (NY-16; ~$25M against), Cori Bush (MO-1; multi-million against), Summer Lee (PA-12; survived); ~10-15 House Democrats targeted
3. **Trump 2024 Israeli-policy positioning** - explicit pro-Israel campaigning; Miriam Adelson + Republican Jewish Coalition increased Trump 2024 giving substantially
4. **Democratic Party internal split** - Biden-era US Israel policy support contrasted with progressive base opposition; contributed to 2024 Democratic coalition fracturing
5. **Tech-right realignment** - Marc Andreessen + David Sacks + adjacent figures publicly cited Israel-policy as factor in Trump 2024 support
6. **Hostage-family lobby** - sustained advocacy throughout 2023-2024 contributed to political pressure

## Operational consequences

- Multiple progressive House Democrats removed from office via primary
- Trump 2024 won record Jewish-American support relative to historical Republican baselines
- Continuing Israel-cluster operational coordination with Trump 2.0 administration policy (RFK Jr. anti-pharma framing; Israeli pharmaceutical industry adjacent)
