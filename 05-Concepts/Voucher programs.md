# Voucher programs

Education funding mechanisms that redirect public-school per-pupil funding to parents in the form of vouchers / education savings accounts that can be used at private (often religious) schools.

## Vault relevance

Vouchers are the structural mechanism by which public-education dollars flow to private religious schools. They are central to the [[Education capture]] strategy and a top priority of [[Project 2025]] and [[Betsy DeVos]]-aligned organizing.
