# Christian Reconstructionism

## Definition

**Christian Reconstructionism** is the theological system developed by **[[R.J. Rushdoony]]** at the **[[Chalcedon Foundation]]** (founded 1965). It holds that **all of society should be reconstructed according to the laws that governed the Israelites in the Old Testament** (theonomy). The central tenet is that **God has tasked Christians with taking dominion over society**, beginning with Genesis 1:28, and that this creation mandate is in fact a dominion mandate commanding humans to bring every sphere of society into subjugation under Christ.
