# Effective Accelerationism (e/acc)

## Definition

Tech Right intellectual movement that emerged in late 2022 / early 2023 on Twitter/X. Stands in **explicit opposition to "Effective Altruism" (EA)** and especially to **AI safety** concerns. Core argument: **technological development should be accelerated, not slowed**, and any framework that calls for slowing AI development (AI safety, AI alignment, AI ethics) is morally wrong because it delays the benefits of technology.
