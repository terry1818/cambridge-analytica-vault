# Foreign Agents Registration Act (FARA)

## Definition

US federal law (1938, 22 U.S.C. Â§ 611 et seq.) requiring **persons working as agents of foreign principals to register with the US Department of Justice and disclose their activities, finances, and the identity of the foreign principal**. Originally enacted to counter Nazi propaganda in the US. Lightly enforced for decades, then **massively expanded in enforcement during and after the [[Mueller Investigation]]** (2017-2019).
