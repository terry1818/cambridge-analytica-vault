# RAGE (Retire All Government Employees)

## Definition

**Yarvin's signature operational doctrine**, abbreviated **R.A.G.E.** = **Retire All Government Employees**. Argues that mass firing of the entire federal civil service is necessary to break "[[The Cathedral]]" - the unified institutional consensus that produces predictable progressive outcomes regardless of which party holds elected office. Replace civil servants with **at-will employees loyal to the new executive**. Dismantle entire government agencies and replace with private corporations where possible.

## Documented citation

- **[[Curtis Yarvin]]'s Unqualified Reservations** (2007-2014) - original
- **[[JD Vance]] 2022 Senate campaign** - cited by name
- **DOGE operational planning** (2024-2025) - implements
- **Schedule F federal-workforce reclassification** (Trump 2.0) - legal mechanism
- **Russ Vought OMB operations** - implementation lead

## Operational implementation 2024-2026

1. **Schedule F** - reclassifies ~50K federal civil servants as political appointees subject to firing
2. **DOGE** - identifies civil-service positions to eliminate
3. **Trump executive orders** - reduce federal-workforce headcount targets
4. **OMB-coordinated agency cuts** - Vought leading
5. **Forced relocation programs** - civil servants forced to relocate or quit (used in Trump 1.0 BLM relocation; expanded Trump 2.0)
6. **Pre-confirmation hiring freezes**
7. **Schedule G + Schedule H** (newer categories) - additional reclassification mechanisms

## Why it matters for vault

RAGE is **not abstract theory** in 2026 - it is **operational policy being implemented at scale**. Schedule F + DOGE = RAGE in implementation. Counter-strategy that does not address this dimension misses the central anti-democratic operational move.
