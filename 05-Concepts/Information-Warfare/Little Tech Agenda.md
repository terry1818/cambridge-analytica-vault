# Little Tech Agenda

## Definition

**The Little Tech Agenda** is the explicit framing introduced by **[[Marc Andreessen]]** and **[[Ben Horowitz]]** (Andreessen Horowitz / a16z) in 2024 positioning **"Little Tech" (startups, crypto, defense-tech, AI startups) against "Big Tech" (Google, Apple, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft)**. The framing claims Big Tech operates as **regulatory capture coordinated with Democratic Party priorities** to **suppress startup competition**, while Little Tech needs **deregulation + protection from antitrust + access to government contracts**.

## Documented framing

### Andreessen's Techno-Optimist Manifesto (October 2023)
- Pro-acceleration, pro-AI, pro-crypto
- Anti-regulation
- Anti-"effective altruism" + AI-safety framing
- Anti-"woke mind virus"
- Setup for 2024 Little Tech Agenda

### Little Tech Agenda (2024)
- Anti-AI regulation (especially Biden-era AI safety EO)
- Anti-FTC antitrust enforcement
- Anti-FDA / regulatory restrictions on biotech startups
- Pro-crypto regulatory framework
- Pro-defense-tech contracts (Anduril)
- Anti-Big-Tech monopoly framing (claims Big Tech captured Democratic Party)

## How Little Tech operationalized in Trump 2.0

- **AI safety EO rescinded** (early 2025)
- **GENIUS Act** crypto framework (favorable to Little Tech crypto)
- **FTC reorganization** under Andrew Ferguson (anti-monopoly enforcement reduced)
- **Anduril Trump 2.0 contracts** surge (Little Tech defense-tech win)
- **DOJ antitrust priorities shifted** toward Big Tech antitrust (while reducing other antitrust)
- **Project Stargate** ($500B AI infrastructure announcement; Little Tech-aligned)
