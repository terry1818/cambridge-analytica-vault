# Seven Mountains Mandate

## Definition

**Seven Mountains Dominionism** (also Seven Mountains Mandate or 7M) is a **charismatic-evangelical variant of [[Dominionism]]** identifying seven cultural "mountains" or "spheres" that Christians must control in order to establish a Christian-governed society. Originated **1975** when **Bill Bright** (Campus Crusade for Christ founder) and **Loren Cunningham** (Youth With a Mission founder) reportedly received the same vision the same week, later joined by **Francis Schaeffer**. Re-emerged in modified form in the **early 2010s** as a manifestation of Kingdom Now theology and is now widely embraced in **New Apostolic Reformation (NAR)** and broader Christian-nationalist circles.

## How it differs from Rushdoony's Reconstructionism

| Reconstructionism (1965-) | Seven Mountains (1975 / 2010s revival) |
|---|---|
| Postmillennial: slow, incremental | Immediate-action, urgent |
| Theonomic: biblical-law focus | Cultural-mountain focus |
| Reformed theology (Calvinist) | Charismatic / Pentecostal / NAR |
| Bottom-up through institutions | Spiritual warfare; "taking" mountains |
| Multi-generational time horizon | This-generation urgency |

The 7M movement explicitly **rejected Rushdoony's slow timeline** in favor of immediate action. The charismatic-evangelical urgency made it more politically mobilizable.

## Application in current politics

- New Apostolic Reformation (NAR) leaders explicitly invoke 7M
- Trump 2.0 cabinet appointments map roughly to the 7 mountains (DeVos to Education; Pompeo to State/Government; etc.)
- [[Project 2025]] implementation structurally maps to 7M priorities (capturing each mountain)
- "Christian nationalism" as currently used is often functional 7M
- Many Trump-era cabinet members + religious advisors are NAR-aligned

## Why it matters

7M provides the **current popular operational framework** for theocratic-wing strategy. Where Rushdoony's framework is intellectually rigorous but politically slow, 7M is intellectually simpler but politically actionable. **Most Christian-nationalist activists you encounter in 2025-26 are 7M-influenced, not Rushdoony-influenced directly.**
