# Dark Enlightenment / Neoreactionary (NRx)

## Definition

**The Dark Enlightenment** (also **Neoreactionary** / NRx) is an **anti-democratic, anti-egalitarian, reactionary** political philosophy. Founded by **[[Curtis Yarvin]]** (writing as Mencius Moldbug) in 2007-2008 on his blog *Unqualified Reservations*. Developed further by **English philosopher [[Nick Land]]**.

**Core thesis:** American democracy is a failed experiment. The "Cathedral" - the unified institutional consensus of universities, media, foundations, civil service, and NGOs - has captured public discourse and produces predictable progressive outcomes regardless of which party holds elected office. **The solution: replace democracy with "accountable monarchy" - governance structured like a corporation with a CEO ("Stuart" in Yarvin's terms) accountable to a board.**

**The intellectual underbelly of the contemporary tech-right.** Direct documented influence on:

- **[[Peter Thiel]]** - personally connected; invested in Yarvin's Tlon
- **[[JD Vance]]** - cited Yarvin's RAGE plan in 2022 Senate campaign
- **DOGE / Elon Musk** - operational implementation of RAGE-style federal workforce purge
- **Trump 2.0 Schedule F** - operational implementation of Yarvin's bureaucracy-as-enemy framework

## Core concepts

### The Cathedral
Yarvin's term for the unified-narrative institutional consensus across:
- Elite universities
- Mainstream media
- Major foundations
- Civil service / administrative state
- Major NGOs
- Hollywood / cultural industries
- The Federal Reserve

The Cathedral produces predictable progressive outcomes by means of **distributed consensus-formation** rather than centralized command. Per Yarvin, replacing elected officials does not defeat the Cathedral; you have to dismantle the institutional capacity itself.

### RAGE (Retire All Government Employees)
Yarvin's signature operational doctrine. **Mass firing of the entire federal civil service**, replacing with at-will employees loyal to the new executive. **JD Vance cited RAGE by name in his 2022 Senate campaign.** **Schedule F + DOGE = direct operational implementation.** Plans to reduce federal workforce by ~50% per various Trump 2.0 statements.

### Accountable Monarchy / CEO-of-America
Yarvin argues democratic accountability is illusory; the only accountability that works is **shareholder-style accountability to a board** that can remove the CEO. Hence the **"CEO of America"** framing for the executive. Trump-as-Cyrus framing (NAR) maps loosely onto this even though Yarvin himself is not religious.

### Patchwork
Yarvin's vision of post-democratic world: thousands of small competing city-states / corporate governance zones (similar to Hong Kong / Singapore / Liechtenstein). Citizens can choose which zone to live in. **The competitive-jurisdictions thesis directly underlies the "network states" project (Balaji Srinivasan; Thiel-orbit).**

### Sovereignty as Property
Political authority should be understood as a property right. The owner (CEO) maximizes value of the asset (state). Voting is value-destroying. **This is the most extreme position in the NRx canon.**

## Connection to other vault elements

### Theological wing
Yarvin himself is not religious; NRx is secular. But operationally, **NAR's spiritual-warfare framing** + **Catholic Integralism's anti-liberal framing** + **NRx's anti-democratic framing** **converge on the same political program** even though they have different theological/philosophical foundations.

The Trump coalition contains:
- **Christian-nationalist mass mobilization** (NAR + Capitol Ministries) - religious framing
- **Catholic Integralism elite-philosophical wing** (Vermeule + Deneen + Ahmari) - Catholic framing
- **Dark Enlightenment / NRx tech-elite wing** (Yarvin + Thiel + Vance) - secular anti-democratic framing

**All three argue against liberal democracy from different starting points.** Counter-strategy must address each wing in its own terms - religious-counter for NAR; theological-Catholic-counter for Integralism; democratic-political-philosophy counter for NRx.

## Why this matters for the vault

The Dark Enlightenment is **what the most consequential figures in Trump 2.0 actually read**. Not Vance's *Hillbilly Elegy*. Not Trump's *Art of the Deal*. **Yarvin's *Unqualified Reservations* + *Gray Mirror***.

Counter-strategy that does not address the NRx framing will lose to it because:

1. NRx provides operational doctrine (RAGE, Cathedral, Patchwork) that maps onto specific policies
2. NRx provides ideological cohesion for the tech-right (atheists + Catholics + Christian nationalists can all sign on)
3. NRx provides intellectual permission structure for dismantling democratic institutions
