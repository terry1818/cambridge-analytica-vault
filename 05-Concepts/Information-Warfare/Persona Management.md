# Persona Management

## Definition

Software platforms and operational practices for **operating large numbers of fake online identities at scale** by a single operator. Each "persona" maintains plausible long-term presence across social networks, email, and other platforms. Used for influence operations, coordinated inauthentic behavior, opinion manipulation.
