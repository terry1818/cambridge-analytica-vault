# GDPR

## Definition

**General Data Protection Regulation** - EU regulation that took effect **May 25, 2018**. Establishes foundational EU data-protection framework including: **right to erasure, right to access, data-portability rights, consent-based processing requirements, breach-notification obligations, substantial fines (up to 4% global revenue), data-protection-officer requirements**.

**UK adopted UK GDPR post-Brexit** maintaining substantially identical framework.

## Why GDPR matters in vault

- **Post-CA-era regulatory response** - CA scandal was significant motivator for GDPR adoption + scope
- **Continues to be primary regulatory framework** for political-data operations in EU jurisdiction
- **Has substantially constrained CA-era data-aggregation methodology** in EU contexts
- **US has no GDPR-equivalent** - making US the relatively under-regulated jurisdiction where federated-database architectures can proliferate (per [[Hypothesis - Federal database supersedes CA model]])
