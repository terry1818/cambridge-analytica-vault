# Make America Healthy Again

Make America Healthy Again (MAHA) is the movement RFK Jr articulated through his 2023-2024 presidential campaign and which has become the operational framing for Trump 2.0 health-and-medical policy. MAHA combines multiple distinct intellectual-and-political traditions into a single operational programme.

## Constituent intellectual traditions

MAHA integrates:

- **Anti-vaccine advocacy** ([[Children's Health Defense]] base, [[Joseph Mercola]] ecosystem, [[Del Bigtree]] media operation, [[Aaron Siri]] legal infrastructure)
- **Alternative-medicine advocacy** (substantial alignment with naturopathy, homeopathy, supplement-industry positioning)
- **Food-industry skepticism** (anti-processed-food, anti-seed-oil, anti-pharmaceutical-industry positioning)
- **Anti-establishment-medical positioning** (substantial alignment with COVID-origin-theory advocates, post-pandemic anti-public-health-establishment positioning)
- **Environmental-toxin advocacy** (substantial alignment with longstanding environmental-health movements, repositioned in MAHA framing)

## Trump 2.0 implementation

The Trump 2.0 administration's MAHA implementation has produced substantial operational outputs:

- CDC restructuring including changes to vaccine-recommendation infrastructure
- FDA restructuring including changes to drug-approval and food-additive-regulation programming
- NIH restructuring including changes to research-funding priorities
- Substantial public-health-communication changes that align with MAHA intellectual positioning
- The broader Project 2025-aligned HHS-restructuring programming

## Why this is load-bearing

MAHA represents the most operationally consequential case of an anti-establishment intellectual programme being implemented at the cabinet level. The policy outputs are substantial and ongoing; the long-run consequences for US public-health infrastructure are a continuing thread.
