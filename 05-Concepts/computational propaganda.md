# Computational propaganda

Term coined by Samuel Woolley and Philip Howard (Oxford Internet Institute) for the use of automation, algorithms, and big data to manipulate public opinion at scale via social media.

## Documented operations

- [[Internet Research Agency]] (Russian state)
- [[Cambridge Analytica]] (CA's psychographic targeting plus Facebook-extracted profile data)
- [[Team Jorge]] (Israeli for-hire firm, 30,000+ fake personas across platforms)
- Various nation-state and commercial operations documented by Oxford's Computational Propaganda Project
