# Tech right

Loosely-bounded political coalition of Silicon Valley founders, venture capitalists, and crypto figures who realigned rightward (toward Trump, [[J.D. Vance]], anti-regulation) 2020-2024.

## Core figures

- [[Peter Thiel]] - founding patron; funded Vance's Senate run; ideological architect via Founders Fund and Palantir
- [[Elon Musk]] - flipped publicly 2022-2024; major Trump donor; ran DOGE
- [[David Sacks]] - All-In podcast host; Trump AI/crypto czar role
- [[Marc Andreessen]] - a16z founder; "Techno-Optimist Manifesto" 2023
- [[Joe Lonsdale]] - Palantir co-founder; American Optimist podcast
- Smaller orbit: [[Balaji Srinivasan]], [[Chamath Palihapitiya]], [[Keith Rabois]]

## Ideological inputs

- [[Curtis Yarvin]] / Mencius Moldbug - "Dark Enlightenment", neo-monarchism, anti-democracy thread
- Public choice theory via Buchanan / [[Cato Institute]]
- [[Ayn Rand]] objectivism via the founder generation
- Effective Accelerationism (e/acc) as a 2022-2023 social-media frame

## Stated grievances

- Antitrust enforcement against Big Tech under Khan FTC
- COVID-era platform content moderation (Twitter Files were a central recruiting frame)
- AI regulation, ESG, DEI - read as captured-administrative-state overreach
- SEC crypto enforcement under Gary Gensler

## Structural relationship to other clusters

Tech right shares the [[Federalist Society]] judicial pipeline with [[Religious right]], but has different end goals. The alliance is transactional - tech right gets deregulation and AI runway, religious right gets judges and culture-war wins. The connecting tissue is [[Heritage Foundation]] / [[Project 2025]] / [[Council for National Policy]].
