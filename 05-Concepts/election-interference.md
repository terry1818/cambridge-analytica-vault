# Election interference

Activities by foreign or domestic actors aimed at affecting the outcome, integrity, or perceived legitimacy of electoral processes.

## Vault-documented cases

- [[Russian interference 2016]] (IRA + GRU)
- [[Cambridge Analytica]] data operation
- [[Team Jorge]] for-hire operations across multiple countries
- 2020 election denial / Stop the Steal (domestic)
- 2018-2024 ongoing Russian operations (Doppelganger, Spamouflage continuity)

## Distinction

Election interference can be:
- Operational (hacking voting systems)
- Informational (disinformation, propaganda)
- Financial (illegal contributions, dark money)
- Coercive (voter intimidation)
- Structural (suppression laws)

The vault treats each case on its merits and tracks where the categories overlap.
