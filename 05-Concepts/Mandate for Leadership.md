# Mandate for Leadership

Heritage Foundation transition-blueprint book series. First published 1981 for the Reagan transition; subsequent editions for every Republican presidential transition through 2025.

## Editions

- 1981: First Mandate for Leadership - Reagan adopted approximately 60% of recommendations
- 1985, 1989, 1993, 1997, 2001, 2005, 2009, 2013, 2017 - sequential editions
- 2025: Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise - the [[Project 2025]] book - 922 pages, most ambitious in scope

## 2025 edition specifics

- Lead editor: [[Paul Dans]]
- ~350 contributing authors
- Coalition of ~110 conservative organizations
- 180-day playbook for each federal department
- Detailed Schedule F implementation guide

## Significance for vault

Mandate for Leadership is the through-line institution connecting the early Reagan-era movement infrastructure ([[Heritage Foundation]] founded 1973, first Mandate 1981) to the contemporary [[Project 2025]] / [[Schedule F]] administrative-state restructuring agenda. The institutional memory and policy-design capability spans 45+ years.
