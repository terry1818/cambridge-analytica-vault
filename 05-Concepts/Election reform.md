# Election reform

Umbrella term for proposed structural changes to US election administration. Includes:

- Voting Rights Act restoration / expansion
- [[National Popular Vote Interstate Compact]] (electoral college)
- [[Electoral Count Act]] reform (completed 2022)
- Ranked-choice voting expansion
- Public financing of campaigns
- Independent redistricting commissions
- Campaign finance disclosure (DISCLOSE Act and successors)

## Vault relevance

Election reform is the majoritarian-democratic counter to the [[Structural Capture Thesis]] minoritarian-coalition path. The two structural projects (election reform package vs. Article V / Schedule F / judicial pipeline) are the competing tracks shaping US democracy's trajectory through 2030.
