# School choice

Policy framework treating K-12 education funding as following the student rather than the institution. Includes vouchers, education savings accounts, tax credits, and charter school expansion.

## Strategic positioning

- Long-time priority of the [[Religious right]] and conservative-libertarian coalition
- [[Betsy DeVos]] and the American Federation for Children are top movement vehicles
- The [[DeVos network]] family fortune has funded multi-decade state-level school-choice organizing
