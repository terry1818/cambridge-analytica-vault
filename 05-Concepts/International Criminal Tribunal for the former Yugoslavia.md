# International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia (ICTY)

UN Security Council-established tribunal (1993-2017) prosecuting war crimes, crimes against humanity, and genocide during the Yugoslav wars (1991-2001).

## Vault relevance

The ICTY is the modern institutional precedent for [[Jack Smith]]'s tenure as a prosecutor at the International Criminal Court (Smith led the Kosovo Specialist Prosecutor's Office 2018-2022 before being named Special Counsel for the Trump investigations).

ICTY is also the precedent referenced in vault material concerning international criminal accountability for political leaders - the comparison case for whether Trump-era US conduct could be prosecuted by international tribunals (it cannot, given US non-participation in ICC).
