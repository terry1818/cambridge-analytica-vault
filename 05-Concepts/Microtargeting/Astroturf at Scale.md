# Astroturf at Scale

## Definition

The **industrial-scale version of [[Astroturfing]]** -- using AI-generated personas, content, and amplification to create the appearance of organic public sentiment at a scale and quality previously impossible.

## How it differs from CA-era astroturfing

CA-era astroturfing (e.g. [[Trinidad 2010 Do So Campaign]]) was **artisanal**: small teams of human operators created a coordinated campaign that appeared spontaneous. Effective but labor-intensive.

Astroturf-at-scale (2022+) is **industrial**: AI generates personas + content + amplification automatically. Effectively unlimited scale at near-zero marginal cost.

## Current operators

- [[Doppelganger]] (Russia)
- [[Spamouflage Dragonbridge]] (China)
- [[Team Jorge]] AIMS platform (Israel, exposed 2023)
- Various commercial CIB firms

## Detection challenges

- AI-generated faces defeat reverse-image-search
- LLM-generated text passes basic perplexity detection
- Cross-platform amplification distributes the signal too widely for single-platform takedown
- Foreign-jurisdiction operators are functionally immune from enforcement
