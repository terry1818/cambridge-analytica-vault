# Surveillance state

Government-controlled or government-leveraged infrastructure of mass observation of citizens. Distinguished from [[Surveillance capitalism]] (private-sector commercial extraction) in that the state actor uses surveillance for governance / coercion.

## US instances

- Pre-9/11: FBI COINTELPRO (1956-1971), domestic intelligence programs
- Post-9/11: [[Section 702]] FISA, PRISM, Stellar Wind
- 2020+: [[ICE]] / [[CBP]] commercial-location-data purchases via [[Data broker]] industry
- [[Palantir]] integration into ICE / DHS / DoD

## Vault relevance

Operational backbone of [[Mass deportation operation 2025]] and broader administrative-state restructuring under [[Project 2025]]. Counter-terror surveillance infrastructure built post-9/11 has been repurposed for immigration enforcement and domestic political surveillance.
