# Theocratic wing

Faction within the broader [[Religious right]] / [[Christian Nationalism]] coalition that explicitly seeks to restructure US government on Christian-theological grounds rather than via secular constitutional democracy.

## Distinguishing features

- Dominionist theology ([[Seven Mountain Mandate]], [[New Apostolic Reformation]])
- Open advocacy of Christian-supremacist governance
- Rejection of pluralism as a positive value
- Often distinguished from "merely conservative" Christians who accept secular constitutional structure

## Significance

The theocratic wing is the specific subset of the broader Religious right whose stated goals overlap most directly with the [[Post-Liberal Constitutional Replacement Thesis]]. Not all Religious right figures are theocratic in this sense, but the wing's increasing public prominence 2016-2024 is one of the structural markers of the coalition's ideological shift.
