# Copyright

Legal protection for original works of authorship, granting exclusive rights to reproduce, distribute, perform, display, and create derivative works for a limited period.

## Vault relevance

Referenced through [[Lawrence Lessig]]'s work and the [[Creative Commons]] alternative-licensing framework. Copyright structure is part of the broader landscape of platform / corporate control of information that intersects with vault themes around media consolidation and information environments.
