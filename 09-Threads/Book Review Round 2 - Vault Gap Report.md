# Book Review Round 2 — Vault Gap Report

## What this is

A second review pass — 41 further books on the vault's subject areas — checked against the refreshed vault inventory (2,211 files, after the ~190-note P1+P2 build). Each book produced a source note in `06-Sources/Books/`; this note aggregates the **gaps** they surfaced. It is a research backlog. Items are **[absent]** (no matching vault file) or **[candidate]** (uncertain — verify before creating).

## Results

41 books reviewed, **41 new source notes** added (none were already in the vault). ~160 gap items surfaced, ~130 distinct after de-duplication. The clearest finding: the P1+P2 build closed the *contemporary* gaps well, but this round exposes two thinner layers — the **historical prehistory** of every wing (1910s–1990s), and the **broadcast-media and electoral-mechanics** layers.
